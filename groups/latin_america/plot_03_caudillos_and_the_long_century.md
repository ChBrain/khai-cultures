# Plot: Caudillos and the Long Century

## Taxonomy

[play_latin_america](play_latin_america.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

The republics are free, but freedom does not settle them. Across the nineteenth and twentieth centuries the new nations live a long contest of oligarchies and strongmen, revolutions and counter-revolutions, dictatorships and the slow, hard return of democracy, an inheritance the colonial order left and the liberators could not resolve.

## Action

The century after independence is the century of the caudillo: the regional strongman and the landed oligarchy fill the space the crown had left, and the Church, the army, and a vast inequality shape the life of every republic. Revolutions remake some nations, the long revolution that convulsed [Mexico](../../cultures/mexico/play_mexico.md) from 1910 among them, and the revolution that transformed [Cuba](../../cultures/cuba/play_cuba.md) at mid-century. Movements of the people rise, populist and labor and agrarian, as in the Argentina that gave its name to a whole style of politics in [Argentina](../../cultures/argentina/play_argentina.md). The twentieth century brings military rule to much of the region: dictatorships hold power in [Chile](../../cultures/chile/play_chile.md) and across the southern cone, and civil conflict marks Central America, [Nicaragua](../../cultures/nicaragua/play_nicaragua.md) among them. Through it all runs the influence and at times the intervention of the United States to the north. By the century's end most of the region has returned to elected government, the long struggle for democracy and dignity neither lost nor finished, carried forward into a new century.

## Stage

- [Mexico](../../cultures/mexico/play_mexico.md): the long revolution from 1910 and the order it built.
- [Argentina](../../cultures/argentina/play_argentina.md): populism, the labor movements, and the cycle of rule and return.
- [Chile](../../cultures/chile/play_chile.md): the southern cone's passage through dictatorship and back to democracy.
- [Cuba](../../cultures/cuba/play_cuba.md): the mid-century revolution that reshaped an island.
- [Nicaragua](../../cultures/nicaragua/play_nicaragua.md): Central America's long revolutionary century.

## Tension

This is the hardest century of the grouping to stage, a record of revolutions, dictatorships, civil wars, and foreign intervention that touched almost every nation. It is set down here soberly and from the public record, without taking a side and without the detail of atrocity, because each member culture answers for its own past in full. What the long century shows is shared: the same inheritance of inequality, strongman rule, and a contested democracy worked itself out, nation by nation, across the whole of Latin America, a common ordeal that binds the region even as its particular wounds divide it.
