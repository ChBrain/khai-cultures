# The Nordics: a khai group

The Nordic countries (**Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland, Iceland**) staged as a
khai **group** - a play in the shared `groups/` collection that **references its
member cultures** rather than being a country of its own. The European north:
five nations bound by a shared social model and a common north, not by a single
tongue or a common state.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this production and answers for it.

## A group, not a culture

The Nordics is a khai **group**. Unlike a culture it has **no `geo.json`** (no ISO
of its own) and **no Hofstede pitch** (no single profile): it is a _referencing_
entry. Its `Company` casts the member plays, and the registry build derives the
group's `references` from exactly those casts - so the membership can never drift
from what the play actually links. Its language is **English**, the group's
logical lingua franca, since the five members share no single tongue.

- **Members:** [Denmark](../../cultures/denmark/play_denmark.md),
  [Sweden](../../cultures/sweden/play_sweden.md),
  [Norway](../../cultures/norway/play_norway.md),
  [Finland](../../cultures/finland/play_finland.md),
  [Iceland](../../cultures/iceland/play_iceland.md).
- **Kind:** `group` (the registry stamps it, so the website can tell it from a
  culture and resolve its members on the map).

## Provenance

The Nordic countries are a long-recognised grouping, formalised in bodies such as
the Nordic Council and the Nordic passport union. It is a cultural and social
sphere, not a single state. The staging and the architecture are original work.
