# Plot: the System of Integration

## Taxonomy

[play_central_america](play_central_america.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

The wars wind down and the peace is made, and the isthmus reaches once more for the old dream of union, this time not by arms or a single constitution but by institutions. A new system is built to bind the small nations together, and it reaches even across the sea.

## Action

After the conflicts come the peace settlements of the late twentieth century, and from that peace grows a new attempt at union: the Central American Integration System (SICA), founded to bind the isthmus through shared institutions, trade, and common cause rather than a single federal state. SICA gathers the seven isthmus states and, as a full member, [the Dominican Republic](../../cultures/dominican_republic/play_dominican_republic.md) across the Caribbean, the island nation joining the system though it sits off the isthmus and not upon it. [Panama](../../cultures/panama/play_panama.md), long a world apart through its canal between the oceans, takes its place in the regional system, and [Costa Rica](../../cultures/costa_rica/play_costa_rica.md), the peaceable republic, anchors the southern isthmus within it. Today the isthmus carries the great northward migration along its length, manages the canal that the world's trade depends on, and works through SICA to act as one region, the nearest thing to Morazan's dream the isthmus has built since 1841.

## Stage

- [Panama](../../cultures/panama/play_panama.md): the canal nation joining the regional system, a world crossroads brought into the fold.
- [Costa Rica](../../cultures/costa_rica/play_costa_rica.md): the peaceable republic anchoring the southern isthmus within the system.
- [the Dominican Republic](../../cultures/dominican_republic/play_dominican_republic.md): the full SICA member across the sea, the group's reach beyond the isthmus.
- [Guatemala](../../cultures/guatemala/play_guatemala.md): the largest isthmus state and a founding pillar of the integration system.
- [Nicaragua](../../cultures/nicaragua/play_nicaragua.md): a member of the system, bound into the postwar reach for union.

## Tension

The system of integration is the grouping's open question made institution: can the small nations, after the broken federation, the century of intervention, and the wars, finally act as one Central America, and even reach across the water to the Dominican Republic, or will old rivalries, the world-apart canal, and the steady pull of the north keep the isthmus a row of separate republics. SICA is the latest answer to Morazan's dream, and whether it holds is the stake the whole group rests on.
