# Central America: a khai group

Central America staged as a khai **group** - a play in the shared `groups/`
collection that **references its member cultures** rather than being a country of
its own. The isthmus between the continents, the slender land bridge of volcanoes
and rainforest that joins North and South America: the seven small states of the
isthmus and, across the water, the Dominican Republic, all bound together in the
Central American Integration System (SICA).

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house

## A group, not a culture

Like the Anglosphere, DACH, the Nordics, Benelux, the Visegrad Four, the Baltics,
Iberia, NATO, and the EU, Central America is a **group**, not a country. It has
**no `geo.json`** and **no Hofstede pitch**: it is a _referencing_ entry. Its
`Company` casts the member plays, and the registry build derives the group's
`references` from exactly those casts, so the membership can never drift from what
the play actually links.

- **Members:** the seven isthmus states and the Dominican Republic across the sea -
  Guatemala, Belize, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, Panama, and the
  Dominican Republic. SICA, the Central American Integration System, includes the
  Dominican Republic as a full member, which is why it joins this group though it
  sits in the Caribbean and not on the isthmus.
- **Kind:** `group`.
- **The growth story:** like NATO and the EU, Central America's plots trace its
  **evolution**, not a single event:
  - [the Maya Isthmus and the Conquest](plot_01_the_maya_isthmus_and_the_conquest.md) - the southern Maya world, the Spanish conquest, and the Captaincy General of Guatemala.
  - [the Federal Dream](plot_02_the_federal_dream.md) - independence in 1821, the Federal Republic of Central America under Morazan, and its fracture into republics by 1841.
  - [Bananas, Intervention and the Wars](plot_03_bananas_intervention_and_the_wars.md) - the banana republics, foreign intervention, and the late-twentieth-century conflicts, kept on the public record.
  - [the System of Integration](plot_04_the_system_of_integration.md) - the peace, SICA, regional integration with the Dominican Republic, and the canal and migration today.

## Provenance

Central America is the isthmus between the continents, the heir of the southern
Maya world and the Spanish Captaincy General of Guatemala, of Morazan's broken
Federal Republic, and of a century of intervention and war, now bound by the
Central American Integration System (SICA), whose full members are the seven
isthmus states and the Dominican Republic. Its members and their histories are
matters of public record. The conquest, the banana-republic era, the intervention,
and the civil wars are staged soberly, without atrocity detail and without taking
any side, and treated honestly in each member culture. The staging and the
architecture are original work.
