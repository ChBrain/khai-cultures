# The Anglosphere: References

The provenance behind this group. A group carries no Hofstede data of its own (it
has no single national profile); it is a referencing entry over its member
cultures.

## The grouping

The Anglosphere is the family of nations born of British settlement and bound by a
shared language, the common-law tradition, and close cooperation, with the Five
Eyes signals-intelligence alliance at its security core. The membership recorded in
the registry is **build-derived** from the member plays this group casts, not
authored by hand. Its five members are the United Kingdom, the United States,
Canada, Australia, and New Zealand. It is not a single state, and four of the five
share the Crown while the United States does not.

## The Five Eyes

The intelligence core grew from the wartime UKUSA Agreement (formalized 1946),
which widened over the following decade to admit Canada, Australia, and New
Zealand, forming the "Five Eyes" signals-intelligence partnership. It binds the
same five nations cast here and is a matter of public record.
