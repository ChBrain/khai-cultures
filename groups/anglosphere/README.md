# The Anglosphere: a khai group

The Anglosphere staged as a khai **group** - a play in the shared `groups/`
collection that **references its member cultures** rather than being a country of
its own. The family of five English-speaking democracies bound by a shared tongue,
the common law, and the Five Eyes intelligence alliance: the United Kingdom, the
United States, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this production and answers for it.

## A group, not a culture

Like DACH, the Nordics, Benelux, the Visegrad Four, the Baltics, Iberia, NATO, and
the EU, the Anglosphere is a **group**, not a country. It has **no `geo.json`** and
**no Hofstede pitch**: it is a _referencing_ entry. Its `Company` casts the member
plays, and the registry build derives the group's `references` from exactly those
casts, so the membership can never drift from what the play actually links.

- **Members:** the five core English-speaking nations and the Five Eyes circle -
  the United Kingdom, the United States, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand.
- **Kind:** `group`.
- **The growth story:** like NATO and the EU, the Anglosphere's plots trace its
  **evolution**, not a single event:
  - [the English Seed](plot_01_the_english_seed.md) - the spread of the language and the common law, and the American break of 1776.
  - [the Dominions](plot_02_the_dominions.md) - Canada, Australia, and New Zealand growing to nationhood under a shared Crown.
  - [the Wars and the Special Relationship](plot_03_the_wars_and_the_special_relationship.md) - the world wars, the dominions' nationhood in blood, and the passing of leadership across the Atlantic.
  - [the Five Eyes](plot_04_the_five_eyes.md) - the UKUSA agreement grown into the deepest intelligence alliance in history, and each member's turn toward its own region.

## Provenance

The Anglosphere is a family of nations descended from British settlement and bound
by the English language, the common-law tradition, the Commonwealth (for all but
the United States), and the Five Eyes signals-intelligence alliance that grew from
the wartime UKUSA Agreement. Its members and their histories are matters of public
record; the settler founding of these nations rested on the dispossession of their
first peoples, treated honestly in the staging and in each member culture. The
staging and the architecture are original work.
