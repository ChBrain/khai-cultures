# Plot: the Bangkok Declaration

## Taxonomy

[play_asean](play_asean.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Southeast Asia in the mid-1960s is a region at odds with itself: Indonesia's Konfrontasi against the new Malaysia has only just ended, the Philippines and Malaysia dispute Sabah, Singapore has been put out of Malaysia to stand alone, and the Vietnam War burns along the region's mainland spine. Out of that quarrelsome moment, five foreign ministers gather in Bangkok to try the opposite of confrontation.

## Action

On 8 August 1967, in the main hall of the Thai foreign ministry in Bangkok, the foreign ministers of [Thailand](../../cultures/thailand/play_thailand.md), [Indonesia](../../cultures/indonesia/play_indonesia.md), [Malaysia](../../cultures/malaysia/play_malaysia.md), [the Philippines](../../cultures/philippines/play_philippines.md), and [Singapore](../../cultures/singapore/play_singapore.md) sign the two-page ASEAN Declaration, the Bangkok Declaration, and found the Association of Southeast Asian Nations. The declaration promises cooperation in economy, society, and culture, and peace and stability in the region; its deeper purpose is plainer still, that neighbours who had lately confronted one another, Indonesia and Malaysia barely a year out of Konfrontasi, would bind themselves to the meeting room instead of the battlefield. The new association is deliberately light: no supranational law, no standing army, a rotating chair, and the habit of consultation. In 1971 the members declare the region a Zone of Peace, Freedom and Neutrality, and in 1976, at their first summit in Bali, they sign the Treaty of Amity and Cooperation, renouncing force among themselves and setting consensus and non-interference as the association's working law.

## Stage

- [Thailand](../../cultures/thailand/play_thailand.md): the host and broker, the one member never colonised, whose capital gives the declaration its name.
- [Indonesia](../../cultures/indonesia/play_indonesia.md): the largest founder, turning from Konfrontasi to leadership of the new association.
- [Malaysia](../../cultures/malaysia/play_malaysia.md): the young federation lately confronted by one neighbour and disputed by another, choosing the table.
- [the Philippines](../../cultures/philippines/play_philippines.md): a founder carrying its Sabah dispute into the room rather than beyond it.
- [Singapore](../../cultures/singapore/play_singapore.md): the newly separated city-state, two years old, buying security by association.

## Tension

The founding is a wager, not a settlement: none of the quarrels of 1967 is resolved in Bangkok, they are only placed inside a room where the parties must keep meeting. The association is born in the shadow of the Cold War and of the war in Vietnam, with the states across that line absent, so the founding five are a fragment of the region claiming to speak for it. Whether a two-page promise of consultation can hold neighbours who were lately at confrontation, and grow to take in the rest of the region, is the open stake the Bangkok signing sets.
