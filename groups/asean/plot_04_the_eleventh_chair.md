# Plot: the eleventh chair

## Taxonomy

[play_asean](play_asean.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

A generation after the ten was completed, the association makes room again. The region's youngest nation, born in 2002 within sight of the archipelago's eastern end, has waited at the door since 2011; its admission would prove the club can still grow, and would finish the map of Southeast Asia at last.

## Action

[Timor-Leste](../../cultures/timor_leste/play_timor_leste.md) applies for membership in 2011, nine years after its independence, and serves the longest apprenticeship in the association's history: agreed in principle and seated as an observer in 2022, given a roadmap of accession in 2023, acceding to the association's treaties and appearing across its meetings while its small administration is built out to carry the load. On 26 October 2025, at the 47th summit in Kuala Lumpur, [Malaysia](../../cultures/malaysia/play_malaysia.md) in the chair and [Indonesia](../../cultures/indonesia/play_indonesia.md), the neighbour whose past occupation of the young nation had been the wound between them, among the sponsors, Timor-Leste is formally admitted as the eleventh member, the first enlargement since 1999. The eleventh chair is drawn up to a table that now carries the association's largest projects: the ASEAN Economic Community knitting the eleven economies into one market, and the claim of ASEAN centrality, the summits at which the surrounding great powers meet on Southeast Asia's ground and terms, the small states of the region insisting on holding their own centre.

## Stage

- [Timor-Leste](../../cultures/timor_leste/play_timor_leste.md): the eleventh member, the region's youngest nation seated in 2025 after a fourteen-year wait.
- [Malaysia](../../cultures/malaysia/play_malaysia.md): the 2025 chair, host of the Kuala Lumpur summit that completed the eleven.
- [Indonesia](../../cultures/indonesia/play_indonesia.md): the once-occupying neighbour turned sponsor, reconciliation sealed by admission.
- [Singapore](../../cultures/singapore/play_singapore.md): the richest member, the measure of the gulf the one market must span.

## Tension

The eleventh chair completes the map and widens the gulf: the newest member is the association's poorest, and its admission stretches the one-market project across the greatest distance yet between the association's richest and poorest tables. And the completed association holds its centre in a harder world, courted and pressed by great powers on every side, its claim to centrality tested by every crisis from the South China Sea to Myanmar. Whether a finally complete ASEAN can be more than the sum of its meetings, a centre that holds, is the stake the eleventh chair leaves open.
