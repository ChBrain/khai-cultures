# Plot: the Widening

## Taxonomy

[play_eu](play_eu.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

The common market of the six prospers, and the nations left outside it begin to want in. Over three decades the community opens its door, north and west and then south, drawing in nations that seek the prosperity of the market and, for some, the anchor of a young democracy to a community of law.

## Action

The community widens in waves. In 1973 [Denmark](../../cultures/denmark/play_denmark.md) and [Ireland](../../cultures/ireland/play_ireland.md) join (with the United Kingdom). In 1981 [Greece](../../cultures/greece/play_greece.md) comes in, fresh from the colonels; in 1986 [Spain](../../cultures/spain/play_spain.md) and [Portugal](../../cultures/portugal/play_portugal.md) follow, anchoring the post-dictatorship south to democracy. In 1995 the neutrals [Austria](../../cultures/austria/play_austria.md), [Finland](../../cultures/finland/play_finland.md), and [Sweden](../../cultures/sweden/play_sweden.md) join a Union that has, by then, become more than a market. Each wave makes the community larger, richer, and more various.

## Stage

- [Ireland](../../cultures/ireland/play_ireland.md): the small nation transformed by membership.
- [Greece](../../cultures/greece/play_greece.md), [Spain](../../cultures/spain/play_spain.md), [Portugal](../../cultures/portugal/play_portugal.md): the southern democracies anchored by accession.
- [Finland](../../cultures/finland/play_finland.md), [Austria](../../cultures/austria/play_austria.md), [Sweden](../../cultures/sweden/play_sweden.md): the neutrals who join in 1995.

## Tension

Each enlargement spreads the market's prosperity and ties young democracies to a community of law, proving the open door a force for stability. But every new member is another sovereign voice at the table, another tongue, another interest, and the wider the community grows the harder it becomes to agree - the price of widening paid in the difficulty of acting as one.
