# The European Union: a khai group

The European Union staged as a khai **group** - a play in the shared `groups/`
collection that **references its member cultures** rather than being a country of
its own. The union of twenty-seven European democracies that pool part of their
sovereignty in a single market and a shared body of law - and that, like NATO,
grew and changed over time.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this production and answers for it.

## A group, not a culture

Like DACH, the Nordics, Benelux, the Visegrad Four, the Baltics, Iberia, and
NATO, the EU is a **group**, not a country. It has **no `geo.json`** and **no
Hofstede pitch**: it is a _referencing_ entry. Its `Company` casts the member
plays, and the registry build derives the group's `references` from exactly those
casts, so the membership can never drift from what the play actually links.

- **Members:** all twenty-seven member states, from the six founders of 1957 to
  Croatia in 2013 (listed with their accession years in the play).
- **Kind:** `group`.
- **The growth story:** like NATO, the EU's plots trace its **evolution** of
  widening and deepening, not a single event:
  - [the Treaty of Rome, 1957](plot_01_rome_1957.md) - the six founders and the common market.
  - [the Widening](plot_02_the_widening.md) - the 1973-1995 enlargements, north and south.
  - [Maastricht and the Euro](plot_03_maastricht.md) - the leap from market to Union, the citizenship, and the single currency.
  - [the Big Bang and the Strains](plot_04_big_bang_and_strains.md) - the eastward enlargement to twenty-eight, the crises, and Brexit back to twenty-seven.

## Provenance

The European Union grew out of the European Coal and Steel Community (1951) and
the European Economic Community founded by the Treaty of Rome (1957), and took its
present form with the Maastricht Treaty (1992). Its membership and accession dates
are matters of public record. The United Kingdom, a member from 1973, left in
2020 and so is not among the twenty-seven. The staging and the architecture are
original work.
