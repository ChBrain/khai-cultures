# DACH: a khai group

DACH (**D**eutschland, **A**ustria/Österreich, **C**onfoederatio **H**elvetica)
staged as a khai **group** — a play in the shared `groups/` collection that
**references its member cultures** rather than being a country of its own. The
German-speaking sphere: three nations bound by a common written language, not by
a common state.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this production and answers for it.

## A group, not a culture

DACH is the house's first **group**. Unlike a culture it has **no `geo.json`**
(no ISO of its own) and **no Hofstede pitch** (no single profile): it is a
_referencing_ entry. Its `Company` casts the member plays, and the registry build
derives the group's `references` from exactly those casts — so the membership can
never drift from what the play actually links.

- **Members:** [Germany](../../cultures/germany/play_germany.md),
  [Austria](../../cultures/austria/play_austria.md),
  [Switzerland](../../cultures/switzerland/play_switzerland.md).
- **Kind:** `group` (the registry stamps it, so the website can tell it from a
  culture and resolve its members on the map).

## Provenance

DACH is the common informal label for the German-speaking area, from the three
countries' codes (D, A, CH). It is a cultural-economic sphere, not a treaty
organisation. The staging and the architecture are original work.
