# Benelux: References

The provenance behind this group. A group carries no Hofstede data of its own (it
has no single national profile); it is a referencing entry over its member
cultures.

## The grouping

Benelux is the standard name for the union of the three Low Countries. The
membership recorded in the registry is **build-derived** from the member plays
this group casts, not authored by hand.

| ISO | Country         | Member culture                                                |
| --- | --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| BE  | Belgium         | [belgium](../../cultures/belgium/play_belgium.md)             |
| NL  | the Netherlands | [netherlands](../../cultures/netherlands/play_netherlands.md) |
| LU  | Luxembourg      | [luxembourg](../../cultures/luxembourg/play_luxembourg.md)    |

The grouping is bound by a real treaty union: the customs convention signed in
London in 1944 (in force 1948), deepened into the Benelux Economic Union by the
treaty of 1958 and renewed in 2008. It is the oldest customs union of modern
Europe and the acknowledged prototype of the European Economic Community, not a
single language or a single state.
