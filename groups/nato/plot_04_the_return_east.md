# Plot: the Return East

## Taxonomy

[play_nato](play_nato.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Freed from Soviet rule, the nations of the East do not wait to be courted; they ask to come in, determined never again to be left in a grey zone between great powers. The alliance opens its door eastward, toward the very ground it once faced across the line, and toward the border of a Russia that watches the advance with deepening anger.

## Action

The growth comes in waves. In 1999 [Poland](../../cultures/poland/play_poland.md), [Czechia](../../cultures/czechia/play_czechia.md), and [Hungary](../../cultures/hungary/play_hungary.md) become the first former Warsaw Pact states to join. In 2004 seven more enter at once, the three Baltic states [Estonia](../../cultures/estonia/play_estonia.md), [Latvia](../../cultures/latvia/play_latvia.md), and [Lithuania](../../cultures/lithuania/play_lithuania.md) among them, carrying the alliance onto former Soviet soil. [Albania](../../cultures/albania/play_albania.md) and [Croatia](../../cultures/croatia/play_croatia.md) follow in 2009, [Montenegro](../../cultures/montenegro/play_montenegro.md) in 2017, [North Macedonia](../../cultures/north_macedonia/play_north_macedonia.md) in 2020. Then Russia's seizure of Crimea in 2014 and its full invasion of Ukraine in 2022 bring collective defence roaring back as the alliance's first purpose, and drive the old neutrals in at last: [Finland](../../cultures/finland/play_finland.md) in 2023, doubling NATO's border with Russia, and [Sweden](../../cultures/sweden/play_sweden.md) in 2024. The alliance reaches thirty-two.

## Stage

- [Poland](../../cultures/poland/play_poland.md): the largest of the first eastern joiners, now a frontline state.
- [Lithuania](../../cultures/lithuania/play_lithuania.md): with the Baltic three, the alliance on former Soviet ground.
- [Finland](../../cultures/finland/play_finland.md): the neutral of generations, brought in by 2022, doubling the Russian border.
- [Sweden](../../cultures/sweden/play_sweden.md): two centuries of neutrality ended, the thirty-second member.

## Tension

The eastward growth returns the alliance to its founding purpose, collective defence against a resurgent Russia, and brings even the neutrals under the pledge. But it leaves NATO vast at thirty-two, stretched from the United States to the Black Sea and the Arctic, having to forge consensus among members whose histories and fears differ sharply; and the open door that drew the East in is itself named, by the power across the border, as the cause of the confrontation the alliance must now deter.
