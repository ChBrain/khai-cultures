# NATO: References

The provenance behind this group. A group carries no Hofstede data of its own (it

## The grouping

NATO, the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, is the transatlantic collective-
defence alliance founded by the North Atlantic Treaty (Washington, 4 April 1949).
The membership recorded in the registry is **build-derived** from the member plays
this group casts, not authored by hand. It binds thirty-two democracies of North
America and Europe under Article 5, by which an armed attack on one is treated as
an attack on all, and decides by consensus among sovereign equals. It is not a
single language or a single state.
