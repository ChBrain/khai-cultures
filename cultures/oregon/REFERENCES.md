# Oregon: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch (kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_oregon.md](pitch_oregon.md) is written from Oregon's profile inflected by its pioneer migration history, its timber harvesting, its organic agriculture, and its modern urban environmentalism, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Oregon base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 32                                       |
| Individualism                 | 86                                       |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 52                                       |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 44                                       |
| Long Term Orientation         | 28                                       |
| Indulgence                    | 66                                       |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Oregon's flat community structures, high focus on outdoor recreation/wellness, environmentalism, and lower achievement focus compared to high-tech industrial hubs).
