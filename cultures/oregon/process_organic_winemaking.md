## Initiated by

Willamette Valley winemakers, cellarmen, and grape harvest crews, conducted annually during the autumn harvest season.

## Direction

In the vineyards and fermentation cellars. Harvesting organic pinot noir grapes, destemming and crushing the fruit, allowing natural yeast fermentation, and aging the wine in French oak barrels.

## Lever

The specialized agricultural fermentation process that defines the valley's wine economy. It supports local sustainable agriculture and culinary trade, though it is highly vulnerable to unseasonable rain and vineyard pests.
