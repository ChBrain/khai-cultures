# Plan: the salmon passage plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and river engineering designed to restore fish passage and protect migratory salmon along the Columbia River Basin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Restore migratory pathways by constructing fish ladders, restoring vegetation, regulating industrial wastewater discharge, and monitoring river temperatures.

## Orders

- To the Department of Environmental Quality: Regulate and monitor industrial discharges along the Columbia River.
- To local business coordinators like [Chloe](persona_chloe.md): Develop fishery campaigns and fund watershed restoration.
- To agricultural managers like [Thomas](persona_thomas.md): Implement water conservation and plant native shoreline vegetation along vineyard creeks.

## Implementation

The fish passage audits; the riparian zone planting projects; and the deployment of water temperature sensors.
