# Plot: The Oregon Trail Migration 1843

## Taxonomy

[play_oregon](play_oregon.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In May 1843, the "Great Migration" of over a thousand pioneers leaves Missouri in covered wagons, traveling the Oregon Trail.

## Action

The migration establishes [Oregon Trail](position_oregon_trail.md) pioneer settlement patterns, cultivating the fertile soils of the Willamette Valley. The [wagon wheel](piece_wagon_wheel.md) represents this overland transportation. Thomas manages vineyards in the valley settled by these early farming families, while Chloe researches pioneer diaries in Portland archives.

## Stage

- [Oregon Trail](position_oregon_trail.md) : the self-reliance, pioneer grit, and agricultural determination of early overland migrants.
- [wagon wheel](piece_wagon_wheel.md) : the wooden wheel representing the transport technology of the trail.
- [the Willamette Valley](place_willamette_valley.md) : the fertile agricultural basin that served as the trail's destination.

## Tension

The rapid settlement of the valley causes the systematic displacement of the native Kalapuya tribes, who are forced onto reservations under unequal treaties.
