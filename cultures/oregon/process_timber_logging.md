## Direction

In the commercial timberlands. Felling marked Douglas fir trees, trimming branches, bucking logs to length, and transporting them to local sawmills.

## Lever

The traditional resource extraction process that shaped the state's early economy. It supports rural logging communities and construction lumber trade, though it faces strict environmental regulations to protect watersheds.
