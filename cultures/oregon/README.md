# Oregon: a khai culture

Oregon staged as a full khai play, ISO US-OR, language en. The Beaver State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its overland pioneer migration, industrial timber boom, and modern Portland coffee/counter-culture encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_oregon.md](play_oregon.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_oregon.md](pitch_oregon.md), the Oregon key.
- **Plots (history):** the Oregon Trail migration of 1843, the Columbia River timber boom, and the Portland coffee and counter-culture rise in the 1970s.
- **Personas:** Chloe (Portland coffee roaster) and Thomas (Willamette Valley organic winemaker), written in English.
- **Positions:** Oregon Trail (pioneer grit), Willamette eco-living (sustainability/localism).
- **Places:** the Columbia River Gorge, the Willamette Valley.
- **Processes:** organic winemaking (agricultural fermentation), timber logging (forestry harvesting).
- **Pieces:** a wagon wheel, a pinot noir bottle.
- **Plan:** the salmon passage plan.
