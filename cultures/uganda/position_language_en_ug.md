# Position: Ugandan English

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The authority of English as the shared official tongue of Uganda, the neutral ground on which a land of many nations - Baganda, Banyankole, Acholi, Basoga, Iteso, Bagisu and dozens more - meets to make one country. It is no one's mother tongue and everyone's working tongue: the language of the school, the law, the parliament and the newspaper, carried above the home tongues so that no single people's speech is made to rule the rest. In it [Okot](persona_okot.md) turned Song of Lawino for the wide world to hear, and by it the many are held as one.

## Orders

It orders that English be the tongue of instruction and of the state, the one language in which a Muganda and an Acholi and a Musoga can transact without either bowing to the other's mother tongue. It fixes what counts as the official record - the printed law, the school certificate, the government form - and makes fluency in it the ladder to office, to trade and to the world beyond the country's hills.

## Loses

It loses ground to the mother tongues that hold the heart - Luganda, Acholi, Kupsabiny and the rest - which carry the proverb, the praise and the joke that no official English can quite carry whole, so that the very song against forgetting the mother tongue lives, for most readers, in the borrowed one. It loses too to Swahili, the trade tongue of the street and the region, and it stays a colonial inheritance, learned at school and seldom sung at home, always a step from the deepest things a Ugandan feels.

## Drives

It drives Uganda to meet as one nation on a tongue that belongs to none of its peoples by birth. It is carried by [Cheptegei](persona_cheptegei.md) as a working tongue of the track and the press, turned by [Okot](persona_okot.md) into the wide readership of his song, read and followed by [Oryema](persona_oryema.md) across the exile's stage, borrowed by [the boda rider](persona_boda.md) for the up-country fare and the cross-town deal, and caught at arm's length by [Mutesa](persona_mutesa.md), who had the strangers' books deciphered to him without ever giving them the inside of his own. So the one official tongue shows who carries it and how far it stays from the home tongue beneath.
