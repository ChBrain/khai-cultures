## Cue

In July 1916, William Boeing founds Pacific Aero Products Co. in Seattle, constructing early wood-and-canvas floatplanes.

## Action

The company establishes [tech frontier](position_tech_frontier.md) manufacturing patterns, turning Seattle into a global aerospace hub. The [aerospace rivet](piece_aerospace_rivet.md) represents the transition to metal commercial aircraft. Alan's father worked for decades in the Boeing assembly bays, witnessing the peak of passenger jet production, while Sarah coordinates with transport networks to export fruit globally.

## Stage

- [tech frontier](position_tech_frontier.md) : the focus on engineering, aerospace, global trade, and software innovation.
- [aerospace rivet](piece_aerospace_rivet.md) : the metal fastener representing commercial aviation manufacturing.
- [coffee roasting](process_coffee_roasting.md) : the local beverage craft that fueled the long shifts of aerospace and tech workers.

## Tension

The rapid growth of the assembly plants in the Puget Sound area causes severe industrial runoff, contributing to the long-term degradation of local river ecosystems.
