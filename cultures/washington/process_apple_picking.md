# Process: apple picking

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Washington orchard managers and seasonal agricultural harvesting crews, conducted annually during the autumn harvest season.

## Direction

In the orchards of the Yakima Valley. Placing ladders against tree branches, selecting ripe apples, hand-picking them with a twist to preserve the stem, and filling padded picking sacks.

## Lever

The traditional agricultural harvest that makes Washington the nation's leading apple exporter. It supports rural farm economies and trade, though it is highly dependent on seasonal labor availability and export tariffs.
