# Washington: a khai culture

Washington staged as a full khai play, ISO US-WA, language en. The Evergreen State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Pacific Northwest wilderness, aerospace, and high-tech boom life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_washington.md](play_washington.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_washington.md](pitch_washington.md), the Washington key.
- **Plots (history):** the Oregon Treaty of 1846, the Boeing Airplane founding in 1916, and the Hanford Site nuclear production of 1943.
- **Personas:** Alan (Seattle software engineer) and Sarah (Yakima Valley apple orchard manager), written in English.
- **Positions:** Cascadia (wilderness environmentalism), the tech frontier (global innovation and trade).
- **Places:** Puget Sound, the Yakima Valley.
- **Processes:** apple picking (agricultural harvest), coffee roasting (beverage craft).
- **Pieces:** an aerospace rivet, an apple crate.
- **Plan:** the salmon recovery plan.
