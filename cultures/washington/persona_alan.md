# Persona: Alan

## Taxonomy

[tech frontier](position_tech_frontier.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A thirty-six-year-old software engineer in Seattle. English, spoken with a rapid, direct West Coast cadence, is his mother tongue, the language in which he [speaks, hears, and thinks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), the defining code of his professional life. He speaks no foreign languages. Yet the loss lies in the seam: The slower, tradition-bound agricultural talk of orchard managers like Sarah and the quiet history of [Cascadia](position_cascadia.md) [he reads only as environmental data points](process_reading_followed.md) on his dashboard, feeling that the physical truth of the forest and rivers is being reduced to digital metrics.

## Action

He manages cloud databases, coordinates data audits under [the salmon recovery plan](plan_salmon_recovery.md), and studies [Cascadia](position_cascadia.md).

## Shadow

His focus on technological innovation and high-cost urban development can make him indifferent to the housing gentrification that displaces long-term working-class families from Seattle neighborhoods.

## Tell

He looks at a software architecture diagram and says directly: "We are building scalable cloud networks that run global trade; you can't solve environmental problems without data-driven engineering."
