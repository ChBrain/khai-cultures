# Washington: References

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_washington.md](pitch_washington.md) is written from Washington's profile inflected by its Pacific Northwest isolation, its history of aerospace and tech innovation, and its unique forest and agricultural heritage, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Washington base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 34                                           |
| Individualism                 | 88                                           |

| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 44                                           |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Washington's low power distance, flat start-up structures, high focus on outdoor recreation, and environmental consciousness).
