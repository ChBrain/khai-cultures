# Process: coffee roasting

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Seattle coffee roasters and beverage technicians, conducted daily in roasting facilities and coffee houses across the Puget Sound region.

## Direction

At the roasting machine. Loading green coffee beans into the heated drum, monitoring temperature and color shifts, discharging the beans at the target crack, and cooling them.

## Lever

The specialized beverage process that shaped global coffee culture and the Seattle start-up economy. It supports local roasting businesses and cafe jobs, though it requires importing raw beans from equatorial regions.

## Echo
