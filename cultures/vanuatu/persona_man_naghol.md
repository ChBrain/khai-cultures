# Persona: Man Naghol

## Taxonomy

[hapi aelan](position_hapi_aelan.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Wan man blong saot Pentecost we i jam daon long wan haed taoa wetem tu vaen nomo we i fas long leg blong hem, mo hed blong hem i klosap tajem graon: hem i man blong naghol, kastom we i kam stamba blong bunjijam blong wol. Hem i wan man we i no fraet, we i mekem naghol blong askem wan gudfala harvest blong yam mo blong soem se hem i wan man tru, wan we i stap insaed long komuniti blong hem mo i holemtaet fasin blong ol bubu. Lanwis blong ples blong hem mo Bislama hem i [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), mo [harem](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) long tufala, mo Bislama hem i [yusum blong joenem](process_speaking_carried.md) ol man we oli kam long ol narafala aelan blong luk naghol. Inglis blong ol turis we oli kam blong luk hem jam, hem i [harem smol](process_hearing_followed.md) nomo, wan tok hia mo wan tok long, mo hem i no traem lan hem fulwan. Hem i toktok long lanwis we i kam daon long ol bubu, be wol i kam long hem long wan narafala tang, mo hem i no save talem long ol turis ful mining blong wanem hem i mekem, ples we gap long en blong tang i stap, from ol tok blong tu fasin i no save mitim long medel.

## Action

Long [hapi aelan tude](plot_04_hapi_aelan_tude.md) hem i karem [hapi aelan](position_hapi_aelan.md) i kam long laef, soem se laef mo kastom oli moa bitim fraet, mo se wan man we i jam daon long taoa wetem fulap fasdom long komuniti blong hem hem i moa rij bitim eni mane. Hem i holem naghol olsem wan saen se Vanuatu i stap hapi mo strong, nating se solwota i girap.

## Shadow

Man we i jam blong soem fasdom i karem sado blong hem: nadia we naghol i kam wan samting blong soem long ol turis nomo mo i lus stamba blong hem, mo trutok se yangfala we i muv long taon oli save lego kastom ya, mo se hapi blong aelan i save brok taem klaemet i tekemaot graon mo kakae we naghol i askem blong.

## Tell

Hem i putum laef blong hem long tu vaen blong soem se hem i no fraet mo blong askem wan gudfala harvest, mo hem i no askem mane long ol turis blong luk hem, be hem i mekem hem blong kastom mo blong komuniti.
