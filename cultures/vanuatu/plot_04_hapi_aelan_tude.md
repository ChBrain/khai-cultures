# Plot: hapi aelan tude

## Taxonomy

[play_vanuatu](play_vanuatu.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Biaen long independens, Vanuatu i kam wan nasen we Bislama i joenem, wan kantri smol long bigfala solwota we i gat plante lanwis be wan tok blong olgeta. Kava i ron long nakamal long evri sava, naghol i stap laef long Pentecost, mo plante taem oli singaot kantri ya wan long ol moa hapi long wol.

## Action

Tude, Vanuatu i stap wan nasen we komuniti mo kastom oli stap stamba blong laef: ol man oli sidaon long nakamal, oli serem kakae, oli no save spit, mo oli stap hapi nating se oli gat smol mane. Long [Tanna](place_tanna.md), [Man Naghol](persona_man_naghol.md) i jam daon long taoa wetem tu vaen, mo Bislama i joenem ol man blong evri aelan long wan tok. Be klaemet i kam stamba blong fraet: solwota i girap, ol saeklon olsem Pam mo Harold oli kam moa strong mo brokem ol ples mo kakae, mo aelan ya i stap long fored laen blong klaemet jenis. Andanit long evri samting, [hapi aelan](position_hapi_aelan.md) i holem, mo komuniti i stanap strong agensem ol niufala fraet.

## Stage

- [Man Naghol](persona_man_naghol.md): man blong Pentecost we i jam daon long taoa mo i soem se laef mo kastom oli moa bitim fraet.
- [Tanna](place_tanna.md): aelan we kastom mo komuniti i stap laef yet andanit long volkeno.
- [hapi aelan](position_hapi_aelan.md): komuniti, kava, mo kwaet tingting we i mekem Vanuatu wan long ol moa hapi nasen.

## Tension

Vanuatu i stap wan nasen we Bislama i joenem mo we komuniti mo kastom oli mekem ol man oli stap hapi nating se oli gat smol mane. Be klaemet i kam wan bigfala fraet long fored laen, solwota i girap mo ol saeklon i kam moa strong, mo trutok se kantri i mas holem hapi mo kastom blong hem mo stap strong long fored laen blong wan jenis we hem i no bin mekem.
