# Persona: Jif Kastom

## Taxonomy

[kastom](position_culture_kastom.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Wan kastom jif blong nakamal, man we i holem rank we i kam tru long ol pig wetem tusk, man we i kipim loa mo fasin blong ol bubu long ples blong hem: hem i man we i jajem ol problem long nakamal, i lukaot long lan mo long ol man blong ples, mo i holem stori mo dans blong kastom. Hem i wan man we i kwaet, we i toktok slou, mo we evri man oli lesin long hem from rank we hem i wok had blong kasem. Lanwis blong ples blong hem mo Bislama hem i [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), mo [harem](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) long tufala, mo [Bislama](position_language_bis.md) hem i [yusum blong joenem](process_speaking_carried.md) ol man we oli kam long ol narafala ples. Tok blong ol misin, Inglis mo Franis blong skul, hem i [ridim mo folem](process_reading_followed.md) smol nomo, be hem i no holem long tang olsem hem i holem lanwis blong ples. Hem i toktok long tok blong ol bubu, be loa blong waetman i kam long hem long wan narafala tang, mo hem i no save putum kastom blong hem fulwan long tok we i no blong hem, ples we gap long en blong tang i stap, from samting we kastom i talem i no save kros fulwan long tok blong narafala man.

## Action

Long [Kondominiom](plot_02_kondominiom.md) hem i holemtaet [kastom](position_culture_kastom.md) long taem we tufala kala blong waet man, ol misin, mo niufala God oli traem jenisim evri samting. Hem i kipim nakamal, rank blong tusk, mo loa blong ol bubu olsem stamba blong ples, mo hem i pasem hem daon long ol pikinini agensem evri samting we i kam blong tekem hem.

## Shadow

Jif we i holemtaet kastom i karem sado blong hem: nadia we rank blong pig mo tusk i kam wan samting blong soem nomo mo i lus stamba blong hem, mo trutok se kastom i save holem agensem ol samting we i save givhan long pipol, mo se yangfala we i muv long taon oli save lego loa we hem i kipim.

## Tell

Hem i jajem wan problem long nakamal i no wetem pepa no kot blong waetman, be wetem stori, kava, mo agri blong evriwan, mo hem i mejarem wan man long sapos hem i givim plante pig wetem tusk, no long hamas mane hem i gat.
