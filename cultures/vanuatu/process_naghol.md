# Process: naghol

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Ol man blong saot Pentecost long taem blong yam, bitwin Eprel mo Jun: oli wokem wan haed taoa long wud, oli fas tu vaen long leg blong ol man, mo wan biaen wan oli jam daon, hed i klosap tajem graon. I tekem wan vilij, wan taoa, mo ol man we oli no fraet blong jam, mo evri yia komuniti i kam wantaem long stamba blong taoa blong sing mo dans mo luk.

## Direction

I go long kastom mo komuniti: naghol hem i wan we ol man oli askem wan gudfala harvest blong yam mo oli soem se wan boe i kam wan man tru. Hem i no jes wan jam; hem i wan fasin blong ol bubu we i joenem komuniti, ples we ol man oli givim laef blong olgeta long tu vaen blong soem fasdom mo blong holemtaet kastom long fored blong evriwan. Naghol i kam tu stamba blong bunjijam blong wol, be long Pentecost hem i stap wan samting tabu, no wan spot.

## Lever

Hem i kam tru long dip tingting se kastom i mas stap laef tede mo i pas daon long ol pikinini, mo tru long wantem blong wan gudfala harvest mo blong soem se wan man i no fraet. Hem i holem agensem taem we i jenis mo agensem fasin blong taon we i pulum yangfala i kam aot; agensem hem i stanap fraet blong jam ya, mo nadia we naghol i save kam wan samting blong soem long ol turis nomo.

## Echo

Naghol i mekem yumi luk wanem [hapi aelan](position_hapi_aelan.md) i nidim: wan fasin we komuniti i kam wantaem mo soem se laef mo kastom oli moa bitim fraet. Long stamba blong taoa ya [Man Naghol](persona_man_naghol.md) i jam daon wetem tu vaen nomo long leg blong hem, blong komuniti mo blong harvest.
