# Plan: kastom mo kava

## Taxonomy

Open mandet we kantri i putum long hem wan: had wok blong holemtaet [kastom](position_culture_kastom.md) we i kam daon long ol bubu, blong joenem evri lanwis long [Kondominiom mo independens](position_kondominiom_na_independens.md) we i wokem wan nasen tru long Bislama, mo blong holem [hapi aelan](position_hapi_aelan.md) we komuniti mo kava i mekem, taem solwota i girap mo klaemet i kam stamba blong fraet long fored laen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Holemtaet kastom: kipim nakamal, rank blong tusk, naghol, mo ol lanwis blong ol ples agensem taon we i pulum yangfala i kam aot; joenem evriwan long Bislama, holem kantri wan agensem ol samting we i traem brokem hem long tu; holem hapi mo komuniti we i kam long ol man raonabaot yu mo no long mane; mo stanap strong long fored laen blong klaemet, redi long ol saeklon mo solwota we i girap, mo singaot wol blong tekem aksen.

## Orders

- Long [Man Naghol](persona_man_naghol.md): holemtaet naghol mo kastom, mekem hem blong komuniti mo no blong ol turis nomo, mo pas daon fasin blong ol bubu long ol pikinini.
- Long [Walter Lini](persona_lini.md): holem kantri wan andanit long Bislama, mekem se independens i stap tru long han blong ol man blong ples, mo no lego se hem i brok long tu.
- Long neson: holemtaet kastom mo komuniti, joen long Bislama, mo stap strong long fored laen blong klaemet, no fogetem se hapi i kam long ol man raonabaot yu.

## Implementation

Wan nasen we i kasem independens long 1980, we i joen long Bislama nating se i gat moa bitim wan handred lanwis, mo we i holemtaet kastom strong moa bitim plante narafala ples long Pasifik. Hem i holem nakamal, naghol, mo rank blong tusk laef tede, be klaemet i kam stamba blong fraet, taon i pulum yangfala i kam aot long ol vilij, mo kantri i mas holem hapi mo kastom blong hem taem solwota i girap mo ol saeklon i kam moa strong.
