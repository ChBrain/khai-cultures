# Position: Bislama

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Bislama i gat wan fasin we i no folem wan grama blong wan lanwis nomo, be i simplifaem: i no gat jenda blong nem, i no olsem Franis we wan samting i mas de o la. I gat fasin blong dablem wod blong soem se samting i strong o i gohed longtaem, mo nem blong lanwis ya hemwan, "toktok," i kamaot olsem daturnwan wod. Antap long hemia, i holem tu defren wod "yumi" mo "mifala" blong soem hu i insaed mo hu i aotsaed long wan storian, wan fasin we i kamaot long ol lanwis blong Pasifik we oli stap biaen long hem.

## Orders

Hem i fosem yu blong jusum stret wod: "mifala" (mi wan mo sam narafala, be i no yu) o "yumi" (mi wan mo yu tu), evritaem yu wantem talem "mifala/yumi," nomata yu tingting long hem o nogat, sipos yu no jusum stret, storian blong yu i save konfusum hu i stap insaed. Hem i fosem tu blong putum "-em" long en blong wan aksin-wod taem i gat wan samting we i kasem aksin ya (olsem "kilim," "wantem," "mekem"), blong soem se aksin ya i go stret long wan samting. Mo from we aksin-wod hemwan i no save senisim fes blong hem olsem long Franis o Spanis, hem i yusum smol wod "bin," "stap," "bae" blong makem taem long fored blong sentens.

## Loses

Hem i no save karem stret evri smol mining we i stap long ol handred lanwis blong ol ples, taem wan wod blong Bislama i mas kavremap plante defren tingting we bifo oli gat seleraet wod blong olgeta, olsem plante nem blong famle o blong fasin we ol lanwis blong aelan oli save divaedem moa faenwan. Fulap wod we i tekem long Inglis o Franis oli karem simpol mining nomo, mo taem yu wantem talem wan haemaen tingting o wan saens wod, yu mas boroem wod stret long Inglis, olsem Bislama i sot smol long saed ya taem i mekem tingting i simpol.

## Drives

Hem i mekem man blong Bislama i lane blong tingting sot mo stret, from we vokabuleri blong hem i no bigwan olsem sam lanwis, mo hem i mekem man i lukluk hu i stap insaed long "yumi" blong hem evritaem hem i storian, bikos wod ya i no letem yu fogetem hu yu tokbaot. Hem i tijim tu blong boro isi long ol narafala tok taem wan wod i mestem, mo blong joenem plante lanwis long wan maot wetaot lusum tingting blong olgeta, mekem man i kam wan we i save joenem be i no save fogetem hamas lanwis i stap andanit long hem.
