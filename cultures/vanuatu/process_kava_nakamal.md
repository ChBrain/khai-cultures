# Process: kava long nakamal

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Ol man long sava, taem san i go daon, oli kam wantaem long nakamal: oli wokem kava long rus blong kava plant, oli filemap wan sel, mo wan biaen wan oli dring long kwaet. I tekem wan nakamal, kava, mo ol man blong ples, mo evri sava long plante vilij ol man oli kam wantaem long semfala ples blong dring mo blong toktok.

## Direction

I go long kwaet mo komuniti: kava i karemdaon evri hariap blong dei mo putum man long wan kwaet ples, mo nakamal i kam ples we ol man oli toktok, jajem ol problem, mo mekem disisen wantaem. Hem i ples we kastom i stap laef, ples we rank blong jif i soem, mo ples we komuniti i kam wan long en blong dei. Hem i no jes dring; hem i fasin we i holem ol man blong wan ples tugeta long kwaet.

## Lever

Hem i kam tru long nid blong wan ples we ol man oli save kam wantaem mo toktok long kwaet, mo tru long fasin blong Vanuatu we man i no save spit mo i no kros kwik. Hem i holem agensem hariap blong taon mo agensem fasin we i pulum man i stap hem wan; agensem hem i stanap nid blong mane long taon, mo nadia we kava i save kam wan bisnes nomo mo i lus stamba blong nakamal.

## Echo

Kava long nakamal i mekem yumi luk wanem [kastom](position_kastom.md) i nidim: wan ples we ol man oli kam wantaem long kwaet, jajem ol problem, mo holem fasin blong ol bubu. Long nakamal ya [Jif Kastom](persona_jif_kastom.md) i sidaon, jajem ol problem wetem kava mo agri blong evriwan, mo holem rank blong tusk blong hem.
