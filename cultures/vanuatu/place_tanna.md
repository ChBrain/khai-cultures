# Place: Tanna

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Wan aelan long saot blong Vanuatu we Maon Yasur i stap long hem, wan volkeno we i laet evri naet mo we i wan long ol moa isi volkeno blong lukluk long wol, faea we i jam aot long top blong hem. Long Tanna ol man oli holemtaet kastom strong, ol vilij blong kastom we ol man oli laef yet long fasin blong ol bubu, mo long hem John Frum muvmen i girap, wan kago we i wet long wan niufala dei. Aelan blong faea, kava, mo kastom we i no jenis.

## Holds

- [Kondominiom](plot_02_kondominiom.md): aelan we John Frum i girap long hem agensem ol misin mo ol waetman, mo we kastom i holem strong long taem blong tufala loa.
- [hapi aelan tude](plot_04_hapi_aelan_tude.md): aelan we kastom mo komuniti i stap laef yet, mo we ol man oli stap hapi andanit long volkeno.

## Offers

Wan ples we kastom i no ded be i stap laef tede, ples we yu save luk fasin blong ol bubu long ol vilij we i no jenis, mo wan volkeno we yu save standap klosap long hem mo luk faea blong wol i jam aot. Wan stejing blong kastom we i holem agensem evri samting we i kam blong jenisim hem.

## Withheld

Isi tingting se evri samting i stap olsem bifo. Tanna i karem gap bitwin kastom we i stap laef mo wol we i pulum yangfala i kam aot, mo trutok se John Frum mo ol muvmen oli kam aot long fraet mo wantem blong wan moa gudfala dei, mo se volkeno we i pulum ol turis i save tu tekemaot laef.
