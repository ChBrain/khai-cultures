# Position: hapi aelan

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Plante taem oli singaot Vanuatu wan long ol moa hapi nasen long wol, mo hapi ya i no kam long mane. Hem i kam long komuniti: ol man oli stap wantaem, oli serem kakae, oli sidaon long nakamal long sava blong dring kava mo toktok, mo wan man i save se hem i no stap hem wan. Graon i givim kakae, solwota i givim fis, mo famili i givim ples. Hapi ya i kam tu long fasin we ol man oli no save spit, oli no kros kwik, oli no run bihaen mane, be oli stap kwaet long tede. Hem i wan rij we i no save kaonem long mane, be long ol man we oli stap raonabaot yu.

## Orders

Se hapi i kam long komuniti mo no long mane; se wan man we i gat ol man blong hem i moa rij bitim man we i gat plante mane be i stap hem wan; se kava long nakamal i karemdaon hariap mo putum man long kwaet; mo se yu mas serem wanem we yu gat, from se wan dei bae yu nidim narafala man tu.

## Loses

Graon taem yangfala oli muv long taon mo oli ron bihaen mane mo oli lego komuniti; taem solwota i girap mo ol saeklon i kam moa strong mo i brokem ples mo kakae; mo graon taem hapi ya i kam jes wan tok blong soem long ol turis nomo. Hem i save brok tu taem fraet blong klaemet i bigwan tumas, be stamba blong hem i stap strong taem komuniti i stanap wantaem mo helpem evriwan we i nidim help.

## Drives

Hem i stap long [Man Naghol](persona_man_naghol.md), man blong Pentecost we i jam daon long taoa wetem fulap fasdom mo we i soem se laef mo kastom oli moa bitim fraet. Hem i pulum Vanuatu long komuniti mo kwaet tingting, mo long strong tingting se laef we yu liv blong ol man raonabaot yu i moa gud bitim eni mane no pawa, nating se solwota i girap long fored laen.
