# Persona: Roi Mata

## Taxonomy

[kastom](position_kastom.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Paramaon jif blong bifo we i rul long Efate mo ol aelan raonabaot, longwe bifo ol waetman: hem i joenem ol haf we oli stap faet wantaem long wan sistem blong pis mo rank, mo dom blong hem, wetem ples we hem i ded mo ples we oli berem hem long Aelan Retoka, i kam wan ples tabu blong wol andanit long UNESCO. Hem i wan jif we ol man oli tingbaot tede yet long ol stori we i kam daon long maot, wan man blong pis we kastom blong Efate i kam tru long hem. Lanwis blong bifo blong ples blong hem hem i [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), mo [harem](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) long hem, wan lanwis we i no gat pepa mo we i kam daon nomo long maot blong ol man, long ol stori we ol bubu oli [karem](process_speaking_carried.md) i kam daon long ol pikinini. Hem i wan jif blong taem we i no gat raet, ples we evri samting i stap long tingting mo long tang nomo, ples we gap long en blong tang i stap, from stori blong hem i mas kros long plante handred yia long maot nomo blong kasem yumi tude, mo plante samting blong hem i lus long rod.

## Action

Long [ol jif mo Roi Mata](plot_01_ol_jif_na_roi_mata.md) hem i joenem ol haf blong Efate wetem pis mo i putum [kastom](position_kastom.md) olsem stamba blong rank mo blong rul. Hem i wokem wan sistem we ol jif mo ol haf oli stap wantaem andanit long wan loa, mo hem i lego long Vanuatu wan fasin blong pis we i kam daon long evri jif we i kam biaen.

## Shadow

Jif we i joenem ol haf wetem pis i karem sado blong hem: pawa we wan paramaon jif i mas holem blong holem evriwan andanit long hem, mo trutok se plante samting blong stori blong hem i lus, from i no gat raet, mo se yumi save hem tede nomo tru long maot mo tru long graon we ol man oli digim.

## Tell

Hem i joenem ol haf we oli stap faet i kam wan andanit long wan loa blong pis, mo hem i lego dom blong hem we i kam wan ples tabu we evri man blong Vanuatu i save hu hem i, longwe bifo eni waetman i kam.
