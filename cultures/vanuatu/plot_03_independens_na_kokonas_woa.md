# Plot: independens mo Kokonas Woa

## Taxonomy

[play_vanuatu](play_vanuatu.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Long ol yia blong 1970, ol man blong ples oli girap blong askem kantri blong olgeta wan, aot long Kondominiom blong Britain mo Franis. [Walter Lini](persona_lini.md), wan pris blong Anglikan jos, i lidim Vanua'aku Pati, mo [Grace Mera Molisa](persona_molisa.md) mo plante narafala oli wok wantaem blong wokem niufala nasen.

## Action

Long 30 Julae 1980, Vanuatu i kasem independens andanit long Fata Walter Lini olsem faswan Praem Minista, mo Bislama, Inglis, mo Franis oli kam tri ofisol tok. Be long semfala taem, long aelan Santo, wan grup i traem brokaot mo mekem wan defren kantri long Kokonas Woa. Niufala nasen i mas faetem blong stap wan, mo wetem help i holem Santo bakegen andanit long wan flag. Long taem ya, Molisa i raetem blong soem se niufala nasen i mas givim rod long ol woman tu, no jes ol man, mo se independens i no fri evriwan sapos i no fri ol woman. Long en, kantri i stap wan, andanit long [Kondominiom mo independens](position_kondominiom_na_independens.md) we i kam tru long han blong ol man blong ples.

## Stage

- [Walter Lini](persona_lini.md): pris mo faswan Praem Minista, Fata blong Neson, we i lidim kantri long independens.
- [Grace Mera Molisa](persona_molisa.md): poet mo voes blong ol woman long niufala nasen.
- [Efate](place_efate.md): taon Port Vila we kantri i kasem independens long hem long 1980.

## Tension

Vanuatu i kasem independens tru long had wok blong plante man wantaem, mo i kam wan kantri tru long han blong ol man blong ples. Be long semfala taem, Santo i traem brokaot, mo niufala nasen i mas faetem blong stap wan, mo trutok se kantri i fri be ol woman blong hem oli no fri olsem ol man, so independens i lego had wok we i no finis blong wokem wan nasen we i fri evriwan.
