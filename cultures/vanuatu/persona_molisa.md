# Persona: Grace Mera Molisa

## Taxonomy

[Kondominiom mo independens](position_kondominiom_na_independens.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Poet, lida, mo faswan woman blong Vanuatu we i girap blong faet blong rod blong ol woman long niufala nasen: hem i raetem long Inglis ol toktok we i strong long saed blong woman mo blong kantri, mo hem i wok klosap long Walter Lini long taem blong independens. Hem i wan woman we i no fraet blong talem trutok, we i lavem kantri blong hem be i luk gud se independens i no fri evriwan sapos i no fri ol woman. Lanwis blong ples blong hem mo Bislama hem i [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) mo [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) long tufala olsem stamba blong hem, mo Bislama hem i [yusum blong joenem](process_speaking_carried.md) evri woman blong ol defren aelan. Inglis hem i [karem](process_speaking_carried.md) olsem tul blong raet mo hem i [raetem ol poem long hem](process_writing_drafted.md), tanem lanwis blong waetman i kam tul blong faet blong ol woman. Wan woman we i toktok long stamba long lanwis blong hem be i raetem long tok blong waetman, hem i karem trutok blong kantri long tang we i no blong stamba blong hem, ples we gap long en blong tang i stap, from tok we hem i raetem long Inglis i no save talem evri samting we hat blong hem i tingting long lanwis.

## Action

Long [independens mo Kokonas Woa](plot_03_independens_na_kokonas_woa.md) hem i stanap insaed long [Kondominiom mo independens](position_kondominiom_na_independens.md) olsem voes blong ol woman, soemaot se niufala nasen i mas givim rod long woman semfala olsem man. Hem i wok long taem blong Lini blong wokem kantri, be hem i holem hem long sapos hem i fri ol woman tu, no jes ol man.

## Shadow

Woman we i faet blong rod blong ol woman insaed long wan kantri blong dip kastom i karem sado blong hem: tensin bitwin kastom we i holem rank blong man mo niufala fasin we i givim rod long woman, mo trutok se plante toktok blong hem oli no kam tru yet, mo se kantri i fri be ol woman blong hem oli no fri olsem ol man.

## Tell

Hem i raetem long Inglis se "Blak Stoned" blong soem se kala blong waetman i no save jenisim hu hem i, mo hem i mejarem independens long sapos ol woman tu oli kasem rod, no long sapos ol man nomo oli rul.
