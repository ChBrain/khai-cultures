# Piece: tabu tusk

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Long nek blong jif, long ol dans blong kastom, long flag blong Vanuatu, mo long mane blong kantri: wan tusk blong man pig we i tan raonabaot i kam wan sirkel, from oli tekemaot ol antap tut blong pig blong mekem hem tan. Tusk ya i wan samting blong rij mo blong rank long kastom, samting we i tekem plante yia blong mekem wan pig i girapim, mo we i soem se wan man i bigman.

## Load Bearing

Hem i stanap blong rank mo rij blong [kastom](position_kastom.md), stamba we i no kam long mane be long pig wetem tusk. Long kastom blong Vanuatu, wan man i girap long rank long fasin we hem i givim plante pig wetem tusk long ol seremoni, mo tusk we i tan fulwan i wan long ol moa tabu samting we wan man i save holem. Hem i no jes wan samting blong soem; hem i mane blong kastom, samting we i kaonem rank, mo we i stap long flag blong kantri olsem saen blong kastom blong evri man blong Vanuatu. Sapos i no gat tusk ya, kastom we i kaonem rank long pig mo no long mane bae i no gat wan samting blong soem hem.

## Apparent

Wan tut blong man pig we i tan raonabaot, waet mo had, samtaem i kam wan ful sirkel no tu sirkel sapos i tan plante yia. Long fored hem i wan tut blong pig nomo; wet blong hem i stap long plante yia mo had wok blong mekem wan pig i girapim tut ya, mo long evri samting we kastom i putum long hem olsem saen blong rank mo rij.
