# Place: Efate

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Wan aelan long medel blong Vanuatu we Port Vila, kapitol blong kantri, i stap long hem: taon we gavman, ol bisnes, mo ol man we oli kam long evri aelan oli mit long hem, wan taon we tri tok oli ron long strit, Bislama, Inglis, mo Franis. Long Efate mo ol aelan raonabaot, dom blong Roi Mata i stap, paramaon jif blong bifo, wetem ples we hem i ded mo Aelan Retoka we oli berem hem long hem, wan ples tabu blong wol andanit long UNESCO. Aelan blong kapitol mo blong kastom blong bifo.

## Holds

- [ol jif mo Roi Mata](plot_01_ol_jif_na_roi_mata.md): aelan we Roi Mata i rul long hem mo i joenem ol haf wetem pis, mo we dom blong hem i stap.
- [independens mo Kokonas Woa](plot_03_independens_na_kokonas_woa.md): taon Port Vila we kantri i kasem independens long hem long 1980 mo we gavman blong niufala nasen i sidaon.

## Offers

Wan ples we kantri i mit long hem, ples we ol man blong evri aelan oli kam wantaem long kapitol mo Bislama i joenem olgeta, mo wan ples we kastom blong bifo i stap yet long dom blong Roi Mata. Wan stejing we bifo mo tede oli mit, ples we niufala nasen i girap mo ples we paramaon jif blong bifo i lego pis blong hem.

## Withheld

Isi tingting se taon i fri long evri trabol. Efate i karem gap bitwin kapitol we i pulum yangfala i kam aot long ol aelan mo ol vilij we oli lego, mo trutok se solwota i girap mo ol saeklon olsem Pam long 2015 oli save brokem taon, mo se kapitol i mas holem wan kantri blong plante lanwis tugeta.
