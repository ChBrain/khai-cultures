# Play: Vanuatu

## Estate

## Name

Vanuatu, wan tijen blong ol aelan blong Melanesia long Pasifik, long is blong Ostrelia mo long saot blong ol Solomon Aelan, samwan eti aelan we oli mekem wan Y. ISO VU, lanwis bis, be hem i gat tri ofisol tok: Bislama, Inglis mo Franis, mo bitim wan handred lanwis blong ol ples i stap insaed long kastom blong hem. Wan kantri we i holemtaet kastom blong hem moa strong bitim eni narafala samting.

## Arc

Wan tijen blong Melanesia we ol man oli kam stap longtaem finis, longwe bifo ol waetman: ol bubu blong olgeta oli sel long solwota mo mekem ol ples blong kastom, mo wan paramaon jif, Roi Mata, i rul long Efate mo ol aelan raonabaot, dom blong hem i kam wan UNESCO ples blong wol. Long taem blong ol waetman, blakberding i tekemaot plante man blong wok long ol plantesen longwe, mo ol misin oli kam wetem niufala fasin mo niufala God. Aelan ya i kasem wan samting we i no gat narafala ples i gat: Britain mo Franis tufala i rul wantaem long wan Kondominiom, we ol man oli singaot "Pandemonium," tufala loa, tufala skul, tufala polis long wan kantri smol. Long Tanna, John Frum muvmen i girap, wan kago we i hop long wan niufala dei. Long 1980, andanit long Fata Walter Lini, kantri i kasem independens, be Santo i traem brokaot long Kokonas Woa. Bislama i kam tok we i joenem olgeta, wan tok blong olgeta we oli no gat semfala lanwis. Long Pentecost, ol man oli mekem naghol, oli jam daon long taoa wetem tu vaen nomo long leg. Kava long nakamal long sava, ol tusk blong pig blong soem rank, ol volkeno we i laet, mo plante taem oli singaot Vanuatu wan long ol hapi nasen blong wol. Andanit long evri samting wan nerve i ron: wan pipol we i holemtaet kastom, i stap kwaet mo hapi, mo i no save spit, be klaemet i kam stamba blong fraet long fored laen.
