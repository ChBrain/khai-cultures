# Persona: Walter Lini

## Taxonomy

[Kondominiom mo independens](position_kondominiom_na_independens.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Pris blong Anglikan jos we i kam wan politik man, lidim ol man blong Vanuatu long rod blong independens, mo kam faswan Praem Minista blong niufala nasen: ol man oli singaot hem Fata blong Neson. Hem i wan man we i kwaet be i strong, we i tingbaot kantri olsem wan famili andanit long God, mo we i wantem ol man blong ples oli rul kantri blong olgeta wan. Lanwis blong ples blong hem mo Bislama hem i [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) mo [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) long tufala olsem hem i blong hem, mo Bislama hem i [yusum blong joenem](process_speaking_carried.md) evri man we i kam long ol defren aelan mo defren lanwis. Inglis blong jos mo blong Britain hem i [karem](process_speaking_carried.md) olsem wan tul we hem i [ridim mo folem long pepa](process_reading_followed.md), mo hem i raetem long hem ol toktok mo ol buk blong faet blong independens. Wan pris we i lan long Inglis blong waetman, hem i tanem tok blong tufala kala blong waet man, Inglis mo Franis, i kam tul blong fri, be stamba blong tingting blong hem i stap long lanwis mo Bislama, ples we gap long en blong tang i no save klosap fulwan, from tok blong fri i kam tru long tok blong narafala man.

## Action

Long [independens mo Kokonas Woa](plot_03_independens_na_kokonas_woa.md) hem i lidim kantri long [Kondominiom mo independens](position_kondominiom_na_independens.md), tekem Vanuatu aot long han blong Britain mo Franis mo putum hem long han blong ol man blong ples. Hem i holem niufala nasen taet taem Santo i traem brokaot, mo hem i wokem wan kantri we Bislama i joenem, andanit long wan flag mo wan gavman.

## Shadow

Man we i lidim kantri long fri i karem sado blong hem tu: pawa we wan lida i mas tekem taem kantri i niufala mo i wik, faet agensem Santo we i brokem hat blong plante man long tufala saed, mo trutok se independens i kam wetem had wok we i no finis, from blong wokem wan nasen blong plante lanwis i tekem moa bitim wan dei.

## Tell

Hem i ansa long brokaot blong Santo i no wetem kros nomo be wetem tingting blong holem evriwan tugeta, mo hem i mejarem kantri long sapos hem i save stanap wan andanit long Bislama, no long hu i winim.
