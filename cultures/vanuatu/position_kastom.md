# Position: kastom

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Kastom hem i evri fasin blong ol bubu we i kam daon: ol loa we i no raet long pepa be i stap long tingting blong pipol, rank blong jif we i kam long ol pig wetem tusk, nakamal we i ples blong ol man, lan we i blong ples mo i no save sel, mo plante lanwis blong ol aelan. Long taem we ol waetman oli rul mo ol misin oli traem jenisim evri samting, ol man blong Vanuatu oli holemtaet kastom strong moa bitim eni narafala pipol long Pasifik. Kastom i no wan olfala samting we i ded; hem i stap laef tude long nakamal, long naghol, long ol dans, mo long fasin we ol jif oli stap rul yet long ol ples.

## Orders

Se lan, rank, mo pawa oli kam tru long kastom mo no tru long mane no pepa; se jif i holem ples blong hem long fasin blong ol bubu, mo se man we i givim plante pig wetem tusk i girap long rank; se nakamal i ples we disisen i kam long hem, slou be wetem agri blong evriwan; mo se wan man i save hu hem i, long ples we i kam long hem mo long lanwis we i toktok long hem.

## Loses

Graon taem kastom i kam wan samting blong soem long ol turis nomo mo i lus stamba blong hem; taem yangfala oli muv long taon mo oli no save lanwis blong ol bubu blong olgeta; mo graon tu taem fasin blong rank mo pig i kam wan samting blong soem mane nomo. Kastom i save brok tu taem hem i holem agensem ol samting we i save givhan long pipol, be stamba blong hem i stap strong taem hem i stap laef long nakamal mo long ol pikinini we oli lan hem.

## Drives

Hem i stap long [Roi Mata](persona_roi_mata.md), paramaon jif blong bifo we i joenem ol haf blong Efate wetem pis mo we dom blong hem i kam wan ples tabu blong wol, mo long [Jif Kastom](persona_jif_kastom.md), jif blong nakamal we i holem rank blong tusk mo we i kipim fasin blong ol bubu tede. Hem i pulum Vanuatu blong holemtaet kastom blong hem agensem evri samting we i kam blong jenisim hem, mo blong pasem hem daon long ol pikinini.
