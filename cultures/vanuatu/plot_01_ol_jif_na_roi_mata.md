# Plot: ol jif mo Roi Mata

## Taxonomy

[play_vanuatu](play_vanuatu.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Longwe bifo ol waetman, ol man blong Melanesia oli sel long solwota mo kam stap long ol aelan blong Vanuatu, oli mekem ol ples blong kastom, ol lanwis, mo ol sistem blong rank. Long Efate mo ol aelan raonabaot, ol haf oli stap faet wantaem, gogo wan paramaon jif, [Roi Mata](persona_roi_mata.md), i girap blong joenem olgeta.

## Action

Roi Mata i rul long Efate mo i joenem ol haf we oli stap faet wantaem long wan sistem blong pis mo rank, putum wan loa we ol jif mo ol haf oli stap andanit long hem. Taem hem i ded, oli berem hem long Aelan Retoka wetem plante man blong soem rank blong hem, mo ples blong hem i kam wan ples tabu we evri man blong Efate i save hu hem i. Hem i lego wan fasin blong pis we i kam daon long evri jif we i kam biaen, mo [kastom](position_kastom.md) i kam stamba blong rank mo blong rul. Longwe biaen, dom blong Roi Mata i kam wan ples tabu blong wol andanit long UNESCO, faswan blong Vanuatu.

## Stage

- [Roi Mata](persona_roi_mata.md): paramaon jif we i joenem ol haf wetem pis mo i putum kastom olsem stamba blong rul.
- [Efate](place_efate.md): aelan we Roi Mata i rul long hem mo we dom blong hem i stap.
- [kastom](position_kastom.md): fasin blong ol bubu we i kam stamba blong rank, lan, mo loa blong ol jif.

## Tension

Vanuatu i girap long fasin blong ol bubu mo blong ol jif, longwe bifo eni waetman i kam, wetem wan paramaon jif we i joenem ol haf wetem pis. Be stori blong taem ya i kam daon nomo long maot mo long graon we ol man oli digim, no long pepa, so plante samting blong hem i lus long rod, mo yumi save Roi Mata tede nomo tru long stori mo tru long dom blong hem we i kam wan ples tabu.
