# Thuringia (Thüringen): a khai culture

Thuringia staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-TH, language de. The green heart of Germany: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its classical, optical, and forest life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_thuringia.md](play_thuringia.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_thuringia.md](pitch_thuringia.md), the Thuringia key.
- **Plots (history):** Weimar Classicism starting in 1775, Luther's Bible translation at the Wartburg in 1521, and the re-establishment of the state in 1990.
- **Personas:** Dieter (traditional glassblower) and Maria (modern optical engineer), written in German.
- **Positions:** die geistige Tiefe (classical/philosophical depth), der Optikstolz (precision engineering pride), die Waldliebe (forest connection).
- **Places:** die Wartburg (Wartburg Castle), das Jenaer Saaletal (optics valley).
- **Processes:** das Glasbläserhandwerk (glassblowing).
- **Pieces:** die Thüringer Rostbratwurst (bratwurst), die Lauschaer Weihnachtskugel (glass bauble).
- **Plan:** die Optik- und Photonikoffensive (optics and photonics research plan).
