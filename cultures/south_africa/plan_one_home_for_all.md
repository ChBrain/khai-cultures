# Plan: one home for all

## Taxonomy

The open mandate the country set itself when it chose to become one: the endless work of holding the [Rainbow Nation](position_rainbow_nation.md) together in real and equal dignity, of keeping [reconciliation](position_reconciliation.md) a living thing chosen again and again rather than a fine word said once, and of living out [ubuntu](position_culture_ubuntu.md) so that the home is truly a home for every one of us, every people and every tongue, with a place at the table for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Hold the many in one home: keep every people and every tongue in equal dignity and let none be set above or below another; keep the choosing of reconciliation alive, telling the truth plainly and forgiving without forgetting the work still to do; live ubuntu in fact and not just in word, holding the least of the country's people as the measure of it; and close the gaps still left between the peoples so the equal home is real in life and not only on paper.

## Orders

- To the elder like [the Gogo](persona_gogo.md): keep the old truth alive, hold the family and the stranger's child alike, and pass on the tongue and the stories the young ones are losing.
- To the people who carry the country's song like [Miriam Makeba](persona_makeba.md): keep the many voices in one chord, the Rainbow at song, and carry the home wherever you go.
- To the nation: keep choosing one home over division, keep reconciliation a living thing, and never mistake the saying of the fine word for the living of it.

## Implementation

A young democracy three decades old, born of one of the rarest things a wounded nation ever did, overcoming its hardest era by forgiving rather than fighting, that has come far in great strides, one vote for all, eleven tongues in equal standing, the Rainbow stood up before the world in 1995 and 2010, and left much still to do, in the gaps that remain between the peoples and the daily work of making the equal home equal in life. The standing question is whether a nation of so many peoples can keep choosing to be one.
