# Place: Table Mountain

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The great flat-topped mountain standing over Cape Town and the Mother City spread below it, the tablecloth of cloud spilling over its rim when the wind comes up, and the two oceans meeting at the Cape below. The oldest peopled corner of the country and its first crossroads, where the ships came in and the world came ashore, where the Cape Malay flavours and the fynbos and the vineyards and the harbour all crowd together at the foot of the continent. Out in the bay lies the island where Madiba was held, in sight of the freedom that was coming. The mountain that the whole country knows on sight, the sign of the meeting place where it all began to mix.

## Offers

A place where the country sees its own beginnings as a mixed nation, the first ground where so many peoples met, the harbour of arrival and the table where the flavours of the world still cook together; a stage where the meeting of peoples is written into the very landscape, the mountain watching over the city that the whole Rainbow calls the Mother City.

## Withheld

The ease of the welcome it offers. Beneath the beauty the Cape carries the long memory of the era overcome, of who was kept from the mountain's city and who was held on the island in its bay, so the loveliest corner of the country also remembers that the meeting of peoples was not at first a meeting of equals, and that the home had to be chosen before it could be shared.
