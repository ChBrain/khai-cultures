# Position: Guyanese English

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The authority of Standard English as it is written and spoken in Guyana, the official language of the state and the one tongue that runs across the whole plural nation, above the Guyanese Creolese of yard and street, above the Amerindian languages of the interior, and above the Bhojpuri and the African and Chinese and Portuguese tongues the plantation carried across the oceans and then thinned to a word of prayer, a song, a name for a dish. It is the language of the paper, the court, the school, and Parliament, the only English-speaking nation on the continent holding, in the empire's own tongue, the many peoples the estate set on one narrow coast. No group has it wholly for a homeland, yet all share it to be understood from the interior to the sea - the English of here, Caribbean in its cadence and its cricket, made the country's own out of being brought to it.

## Orders

It orders what counts as the official word of the nation: the spelling, the register of the paper and the record, the language in which a name is entered in a register, a law in the gazette, a lesson in the classroom, and a debate on the floor of Parliament. It commands which tongue passes into the writing that makes proof, and holds together, under one flag and one motto, the peoples the mouth keeps apart and the page draws into one nation - the many made, on the record at least, one.

## Loses

It loses ground to Guyanese Creolese, the language of the yard and the street and the market, the everyday voice in which the country talks to itself, laughs, quarrels, and prays, and which the Standard English of the paper never quite reaches without losing a little. It loses ground to the Amerindian languages of the interior, the first tongues of the rivers and the heartland, older than the coast and least heard in its councils. And it loses ground above to the metropolitan British and American "standard," taken for the high norm against which the local English is measured, as though Guyanese English were only a falling-off and not a whole tongue of its own.

## Drives

It drives the plural nation to speak with one official voice above the division of its peoples. It is held whole, as a mother tongue, by [Cheddi Jagan](persona_cheddi_jagan.md), who turned the Creolese-inflected English of the estate into the speech of the labour platform, by [Martin Carter](persona_martin_carter.md), who seized the imposed tongue and forged it into the poetry of resistance, and by [Walter Rodney](persona_walter_rodney.md), who drafted in it the history and the summons that crossed the world. It is carried, over another first tongue, by [Stephen Campbell](persona_stephen_campbell.md), who kept his Amerindian mother tongue in the river village and bore the interior's claim into Parliament in the coast's borrowed English, and by [the canecutter](persona_the_canecutter.md), whose African and Bhojpuri mother tongues the estate pressed under the overseer's English until only the working tongue was left. So the one shared language shows who holds it for a home and who crossed into it to reach the state that governs them.
