# Process: citrus harvesting

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Florida orange growers and seasonal agricultural laborers, conducted from autumn through spring.

## Direction

In the orange groves of central and southern Florida. Picking fruit by hand, placing it in sacks, collecting it in bins, and transporting it to packaging plants.

## Lever

The agricultural process that defines the state's traditional economy and fruit juice production. It supports farming families and local trade, though it faces severe damage from citrus greening disease and winter freezes.
