## Shown

The launch center on the Atlantic coast, featuring launch towers, assembly buildings, and pads surrounded by forests and lagoons.

## Holds

## Withheld

The displacement of local fishing communities. While celebrated as a high-tech scientific center, the government seized land from local families in the 1950s, forcing them to relocate, a history not featured at the visitor center.
