# Plot: The Space Coast and Rockets 1950

## Taxonomy

[play_florida](play_florida.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the 1950s, the federal government establishes launch facilities at Cape Canaveral, creating the Space Coast.

## Action

The launch complex makes [Cape Canaveral](place_cape_canaveral.md) the primary portal for space exploration, bringing high-tech engineering and national attention to the peninsula. The launches become a tourist spectacle. The [space shuttle](piece_space_shuttle.md) represents the peak of this aerospace era. Jackson works on launch systems engineering, coordinating with global satellite networks.

## Stage

- [Cape Canaveral](place_cape_canaveral.md) : the Atlantic barrier island that serves as the launching pad for space missions.
- [space shuttle](piece_space_shuttle.md) : the reusable spacecraft that defined American space flight for three decades.
- [the Everglades restoration plan](plan_everglades_restoration.md) : the environmental plan that must balance coastal space launches with wetlands protection.

## Tension

The growth of the aerospace and defense facilities on the barrier islands increases real estate development, threatening local turtle nesting beaches and coastal ecosystems.
