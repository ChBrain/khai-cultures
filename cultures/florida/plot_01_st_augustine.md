# Plot: St. Augustine Founding 1565

## Taxonomy

[play_florida](play_florida.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In September 1565, Spanish explorer Pedro Menéndez de Avilés lands in Florida and founds the settlement of St. Augustine.

## Action

The founding establishes [the gateway identity](position_gateway.md) as the oldest European outpost in the continental United States, linking the peninsula to Spanish Caribbean networks. Mateo guides visitors through the historic stone fort, explaining the colonial history and Spanish heritage of the region.

## Stage

- [the gateway identity](position_gateway.md) : the role of the state as a portal connecting North America to the Caribbean and space.
- [citrus harvesting](process_citrus_harvesting.md) : the agricultural heritage introduced by Spanish colonists who planted the first orange trees.
- [Mateo](persona_mateo.md) : the tour guide who presents the Spanish roots of the settlement.

## Tension

The establishment of the fort leads to violent conflicts with French Huguenot settlers and the systematic exploitation of the local Timucua population.
