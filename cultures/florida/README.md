# Florida: a khai culture

Florida staged as a full khai play, ISO US-FL, language en. The Sunshine State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its tourism, space exploration, and wetlands conservation life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_florida.md](play_florida.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_florida.md](pitch_florida.md), the Florida key.
- **Plots (history):** the founding of St. Augustine in 1565, the 1920s land boom and railways, and the Space Coast rocket launches.
- **Personas:** Mateo (St. Augustine bilingual guide) and Jackson (Space Coast aerospace engineer), written in English.
- **Positions:** the Sunshine Dream (leisure/growth), the gateway identity (Caribbean/space connection).
- **Places:** the Everglades, Cape Canaveral.
- **Processes:** airboat patrols (wetlands protection), citrus harvesting (orange groves).
- **Pieces:** a space shuttle model, a citrus crate.
- **Plan:** the Everglades restoration plan.
