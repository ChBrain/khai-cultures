# Process: airboat patrols

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Everglades conservation rangers and researchers, conducted year-round to monitor wildlife and water levels in the wetlands.

## Direction

Across the shallow sawgrass rivers and swamps. Navigating flat-bottomed boats powered by aircraft engines and large propellers, scanning the waterways, and recording environmental data.

## Lever

The specialized method of wetland navigation that allows rangers to patrol areas inaccessible to standard boats. It supports conservation monitoring, though it is noisy and can disturb local wildlife.

## Echo

The patrol provides vital ecological data. It supports the educational tours of [Mateo](persona_mateo.md) in the park, while [Jackson](persona_jackson.md) respects the method as a necessary field monitoring protocol.
