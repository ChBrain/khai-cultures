# Florida: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch (kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_florida.md](pitch_florida.md) is written from Florida's profile inflected by its Spanish colonial history, its rapid real estate and railway development, and its Space Coast technology, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Florida base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 38                                        |
| Individualism                 | 89                                        |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 64                                        |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 42                                        |
| Long Term Orientation         | 24                                        |
| Indulgence                    | 72                                        |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Florida's high focus on indulgence, leisure and tourism, short-term real estate investment, and low uncertainty avoidance in coastal speculation).
