## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

## Shown

The vast subtropical wilderness in southern Florida, featuring slow-moving sawgrass rivers, mangrove forests, and cypress swamps.

## Holds

- the conservation battles in the plot [The Land Boom and Tourism 1920](plot_02_land_boom_1920s.md) : the river of grass.

## Offers

The airboat observation platforms and waterway trails where [Mateo](persona_mateo.md) conducts ecological education programs.

## Withheld

The severe disruption of water flow by early agricultural canals. While celebrated as a pristine wilderness, the park represents only a fraction of the original ecosystem, which was permanently altered by developers, a history kept out of park tours.
