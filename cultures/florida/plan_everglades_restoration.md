# Plan: the Everglades restoration plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and water engineering designed to restore the natural freshwater flow of the Everglades.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Restore water flow by removing canals, constructing water preservation areas, restricting agricultural runoff, and managing freshwater deliveries.

## Orders

- To the Water Management District: Regulate canal gates to divert freshwater back into the sawgrass valleys.
- To historic guides like [Mateo](persona_mateo.md): Conduct public education programs on native ecosystem preservation.
- To aerospace engineers like [Jackson](persona_jackson.md): Ensure launch facility wastewater does not impact surrounding lagoons.

## Implementation

The removal of road barriers; the construction of reservoir facilities; and the deployment of water quality monitors.
