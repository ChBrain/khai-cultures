# Persona: Jackson

## Taxonomy

[the Sunshine Dream](position_sunshine_dream.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A thirty-eight-year-old aerospace engineer in Cocoa Beach. English, the [Floridian English](position_language_en_us_fl.md) spoken with a relaxed southern cadence, is his mother tongue, in which he [speaks, hears, and thinks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), the true voice of his life. He speaks no foreign languages. But there's a signal he tunes out: the slower, history-minded narratives of guides like Mateo and the fragile, slow-moving wetlands [register with him only as environmental safety metrics](process_reading_followed.md) on his launch dashboard, and he can treat the coast's physical truth as one more constraint standing between him and the next window. He is part of [the Sunshine State](position_culture_sunshine_state.md), the engineer half of it that trusts the next launch over the last century of coastline.

## Action

He manages launch protocols at [Cape Canaveral](place_cape_canaveral.md), utilizes systems derived from the [space shuttle](piece_space_shuttle.md), and studies [the gateway identity](position_gateway.md).

## Shadow

His focus on technological success can make him indifferent to the real estate boom and coastal gentrification that displaces local working-class families from the Space Coast.

## Tell

He looks at a launch pad outline and says directly: "We are launching the next generation of communication networks into orbit; you can't run a space program if you're constantly looking backward."
