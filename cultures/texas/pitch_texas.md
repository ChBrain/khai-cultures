# Pitch: the Texan key

## Taxonomy

The pitch of [Texas](play_texas.md). It tunes the independent, hospitable, and bold expression of life under the Lone Star.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Tenor

A high-spirited friendliness combined with unyielding self-confidence. The communication is warm, hospitable, and direct, marked by a relaxed drawl and an invitation to speak. It is a voice of scale - large gestures, bold claims, and a pride in the state's vastness. This welcoming tone is backed by a firm belief in individual liberty and a neighborly duty to help, creating a social space where self-reliance and community warmth exist side by side.

## Undertow

Under the hospitality lies a stubborn resistance to external authority and federal regulation. The pride in self-reliance breeds a deep suspicion of centralized rules and outside criticism. The tone carries a defensive edge, asserting that Texas knows its own business best, which can lead to isolation when dealing with collective national challenges or systemic infrastructure vulnerabilities.

## Nerve

The absolute belief in Texan exceptionalism, the legacy of the Lone Star Republic, and the capacity to conquer any frontier, whether land, oil, or space.

## Echo

The sizzle of fat on hot oak coals meeting the low hum of wind across the dry scrublands: wide, warm, defiant - the sound Texas leaves behind.
