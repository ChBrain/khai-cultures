# Process: cattle drives

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Texas ranchers and cowboys, historically peaking in the late 19th century and maintained today as seasonal herd management.

## Direction

Across the dry plains and rangelands of West Texas. The gathering, herding, and moving of large herds of Longhorn cattle over long distances to pastures or railheads.

## Lever

The traditional ranching method that manages livestock on open ranges. It maintains the land management heritage and cowboy skills, though it is limited by highway traffic, fencing, and industrial feedlots.

## Echo

The cattle drive reinforces the frontier connection. It demands the grit and experience of [Carter](persona_carter.md) on the range, while [Elena](persona_elena.md) respects the practice as a symbol of the state's historical endurance.
