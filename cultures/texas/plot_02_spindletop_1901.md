# Plot: The Spindletop Oil Boom 1901

## Taxonomy

[play_texas](play_texas.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

On January 10, 1901, the Lucas Gusher erupts at Spindletop Hill near Beaumont, spraying oil over a hundred feet into the air.

## Action

The massive oil strike launches the Texas petroleum industry, creating immense wealth and fueling [the frontier legacy](position_frontier.md) of speculation and risk-taking. The state transforms from rural land into an economic powerhouse. The construction of [the Spindletop oil derrick](piece_oil_derrick.md) marks the new landscape. Carter's grandfather built his ranch on early oil lease payments.

## Stage

- [the frontier legacy](position_frontier.md) : the willingness to gamble, speculate, and conquer new economic boundaries.
- [the Spindletop oil derrick](piece_oil_derrick.md) : the wooden and steel towers that came to define the Texan oilfields.
- [cattle drives](process_cattle_drive.md) : the older ranching economy that now coexists with industrial drilling.

## Tension

The rapid influx of oil workers and speculative capital causes severe environmental damage, resource depletion, and the rise of lawless boomtowns.
