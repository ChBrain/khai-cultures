# Texas: a khai culture

Texas staged as a full khai play, ISO US-TX, language en. The Lone Star State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its independent, frontier, and high-tech life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_texas.md](play_texas.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_texas.md](pitch_texas.md), the Texas key.
- **Plots (history):** the Texas Revolution of 1836, the Spindletop oil boom of 1901, and the space race/Apollo era.
- **Personas:** Carter (traditional rancher) and Elena (modern Austin tech engineer), written in English.
- **Positions:** Texan independence (Lone Star spirit), the frontier legacy (rugged individualism).
- **Places:** the Alamo (historical mission), Houston Space Center (NASA).
- **Processes:** Texas barbecue cooking/gathering, traditional cattle drives.
- **Pieces:** cowboy boots, the Spindletop oil derrick.
- **Plan:** the independent Texas power grid maintenance and winterization.
