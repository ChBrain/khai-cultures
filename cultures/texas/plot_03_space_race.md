## Cue

In September 1961, NASA selects Houston as the site for its new Manned Spacecraft Center to direct the Apollo missions.

## Action

The facility establishes [Houston Space Center](place_houston_space_center.md) as the command hub for space travel, bringing high-tech engineering and global attention to the state. The voice of mission control becomes synonymous with Texas. Elena works on modern software integrations that coordinate with aerospace networks, carrying on the state's technical heritage.

## Stage

- [Houston Space Center](place_houston_space_center.md) : the mission control room that directed the first Moon landings.
- [the Texas power grid plan](plan_texas_power_grid.md) : the independent energy infrastructure that must power the state's tech hubs.
- [Elena](persona_elena.md) : the software developer who designs tools for the modern Austin and Houston tech corridors.

## Tension

The rapid growth of the aerospace and petrochemical sectors in Houston exacerbates urban sprawl and increases strain on the regional environment.
