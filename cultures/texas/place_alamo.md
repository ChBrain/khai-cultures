# Place: the Alamo

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The Alamo mission in downtown San Antonio, a low, weathered limestone chapel with its curved facade, surrounded by gardens and stone walls.

## Holds

- the memory of the 1836 siege in the plot [The Texas Revolution 1836](plot_01_alamo_1836.md) : a sanctuary of Texan identity.

## Offers

The historic exhibits and stone courtyard where [Carter](persona_carter.md) guides visitors to explain the frontier history.

## Withheld

The complex motivations of the defenders. While celebrated as a battle for liberty, the struggle was also intertwined with the preservation of slavery and land speculation, which is not highlighted in the visitor sanctuary.
