# Position: the frontier legacy

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The rugged individualism, risk-taking, and endurance born of borderland life. It possesses a tolerance for economic speculation and a preference for private initiative.

## Orders

That one faces hardship with resilience, respects private property, and seeks opportunity through personal work and venture.

## Loses

Its balance when lack of regulation results in unchecked pollution, environmental degradation, or corporate exploitation.

## Drives

Encourages the speculative ventures in the plot [The Spindletop Oil Boom 1901](plot_02_spindletop_1901.md) and guides the ranching operations of [Carter](persona_carter.md).
