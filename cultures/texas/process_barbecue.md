Parent group: processes

## Owner

## Initiated by

Texan pitmasters and families, practiced year-round, especially during community gatherings and holidays.

## Direction

In the smokehouse and backyard pits. The slow-smoking of beef brisket over post oak wood at low temperatures for twelve to eighteen hours, seasoned with simple salt and black pepper.

## Lever

The culinary tradition that fosters community gathering and neighborhood support. It brings together diverse groups under a shared heritage, though it faces commercialization and rising meat costs.
