# Piece: the Spindletop oil derrick

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Erected at the Spindletop historical site near Beaumont, with replicas in museums across East Texas.

## Load Bearing

It represents the birth of the modern oil boom, the industrialization of the state, and the speculative energy that transformed Texas into a global petrochemical hub.

## Apparent

A tall, open-frame wooden structure rising over seventy feet, holding a drilling cable, pulley system, and rotary machinery.
