# Texas: References

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_texas.md](pitch_texas.md) is written from Texas's profile inflected by its unique history as an independent republic, its frontier ranching culture, and its modern oil and high-tech prominence, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Texas base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 40                                      |
| Individualism                 | 91                                      |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Texas's extreme emphasis on rugged individualism, high risk-tolerance in oil/tech speculation, and strong regional pride).
