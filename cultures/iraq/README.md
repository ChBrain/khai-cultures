# Iraq: a khai culture

Iraq staged as a full khai play, ISO IQ, language ar. Mesopotamia, the cradle of
writing and the first cities, staged as a theatre: its history as the plot line,
its people as the cast, the roles its language and heritage encode, its places,
its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_iraq.md](play_iraq.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_ar.md](pitch_ar.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Iraq's
  profile (a deep collective bond of family and clan, a strong sense of hierarchy
  and honor, a long memory and reverence for the past, and the warmth of guest and
  host) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the birth of the word in Sumer, Babylon and the Code of
  Hammurabi, the golden age of Baghdad, and the renewal of modern poetry - each
  casting the elements it needs, and each kept on enduring cultural ground.
- **Personas:** Hammurabi the law-giver, al-Mutanabbi the greatest classical Arabic
  poet, al-Jahiz the polymath of the golden age, and Nazik al-Malaika the pioneer
  of Arabic free verse, plus one archetype, the Marsh Dweller (Ibn al-Ahwar), the
  living heir of the oldest life on the land. Each is wired to the language engine
  by the relationship to the mother tongue it holds.
- **Positions:** the First Word (the cradle of writing, the city, and the first
  law), the House of Wisdom (the golden age of learning and the great translation
  movement), the Two Rivers and the Palm (the Tigris, the Euphrates, the marshes,
  and the date palm as the life of the land).
- **Places:** Babylon (the great city of Hammurabi, the cradle of written law) and
  Baghdad (the City of Peace, the House of Wisdom, and the Thousand and One
  Nights).
- **Processes:** the Iraqi maqam (the sung performance of poetry before a circle of
  listeners) and building the mudhif (the reed guest-house of the marshes, the
  oldest architecture on the land).
- **Pieces:** the cuneiform tablet (the birth of writing and the Epic of
  Gilgamesh) and the oud (the voice of Iraqi and Arab feeling, the lord of stringed
  instruments).

## Provenance

The Hofstede scores behind the pitch and the public-domain historical and heritage

and the maqam, and the rivers and the palm - and the staging and the architecture
