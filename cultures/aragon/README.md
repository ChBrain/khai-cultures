# Aragon: a khai culture

Aragon staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-AR, language es. Aragón: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Union of Aragon and Catalonia, Siege of Zaragoza, and Monegros reclamation encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_aragon.md](play_aragon.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_aragon.md](pitch_aragon.md), the Aragon key.
- **Plots (history):** the Union of Aragon and Catalonia of 1137, the Siege of Zaragoza of 1808, and the Monegros irrigation of the 20th century.
- **Personas:** Javier (dryland wheat farmer) and Piluca (Pyrenean shepherd and artisan), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** aragonese fueros (provincial charters/historical rights/local assembly), pyrenean conservation (mountain preservation/forest management/valleys board).
- **Places:** Pyrenees Valleys, Basílica del Pilar.
- **Processes:** jota dancing (traditional aragonese dance), mutton roasting (traditional spit-roasting ternasco).
- **Pieces:** jota castanets, pyrenean shepherd staff.
- **Plan:** the Pyrenees ecosystem conservation plan.
