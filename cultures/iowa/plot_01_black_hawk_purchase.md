## Cue

In September 1832, treaty cessions along the Mississippi River displace Sac and Fox families, opening the area to farming.

## Action

The treaty opens land for settlement, developing [heartland agriculture](position_heartland_agriculture.md). The [corn ear](piece_corn_ear.md) represents early farming harvests. George coordinates traditional corn seed collections, while Ruth records oral histories of early river boundary lines.

## Stage

## Tension
