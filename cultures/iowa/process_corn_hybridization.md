## Direction

In the corn breeding rows. Bagging tassel stalks, collecting male pollen, dusting pollen onto female ear silks, labeling hybrid parent lines, and logging seed crosses.

## Lever

The traditional agricultural process that drives the state's grain economy. It supports thousands of seed farming jobs and crop feed exports, though it is limited by weather conditions and requires strict genetic purity audits.
