# Process: lobster trapping

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Lobster boat captains, sternmen, and harbor fishery workers, conducted during the open trapping seasons.

## Direction

On the lobster boat deck. Baiting wire or wood traps, setting trap lines, hauling traps from the sea floor, measuring lobster shell sizes, and banding claws.

## Lever

The traditional maritime harvesting process that drives the state's coastal economy. It supports local fishing families and seafood exports, though it is limited by strict minimum-size gauges and faces ocean warming threats.
