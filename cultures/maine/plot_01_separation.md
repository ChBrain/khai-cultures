# Plot: The Separation of 1820

## Taxonomy

[play_maine](play_maine.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In March 1820, Congress admits Maine as a free state separating from Massachusetts under the Missouri Compromise.

## Action

The act establishes independent statehood, developing [forest independence](position_forest_independence.md). The [lighthouse lens](piece_lighthouse_lens.md) represents early maritime safety. Eleanor documents early statehood legal records, while Arthur records oral histories of early coastal shipping families.

## Stage

- [forest independence](position_forest_independence.md) : the self-reliant winter survival, timber craft, and interior town pride.
- [lighthouse lens](piece_lighthouse_lens.md) : the glass Fresnel lens representing maritime coastal navigation.
- [place_portland_head_light](place_portland_head_light.md) : the historic stone Cape Elizabeth lighthouse.

## Tension

The separation is tied to the expansion of slavery in Missouri, causing deep moral polarization among early Maine residents.
