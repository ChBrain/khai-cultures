# Maine: a khai culture

Maine staged as a full khai play, ISO US-ME, language en. The Pine Tree State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Missouri Compromise separating Maine, lobster fishery boom, and Acadia forest fire encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_maine.md](play_maine.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_maine.md](pitch_maine.md), the Maine key.
- **Plots (history):** the Separation of 1820, the Lobster Fishery Boom of the 1880s, and the Great Acadia Forest Fire of 1947.
- **Personas:** Arthur (lobster boat captain) and Eleanor (lighthouse keeper), written in English.
- **Positions:** coastal stewardship (fishery protection/maritime navigation), forest independence (lumber craftsmanship/self-reliant winter survival).
- **Places:** Portland Head Light, Acadia Forest.
- **Processes:** lobster trapping (wooden trap harvesting), timber grading (sustainable forest management).
- **Pieces:** a lobster buoy, a lighthouse lens.
- **Plan:** the lobster plan.
