# Plan: the lobster plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and marine science designed to manage lobster quotas and monitor warming trends in the Gulf of Maine.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Manage the fishery by enforcing shell size limits, regulating trap numbers, monitoring water temperatures, and protecting coastal kelp habitats.

## Orders

- To the Department of Marine Resources: Enforce commercial harvesting quotas and audit lobster trap tag allocations.
- To boat captains like [Arthur](persona_arthur.md): Support catch telemetry networks and implement eco-efficient vessel propulsion systems.
- To forestry conservationists like [Eleanor](persona_eleanor.md): Coordinate coastal water quality surveys and advocate for traditional watershed protection.

## Implementation

The ocean temperature measurements; the shell size audits; and the deployment of coastal harbor water monitors.
