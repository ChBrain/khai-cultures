# the Democratic Republic of the Congo: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain cultural and historical
sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_fr.md](pitch_fr.md) is written from the DRC's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
Country-specific scores for the DRC are not all individually published by Hofstede
Insights; the profile below draws on the West Africa / Central Africa regional
estimates used in the Hofstede framework, recorded here so the data is on the
record without entering the prose.

| Dimension                     | DRC profile (regional estimate, approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | high                                     |
| Individualism                 | low (strongly collective)                |
| Motivation toward Achievement | moderate                                 |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | moderate to high                         |
| Long Term Orientation         | low to moderate                          |
| Indulgence                    | moderate                                 |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the six-dimensions model of national
culture, with West/Central African regional estimates). Values are approximate and
regional, as published by Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group.

claimed; the staging is original work. The nation is staged soberly through its
grandeur, peoples, arts, and music; no political side is taken and no conflict is
depicted.
