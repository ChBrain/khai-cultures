# Plot: The Ski Industry Boom 1946

## Taxonomy

[play_colorado](play_colorado.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In 1946, 10th Mountain Division veterans help establish the Aspen Skiing Corporation, launching the modern alpine ski resort era.

## Action

The resort boom establishes Colorado as a premier global winter sports destination, developing [ski patrol control](process_ski_patrol_control.md) methods. The [ski patrol pack](piece_ski_patrol_pack.md) represents this safety infrastructure. Lucas coordinates patrol operations at Aspen, while Elena studies the economic impact of winter sports on Front Range transport.

## Stage

- [ski patrol control](process_ski_patrol_control.md) : the avalanche mitigation, snow safety, and medical support on mountain slopes.
- [ski patrol pack](piece_ski_patrol_pack.md) : the emergency pack containing avalanche beacons and medical supplies.
- [high-altitude training](process_high_altitude_training.md) : the physical training that athletes undergo in mountain resorts.

## Tension

The growth of ski resorts causes massive real estate inflation, converting quiet mountain towns into elite enclaves and pricing out local working-class residents.
