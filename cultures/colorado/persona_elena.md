# Persona: Elena

## Taxonomy

[Front Range innovation](position_front_range_innovation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A thirty-six-year-old clean-energy startup founder in Denver. English, the [Coloradan English](position_language_en_us_co.md) of the Front Range spoken with an articulate, fast-paced cadence, is her mother tongue, the language in which she [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) and writes her business briefs. She speaks no foreign languages. Yet the loss lies in the seam: The slower, nature-dependent mountain talk of ski patrollers like Lucas and the traditional water histories of the Western Slope [she hears only like a distant echo](process_hearing_followed.md), feeling that the physical legacy of the land is treated as a secondary constraint in the race for technological carbon reductions.

## Action

She manages startup plans, utilizes data under [the Colorado River plan](plan_colorado_river_allocation.md), and studies [Rocky Mountain high](position_rocky_mountain_high.md).

## Shadow

Her focus on venture capital metrics and clean-tech expansion can make her indifferent to the water-rights concerns of agricultural communities in the Eastern Plains.

## Tell

She looks at a grid performance dashboard and says directly: "We are building scalable battery software to integrate solar power; you can't optimize regional energy transmission if you're stuck in 20th-century utility layouts."
