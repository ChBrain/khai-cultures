# Persona: Lucas

## Taxonomy

[Rocky Mountain high](position_rocky_mountain_high.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A forty-two-year-old ski patroller in Aspen. English, spoken with a relaxed western cadence, is his mother tongue, the language in which he [speaks, hears, and thinks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), the defining code of his daily safety briefings. He speaks no foreign languages. Yet the loss lies in the seam: The fast, start-up talk of urban tech developers like Elena and the digital planning of Denver transit boards [he reads only as administrative metrics](process_reading_followed.md) on his weather tablet, feeling that the raw, physical reality of the snowpack and wilderness is being treated as a secondary metric for real estate investment.

## Action

He preserves [ski patrol pack](piece_ski_patrol_pack.md) gear, coordinates safety projects under [the Colorado River plan](plan_colorado_river_allocation.md), and studies [Front Range innovation](position_front_range_innovation.md).

## Shadow

His focus on mountain safety and wilderness preservation can make him protective, overlooking the housing and transit struggles of seasonal service workers who commute long distances.

## Tell

He looks at a steep snow slope and says directly: "We aren't just managing ski runs; we are studying the snowpack stability to protect the community from mountain hazards."
