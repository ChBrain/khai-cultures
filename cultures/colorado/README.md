# Colorado: a khai culture

Colorado staged as a full khai play, ISO US-CO, language en. The Centennial State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its gold rush settlement, Rocky Mountain wilderness preservation, and alpine ski industry boom encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_colorado.md](play_colorado.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_colorado.md](pitch_colorado.md), the Colorado key.
- **Plots (history):** the Pike's Peak Gold Rush of 1859, the Rocky Mountain National Park Act of 1915, and the post-WWII ski industry development.
- **Personas:** Lucas (Aspen ski patroller) and Elena (Denver clean-tech founder), written in English.
- **Positions:** Rocky Mountain high (alpine wilderness living), Front Range innovation (clean-tech start-up culture).
- **Places:** Rocky Mountain National Park, Leadville.
- **Processes:** ski patrol control (avalanche mitigation), high-altitude training (athletic preparation).
- **Pieces:** a ski patrol pack, a mining pickaxe.
- **Plan:** the Colorado River water allocation plan.
