# Plot: Pike's Peak Gold Rush 1859

## Taxonomy

[play_colorado](play_colorado.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In 1859, prospectors discover gold along the Rocky Mountains, drawing over a hundred thousand settlers under the slogan "Pike's Peak or Bust."

## Action

The rush establishes [Front Range innovation](position_front_range_innovation.md) settlement patterns, constructing mining supply hubs that would grow into major cities. The [mining pickaxe](piece_mining_pickaxe.md) represents this early extraction era. Lucas preserves mining relics at Leadville, while Elena studies the historical resources that financed early Denver.

## Stage

- [Front Range innovation](position_front_range_innovation.md) : the commercial hubs and cities established along the eastern base of the mountains.
- [mining pickaxe](piece_mining_pickaxe.md) : the iron pickaxe representing the early mineral extraction boom.
- [Leadville](place_leadville.md) : the high-altitude mining district where early silver and gold veins were struck.

## Tension

The rapid influx of white settlers violates the Treaty of Fort Laramie, leading to the displacement of the Arapaho and Cheyenne and subsequent military conflicts.
