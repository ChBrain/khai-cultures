## Cue

In January 1915, President Woodrow Wilson signs the bill establishing Rocky Mountain National Park, protecting the continental divide.

## Action

The conservation victory establishes [Rocky Mountain high](position_rocky_mountain_high.md) values, protecting alpine peaks and forests from commercial exploitation. Lucas monitors trail safety along the high passes, while Elena develops clean-energy programs to reduce pollution over the park.

## Stage

- [Rocky Mountain high](position_rocky_mountain_high.md) : the wilderness environmentalism and outdoor recreation culture of the peaks.
- [Rocky Mountain National Park](place_rocky_mountain_park.md) : the protected high-altitude parkland containing major peaks and rivers.
- [plan_colorado_river_allocation](plan_colorado_river_allocation.md) : the water plan that manages headwaters originating in the park.

## Tension

The park's creation restricts traditional hunting and timber gathering rights for local settlers and Ute tribes, prioritizing tourists over local subsistence.
