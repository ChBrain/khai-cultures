# Plan: the Colorado River plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and water engineering designed to manage and allocate the water resources of the Colorado River Basin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Manage water allocation by implementing water-saving incentives, monitoring snowpack levels, regulating trans-mountain diversions, and enforcing targets.

## Orders

- To the Division of Water Resources: Monitor and regulate diversions along the river's headwaters.
- To patrollers like [Lucas](persona_lucas.md): Support headwater snowpack monitoring and meteorological data collection.
- To innovators like [Elena](persona_elena.md): Develop water-use monitoring software and conservation platforms.

## Implementation

The headwater snowpack measurements; the trans-mountain diversion flow audits; and the deployment of river telemetry sensors.
