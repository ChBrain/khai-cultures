# Place: the Mississippi Confluence

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The geographic meeting point of the Missouri and Mississippi rivers near St. Louis, featuring broad muddy waters, sandbars, and low forested floodplains.

## Holds

- the pioneer transit history in the plot [The Missouri Compromise 1820](plot_01_missouri_compromise.md) : the meeting of rivers.

## Offers

The boat launches and navigation channels where [Clara](persona_clara.md) pilots commercial riverboats.

## Withheld

The toxic industrial pollution. While celebrated as a historic geographic crossroads, the confluence gathers pesticide and chemical runoff from agricultural and industrial sites across the entire Midwest, environmental hazards omitted from regional tourism promotions.
