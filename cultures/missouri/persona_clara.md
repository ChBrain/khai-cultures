# Persona: Clara

## Taxonomy

[gateway west](position_gateway_west.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A forty-two-year-old riverboat pilot in St. Louis. English, spoken with a melodic river drawl, is her mother tongue, the language in which she [speaks, hears, and thinks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), the defining code of her navigation operations. She speaks no foreign languages. Yet the loss lies in the seam: The fast, syncopated urban talk of jazz musicians like Marcus and the digital demands of Kansas City club boards [she reads only as scheduling updates](process_reading_followed.md) on her weather tablet, feeling that the patient, river-dependent rhythm of the valleys is being forgotten in the race for digital speed.

## Action

She preserves [river barge](piece_river_barge.md) logs, coordinates navigation safety under [the floodplain plan](plan_mississippi_floodplain_management.md), and studies [gateway west](position_gateway_west.md).

## Shadow

Her focus on cargo shipping quotas and lock navigation systems can make her indifferent to municipal wastewater regulations in expanding urban residential suburbs.

## Tell

She looks at a river depth chart and says directly: "The river has its own currents; you can't navigate safety if you're only focused on digital speed and jazz recording metrics."
