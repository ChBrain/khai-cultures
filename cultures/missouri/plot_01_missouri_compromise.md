## Cue

In March 1820, President James Monroe signs the Missouri Compromise, admitting Missouri to the Union.

## Action

The act establishes geographical boundaries for expansion, developing [gateway west](position_gateway_west.md) travel routes. Clara guides visitors along the historic river terminals, while Marcus records oral histories of early border-state musicians.

## Stage

- [gateway west](position_gateway_west.md) : the pioneer trail history, geographical crossroads, and river transit legacy.

## Tension

The compromise admits Missouri as a slave state, deepening national legislative polarization and setting the stage for future conflict.
