# Plan: the floodplain plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and civil engineering designed to manage floodwaters and restore wetland floodplains.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Manage floodplains by designating agricultural floodways, restoring wetland vegetation, auditing levee structures, and restricting development.

## Orders

- To the Emergency Management Agency: Coordinate flood emergency operations and audit levee inspections.
- To river pilots like [Clara](persona_clara.md): Support navigation safety protocols and report channel debris during high waters.
- To civic coordinators like [Marcus](persona_marcus.md): Support clean-water conservation campaigns and coordinate riverfront benefit performances.

## Implementation

The river height measurements; the levee structural audits; and the deployment of wetland water quality monitors.
