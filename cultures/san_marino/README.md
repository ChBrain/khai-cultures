# San Marino (Repubblica di San Marino): a khai culture

San Marino staged as a full khai play, ISO SM, language it. The oldest sovereign republic in the world: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its independent and democratic life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_san_marino.md](play_san_marino.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_san_marino.md](pitch_san_marino.md), the San Marino key.
- **Plots (history):** the foundation by Saint Marinus in 301, the statutes of 1600, and the Garibaldi asylum in 1849.
- **Personas:** Marino (traditional stonemason/craftsman archetype, representing historical roots) and Giulia (modern lawyer/public servant), written in Italian.
- **Positions:** la libertà perpetua (eternal liberty), la repubblica (republican governance), il Monte Titano (Titano mountain).
- **Places:** Palazzo Pubblico (seat of government), le Tre Penne (the three towers on Mount Titano).
- **Processes:** il Palio dei Balestrieri (crossbower tournament).
- **Pieces:** gli Statuti del 1600 (constitutional book), la balestra antica (crossbow).
- **Plan:** la neutralità attiva (active neutrality / European integration).
