# Position: Australian culture

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The belonging of a young nation raised on the oldest continuously inhabited land on earth, a people who take their measure from [the fair go](position_the_fair_go.md), the egalitarian creed that no one is anyone's better, and from [mateship](position_mateship.md), the loyalty between equals forged in the convict gang and the digger's trench. It carries the easygoing, irreverent temper of the goldfields and the shearing shed, a nation of settlers, migrants, and the descendants of hundreds of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander nations whose belonging to this land runs tens of thousands of years deeper than the Commonwealth itself. And it carries, from its very first day, [terra nullius](position_terra_nullius.md): the founding fiction of an empty land, and the debt it still owes.

## Orders

Orders that the ordinary person be protected and the underdog backed, that no one lord it over another, and that the tall poppy who puts on airs be cut back down to the height of everyone else. It orders loyalty to the mate over deference to the master, plain speech over pomp, and a laconic, self-deprecating humour as the accepted register of everyday life. And increasingly it orders the harder work begun at Mabo and the National Apology: that the nation keep widening the fair go until it reaches the first Australians it was founded by dispossessing, and each new arrival its shores still draw.

## Loses

Ground where the levelling turns from fairness into envy, punishing excellence as if it were airs rather than earning it, and where mateship's warm circle closes into a wall against the migrant or the outsider it does not count as one of its own. It loses ground, too, in the gap between the promise recited and the reality lived, when the fair go is claimed as already finished while health, justice, and belonging still fall short for the people it was withheld from at the founding. And it frays wherever the easygoing surface is used to avoid the harder reckoning underneath it, comfort mistaken for completion.

## Drives

Drives the one raised in it to expect a fair go and to give one, to stand by a mate before an office, and to meet pomp and rank with an irreverent grin rather than a bow. It drives the multicultural nation, remade since the war by migration from Europe, then Asia, then everywhere, to keep asking whether the fair go reaches everyone it touches, not only the ones it was first written for. And it drives Australia to hold its two stories together at once, the newest democracy and the oldest living culture on earth, measuring itself less by what it has already achieved than by how honestly it keeps closing the gap between them.
