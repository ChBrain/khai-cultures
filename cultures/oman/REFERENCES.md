# Oman: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind
the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_ar.md](pitch_ar.md) is written from Oman's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without
entering the prose. Where Oman is not separately published, the regional (Arab
world) profile is used as the nearest published proxy.

| Dimension                     | Oman / Arab world score (approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 80                                |
| Individualism                 | 38                                |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 53                                |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 68                                |
| Long Term Orientation         | 36                                |
| Indulgence                    | 34                                |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the Country Comparison tool and the
six-dimensions model of national culture). Scores are approximate, as published by
Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group; Oman is commonly read through the
published Arab-world cluster, marked by high power distance and a collective,
uncertainty-avoiding profile.
