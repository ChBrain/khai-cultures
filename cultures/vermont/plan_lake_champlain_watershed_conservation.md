# Plan: the Lake Champlain plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and agricultural science designed to regulate fertilizer runoff and protect Lake Champlain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Conserve the lake watershed by regulating fertilizer applications, restoring lakeside wetland buffers, upgrading municipal wastewater facilities, and monitoring lake chemical levels.

## Orders

- To the Agency of Natural Resources: Enforce water quality standards and audit agricultural watershed permits.
- To site coordinators like [Ira](persona_ira.md): Support historic cabin water runoff telemetry audits and maintain safe visitor trail drainage limits.
- To cooperative coordinators like [Emma](persona_emma.md): Coordinate local community environmental education logs and advocate for traditional dairy farm watershed conservation.

## Implementation

The phosphorus lake measurements; the shoreline forest soil erosion audits; and the deployment of field water monitors.
