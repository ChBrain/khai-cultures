# Vermont: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch (kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_vermont.md](pitch_vermont.md) is written from Vermont's profile inflected by its Green Mountains, maple sugarhouses, and town halls, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Vermont base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 35                                        |
| Individualism                 | 78                                        |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 60                                        |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 44                                        |
| Long Term Orientation         | 24                                        |
| Indulgence                    | 64                                        |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Vermont's very low power distance in town meetings, active local boards, and low uncertainty avoidance in social corporate and environmental innovations).
