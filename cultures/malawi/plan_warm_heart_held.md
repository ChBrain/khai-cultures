# Plan: the Warm Heart held whole

## Taxonomy

The open mandate the country set itself the day it took its own name: to keep the welcome of [the Warm Heart](position_warm_heart.md) alive against every hard season, to hold [the many peoples](position_the_peoples.md) of north and south and every tongue in one house with none made a guest, and to keep [the Lake of Stars](position_lake_of_stars.md) and the fishing life it feeds alive as the waters thin and the people grow.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Keep the heart open, holding the famed warmth and the shared plate alive through poverty and the hungry season rather than letting hardship close the hand; hold the country whole, north and south, lakeshore and highland, many tongues kept and the bridge tongue shared so none is set above; and tend the lake, keeping the great water and the fishing life it feeds alive for a shore that grows each year, so the larder is not asked for more than it can give.

## Orders

- To the son of the lake like [Legson Kayira](persona_kayira.md): carry the warmth and the iron of the shore into the world, and bring it home again.
- To the keeper of the tongue like [Jack Mapanje](persona_mapanje.md): carry the proverbs of the village into the world's hearing, and keep the mother tongues alive beneath the bridge.
- To the nation: keep the heart open and the house whole, holding the many peoples as equals and the welcome as the one wealth no hard year can take.

## Implementation

A self-governing nation of many peoples held in one house, that has kept its name as the Warm Heart of Africa and its peace among its tongues, and seen its dances and its songs honoured by the world; and that carries the harder work still open, the poverty the map keeps pressing, the lake asked to feed a growing shore, the gifted who must leave to be heard, and the long task of keeping the heart open against the lean years that test it.
