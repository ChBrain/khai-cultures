# Position: the Warm Heart

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The famed friendliness that earned the country the name the whole continent uses for it, the Warm Heart of Africa: the conviction that the greeting comes before the business, that a stranger is fed before he is questioned, and that warmth is the one wealth a poor country can always afford. Moni is the first word and the chitenje is spread for the guest; you ask after a man's home and his people before you ask anything of him. It is the belief that no one who has eaten at your fire is a stranger after, and that to share the last of the plate is not a sacrifice but the plain way of being a person among people, umunthu, the dignity that comes of belonging to others.

## Orders

That a guest is welcomed and fed before anything is asked of him, and that the open hand is the measure of a person; that the elder and the chief are honoured as a matter of course and the soft answer is the strong one; and that warmth given freely, north or south, to kin or to stranger, is the true wealth of the country and the thing it will not let hardship take.

## Loses

Ground when the gentleness is leaned on too hard and becomes a deference that swallows the grievance rather than speak it, when patience curdles into a resignation that bears the hungry season in silence rather than name it; and ground, too, when the open hand is taken for weakness by those who would mistake a warm heart for a soft head.

## Drives

Lives in [Wambali Mkandawire](persona_wambali.md), who carried the warmth of the lakeshore out into the world in song and home again to every Malawian far from the lake. It drives Malawi to greet first and feed first, to hold the head high in a poor country, and to keep the heart open against every hard year, because the welcome is the one thing no map can take away.
