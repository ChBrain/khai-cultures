# Persona: the Mighty Sparrow

## Taxonomy

[mas, pan and calypso](position_mas_pan_and_calypso.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

The Calypso King of the World, who won the crown again and again from the 1950s on and put the whole life of the nation into kaiso - the politics, the scandal, the love and the picong, the news set to a sweet melody no radio could refuse. Charismatic, sharp-tongued, tireless, and beloved, he made calypso the voice of the people and the conscience of the carnival, and stood at the head of [mas, pan and calypso](position_mas_pan_and_calypso.md) for half a century. [English and the Trini Creole](position_language_en_tt.md) he has whole and plays as one instrument: he [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [thinks](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), and [sings in his mother tongue](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), the standard and the Creole bent together so the kaiso slides from one to the other in a single line, the dialect of the yard carrying the bite the standard could not. The high English of the broadcast and the official he [carries when he wants it](process_speaking_carried.md) and [hears it followed](process_hearing_followed.md), then drops it for the Creole the picong lives in. He sang the whole society into being in the tongue of the road and the rumshop, made the dialect a national instrument and the kaiso the people's own newspaper - yet the very Creole that gives the calypso its sting is the one no school will teach and no dictionary will hold, so the gap at the limit of the tongue is the official silence around the language the nation actually sings in.

## Action

In [Pan, Mas and Calypso](plot_03_pan_mas_and_calypso.md) he crowns calypso and makes it the voice of the young nation, singing Eric Williams's people into one and the carnival into the conscience of the place. He carries [mas, pan and calypso](position_mas_pan_and_calypso.md) to the world and makes the kaiso the sound by which the island knows itself.

## Shadow

The voice of the people can be courted by the powerful: the king whose endorsement a politician craves, the kaiso that bites the mighty and can be bought to praise them, the picong that levels everyone turned, for a fee or a friendship, to flattery. The conscience of the carnival is only as free as the singer who will not be bought.

## Tell

He takes the gravest scandal in the land and turns it into the road march everyone is singing by Carnival Monday - the harder the truth, the sweeter the melody he hangs it on, so the whole island dances to the news it cannot say plain.
