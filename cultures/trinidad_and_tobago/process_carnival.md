# Process: Carnival

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

The whole nation in the days before Lent, every band and yard and tent: the masqueraders sewing costumes for months, the panyards rehearsing for Panorama, the calypsonians in the tents, and then, before dawn on Carnival Monday, J'ouvert - the city covered in mud and oil and paint, the people taking back the streets. It takes only the season and the road, and two islands stop everything to play mas.

## Direction

Toward release and belonging: the great pre-Lenten Carnival of mas, J'ouvert, pan and soca, two days when the road belongs to the people and the whole callaloo crosses the stage as one. Born of the masquerade the enslaved and the freed made their own, turning the masters' ball into the people's road, it is the day the nation puts on a costume and becomes most fully itself, every race and creed in one band.

## Lever

It is driven by the deep Trini faith that you answer everything with a fete and a tune, and by the road that turns the whole city into one stage. It holds against every hard thing the year held, the divisions and the want; against it stand the cost that turns the mas to bikini-and-beads for the tourist, and the Wednesday after, when the costume comes off and the divisions the band dissolved come back.

## Echo

Carnival makes audible what drives [mas, pan and calypso](position_mas_pan_and_calypso.md): the genius of a people who turn castoff and trouble into the loudest, sweetest fete in the hemisphere. Across the Savannah stage [the panman](persona_the_panman.md) leads his band for Panorama, the yards' invention crossing as the nation's own.
