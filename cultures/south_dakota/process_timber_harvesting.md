# Process: timber harvesting

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Forest rangers, logging crews, and timber mill operators, conducted during dry weather weeks.

## Direction

Across the pine slopes. Marking mature ponderosa pine trees, felling logs with chainsaws, trimming branches, hauling logs to transport trucks, and logging lumber yields.

## Lever

The traditional forestry process that secures the state's wood product economy. It supports thousands of lumber jobs and forest health thinning, though it is limited by bark beetle damage and requires strict soil compaction audits.

## Echo

The harvesting requires lumber yield checks. It defines the daily work of [Alma](persona_alma.md) at Rushmore, while tribal coordinator [Dakota](persona_dakota.md) respects the method as an analog to detailed quilt fabric sorting.
