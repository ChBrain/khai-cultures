## Name

South Dakota, the Mount Rushmore State in the West North Central region of the United States. ISO US-SD, language en.

## Arc

A land of mineral wealth and Lakota resilience, defined by the granite spires of the Black Hills. From the treaty violations of the Homestake Gold Rush and the mechanical engineering of Mount Rushmore, to the civil rights activism of the Wounded Knee occupation. A culture that balances the monument preservation of its national memorials with the tribal heritage of its reservation communities, even as it plans the protection of its mountain aquifers.
