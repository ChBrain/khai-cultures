# South Dakota: References

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_south_dakota.md](pitch_south_dakota.md) is written from South Dakota's profile inflected by its Black Hills, granite monuments, and reservation lands, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | South Dakota base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 40                                             |
| Individualism                 | 78                                             |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 68                                             |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 44                                             |
| Long Term Orientation         | 24                                             |
| Indulgence                    | 64                                             |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for South Dakota's low power distance in mining locals, active community panels, and low uncertainty avoidance in aquifer protection innovations).
