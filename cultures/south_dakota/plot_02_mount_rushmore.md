## Cue

In October 1927, Gutzon Borglum begins carving presidential faces into the granite of the Black Hills.

## Action

The project establishes a monument carving legacy, developing [monument preservation](position_monument_preservation.md). The [sculptor chisel](piece_sculptor_chisel.md) represents carving tools. Alma coordinates sculpture safety records, while Dakota archives native names of the mountain slopes.

## Stage

- [monument preservation](position_monument_preservation.md) : the granite engineering, park safety, and memorial preservation.
- [sculptor chisel](piece_sculptor_chisel.md) : the pneumatic carving chisel representing monument construction.
- [Mount Rushmore](place_mount_rushmore.md) : the national memorial mountain monument.

## Tension

Desecration of sacred native mountains triggers intense tribal protests, prompting land restoration debates.
