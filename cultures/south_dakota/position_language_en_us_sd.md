# Position: South Dakota English

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The Northern Plains English of the Mount Rushmore State, the flat Upper Midwest mountain cadence of the Black Hills monument crews and the unhurried prairie drawl of the Pine Ridge reservation. It holds a level, plainspoken plains speech that both the granite face and the treaty map carry.

## Orders

Where it passes as native ground: the Mount Rushmore work platform, the tourism board room, the Pine Ridge council hall, the prairie watershed. It orders which turns of phrase and vowels mark a speaker as South Dakotan and sets the Black Hills mountain ear against the reservation-prairie one.

## Loses

Ground to General American, the flat national broadcast standard the screen and the tourism board carry, which levels what little marks the young and stamps the reservation-prairie speech as merely rural. The high-volume tourism register also crowds the older treaty-country talk toward the margin.

## Drives

It carries [Alma](persona_alma.md), whose mountain cadence is the working English of the Black Hills monument crews, and [Dakota](persona_dakota.md), whose prairie drawl holds the English of the Pine Ridge reservation. So the one South Dakotan speech shows who turns toward the monument tourism and who keeps the treaty ground.
