# Plan: the Black Hills plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and aquifer hydrology designed to manage mining runoff and protect Black Hills aquifers.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Conserve the mountain watershed by regulating mineral extraction discharges, restoring native forest creek beds, upgrading municipal water treatment, and monitoring stream chemical levels.

## Orders

- To the Department of Agriculture and Natural Resources: Enforce discharge standards and audit mining watershed permits.
- To site coordinators like [Alma](persona_alma.md): Support historic run telemetry audits and maintain safe monument trail water runoff limits.
- To tribal coordinators like [Dakota](persona_dakota.md): Coordinate local community conservation committee logs and advocate for traditional stream fishing protection.

## Implementation

The heavy metal water measurements; the forest soil erosion audits; and the deployment of field water monitors.
