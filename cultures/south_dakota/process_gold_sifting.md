# Process: gold sifting

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Prospectors, historic mine guides, and geologic hobbyists, conducted along Black Hills streams.

## Direction

In the gravel creek bed. Scooping sediment into a metal pan, swirling water to wash away light sand, collecting heavy gold flakes, and logging deposit locations.

## Lever

The traditional mining process that drove the early mineral economy. It supports historical heritage tourism and mineral education, though it faces mercury runoff pollution risks and requires strict stream bed permits.

## Echo

The sifting requires gravity separation checks. It defines the archive logs of [Dakota](persona_dakota.md) in Pine Ridge, while monument coordinator [Alma](persona_alma.md) respects the method as an analog to detailed stone debris sorting.
