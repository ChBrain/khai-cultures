# Process: whiskey distillation

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Tennessee distillers, mash operators, and warehouse cooperage crews, conducted year-round in regional distillery facilities.

## Direction

In the distillery. Fermenting corn, rye, and barley malt mash, distilling to high proof, filtering the spirit through sugar maple charcoal (the Lincoln County Process), and aging in charred white oak barrels.

## Lever

The traditional beverage fermentation process that supports the regional agricultural and tourism economy. It establishes global brand exports, though it is highly regulated by state licensing laws and requires years of warehouse aging.

## Echo

The distillation process requires biochemical consistency. It drives the barrel storage projects of [Mae](persona_mae.md) in the mountains, while songwriter [Jackson](persona_jackson.md) references the product as a classic element of local narrative themes.
