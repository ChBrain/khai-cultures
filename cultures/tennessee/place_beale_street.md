## Owner

## Shown

The historic entertainment street in Memphis, featuring neon signs, brick storefronts, blues clubs, and street music performances.

## Holds

- the blues and soul music recording history in the plot [The Memphis Blues and Rock Revolution 1954](plot_03_memphis_music.md) : the home of the blues.

## Offers

The music club tours and street festivals where [Mae](persona_mae.md) conducts historical preservation presentations.

## Withheld

The municipal redevelopment displacements. While today celebrated as a preserved tourist music district, the urban renewal projects of the late 20th century demolished surrounding historic Black-owned businesses, displacing local residents and altering the neighborhood's original community structure.
