# Plan: the Smoky Mountains air plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and environmental science designed to manage air quality and protect the Great Smoky Mountains forest ecosystems from industrial haze.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Manage air quality by enforcing emissions limits on coal-fired power plants, monitoring ozone along ridge lines, upgrading vehicle standards, and planting spruce.

## Orders

- To the Department of Environment: Monitor and enforce emissions limits for all regional power plants.
- To park rangers like [Mae](persona_mae.md): Support ridge-line ozone monitoring and report forest canopy damage.
- To studio coordinators like [Jackson](persona_jackson.md): Encourage energy-efficient studio designs and support clean-air campaigns.

The ridge-line ozone measurements; the power plant emissions audits; and the deployment of acid rain monitoring stations.
