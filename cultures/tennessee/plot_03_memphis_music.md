## Cue

In 1954, Sam Phillips' Sun Studio releases breakthrough rock-and-roll tracks, integrating blues and country styles in Memphis.

## Action

The recording success establishes Memphis as a global pop-music epicenter, developing [Beale Street](place_beale_street.md) tourism. The [whiskey barrel](piece_whiskey_barrel.md) represents regional distillery exports. Jackson analyzes Memphis rhythm arrangements, while Mae coordinates visitor access to historic blues heritage sites.
