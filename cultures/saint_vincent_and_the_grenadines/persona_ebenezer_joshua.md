# Persona: Ebenezer Joshua

## Taxonomy

[colony to nation](position_colony_to_nation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

The labour leader and the people's champion, the union man and the orator who give the worker and the small farmer a voice in the colony and press the crown for the vote and a say in their own affairs: the firebrand who carry the people's cause up the long road to nationhood. Fiery, plain-talking, and never tired fighting for the working man, he stand up on the platform and on the picket and turn the labourer grievance into a politics. English is his whole mother tongue, the English of the Caribbean turn to the people's purpose: he [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) and [thinks](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) in it, and up on the stump he does drop without even thinking into the warm Vincy talk of the cane field and the market, the tongue the worker answer to, [the Vincentian tongue](position_language_en_vc.md) held whole and bent to the people purpose. The crown English of statute and dispatch he [read and followed](process_reading_followed.md) close enough to turn the colony own rules back against it, and the borrow grammar of the bigger labour movement he [carried](process_speaking_carried.md) into island talk. He speak for a people in the very tongue of the empire that ruling them, bending the master English into the worker's cutlass and the dialect into the people's own, so that the gap at the limit of the tongue was the one he close every time the platform crowd answer him back in the speech of home.

## Action

In [the Road to Independence](plot_03_the_road_to_independence.md) he give the worker and the small farmer their voice and drive the colony up the road of labour toward self-rule, making [colony to nation](position_colony_to_nation.md) a thing the people themselves demand. By turning grievance into politics he open the way the nation going walk to its flag.

## Shadow

The champion of the people can end up remember as just a name on a road sign and forget as a cause: the labour win take for granted by them that inherit it, the firebrand cut down to a figure in the textbook while the worker he did fight for get leave out of the story of the nation he help to make.

## Tell

He stand up before a crowd of cane workers and market women and give them their grievance back as a politics, in the dialect they talk at home and the English the crown bound to answer, and the crowd bawl out because at long last somebody talking for them.
