# Process: Nine Mornings

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

The whole nation in the nine days before Christmas, getting up in the dark to gather before day clean: the people of the towns and villages out in the cool fore-day streets, the bands and the carollers, the sea baths and the early services, the bicycle rides and the dancing, all in the hours before the sun. It take only the village and the dark of a early morning, and a festival you ain't find nowhere else on God earth wake up the whole country before light, nine mornings straight.

## Direction

Toward joy and belonging deep in the Christmas season: a rite that belong to Vincy and Vincy alone, the fore-day carols and revelling of the nine days before Christmas, when the nation gather in the dark to sing, bathe, ride, and worship as one. It tie the village and the country in one shared waking, the warmth and the faith of a small people make plain in the streets before sun-up, a custom the nation claim as its own and share with nobody.

## Lever

It is drive by the devotion and the neighbourliness of a small-island people and by the long habit of gathering as a village, the church and the street make one in the dark before Christmas. It hold up against the scattering of families and the pull of the big world plainer Christmas; against it stand the drift of custom and the risk that a thing you ain't find nowhere else get let fade into the ordinary holiday everybody else keep.
