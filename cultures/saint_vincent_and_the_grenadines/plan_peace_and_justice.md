# Plan: Peace and Justice

## Taxonomy

The charge the nation set itself in its motto: to honour the deep root of [Hairouna and the Garifuna](position_hairouna_and_the_garifuna.md), the free people and the chief who dead for the land, holding the heritage and the wound soberly; to ride out the fire of the living volcano with the calm strength [the sea and the land](position_the_sea_and_the_land.md) demand; and to carry the long work of [colony to nation](position_colony_to_nation.md) forward, making a small scatter-out country stand on its own and hold its mainland and its islands as one home.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Keep the Garifuna heritage alive, honouring Chatoyer and the free people, remembering the deportation soberly, and holding the wound and the blessing together instead of letting the calm bury the root; meet the volcano with strength, sheltering and building back through every eruption and farming the blessed and dangerous soil with faith and care; develop the scatter-out islands as one, tying the mainland and the Grenadines across the sea and keeping a living for the grower and the sailor both; and answer the charge of independence, making the small nation stand on its own and keeping its young ones at home.

## Orders

- To the grower like [the arrowroot farmer](persona_the_arrowroot_farmer.md): keep the blessed soil in cultivation, hold the heritage of the land through the volcano turns, and farm the slope with faith and strength.
- To the founder's heirs like [Milton Cato](persona_milton_cato.md): answer the charge of the flag they raise, making the nation truly stand on its own and finishing the work of standing alone that independence did begin.
- To the nation: honour the Garifuna root and the chief who dead for it, ride out the volcano as one, and hold the mainland and the islands together as one same home.

## Implementation

A young nation, independent since 1979, that hold itself together as one across the volcano and the sea, keep its festivals and its faith, and carry the calm strength of a small island people through eruption after eruption, the latest one in 2021. It start the honouring of its Garifuna root, naming Chatoyer its first national hero, and leave open still the deeper work of keeping the heritage living, of making the scatter-out islands prosper as one, of keeping the young ones at home, and of standing fully on its own in the world.
