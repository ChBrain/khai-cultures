# Piece: the Bequia model boat

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

On the workbench of the model-maker in Bequia, in the windows of the island small shops, and on the shelf and the mantel of homes across the nation and far beyond it: a handmade little model of a Bequia sailing craft, a whaleboat or a schooner, carve and rig plank by plank and sail by sail by the seafaring island that know the real boats best. The work of the same hands and the same eye that build and sail the full-size vessels, the island craft make small.

## Load Bearing

It carry the story of [the sea and the land](position_the_sea_and_the_land.md): the heritage of the Grenadine island where the sea is the living, the boatbuilding and the sailing and the old whaling tradition make into a thing a hand can hold. The model is how the island keep and share its sea-knowledge, the proof that on a place of little land the craft of the boat is the real inheritance. It carry the skill of the seafarer into the home and the big world, the little wooden hull standing for a whole way of living by the water. Without it the heritage of the sea-island would have no shape on the shelf to remember it by.

## Apparent

A little wooden boat, hull and mast and rigging and tiny sails, carve and paint by hand: in itself just a model, a ornament, a thing make to look at. Its whole weight is in the hands that make it, the same hands that build the real boats, and in the sea-heritage of the island the little hull hold in miniature.

## Yearbook

- Carve and rig by hand in Bequia, the work of the same seafaring tradition that build and sail the island full-size craft.
- Come to be the signature craft of the island, carrying the Grenadines boatbuilding heritage into homes across the nation and abroad.
- Keep the sea-skill of the island alive in miniature as the world that did need the full-size boats fall back.
