# Position: the Vincentian tongue

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The standing of English as the tongue of these islands, held in two registers the people slide between: the formal English of the court, the constitution, the statute and the dispatch, the tongue the colony own rules was written in and the nation drawed up in; and the warm Vincy talk of the cane field, the market, and the village, the soft island speech of the country districts and, thicker still, the close Bequia talk of the boat crews out on the water. It is the proposition that the islands hold English whole and turn it to the people's purpose, bending the master own tongue into the worker cutlass and the constitutional clause both, so the crown English and the ole talk of home are not two tongues but the one the platform crowd answer back in.

## Orders

Which register go where: the formal English on the constitution, the statute, the buyer contract and the produce grade; the warm Vincy talk in the market, the village, and the boat. It orders that the crown tongue of the record and the people tongue of the yard both count as the island own, and that the country be governed and judged in the same English it does joke and bargain and pray in.

## Loses

Ground where the paper of the buyer and the grade set the worth of a crop in an English the country farmer read only haltingly, the price fixed in a register not quite her own. And behind it, the deeper loss: the Garifuna tongue of Hairouna, the Igneri speech married to the African inheritance, that held the island before ever English name it, broke up and carried away with the people into exile, so the land remember plant and place names in a speech the island ain't have no more, the older tongue gone under the English that took its ground.

## Drives

It drives the islands to answer the world in a tongue they bend to their own purpose. It gives [Ebenezer Joshua](persona_ebenezer_joshua.md) the mother English he turn into the worker politics, dropping into the warm Vincy talk the crowd answer to, and [Milton Cato](persona_milton_cato.md) the formal English he build a sovereign nation out of clauses in; it gives [the arrowroot farmer](persona_the_arrowroot_farmer.md) the warm Vincy talk of the country districts as her whole mother tongue, and [the Bequia whaler](persona_the_bequia_whaler.md) the salt English of the Grenadines that thicken into the close talk of the boat. And it is the coloniser tongue [Chatoyer](persona_chatoyer.md) met across the gulf of war and would not take, deciphered only through go-betweens and never in his own mouth, the English that came for Hairouna and outlasted the Garifuna he led his free people in. So the one tongue show who hold it whole as their own, who bend it to the people purpose, and whose land it took the older speech from.
