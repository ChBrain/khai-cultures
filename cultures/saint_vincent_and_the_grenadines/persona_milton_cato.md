# Persona: Milton Cato

## Taxonomy

[colony to nation](position_colony_to_nation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Robert Milton Cato, the father of independence and the nation first Prime Minister, the lawyer and statesman who carry Saint Vincent and the Grenadines across from colony to sovereign nation in 1979: the steady hand who take the people's long climb and make it a flag. Measured and lawyer-like and constitutional where the firebrand was fiery, he serve in the war, come home to the law and to politics, and lead the country through self-rule to the day of independence. English is his whole mother tongue, the formal English of the court and the constitution: he [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) and [thinks](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) in it, and he [writes](process_writing_mother_tongue.md) it with the precision of a man who build a whole nation out of clauses and statutes, [the Vincentian tongue](position_language_en_vc.md) held whole in its formal register. The legal Latin and the inherited law of the Westminster mould he [read and followed](process_reading_followed.md) deep, and turn the master own constitutional language into the very tool of the colony release. He draw up a free nation in the same tongue and the same legal forms of the empire it was leaving, making the white man English carry the country sovereignty, so that the gap at the limit of the tongue close into a paradox: the nation call itself free in the only tongue it did have, the one the empire leave behind. He belong to [Vincy](position_culture_vincy.md) as the steady hand who set the culture's long climb into a constitution, answering the charge of standing on its own that independence lay down.

## Action

In [the Road to Independence](plot_03_the_road_to_independence.md) he carry the country across the threshold to nationhood, taking the long labour and the people's climb and setting them into a constitution and a flag in 1979, the keystone of [colony to nation](position_colony_to_nation.md). By turning the struggle into a state he give the nation the steady forms it need to stand on its own.

## Shadow

The father of the nation can get honoured and the nation charge forget: independence treat like a finish thing, the flag raise up and the hard work of standing alone leave half done, the founder remember while the country he found does lean where it should stand.

## Tell

He take the firebrand cause and the people's long climb and set them down, clause by careful clause, into the document by which one small scatter-out colony come to be a nation in its own right.
