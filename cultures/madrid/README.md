# Madrid: a khai culture

Madrid staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-MD, language es. Madrid: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Capitality Choice, March 1808 uprising, and Autonomy Statute encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_madrid.md](play_madrid.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_madrid.md](pitch_madrid.md), the Madrid key.
- **Plots (history):** the Capitality Choice of 1561, the Uprising of March 1808, and the Statute of Autonomy of 1983.
- **Personas:** Felipe (public administrator) and Elena (traditional bar owner), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** madrid capitality (centralized administration/political influence), castilian dialect (standard speech/urban idiom/laísmo features).
- **Places:** Puerta del Sol, Guadarrama.
- **Processes:** tapas crawl (bar-to-bar socializing), cocido cooking (slow-cooking chickpea stew).
- **Pieces:** capitol model, cocido clay pot.
- **Plan:** the Madrid clean air plan.
