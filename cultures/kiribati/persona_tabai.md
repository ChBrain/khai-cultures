# Persona: Ieremia Tabai

## Taxonomy

[te maneaba](position_te_maneaba.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Te rorobuaka mai abamwakoro aika a uarereke, ae e a tia ni kairiri te aba nakon te inaomata ngke e uarereke ana ririki, ao e a riki bwa te moan beretitenti i Kiribati. E rabakau, e nimamannei, ao e kakaonimaki nakon te katei ni maneaba ao te boowi ae e reke iroun te koraki. E taua te tautaeka ae boou n te aro are e karinea iai te unimwane ao te maneaba. E [taetae](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) ao e [iaiangoa](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) [te taetae ni Kiribati](position_language_gil.md) bwa oin ana taetae, ae te taetae are e botiia iai kaain te kawa ni kabane. Te taetae n Ingiriti ae te taetae n te tautaeka ngkoa e [uotia](process_speaking_carried.md), ao e [warekia ao e ira nanona n te beeba](process_reading_followed.md) ngke e karaoi ana babaire ma aaba nako. E kairiri te aba nakon te inaomata n ana taetae oin, ma e bon kakaraoa te babaire n te tautaeka n te taetae n Ingiriti ae e uotia mai irouia ake a tautaeka ngkoa: ngaia are e mena te aki kona n taekina te koaua ni kabane n te taetae ae tiaki oin ana taetae, ao e tei te boowi ni Kiribati i marenan uoua taetae.

## Action

I nanon [te inaomata](plot_03_te_inaomata.md) e taua [te maneaba](position_te_maneaba.md) bwa aana te tautaeka ae boou, ao e karaoa te aba ae e tautaeka i aana. E uota te katei ni maneaba ao te boowi ae e reke iroun te koraki nakon te tautaeka ae boou, n te aro are e botiia iai aomata bwa a na ti teuana e ngae ngke a uamwawa i aon abamwakoro aika a bati.

## Shadow

Te tia kairiri ae e uarereke ana ririki ngke e taua te aba e mena i aana te kangaanga, bwa e kona n rangi ni korakora te kantaninga nakon te aomata ae ti temanna ae e taua te tautaeka, n te aro are e na kona ni mka iai te boowi ae e reke iroun te koraki ae e taua te maneaba.

## Tell
