# Process: te botaki n te maneaba

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Kaain te kawa ngkana iai te bwai ae e na karaoaki: te mare, te ruoia, te boowi n te aba, ke te kabwaeua n te kawa. A botaki aomata nako i nanon te maneaba, a katauraoa te amwarake ae te botaki, ao a tekateka inanon te boti are e tau ibukin te utu nako. E kainnanoaki tii te maneaba ma te amwarake, ao a botaki aomata ni kabane bwa a na ti teuana.

## Direction

Nakon te boowi ao te bootaki: te botaki ae e botiia iai aomata ni kabane bwa a na karaoa aia babaire ma te raoiroi ae ti teuana, ao a na amwarake n te botaki ae teuana. E reke iai te boowi ae e raoiroi nakoia kaain te kawa, ao e taekinaki iai te katei ni Kiribati, ao e karineaki iai te unimwane ao te kara.

## Lever

E kairaki iroun te kainnano nakon te boowi ae e reke iroun te koraki n te aba ae e aki tautaekaki iroun te aomata ae ti temanna. E teimatoa n aki ongeaba nakon te uamwawa ao te raroa i marenan abamwakoro, bwa e botiia aomata n te tabo ae teuana; ma e kona n kangaanga ngkana a a mwaing aomata nako aaba aika boou ike e aki tei iai te maneaba, ao a a aki manga botaki bwa a na ti teuana.

## Echo

E kaota te botaki n te maneaba te bwai ae e kainnanoaki iroun [te maneaba](position_te_maneaba.md): te kawai are a na botaki iai aomata ni kabane bwa a na ti teuana ao a na karaoa aia babaire. Nakon te botaki aei e taua iai te boowi [te unimwane](persona_te_unimwane.md), ao e taekina te raoiroi nakoia kaain te kawa ni kabane.
