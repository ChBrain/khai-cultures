# Place: Banaba

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Te abamwakoro ae e rietata riki nakon abamwakoro nako i Kiribati, ae tiaki te abamwakoro ae e maraurau ma e kateaki man te atibu ae e rietata. Iai i aona te kaubwai ae te bwaoki, ae e a mwamwakuriaki ni karokoa e a bati ni kerikaki te aba. Kaaina, ake I-Banaba, a kakaaki nako Rabi i Biiti ngke e a mwamwakuriaki te bwaoki, ao e a riki te abamwakoro nakon te tabo are e a kerikaki abana. E uota te karaki ae e kammaraki, ae te karaki n te aba are e a tia ni mwamwakuriaki ana kaubwai ao a a kakaaki kaaina nako abaia ae boou.

## Offers

Te tabo are e kaotaki iai te kangaanga are e roko nakon te aba ngke e a mwamwakuriaki ana kaubwai iroun te tautaeka mai tinaniku, ao e tei bwa te karaki nakoia I-Kiribati ni kabane bwa e na aki manga riki te kabuanibwai aei nakoia.

## Withheld

Te maeka ae e teimatoa i aon te aba. E uota Banaba te maraki are a a kakaaki kaaina nako abaia ae boou, ao e tei bwa te karaki are e na aki mwaninga te botanaomata, bwa e kona n reke te maraki ngkana e a kabuaa te aomata abana ma ana katei.
