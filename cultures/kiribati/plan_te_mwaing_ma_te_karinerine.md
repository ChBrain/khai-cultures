# Plan: te mwaing ma te karinerine

## Taxonomy

Te babaire: te aekaki ni babaire are e taua te [aba ae e a kerikaki](position_te_aba_ae_e_a_kerikaki.md) - te kawai are e na kawakinaki iai te maneaba, te taetae, ao te aba ngkana e a rake te taari, ao e na mwaing iai te botanaomata ma te karinerine ao tiaki bwa kaain te kabuanibwai.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Katauraoa te botanaomata ibukin te taari ae e a rake: kamarurungaki te aba ao kawakinaki te kawa mai aon te taari ngkana e kona; reireinaki ataei aika a rikirake bwa a na kona ni mwakuri n aaba aika boou ngkana a a mwaing; ao kawakinaki te katei ni Kiribati ma te taetae ni Kiribati, te maneaba, ao te mwaie, bwa a na aki bua e ngae ngke a a mwaing aomata nako aaba aika boou. E na mwaing te botanaomata ma te karinerine, ao tiaki bwa kaain te kabuanibwai.

## Orders

- Nakon [te unimwane](persona_te_unimwane.md): taua te katei ni Kiribati ma te taetae, ao uotia nakoia ataei aika a rikirake bwa a na aki bua e ngae ngke a a mwaing.
- Nakon te tia kairiri n aron [Anote Tong](persona_anote_tong.md): teimatoa n taekina te kangaanga nakon te aonnaba, ao karaoa te babaire ibukin te mwaing ma te karinerine.
- Nakon te botanaomata: kawakina te maneaba, te taetae, ao te aba; ao ngkana e a kainnanoaki te mwaing, mwaing ma te karinerine ao taua te katei i aaba aika boou.

## Implementation

Te babaire n "te mwaing ma te karinerine" are e karaoaki iroun te tautaeka; te reirei nakoia ataei aika a rikirake bwa a na kona ni mwakuri i aaba aika boou; te ukeuke nakon kawakinan te aba ao kamarurungana ngkana e kona; ao te taekina te kangaanga ni bibitaki ni boong nakon aaba aika abwabwaki ao botaki aika bubura n te aonnaba.

- [ ] Kawakinaki te maneaba, te taetae ni Kiribati, ao te mwaie i aaba aika boou, bwa a na aki bua nakoia ataei aika a rikirake.
