# Process: te mwaie

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Te koraki ngkana iai te botaki n te maneaba ke te bukamaru: a anene tabeman, ao a mwaie tabeman ni katei te aroaroni baia, roroaia, ao mataia. E kateaki te tia mwaie n tei, ao e mwaie te karaki ma te tangira nakon te aba n te aroaroni rabwatana. E kainnanoaki tii te anene ma te koraki, ao e a riki te mwaie bwa kanoan te botaki ni kabane.

## Direction

Nakon te kaotaki n te tangira ao te karaki: te mwaie bon te kawai are e taekinaki nako iai te tangira nakon te aba ma te taari, ao e uotaki iai te karaki man te roro nakon te roro. Tiaki tii te mwaie ibukin te kakukurei, ma bon te aro ni katei are e kaotaki iai te tangira ao te karaki ae e na aki taekinaki n taeka tii ngaia.

## Lever

E kairaki iroun te kainnano nakon te kaotaki n te tangira nakon te aba ae e tangiraki, ao iroun te anene are e taua te karaki ma te katei. E teimatoa n aki ongeaba nakon te uamwawa ao te raroa, bwa e uotaki te mwaie nako aaba aika boou irouia ake a a mwaing; ma e kona n kangaanga ngkana a a aki manga reireiaki ataei aika a rikirake i aaba aika boou, ao a a aki ataa nanon te anene are e mwaie iai.

## Echo

E kaota te mwaie te bwai ae e kairaki iroun [te marawa](position_te_marawa.md): bwa te tangira nakon te aba ma te taari e taekinaki nako n te aroaroni rabwatana ao tiaki tii n taeka. N te mwaie aei e kaota iai te tangira nakon te aba [te tia mwaie](persona_te_tia_mwaie.md), ao e uota te karaki man te roro nakon te roro.
