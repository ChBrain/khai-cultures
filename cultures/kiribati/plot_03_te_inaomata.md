# Plot: te inaomata

## Taxonomy

[play_kiribati](play_kiribati.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Imwin te kolonia ae maan, e a roko te tai are e a kani kairiri te botanaomata i Kiribati n oin nanona. A botaki aomata ni kabane, ao e a katauraoaki te aba bwa e na taua oin ana tautaeka, ao a a rineaki taani kairiri mai buakoia oin kaain te aba.

## Action

N 1979 e reke te inaomata, ao e a riki Kiribati bwa te aba ae e tautaeka i aana. E tei Ieremia Tabai bwa te moan beretitenti i Tarawa, ae e uarereke ana ririki ma e rabakau ao e nimamannei. E taua te tautaeka ae boou n te aro are e karinea iai te maneaba ao te unimwane, ao e uota te katei ni maneaba ao te boowi ae e reke iroun te koraki nakon te tautaeka ae boou. E a riki te botanaomata bwa te aba ae teuana, e ngae ngke a uamwawa i aon abamwakoro aika a bati.

## Stage

- [Ieremia Tabai](persona_tabai.md): te moan beretitenti, ae e taua te tautaeka ae boou ao e karinea te maneaba ao te unimwane.
- [Tarawa](place_tarawa.md): te tabo are e reke iai te inaomata ao e tei iai te tautaeka ae boou.
- [te maneaba](position_te_maneaba.md): te katei are e taua bwa aana te tautaeka ae boou, ae e botiia iai aomata bwa a na ti teuana.

## Tension

E reke te inaomata nakon te botanaomata ae e uamwawa i aon abamwakoro aika a bati, ao e taua te tautaeka ae boou n oin ana katei ni maneaba. Ma e na teimatoa te tautaeka aei tii ngkana e taua te boowi ae e reke iroun te koraki, ao e botiia aomata mai abamwakoro nako bwa a na ti teuana, e ngae ngke a raroa i marenaia n te taari ae e raababa.
