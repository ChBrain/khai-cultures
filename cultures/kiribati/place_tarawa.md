# Place: Tarawa

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Te abamwakoro ae te tabo n tautaeka i Kiribati, ae e karaoaki man te kawa aika a bati i etan te taari. I maiakin Tarawa e mena te tabo ae e a rangi ni mwaiti aomata iai, ae e a kanga ribuakoia, ike e mena iai te tautaeka, te makete, ao te kawa aika a botiia aomata mai abamwakoro nako. Bon te abamwakoro naba are e roko iai te buaka are e bati n te aonnaba, ngke a buaka iai aomata aika a bati i aon te tabo ae uarereke aei, ao e kammaraki nakoia aomata aika a bati.

## Holds

- [te kolonia ao te buaka](plot_02_te_kolonia_ao_te_buaka.md): te abamwakoro are e roko iai te buaka are e bati n te aonnaba, ae e kammaraki ao e bati iai te mate.
- [te inaomata](plot_03_te_inaomata.md): te tabo are e reke iai te inaomata n 1979 ao e tei iai te tautaeka ae boou i aan te kairiri are Tabai.

## Offers

Te tabo are e botiia iai aomata mai abamwakoro nako bwa a na ti teuana n te tautaeka, ao e tei iai te maneaba bwa nnen te boowi nakon te aba ni kabane. Bon te tabo are e reke iai te inaomata, ao e tei iai te tautaeka ae boou ae e taua te katei ni maneaba.

## Withheld

Te raoiroi ae aki kammaraki. E uota Tarawa te kammaraki are e roko man te buaka are e bati i aona, ao e uota naba te kangaanga are e a rangi ni mwaiti aomata i maiakina ae e a kanga ribuakoia ao e a roko te taari nakon te kawa, n te aro are e kaotaki iai te kangaanga ae e roko nakon te aba ae moan te uarereke.
