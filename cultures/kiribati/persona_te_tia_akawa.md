# Persona: te tia akawa

## Taxonomy

[te marawa](position_te_marawa.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Te tia akawa ae e rabakau, ae te tia borau n te baurua, ae e ataa te taari kanga oin abana. E atatai n te itoi, te naao, ao te ang, ao e kona ni borau i marenan abamwakoro ae raroa n aki bua. E ninikoria, e nimamannei, ao e ataibwai n te taari ae e na karekea iai kanaia ana utu. E [taetae](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) ao e [iaiangoa](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) te taetae ni Kiribati, ae te taetae are e mena iai araia ika, naao, ao ang ni kabane, te taetae are e a tia ni karaoaki ibukin te taari. Araia bwaai man aaba aika boou ake a roko ma kaibuke e [uotia](process_speaking_carried.md), ao e [warekia ao e iria n aki kangaanga te beeba ni borau ae boou](process_reading_followed.md) ngkana e karekea. E ataa te taari ni kabane n oin ana taetae, ae iai araia bwaai ni kabane are e ataai te aomata are e maeka i aon te taari, ma araia bwaai aika boou aika a roko mai aon te aonnaba a aki bane n reke i nanon ana taetae: ngaia are e mena te aki kona n uota te bwai ni kabane nakon oin te taetae, ao iai te baa ae e bua iai te ataibwai i tabon te taetae.

## Action

I nanon [aomata n te maneaba](plot_01_aomata_n_te_maneaba.md) e ataa te taari ao e karekea kanaia ana utu mai iai, ao e kaota [te marawa](position_te_marawa.md) bwa kanaia ao kawaia I-Kiribati. E uota te rabakau n te borau ma te akawa man te roro nakon te roro, ao e taua te ataibwai n te taari ae moan te kakawaki n te katei.

## Shadow

Te tia akawa ae e ataa te taari e kona n aki nora te kangaanga ae e roko: e kona n aki kakoauaa bwa e a kona ni bibitaki te taari ao e a rake, ke n aki katauraoia ibukin te bong are e a aki manga reke iai kanaia man te taari are e a tia n ataai.

## Tell

E nora te ang ao e ataa te kanga ae e na roko, ao e borau n te baurua nakon te tabo are e ataia bwa iai iai te ika, n aki kainnanoa te bwai teuana ma ana atatai n te taari are e a tia n reke man te roro nako.
