# Plot: te kolonia ao te buaka

## Taxonomy

[play_kiribati](play_kiribati.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

N te tienture ae nimaua tabwina, e roko te tautaeka n Ingiriti ao e karaoa Kiribati bwa aana te kolonia ae e atongaki bwa te Gilbert and Ellice Islands. Ao e reke i aon Banaba te kaubwai ae te bwaoki, ae e a riki bwa te bwai ae e kani karekeaki iroun te tautaeka mai tinaniku.

## Action

E mwamwakuriaki te bwaoki i aon Banaba ni karokoa e a bati ni kerikaki te aba, ao kaaina, ake I-Banaba, a a kakaaki nako Rabi i Biiti, n te aro ni kammaraki are e na aki mwaninga te botanaomata. Ao i nanon te buaka are e bati n te aonnaba, e roko te buaka are e bati i Tarawa, ngke a buaka iai aomata aika a bati i aon te tabo ae uarereke aei, ao e bati iai te mate ao te kammaraki nakoia aomata aika a bati. E reke nakoia I-Kiribati te kangaanga are e roko man te tautaeka mai tinaniku ao man te buaka are tiaki oin aia buaka.

## Stage

- [Banaba](place_banaba.md): te abamwakoro are e a mwamwakuriaki ana kaubwai, ao a a kakaaki kaaina nako abaia ae boou.
- [Tarawa](place_tarawa.md): te abamwakoro are e roko iai te buaka are e bati i aona, ae e kammaraki ao e bati iai te mate.
- [te marawa](position_te_marawa.md): te taari ae e raababa are e roko man iai te tautaeka mai tinaniku ao te buaka.

## Tension

E roko te kangaanga nakon te botanaomata man te tautaeka mai tinaniku, are e mwamwakuria te kaubwai n Banaba ao e kakaaki kaaina nako abaia ae boou, ao man te buaka are tiaki oin aia buaka ma e bati iai te mate i aon abaia. E kaotaki iai te kangaanga are e roko nakon te aba ae moan te uarereke, are e na uotaki bwa te karaki nakon te roro ae boou bwa a na aki manga maamatebwai abaia ma aia katei.
