# Piece: te baurua

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

I aon te taari i marenan abamwakoro, i aan baia taani borau aika a rabakau: te baurua, te waa ae e mwawa ae iai rama, ae e karaoaki man te kai ao e bwabwainaki te raabono. Bon te waa are e borauaki iai te taari ae e raababa man te abamwakoro teuana nakon teuana.

## Load Bearing

E tei ibukin te ataibwai n te taari ae e karaoa [te marawa](position_te_marawa.md) bwa kawaia I-Kiribati. E borauaki iai te taari ae e raababa, ao e karekeaki iai te ika, ao e botiia iai aomata mai abamwakoro nako. N te aba ae e karaoaki man abamwakoro aika a uamwawa i etan te taari, te baurua bon te kawai are e a aki uamwawa iai aomata: bon te waa are e ataaki iai te itoi, te naao, ao te ang. Ngkana akea te baurua, e na uamwawa te aba ao e na bua te ataibwai n te taari are e karaoa te botanaomata bwa a na ti teuana.

## Apparent

Te waa ae e karaoaki man te kai, ae iai rama i rarikina bwa e na aki tabwena, ao iai te raa ae e karaoaki man te biti ke te bwai teuana ae e tau iai te ang. E mwawa ao e ririko n te taari; ma nanona ae e bati e mena n te ataibwai are e kainnanoaki ibukin borauna, ao n aroia taani borau aika a ataa te taari.

## Yearbook

- E karaoaki man te kai ma rama ao te raa, bwa e na borau i marenan abamwakoro i etan te taari ae e raababa.
- E borauaki iai te taari iroun taani borau aika a atatai n te itoi, te naao, ao te ang, n aki kainnanoa te bwai teuana.
- E teimatoa bwa te bwai ni katei ae e kakawaki, are e kaotaki iai te ataibwai n te taari ae e a tia ni karekeaki man te roro nako.
