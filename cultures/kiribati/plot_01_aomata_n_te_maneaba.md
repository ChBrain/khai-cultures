# Plot: aomata n te maneaba

## Taxonomy

[play_kiribati](play_kiribati.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

I taai ake rimoa a roko aomata mai Mikronesia ni borau i aon te taari ae e raababa, ao a karekei aia tabo i aon abamwakoro aika a uarereke aika a uamwawa i etan te taari. A maeka i aon te aba ae e aki bati iai te bwai n amwarake, ao a reireiaki te akawa, te borau, ao te taua n te ni.

## Action

A karekei aia kawa aomata i aon abamwakoro nako, ao a katea te maneaba bwa nnen te boowi ao te katei. I nanon te maneaba a botaki aomata ni kabane bwa a na karaoa aia babaire ma te raoiroi ae ti teuana, ao e taua te boowi ao e taekina te raoiroi te unimwane. E reke iai te katei ni Kiribati: te karinerine nakon te kara, te boowi ae e reke iroun te koraki, ao te bootaki nakon te utu ao te kawa. A reireiaki naba te ataibwai n te taari, te itoi, te naao, ao te ang, ake a karekea iai kanaia man te taari ae e raababa.

## Stage

- [te unimwane](persona_te_unimwane.md): te tia taua te katei ao te boowi i nanon te maneaba, ae e taekina te raoiroi nakoia kaain te kawa.
- [te tia akawa](persona_te_tia_akawa.md): te tia akawa ae e rabakau, ae e karekea kanaia ana utu man te taari ae e raababa.
- [te maneaba](position_te_maneaba.md): te auti ae e botiia iai aomata bwa a na ti teuana ao a na karaoa aia babaire ma te boowi.

## Tension

E karaoaki te aba ae boou irouia aomata aika a roko ni borau i aon te taari, ao a katea te maneaba bwa nnen te katei ae e botiia iai aomata bwa a na ti teuana. Ma e karaoaki te aba aei man abamwakoro aika a uarereke aika a aki bati iai te bwai, ngaia are e na teimatoa te maeka tii ngkana e taua te aba te boowi ae e reke iroun te koraki, ao e taua te ataibwai n te taari are e karekea iai kanaia.
