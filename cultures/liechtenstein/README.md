# Liechtenstein (Fürstentum Liechtenstein): a khai culture

Liechtenstein staged as a full khai play, ISO LI, language de. The sovereign principality of the Rhine Valley: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Alpine and financial life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_liechtenstein.md](play_liechtenstein.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_liechtenstein.md](pitch_liechtenstein.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Liechtenstein's profile inflected by its sovereignty heritage and Alpine Rhine Valley, naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Vaduz and Schellenberg acquisition, the Holy Roman Empire sovereignty of 1719, and the post-WWII financial hub transition.
- **Personas:** Franz (traditional Alpine craftsman archetype) and Katharina (modern trust officer/lawyer), written with German language reads.
- **Positions:** das Fürstenhaus (the princely house), das Rheintal (the Rhine valley), der Finanzplatz (the financial hub).
- **Places:** Schloss Vaduz (the princely castle), Malbun (the Alpine resort).
- **Processes:** der Alpabtrieb (cattle drive).
- **Pieces:** der Fürstenhut (the princely crown), die Stiftung (the Liechtenstein trust).
