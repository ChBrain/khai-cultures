# Kansas: a khai culture

Kansas staged as a full khai play, ISO US-KS, language en. The Sunflower State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Bleeding Kansas conflicts, Homestead winter wheat boom, and Wichita aviation boom encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_kansas.md](play_kansas.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_kansas.md](pitch_kansas.md), the Kansas key.
- **Plots (history):** the Bleeding Kansas Conflicts of 1854, the Homestead Wheat Boom of the 1870s, and the Wichita Aviation Boom of the 1920s.
- **Personas:** Henry (aviation inspector) and Sarah (wheat farm manager), written in English.
- **Positions:** homestead resolve (prairie agricultural endurance/free-state legacy), aviation pioneer (aerospace design ambition/manufacturing precision).
- **Places:** Wichita Hangar, Kansas Wheatfield.
- **Processes:** wheat harvesting (row crop combine harvesting), aircraft riveting (aluminum fuselage assembly).
- **Pieces:** a wheat sheaf, an altimeter.
- **Plan:** the aquifer plan.
