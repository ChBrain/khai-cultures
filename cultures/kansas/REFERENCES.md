## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_kansas.md](pitch_kansas.md) is written from Kansas's profile inflected by its aviation hangars, wheat fields, and prairie communities, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Kansas base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 44                                       |
| Individualism                 | 78                                       |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Kansas's low power distance in hangar crews, active community panels, and low uncertainty avoidance in soil conservation innovation).
