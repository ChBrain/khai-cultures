# Plot: Homestead Wheat Boom 1870

## Taxonomy

[play_kansas](play_kansas.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the 1870s, Mennonite immigrants introduce Turkey Red winter wheat to central Kansas, launching the wheat boom.

## Action

The boom establishes the state as a global breadbasket, developing [wheat harvesting](process_wheat_harvesting.md). Sarah manages harvest logistics on family farms, while Henry coordinates regional transportation records for grain shipments.

## Stage

- [homestead resolve](position_homestead_resolve.md) : the prairie agricultural endurance, cooperative farming, and soil pride.
- [wheat sheaf](piece_wheat_sheaf.md) : the Turkey Red wheat stalks representing the agricultural boom.
- [place_kansas_wheatfield](place_kansas_wheatfield.md) : the central winter wheat plains.

## Tension

Intensive plowing and crop specialization deplete soil nutrients and set the stage for severe wind erosion.
