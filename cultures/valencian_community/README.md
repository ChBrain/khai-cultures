# Valencian Community: a khai culture

Valencian Community staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-VC, language es. Comunitat Valenciana: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Expulsion of Moriscos, Battle of Almansa, and Fallas festival origin encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_valencian_community.md](play_valencian_community.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_valencian_community.md](pitch_valencian_community.md), the Valencian Community key.
- **Plots (history):** the Expulsion of the Moriscos of 1609, the Battle of Almansa of 1707, and the Fallas festival origin of the 19th century.
- **Personas:** Vicent (rice farmer) and Carme (Fallas artisan), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** huerta farming (irrigated agriculture/irrigation court/cooperative ditch), valencian language (bilinguism/regional variety/cultural identity).
- **Places:** Albufera Lake, Central Market.
- **Processes:** paella cooking (traditional wood-fired paella), valencian firework (traditional pyrotechnics).
- **Pieces:** paella pan, fallas puppet (ninot).
- **Plan:** the Albufera preservation plan.
