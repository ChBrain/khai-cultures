## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_arkansas.md](pitch_arkansas.md) is written from Arkansas's profile inflected by its Ozark valleys, corporate offices, and hot springs, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Individualism                 | 78                                         |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Arkansas's low power distance in retail teams, active community panels, and low uncertainty avoidance in logistics technology innovation).
