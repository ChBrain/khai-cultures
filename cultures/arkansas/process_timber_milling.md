## Initiated by

Timber workers, sawmill operators, and forestry safety coordinators, conducted year-round in regional mills.

## Direction

On the sawmill floor. Debarking raw pine logs, sawing logs into structural lumber, drying lumber in kilns, grading timber quality, and loading transport flatbeds.

## Lever

The traditional timber processing method that supports the state's forestry economy. It drives local manufacturing jobs and paper exports, though it requires strict watershed protection audits and faces fire safety hazards.
