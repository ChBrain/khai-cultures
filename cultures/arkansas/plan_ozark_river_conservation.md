# Plan: the Ozark plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and agricultural science designed to manage agricultural runoff and protect the Ozark Mountain watersheds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Conserve the river watershed by regulating poultry farm waste, restoring forested river buffers, upgrading municipal wastewater systems, and monitoring river nitrogen levels.

## Orders

- To the Department of Environmental Quality: Enforce agricultural waste standards and audit poultry processing permits.
- To civil rights advocates like [Daisy](persona_daisy.md): Coordinate local community environmental education campaigns and advocate for historic watershed protection.
- To logistics planners like [Roy](persona_roy.md): Support corporate warehouse runoff telemetry audits and implement eco-efficient packaging waste reductions.

## Implementation

The nitrogen water measurements; the forest buffer erosion audits; and the deployment of field water monitors.
