## Cue

In July 1962, Sam Walton opens the first Walmart store in Rogers, launching a high-volume retail supply chain.

## Action

The retail model transforms global logistics, developing [retail distribution](position_retail_distribution.md). The [shopping cart](piece_shopping_cart.md) represents consumer design. Roy audits early store blueprints, while Daisy coordinates logistics history workshops at regional libraries.
