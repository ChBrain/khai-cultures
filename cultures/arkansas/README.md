# Arkansas: a khai culture

Arkansas staged as a full khai play, ISO US-AR, language en. The Natural State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Hot Springs bathhouse era, Little Rock Nine integration, and Wal-Mart retail revolution encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_arkansas.md](play_arkansas.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_arkansas.md](pitch_arkansas.md), the Arkansas key.
- **Plots (history):** the Hot Springs Bathhouse Era of the 1910s, the Little Rock Nine Integration of 1957, and the Wal-Mart Retail Revolution of 1962.
- **Personas:** Daisy (civil rights archivist) and Roy (Ozarks park ranger), written in English.
- **Positions:** ozark heritage (mountain folklore/thermal tourism), retail distribution (high-volume supply chains/corporate logistics).
- **Places:** Hot Springs, Walmart Store.
- **Processes:** diamond mining (crater of diamonds search), timber milling (pine timber milling).
- **Pieces:** a diamond crystal, a shopping cart.
- **Plan:** the Ozark river conservation plan.
