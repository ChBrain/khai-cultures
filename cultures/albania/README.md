# Albania: a khai culture

Albania staged as a full khai play, ISO AL, language sq. An ancient people whose
tongue is kin to none, as a theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as
the cast, the roles its language, its sworn word, and its nation-above-creed
encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a
single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_albania.md](play_albania.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_sq.md](pitch_sq.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Albania's
  profile (proud and clan-bound, hierarchical, honor-driven and hospitable,
  anxious for security, hardy and defiant, family above all) and naming no
  dimension.
- **Plots (history):** Skanderbeg (15th c.), the National Awakening (the Rilindja,
  19th c. to 1912), the Hoxha dictatorship, and the 1990s opening and exodus - each
  casting the elements it needs.
- **Personas:** Skënderbeu (Skanderbeg), Naim Frashëri, and Nënë Tereza (Mother
  Teresa) as the historic cast, plus two defining archetypes, Drita (older,
  highland, the kanun and besa, religious harmony) and Ardit (younger, the
  post-1991 emigrant whose Albanian thins toward Italian and Greek). Each is wired
  to the language engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** gjuha shqipe (the Albanian language, an Indo-European isolate
  alone on its own branch, kin to none, with Gheg and Tosk), besa (the sworn word
  and sacred hospitality, the code by which Albanians sheltered Jews in WWII),
  shqiptaria (Albanianism, the nation above creed, the coexistence of Muslim,
  Orthodox, Catholic and Bektashi).
- **Places:** Kruja (Skanderbeg's fortress town, the national shrine), Tirana (the
  capital, Hoxha's pyramid and the bunkers, the colourful post-communist opening).
- **Processes:** mikpritja (sacred hospitality, besa in practice), iso-polifonia
  (the UNESCO drone-based multipart folk singing of the south).
- **Pieces:** shqiponja (the double-headed black eagle, Skanderbeg's standard and
  the flag of the "land of the eagles"), kulla (the stone tower-house of the
  highlands, the kanun and refuge in blood feud).
