# Plot: The Pearl Harbor Attack 1941

## Taxonomy

[play_hawaii](play_hawaii.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In December 1941, Japanese aircraft launch a surprise bombardment on the naval base at Pearl Harbor, sinking major warships.

## Action

The attack draws the United States into World War II, turning the islands into a critical military outpost in the Pacific. Keanu coordinates with restoration projects in the harbor area, while Leilani researches historical records of the civilian impact of the raid.

## Stage

- [place_pearl_harbor](place_pearl_harbor.md) : the deep-water harbor that served as the base for the Pacific Fleet.
- [plan_native_forest_restoration](plan_native_forest_restoration.md) : the environmental plan developed to clean up surrounding lands.
- [Aloha spirit](position_aloha_spirit.md) : the community resilience and hospitality maintained during military martial law.

## Tension

The attack leads to the declaration of martial law across the islands, suspending civil liberties and placing the civilian population under military control.
