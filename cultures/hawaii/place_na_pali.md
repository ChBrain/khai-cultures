## Shown

The secluded green cliffs and deep valleys along Kauai's northwest coast, featuring towering coastal ridges, sandy beaches, and native forest canopies.

## Holds

- the traditional agricultural settlement in the plot [Polynesian Voyaging c. 1000 AD](plot_01_voyaging.md) : the native sanctuary.

## Offers

## Withheld

The impact of invasive feral animals. While celebrated as a pristine, untouched coastal paradise, the valleys face severe soil erosion and native plant loss caused by herds of introduced goats and pigs, a degradation kept out of scenic helicopter tour commentary.
