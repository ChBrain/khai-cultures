# Plot: Polynesian Voyaging c. 1000 AD

## Taxonomy

[play_hawaii](play_hawaii.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Around 1000 AD, Polynesian navigators travel thousands of miles across the Pacific, settling the Hawaiian archipelago.

## Action

The settlement establishes [Aloha spirit](position_aloha_spirit.md) connection to the ocean and land, utilizing double-hulled outrigger canoes. The [outrigger paddle](piece_outrigger_paddle.md) represents this early maritime technology. Keanu builds and navigates canoes using these traditional methods, while Leilani documents oral histories of the early voyages.

## Stage

- [Aloha spirit](position_aloha_spirit.md) : the welcoming respect, community harmony, and connection to the ocean.
- [outrigger paddle](piece_outrigger_paddle.md) : the wooden paddle representing traditional voyaging technology.
- [outrigger carving](process_outrigger_carving.md) : the manual wood-carving process of shaping voyaging canoes.

## Tension

The long voyages across the open Pacific carry extreme risk of disorientation and dehydration, demanding perfect astronomical navigation.
