# Position: Aloha spirit

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The communal hospitality, mutual respect, and ecological stewardship born of island history. It possesses a belief in sharing resources, welcoming visitors, and preserving family networks.

## Orders

That one acts with kindness, respects local customs, and cares for the community.

## Loses

Its balance when commercialized tourism reduces the spirit to a shallow marketing gimmick, pricing out local residents and depleting resources.

## Drives

Influences the oceanic landing in the plot [Polynesian Voyaging](plot_01_voyaging.md) and drives the navigator work of [Keanu](persona_keanu.md).
