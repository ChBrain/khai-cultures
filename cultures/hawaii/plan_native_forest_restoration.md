# Plan: the native forest plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and conservation forestry designed to restore native forest canopies and protect endangered species.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Restore native forests by constructing ungulate-proof fencing, eradicating invasive weeds, planting native koa and ohia saplings, and monitoring bird populations.

## Orders

- To the Department of Land: Establish and monitor forest reserve fencing projects.
- To navigators like [Keanu](persona_keanu.md): Advise on sustainable koa harvesting and assist in native sapling propagation.
- To archivists like [Leilani](persona_leilani.md): Document traditional ecological knowledge and coordinate community planting forums.

## Implementation

The ungulate fencing construction; the invasive species eradication sweeps; and the deployment of forest canopy monitors.
