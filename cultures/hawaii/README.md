# Hawaii: a khai culture

Hawaii staged as a full khai play, ISO US-HI, language en. The Aloha State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Polynesian voyaging, kingdom overthrow, and Pearl Harbor legacy encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_hawaii.md](play_hawaii.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_hawaii.md](pitch_hawaii.md), the Hawaii key.
- **Plots (history):** the Polynesian voyaging settlement (c. 1000 AD), the overthrow of the Kingdom of Hawaii in 1893, and the Pearl Harbor attack in 1941.
- **Personas:** Keanu (outrigger navigator) and Leilani (cultural historian), written in English.
- **Positions:** Aloha spirit (welcoming community), Hawaiian sovereignty (cultural protection).
- **Places:** Pearl Harbor, Na Pali Coast.
- **Processes:** outrigger carving (canoe craftsmanship), hula storytelling (sacred dance).
- **Pieces:** an outrigger paddle, a traditional lei.
- **Plan:** the native forest restoration plan.
