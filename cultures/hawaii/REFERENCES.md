# Hawaii: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch (kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_hawaii.md](pitch_hawaii.md) is written from Hawaii's profile inflected by its Polynesian settlement, royal kingdom history, US annexation, military installations, and cultural revival, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Hawaii base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 44                                       |
| Individualism                 | 70                                       |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 50                                       |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 40                                       |
| Long Term Orientation         | 28                                       |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Hawaii's close community networks, _ʻohana_ collective values, high indulgence in recreation/festive arts, and royal historical legacy).
