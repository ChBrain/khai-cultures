# Plot: The Overthrow of the Hawaiian Kingdom 1893

## Taxonomy

[play_hawaii](play_hawaii.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In January 1893, American business interests, supported by US marines, depose Queen Liliʻuokalani and seize government buildings.

## Action

The coup establishes a provisional government, dismantling the sovereign monarchy and paving the way for annexation. The [traditional lei](piece_traditional_lei.md) remains a symbol of native hospitality and identity. Leilani curates royal palace documents from the overthrow, while Keanu coordinates community rallies supporting land reclamation.

## Stage

- [Hawaiian sovereignty](position_hawaiian_sovereignty.md) : the native movement for self-determination and cultural preservation.
- [traditional lei](piece_traditional_lei.md) : the flower lei representing native protocol and diplomatic respect.
- [hula storytelling](process_hula_storytelling.md) : the sacred dance used to preserve historic narratives during the suppression of the monarchy.

## Tension

The overthrow strips native Hawaiians of political voting power and land control, violating international law and creating enduring sovereignty campaigns.
