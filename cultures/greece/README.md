# Greece: a khai culture

Greece staged as a full khai play, ISO GR, language el. The cradle of Western
civilization and the oldest living European tongue, staged as a theatre: its history as
the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its language and faith encode, its
places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds
this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its Estate; a culture
with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_greece.md](play_greece.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_el.md](pitch_el.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Greece's
  profile (proud, hospitable, family- and community-bound, with the highest need for
  security of all and a long melancholy beneath the kefi) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** Classical Athens, Byzantium and Ottoman rule, the War of
  Independence of 1821, and the Asia Minor Catastrophe of 1922 - each casting the
  elements it needs.
- **Personas:** Homer (the oral source of the language) and Dionysios Solomos (the
  national poet of Demotic) as the historic cast, plus three archetypes: Eleni (the
  Orthodox village grandmother), Nikos (the young, crisis-and-emigration generation), and
  Stavros (the sea captain of the maritime diaspora). Each is wired to the language engine
  by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** η ελληνική γλώσσα (the oldest living European tongue and its diglossia),
  η Ορθοδοξία (the Church fused with national identity), το φιλότιμο (the untranslatable
  virtue of honour).
- **Places:** η Αθήνα (the city of the Parthenon and of the refugees), το Αιγαίο (the sea
  of islands, the maritime life and the diaspora).
- **Processes:** το γλέντι (the feast and dance), το καφενείο (the coffeehouse).
- **Pieces:** το κομπολόι (the worry beads), το καράβι (the ship of a seafaring people).
- **Plan:** το γλωσσικό ζήτημα, the century-long settling of the language question in
  favour of Demotic.
