# Madagascar: a khai culture

Madagascar staged as a full khai play, ISO MG, language mg. The great red island,
a world apart born of an ocean crossing, as a theatre: its history as the plot
line, its people as the cast, the roles its language and ancestors encode, its
places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_madagascar.md](play_madagascar.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_mg.md](pitch_mg.md), the layer written from Madagascar's own
  profile (a deep collectivism bound by kinship, a long memory turned toward the
  ancestors, and the patience and indirection of a island people) and naming no
  dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the crossing (the Austronesian voyage across the Indian
  Ocean that peopled the island, joined by African and Arab strands), the
  unification under Andrianampoinimerina, the royal hill of the Rova and the
  highland kingdom, and the voice of the island carried into the modern age - each
  casting the elements it needs.
- **Personas:** Andrianampoinimerina (the king who unified the highlands),
  Jean-Joseph Rabearivelo (the great poet), Rakotozafy (the master of the valiha),
  and Flavien Ranaivo (the poet of the hain-teny) as the historic cast, plus the
  archetype Ramboa, the everyday Malagasy of the rice terraces. Each is wired to
  the language engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** the distant origin (ny fiaviana lavitra, the island peopled from
  across the sea), fihavanana (the deep ethic of kinship and solidarity), and the
  ancestors (ny razana, the dead who remain present and binding).
- **Places:** Antananarivo (the highland capital and the royal hill), Morondava
  (the western coast and the avenue of the baobabs).
- **Processes:** the famadihana (the joyful turning of the bones, the reburial
  that renews the bond with the ancestors), rice-growing (ny fambolena vary, the
  terraced paddies that order the year).
- **Pieces:** the zebu (ny omby, cattle as wealth, honour, and sacrifice), the
  valiha (the bamboo tube-zither, the voice of the highlands).
