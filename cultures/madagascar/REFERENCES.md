# Madagascar: References

The provenance behind this culture: the cultural profile that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical and cultural
sources behind the plots, personas, places, processes, and pieces.

## Pitch profile

The pitch [pitch_mg.md](pitch_mg.md) is written from Madagascar's cultural
profile and names no dimension and carries no number. Madagascar is not scored
individually in Geert Hofstede's classic six-dimension dataset, so the pitch is
tuned qualitatively rather than from published figures: a strongly collective
society ordered by fihavanana (kinship and mutual obligation), a long-term
orientation anchored in the ancestors (razana), high power distance in the older
hierarchies, and a marked preference for indirection and consensus. This profile
is recorded here so the basis is on the record without entering the prose.
