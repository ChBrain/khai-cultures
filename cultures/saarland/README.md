# Saarland: a khai culture

Saarland staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-SL, language de. The borderland state between Germany and France: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its coal, steel, and French-influenced life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_saarland.md](play_saarland.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_saarland.md](pitch_saarland.md), the Saarland key.
- **Plots (history):** the Saar Statute referendum of 1955, the "Little Reunification" of 1957, and the history of the Völklingen Ironworks.
- **Personas:** Michel (traditional retired steelworker archetype) and Charlotte (modern French-German cultural mediator), written in German.
- **Positions:** das Grenzland (borderlands identity), der Schwenker (convivial grilling ethos), das Bergbau-Erbe (industrial legacy).
- **Places:** die Saarschleife (Saar Loop), die Völklinger Hütte (ironworks heritage site).
- **Processes:** das Schwenken (tripod grilling practice).
- **Pieces:** der Schwenker-Grill (tripod grill), der Saar-Vertrag (the treaty of 1956).
- **Plan:** der ökologische Stahlumbau (ecological steel transition plan).
