# Plan: the forest preservation plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and conservation management designed to protect the Appalachian mountain forests and Piedmont parklands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Foster forest preservation by restricting commercial timber logging in ecological zones, planting native hardwood buffers, and conducting wildfire mitigation.

## Orders

- To the Forest Service: Enforce logging restrictions and monitor forest canopy health.
- To scientists like [Ethan](persona_ethan.md): Conduct soil runoff modeling and analyze climate impacts on plant species.
- To community coordinators like [Sarah](persona_sarah.md): Coordinate native tree planting and advocate for sustainable timber harvesting.

## Implementation

The forest canopy audits; the controlled burn operations; and the deployment of riparian watershed monitors.
