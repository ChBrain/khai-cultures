# North Carolina: a khai culture

North Carolina staged as a full khai play, ISO US-NC, language en. The Tar Heel State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its early colonizing attempts, aviation triumphs, and high-tech Research Triangle life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_north_carolina.md](play_north_carolina.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_north_carolina.md](pitch_north_carolina.md), the North Carolina key.
- **Plots (history):** the Lost Colony of Roanoke in 1587, the Wright brothers' flight at Kitty Hawk in 1903, and the Research Triangle Park founding in 1959.
- **Personas:** Ethan (Research Triangle scientist) and Sarah (Appalachian hand-weaver), written in English.
- **Positions:** Research Triangle (scientific progress), Appalachian heritage (rural tradition).
- **Places:** Research Triangle Park, Roanoke Island.
- **Processes:** biotech synthesis (biomedical research), hand weaving (mountain craft).
- **Pieces:** a biotech vial, a hand loom.
- **Plan:** the forest preservation plan.
