## Cue

In December 1903, the Wright brothers conduct the first powered and controlled aircraft flights at Kitty Hawk.

## Action

The achievement establishes the state as a historic aviation landmark, launching the era of modern flight. Ethan studies the early aerodynamics and engineering records, while Sarah sings traditional ballads commemorating the flight.
