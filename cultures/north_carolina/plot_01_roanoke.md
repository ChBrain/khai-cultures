## Cue

In 1587, Governor John White establishes the Roanoke Colony with 115 English settlers, but return expeditions find it completely abandoned.

## Action

The colony's mystery establishes early regional stories, transitioning from a desperate colonial outpost to a symbol of historical intrigue. The [hand loom](piece_hand_loom.md) represents early domestic crafts that disappeared with the colony. Sarah studies these colonial weaving styles, while Ethan researches early colonial settlement archives.
