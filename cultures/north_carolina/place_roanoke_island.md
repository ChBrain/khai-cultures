## Shown

The historic coastal island in the Outer Banks, featuring sandy shorelines, maritime pine forests, and reconstructed Elizabethan fortifications.

## Holds

- the early colonial settlement in the plot [The Lost Colony of Roanoke 1587](plot_01_roanoke.md) : the birth of mystery.

## Offers

## Withheld

The displacement of the Croatoan. While the "lost" colony is celebrated in tourist plays, the indigenous Croatoan community that inhabited the island and likely integrated the starving colonists was largely ignored and displaced by subsequent settlers, a history omitted from theater pamphlets.
