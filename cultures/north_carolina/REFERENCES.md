## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_north_carolina.md](pitch_north_carolina.md) is written from North Carolina's profile inflected by its early colonial settlement, its aviation breakthroughs, its mountain heritage, and its modern high-tech research centers, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for North Carolina's traditional mountain self-reliance, academic collaboration, and low uncertainty avoidance in science and innovation).
