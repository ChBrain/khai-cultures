# Rhode Island: a khai culture

Rhode Island staged as a full khai play, ISO US-RI, language en. The Ocean State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Roger Williams charter, Newport Gilded Age boom, and Newport folk festival launch encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_rhode_island.md](play_rhode_island.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_rhode_island.md](pitch_rhode_island.md), the Rhode Island key.
- **Plots (history):** the Roger Williams Charter of 1663, the Newport Gilded Age Boom of the 1880s, and the Newport Folk Festival Launch of 1959.
- **Personas:** William (yacht builder) and Mary (marine biologist), written in English.
- **Positions:** charter liberty (religious tolerance/town independence), bay stewardship (estuary conservation/sailboat craftsmanship).
- **Places:** Newport Mansion, Boatyard.
- **Processes:** yacht building (wood and fiberglass boat building), shellfish harvesting (quahog clam raking).
- **Pieces:** a quahog shell, a brass sextant.
- **Plan:** the Narragansett Bay estuary plan.
