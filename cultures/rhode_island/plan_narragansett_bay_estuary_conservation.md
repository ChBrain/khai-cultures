# Plan: the Narragansett Bay plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and marine ecology designed to manage coastal wastewater and protect Narragansett Bay shellfish beds.

## Owner

## Direction

Conserve the bay estuary by regulating wastewater treatment, restoring salt marsh wetlands, upgrading stormwater overflow, and monitoring nitrogen levels.
