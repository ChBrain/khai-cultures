## Cue

In July 1663, Roger Williams secures a royal charter establishing absolute religious freedom and separation of church and state.

## Action

The charter establishes the colony's legal identity, developing [charter liberty](position_charter_liberty.md). The [brass sextant](piece_brass_sextant.md) represents early navigation. Mary archives historic charter documents, while William records oral histories of early boatbuilding families.

## Stage

- [charter liberty](position_charter_liberty.md) : the religious tolerance, separation of church and state, and town meeting pride.
- [brass sextant](piece_brass_sextant.md) : the maritime celestial navigation device.
- [place_boatyard](place_boatyard.md) : the Narragansett Bay boatyard and sailmaking facility.

## Tension

Boundary disputes with neighboring Massachusetts and Connecticut threaten the colony's land holdings and create political polarization.
