## Initiated by

Commercial quahoggers, shellfish rakers, and bay conservation monitors, conducted during open harvesting hours.

## Direction

On the shallow mudflats. Raking the bay floor with long-handled bull rakes, hauling up mud and clams, sorting quahogs by legal shell size, and placing clams in mesh bags.

## Lever

The traditional maritime harvesting process that preserves the state's coastal shellfishery. It supports local family income and seafood markets, though it is limited by pollution closures and requires manual physical labor.
