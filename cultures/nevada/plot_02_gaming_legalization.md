## Cue

In March 1931, Governor Fred Balzar signs the bill legalizing gambling in Nevada to generate tax revenues.

## Action

The act establishes the state as a global entertainment hub, developing [desert chance](position_desert_chance.md). The [casino chip](piece_casino_chip.md) represents this gaming era. Jack manages table logistics in Las Vegas casinos, while Elena coordinates solar energy power feeds to the resort grid.
