# Persona: Jack

## Taxonomy

[desert chance](position_desert_chance.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A casino dealer in Las Vegas, thirty-six and running the odds in his head before the cards even land. English, in a fast-paced drawl, is his mother tongue, the language he [speaks, hears, and thinks in](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), the code his daily gaming runs on. It is the [Nevadan English](position_language_en_us_nv.md) of the Las Vegas Strip, the drawl of the casino floor. He speaks no foreign languages. Where it thins is with the desert itself: the slower, nature-bound talk of solar engineers like Elena and the quiet history of [basin resilience](position_basin_resilience.md), he [reads only as regional logistics updates](process_reading_followed.md) on his workstation, sensing that the patient, resource-conserving rhythm of early desert communities counts for less against the pull of high-stakes tourism. He carries [the Silver State](position_culture_silver_state.md) in his own key - treating every wager, and every rule of the house, as a thing to be honored to the letter.

## Action

He preserves [casino chip](piece_casino_chip.md) tokens, coordinates gaming safety programs under [the Lake Mead plan](plan_lake_mead_water_management.md), and studies [desert chance](position_desert_chance.md).

## Shadow

His focus on casino performance metrics and venture-capital resort investments can make him indifferent to municipal environmental problems in rural agricultural counties.

## Tell

He shuffles a deck of playing cards and says directly: "We are running high-volume table action; you can't build a competitive casino if you're only focused on historic local preservation."
