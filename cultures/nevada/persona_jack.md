# Persona: Jack

## Taxonomy

[desert chance](position_desert_chance.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A thirty-six-year-old casino dealer in Las Vegas. English, spoken with a fast-paced drawl, is his mother tongue, the language in which he [speaks, hears, and thinks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), the defining code of his daily gaming operations. He speaks no foreign languages. Yet the loss lies in the seam: The slower, nature-bound desert talk of solar engineers like Elena and the quiet history of [basin resilience](position_basin_resilience.md) [he reads only as regional logistics updates](process_reading_followed.md) on his workstation, feeling that the patient, resource-conserving rhythm of early desert communities is being forgotten in the race for high-stakes tourism.

## Action

He preserves [casino chip](piece_casino_chip.md) tokens, coordinates gaming safety programs under [the Lake Mead plan](plan_lake_mead_water_management.md), and studies [desert chance](position_desert_chance.md).

## Shadow

His focus on casino performance metrics and venture-capital resort investments can make him indifferent to municipal environmental problems in rural agricultural counties.

## Tell

He shuffles a deck of playing cards and says directly: "We are running high-volume table action; you can't build a competitive casino if you're only focused on historic local preservation."
