# Nevada: a khai culture

Nevada staged as a full khai play, ISO US-NV, language en. The Silver State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Comstock lode discovery, gaming legalization, and desert test site opening encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_nevada.md](play_nevada.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_nevada.md](pitch_nevada.md), the Nevada key.
- **Plots (history):** the Comstock Lode Discovery of 1859, the Gaming Legalization Act of 1931, and the Nevada Test Site Opening of 1951.
- **Personas:** Jack (casino dealer) and Elena (solar engineer), written in English.
- **Positions:** desert chance (gaming luck/entertainment ambition), basin resilience (arid wilderness survival/solar innovation).
- **Places:** Las Vegas Strip, Hoover Dam.
- **Processes:** casino dealing (table croupier), solar engineering (photovoltaic array calibration).
- **Pieces:** a casino chip, a silver ore specimen.
- **Plan:** the Lake Mead plan.
