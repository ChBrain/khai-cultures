## Has

The mobile Western speech of the Silver State, the fast casino-floor patter of the Las Vegas Strip and the plain high-desert cadence of the Great Basin. It holds the level Western vowels of the interior and the quick, transactional rhythm of the gaming table, a transient English carried by a population drawn from everywhere and settled in the dry valleys.

## Orders

Where it passes as home ground: the Las Vegas card table, the Nye County solar field, the Comstock mining town, the desert basin work site. It orders which turns of phrase and cadences mark a speaker as of this desert country and sets the quick, plain Western register as the local measure.

## Loses

Ground to General American, the unmarked national standard the screen and the resort board carry, which levels the high-desert cadence toward the neutral national middle. The fast, commercial talk of the tourism industry also crowds the slower, season-dependent diction of the desert engineer toward the margin.
