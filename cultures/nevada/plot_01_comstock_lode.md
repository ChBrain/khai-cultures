## Cue

In June 1859, miners discover massive silver ore deposits on the eastern slope of Mount Davidson, starting the rush.

## Action

The discovery establishes Virginia City as a mining hub, developing [basin resilience](position_basin_resilience.md). The [silver ore](piece_silver_ore.md) specimen represents this mining era. Elena maps geological solar sites near the historic mine shafts, while Jack records oral histories of early mining families.

## Stage

- [basin resilience](position_basin_resilience.md) : the desert survival, resource stewardship, and solar innovation focus.
- [silver ore](piece_silver_ore.md) : the mineral specimen representing mining expansion.
- [Hoover Dam](place_hoover_dam.md) : the massive concrete hydroelectric dam downstream.

## Tension

Deep underground mining causes frequent cave-ins and severe geothermal water flooding, demanding extreme engineering solutions.
