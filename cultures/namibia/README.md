# Namibia: a khai culture

Namibia staged as a full khai play, ISO NA, language en. The oldest desert, many
peoples held in one dignity, and a land that keeps its wild, as a theatre: its
history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its many tongues and
its land encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned
by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_namibia.md](play_namibia.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_en.md](pitch_en.md), the Hofstede layer, written from
  Namibia's profile (a low, dry, unhurried temper, hardy self-reliance far from
  help, a sharing courtesy across many tongues, and the patience a dry land
  breeds) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the first readers of the land (the San and the rock art),
  the gathering of the peoples, independence (1990), and the conservation nation,
  each casting the elements it needs and each staged soberly on the public record.
- **Personas:** Hosea Kutako, John Muafangejo, Mvula ya Nangolo, and Gazza as the
  real cast, plus one archetype, the conservancy ranger (the herder turned game
  guard who keeps the community's wild in trust). Each is wired to the language
  engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** the desert (the oldest desert on earth and the space, silence
  and patience it teaches), the many peoples (the nation of many peoples held as
  one in equal dignity), conservation (the bargain that gave the wild back to the
  people to guard and gain from).
- **Places:** Windhoek (the meeting-ground capital where the nation governs
  itself) and the Namib (the named desert of Sossusvlei, Deadvlei and the
  Skeleton Coast).
- **Processes:** the braai (the one fire where the many peoples meet) and the
  conservancy (the community taking its own wild into its own hands).
- **Pieces:** the welwitschia (the thousand-year plant of endurance on the
  national arms) and biltong (the dried meat of the long road and the dry land).
