# Morocco: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind
the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_ar.md](pitch_ar.md) is written from Morocco's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without
entering the prose.

staging is original work. The Amazigh and Arab heritages are honored with equal

The one defining archetype, [the tea-pourer](persona_sabb_atay.md), is not a
historical individual but a composite: every Moroccan, Amazigh of the mountain
tent or Arab of the city house, who lifts the pot and pours the atay high for a
guest, carrying the hospitality the country is known for.
