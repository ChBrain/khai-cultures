# Berlin (Freistaat Berlin): a khai culture

Berlin staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-BE, language de. The capital city state of Germany: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its divided and reunified life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_berlin.md](play_berlin.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_berlin.md](pitch_berlin.md), the Berlin key.
- **Plots (history):** the division of the city in 1961, the fall of the Wall in 1989, and the rise of the techno subculture.
- **Personas:** Dieter (older East/West Berliner) and Amelie (young start-up worker/clubgoer), written in German.
- **Positions:** die Freiheit (freedom), die Mauer (the Wall), die Kreativität (creativity).
- **Places:** das Brandenburger Tor (Brandenburg Gate), das Berghain (the techno club).
- **Processes:** das Feiern (clubbing/nightlife).
- **Pieces:** das Mauerstück (piece of the Wall), der Einlassstempel (club stamp).
- **Plan:** die Förderung der Toleranz (promotion of tolerance).
