# Bremen (Freie Hansestadt Bremen): a khai culture

Bremen staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-HB, language de. The Hanseatic city state: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its maritime and mercantile life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_bremen.md](play_bremen.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_bremen.md](pitch_bremen.md), the Bremen key.
- **Plots (history):** the Hanseatic League accession in 1358, the erection of the Roland statue in 1404, and the founding of Bremerhaven in 1827.
- **Personas:** Hinnerk (traditional dockworker archetype) and Gesche (modern shipping logistics manager), written in German.
- **Positions:** die Hanse (Hanseatic trade), der Bürgerstolz (civic pride), der maritime Handel (maritime connection).
- **Places:** der Marktplatz (Marktplatz with Roland), der Überseehafen (Bremerhaven harbor).
- **Processes:** der Freimarkt (traditional fair).
- **Pieces:** der Bremer Roland (Roland statue), die Stadtmusikanten (sculpture).
- **Plan:** die grüne Hafenlogistik (green port development plan).
