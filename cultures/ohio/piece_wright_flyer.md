## Place

Maintained in flight exhibitions at the Dayton Aviation Heritage National Historical Park and Carillon Historical Park in Dayton.

## Load Bearing

It represents the breakthrough of controlled powered flight, the workshop engineering legacy of Dayton, and the national covenant of technological innovation.

## Apparent

A large biplane constructed of spruce wood, muslin canvas, and steel wire rigging, powered by a custom-built four-cylinder engine.
