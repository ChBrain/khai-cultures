# Plot: The Northwest Ordinance 1787

## Taxonomy

[play_ohio](play_ohio.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In July 1787, the Confederation Congress passes the Northwest Ordinance, establishing the governance structure for the territory that would become Ohio.

## Action

The act establishes [middle ground](position_middle_ground.md) political principles, prohibiting slavery and mandating support for public schools. It attracts thousands of settlers to the fertile plains. Samuel manages farmland originally surveyed under this township grid system, while Harper researches early land titles.

## Stage

- [middle ground](position_middle_ground.md) : the belief in consensus, public education, and moderate political compromise.
- [county fairs](process_county_fair.md) : the agricultural exhibitions that grew out of early township farming cooperatives.
- [Samuel](persona_samuel.md) : the western Ohio farmer whose lands are bound by the township survey lines.

## Tension

The rapid settlement under the ordinance leads to the forced displacement and violent removal of the Shawnee and Miami nations who lived on the land.
