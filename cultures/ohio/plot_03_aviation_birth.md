# Plot: The Birth of Aviation 1903

[play_ohio](play_ohio.md)

## Owner

## Cue

In the years leading to 1903, bicycle mechanics Orville and Wilbur Wright conduct engineering research in Dayton, leading to the first controlled powered flight.

## Action

The Wrights' experiments at [Huffman Prairie](place_huffman_prairie.md) establish a legacy of mechanical innovation. The [Wright Flyer](piece_wright_flyer.md) represents their breakthrough. Harper coordinates museum exhibits of early aviation components, while Samuel's farm is located near the historic flight testing grounds.

## Stage

- [Huffman Prairie](place_huffman_prairie.md) : the pasture land where the Wright brothers tested their early biplanes.
- [Wright Flyer](piece_wright_flyer.md) : the experimental biplane constructed of spruce and canvas.
- [industrial heartland](position_industrial_heartland.md) : the hands-on mechanical skill and workshop engineering that enabled the invention of flight.

## Tension

The Wright brothers' intense focus on patent protection and licensing leads to decades of litigation, delaying the commercial growth of American aviation.
