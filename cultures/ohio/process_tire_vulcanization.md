Akron rubber factory operators and tire mold technicians, historically peaking in the mid-20th century and maintained in specialized manufacturing facilities today.

## Direction

In the rubber factories. Mixing raw rubber with sulfur, placing the uncured tire into a heated mold under pressure, and heating it to cure and harden the compound.

## Lever

The chemical process that made Akron the rubber capital, supplying tires for the global automotive industry. It supports union wages and manufacturing skills, though it releases toxic fumes and faces strict environmental controls.
