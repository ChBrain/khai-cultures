## Shown

The historic grassy pasture near Dayton, featuring a replica hangar and catapult rail used during early flight testing.

## Withheld

The military acquisition of the site. While celebrated as a serene birthplace of flight, the land was incorporated into a massive military base, restricting public access for decades, a detail omitted from maps.
