# Process: county fairs

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

County agricultural societies and local communities, conducted annually during the late summer across Ohio's eighty-eight counties.

## Direction

At the county fairgrounds. Exhibiting livestock, judging crops, hosting grandstand races, and gathering at community food booths.

## Lever

The traditional assembly that reinforces rural community ties and celebrates agricultural heritage. It supports local youth programs and farmers, though it requires extensive funding and faces declining rural youth participation.

## Echo

The fair reinforces the heartland covenant. It rewards the agricultural dedication of [Samuel](persona_samuel.md) in western Ohio, while [Harper](persona_harper.md) values the events as a preservation of historic community culture.
