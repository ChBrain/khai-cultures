# Plan: the Clark Fork plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and environmental science designed to rehabilitate copper mine tailing areas and protect the Clark Fork River.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Restore the river watershed by excavating toxic heavy metal tailings, capping old mine waste sites, reconstructing natural river channels, and monitoring water acidity levels.

## Orders

- To the Department of Environmental Quality: Enforce cleanup standards and audit toxic tailing removal contracts.
- To mine historians like [Clark](persona_clark.md): Support mine shaft runoff telemetry audits and document historical mining waste deposit sites.
- To tribal coordinators like [Cheyenne](persona_cheyenne.md): Coordinate local community river monitoring logs and advocate for traditional fishing stream protection.

## Implementation

The heavy metal water measurements; the soil capping compaction audits; and the deployment of river telemetry monitors.
