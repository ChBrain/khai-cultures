# Montana: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch (kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_montana.md](pitch_montana.md) is written from Montana's profile inflected by its copper mines, mountain valleys, and reservation lands, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Montana base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 40                                        |
| Individualism                 | 78                                        |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 68                                        |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 44                                        |
| Long Term Orientation         | 24                                        |
| Indulgence                    | 64                                        |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Montana's low power distance in union mining locals, active community panels, and low uncertainty avoidance in watershed restoration innovations).
