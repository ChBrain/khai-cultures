## Place

Maintained in mineral storage bins and displayed in the World Museum of Mining in Butte.

## Load Bearing

It represents the preservation of early industrial mining, the scale of regional shaft extraction, and the legacy of underground mining labor.

## Apparent

A heavy, gray-green rock displaying bright metallic brass-yellow chalcopyrite crystals, copper carbonate veins, and jagged fractures.
