# Process: copper smelting

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Smelter operators, metal refinery workers, and industrial safety managers, conducted year-round in mineral smelters.

## Direction

In the smelting furnace. Crushing copper ore, heating ore in reverb furnaces with flux, separating copper matte, and casting copper bars.

## Lever

The traditional industrial process that defined the state's copper economy. It supported thousands of smelter jobs and copper wire exports, though it is limited by carbon emissions and requires strict heavy metal runoff audits.
