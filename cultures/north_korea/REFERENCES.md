# North Korea: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain cultural and historical
sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## A note on neutrality

This culture is staged strictly from within the country's and the Korean people's
own self-understanding, on the public record. It takes no political side, makes no
political judgment, offers no praise or condemnation, and gives no contested
detail. Where it touches the state's own guiding idea (Juche / self-reliance) it
presents it as self-description, explicitly not as endorsement. It stays on
enduring, shared cultural ground - the Korean language and script, the land,
family, the seasons, and the heritage common to the whole Korean people - and
treats the division of the peninsula soberly, with dignity for ordinary people.

number. Published Hofstede data covers Korea as a shared cultural profile; the
