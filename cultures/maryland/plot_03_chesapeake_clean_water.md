# Plot: The Chesapeake Bay Clean Water Act 1983

[play_maryland](play_maryland.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In December 1983, the governors of Maryland, Virginia, Pennsylvania, and D.C. sign the first Chesapeake Bay Agreement.

## Action

The agreement establishes the Chesapeake Bay Commission, developing [the oyster plan](plan_chesapeake_oyster_restoration.md) to manage runoff. Ann coordinates community cleanups on the Eastern Shore, while David audits water toxicity levels in bay tributaries.

## Stage

- [chesapeake stewardship](position_chesapeake_stewardship.md) : the ecological protection and traditional waterman practices of the bay.
- [the oyster plan](plan_chesapeake_oyster_restoration.md) : the restoration plan designed to rebuild filter reefs.
- [vaccine synthesis](process_vaccine_synthesis.md) : the scientific laboratory processes that monitor public health and water safety.

## Tension

The program relies on voluntary state targets, struggling to control agricultural runoff from upstream non-signatory states like New York and West Virginia.
