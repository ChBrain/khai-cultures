## Owner

## Shown

The historic star-shaped brick fort located at the entrance of Baltimore harbor, featuring high earthen ramparts, historic cannon batteries, and a towering flag staff.

## Holds

- the harbor defense history in the plot [The Battle of Baltimore 1814](plot_02_battle_of_baltimore.md) : the star rampart.

## Offers

The walking tours and military history presentations where [Ann](persona_ann.md) conducts local history presentations.

## Withheld

The confinement of political prisoners. While celebrated as the birthplace of the national anthem, the fort was used during the American Civil War to imprison thousands of Maryland citizens without trial under the suspension of habeas corpus, a history excluded from patriotic tourist guides.
