# Plan: the oyster plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and marine biology designed to rebuild native oyster reefs and restore water filtration capacity.

Manage oyster reefs by planting clean shells, deploying hatchery spat, establishing sanctuaries, and monitoring salinity levels.
