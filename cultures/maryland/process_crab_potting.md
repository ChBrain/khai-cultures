# Process: crab potting

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Chesapeake Bay watermen, crab boat crews, and seafood packing plant workers, conducted during the spring and summer crabbing season.

## Direction

On the bay crabbing waters. Baiting wire mesh crab pots with fish, lowering them to the estuary floor, retrieving the pots with boat winches, and sorting crabs by size and gender.

## Lever

The traditional maritime harvesting process that supports the Eastern Shore's economy and seafood packing industry. It provides high-demand blue crabs for local markets, though it is limited by strict seasonal regulations and declining crab populations.
