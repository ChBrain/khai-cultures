# Plot: the Mother Colony

## Owner

## Cue

Long before the sail come, the Kalinago hold the big isle and call it Liamuiga, the fertile land. In 1624 a English party land and plant the first lasting English colony in the whole Caribbean, the French soon come share it and fight over the same small island, and ah tell yuh, the cane start to climb up from the shore.

## Action

St. Kitts come the Mother Colony of the West Indies, the seed-bed the English settle Antigua, Montserrat, Barbuda and beyond from. The land turn almost whole to sugar, cane right down to the waterline, work by enslave Africans carry across the sea, and high up over the estates the great black-stone fort rise on Brimstone Hill, the Gibraltar of the West Indies, standing guard over the prize below. Cross the strait, on the small sister Nevis, under she cloud-ringed peak, a boy name Alexander Hamilton is born who will cross the sea and help found another nation. The English Caribbean start right here, on these two small isles, and ah tell yuh, it start already build on bondage.

## Tension

The islands are found as the cradle of the whole English Caribbean, the proud first colony and the mighty fort, and found already in contradiction, the wealth of the cane build on the bondage of the field, the mother of colonies and the mother of a unfree labour both. The same small isles that send out a founder to another nation hold thousands in slavery to grow they sugar, so the cradle carry both the founding and the wound from the very first furrow.
