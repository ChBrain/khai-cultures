# Persona: Sir Robert Bradshaw

## Taxonomy

[the labour nation and the federation](position_the_labour_nation_and_the_federation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A factory man who rose from the sugar works to lead the cane workers, build the labour movement into a power, and carry St. Kitts toward self-government, honored as the father of the nation: the man in whom the labour founding is embodied, the worker turned leader who made the field's struggle into a country. Forceful, proud, formal in his bearing, a labour chief who dressed and spoke with the dignity of the office he had won from nothing. English he has whole and shaping: he [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) and [thinks](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) in his mother tongue, the King's English of the platform and the legislature, and he [writes](process_writing_mother_tongue.md) in it the resolutions and demands that organized a people. But the tongue of the worker he led was the warm Kittitian Creole of the yard and the cane row, and that he [carries as his own](process_speaking_carried.md) and [hears](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) in every meeting, the lilt in which the field truly spoke. He won a nation in the formal English of the empire he was leaving, yet the labour he raised it from spoke a Creole the records seldom kept, so that the platform tongue and the cane-row tongue never quite met on the page - the gap at the limit of the tongue, where the language that wins the nation is not always the language that suffered for it.

## Action

In [Labour and Federation](plot_03_labour_and_federation.md) he leads the cane workers and the labour movement, building the power that carries the islands from colony toward independence. By organizing the field into a force, he founds [the labour nation and the federation](position_the_labour_nation_and_the_federation.md): the worker made the nation, and the union hall is its true cradle.

## Shadow

The strong founder who held the larger island can become the larger island's own shadow over the smaller: the labour power centred on St. Kitts that left Nevis feeling a partner unheard, the very strength that won the nation straining the federation it was meant to hold. The leader so sure of the union's centre that the sister isle reached for the door.

## Tell

He is the factory hand who wore the dignity of office like a tailored coat, and made a country out of a cane field - the worker who would answer the empire in its own formal English and win.
