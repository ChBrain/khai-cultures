# Persona: Sir Simeon Daniel

## Taxonomy

[the labour nation and the federation](position_the_labour_nation_and_the_federation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

The first Premier of Nevis, the founding voice of the sister isle within the federation, who fought for Nevis to be heard and not swallowed, and who held the island's place in a union that very nearly came apart: the man in whom Nevis's voice is embodied, the partner across the strait insisting the federation is two and not one. Measured, dignified, stubborn for his island, a Nevisian who loved the union enough to demand his island's standing within it. English he has whole and shaping: he [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) and [thinks](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) in his mother tongue, the English of the assembly and the negotiation, and he [writes](process_writing_mother_tongue.md) in it the case for Nevis put to the larger isle. The soft Nevisian Creole of his island's villages he [carries as his own](process_speaking_carried.md) and [hears](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) in every parish, the warm tongue of the home he spoke for. He argued Nevis's place in the formal English the federation was negotiated in, yet the island he spoke for had its own lilt the strait could muffle, so that the sister isle's voice could be heard in the union and still feel unheard within it - the gap at the limit of the tongue, where to be heard in the common language is not always to be heard.

## Action

In [Labour and Federation](plot_03_labour_and_federation.md) he leads Nevis into the federation as its first Premier, holding the sister isle's voice against the pull of the larger island. By insisting Nevis be a partner and not a province, he keeps faith with [the labour nation and the federation](position_the_labour_nation_and_the_federation.md): a union of two, country held above either isle.

## Shadow

The voice that holds the sister isle's standing can become the shadow of secession itself: the loyalty to Nevis that, pressed too far against St. Kitts, reaches for the door and threatens to split the smallest nation in two. The advocate for the island who must somehow want the union and his island's separate dignity at once.

## Tell

He is the Nevisian who loved the federation by fighting it for his island's place - the partner across the strait who would rather mend the union than leave it, and would rather leave it than be unheard.
