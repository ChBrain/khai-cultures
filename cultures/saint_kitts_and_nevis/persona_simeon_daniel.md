# Persona: Sir Simeon Daniel

## Taxonomy

[the labour nation and the federation](position_the_labour_nation_and_the_federation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

The first Premier of Nevis, the founding voice of the small sister inside the federation, who fight for Nevis to be heard and not swallow up, and who hold the island's place in a union that near enough come apart: the man in whom the whole Nevisian voice stand up, the partner cross the strait insisting the federation is two and not one. Measured, dignified, hard-headed for his island, a Nevis man who love the union enough to stand up and demand his island's standing inside it. English he has whole and shaping: he [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) and [thinks](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) in his mother tongue, the proper English of the assembly and the negotiation, and he [writes](process_writing_mother_tongue.md) in it the case for Nevis put to the big isle, [the Kittitian-Nevisian tongue](position_language_en_kn.md) held in the proper English of the assembly. The soft Nevisian of his island's villages, the talk you hear in Charlestown and round the Culturama ground, he [carries as his own](process_speaking_carried.md) and [hears](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) in every parish, the warm home tongue he was speaking up for. He argue Nevis's place in the formal English the federation was negotiate in, yet the island he speak for had she own lilt the strait could muffle, so the small sister's voice could be heard in the union and still feel like nobody hearing it. To shout cross the water, we say, is not the same as to be answered; the gap at the limit of the tongue, where to be heard in the common language is not always to be heard.

## Action

In [Labour and Federation](plot_03_labour_and_federation.md) he lead Nevis into the federation as its first Premier, holding the small sister's voice against the pull of the big island. By insisting Nevis be a partner and not a province, he keep faith with [the labour nation and the federation](position_the_labour_nation_and_the_federation.md): a union of two, country hold up above either isle.

## Shadow

The voice that hold up the small sister's standing can come the shadow of secession itself: the loyalty to Nevis that, push too far against St. Kitts, reach for the door and threaten to split the smallest nation clean in two. The man who speak for the island and must somehow want the union and his island's own separate dignity at the one same time.

## Tell

He is the Nevis man who love the federation by fighting it for his island's place: the partner cross the strait who would sooner mend the union than leave it, and would sooner leave it than sit there and nobody hearing him.
