## Shown

Bikpela wara i ran longpela rot tru namel long not bilong bikpela ailan Niugini, na long arere bilong en planti ples i sindaun. Em i hap i pulap long art: ol man bilong Sepik i save sanapim haus tambaran, em i bikpela haus bilong ol spirit na bilong ol man bilong ples, na ol i katim ol piksa bilong ol tumbuna na ol spirit long diwai na long maskim. Long dispela wara, ol kanu i ran, na art bilong ol man i kamap nem long olgeta hap.

## Offers

Hap we yu inap lukim art bilong tumbuna long ai bilong yu: ol haus tambaran i sanap antap, ol piksa long diwai i kolim ol nem bilong ol spirit, na save bilong ol kava i kam daun long ol tumbuna i go long ol pikinini. Em i hap we kastom na art i wanpela samting tasol.

## Withheld

As bilong olgeta piksa long ai bilong man bilong narapela ples. Stori bilong ol piksa i pas long tok ples bilong ples na long save bilong ol man bilong ples, na man bilong narapela ples i lukim ol piksa olsem samting naispela tasol na i no save long as bilong ol. Sepik i holim art na as bilong en wantaim, na em i no givim as bilong en long olgeta man i lukluk tasol.
