# Process: mumu

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Ol man bilong ples i wokim hul long graun, putim ston i hat long paia insait, na putim kaikai antap: pik, kaukau, taro, na ol arapela kaikai bilong ples, na karamapim long lip na long graun, na larim em kuk isi isi aninit long graun. Em i kamap long taim bilong bikpela bung, long taim wanpela bikpela samting i kamap, na taim ol lain i laik bung na kaikai wantaim.

## Direction

I go long pasin bilong givim na bilong bungim lain wantaim: mumu i no kaikai bilong wanpela man tasol, em i kaikai bilong olgeta lain wantaim. Taim ol man i wokim mumu, ol i bungim kaikai bilong olgeta na givim long olgeta, na long dispela ol i soim pasin bilong wantok na pasin bilong givim na kisim. Em i wanpela rot we lain i kamap wanpela long wankain kaikai.

## Lever

Em i ran long pasin bilong wantok na long laik bilong ol lain long givim na kisim na bung wantaim. Em i sanap long taim ol lain i laik amamas na soim pasin bilong givim. Tasol em i pas tu long hevi: long wokim bikpela mumu, wanpela man i mas givim planti pik na kaikai, na dispela i ken kamap bikpela hevi long em sapos lain i wok long askim moa na moa.

## Echo

Mumu i mekim olsem manmeri inap pilim wanem i stap aninit long [wantok na kastom](position_wantok_na_kastom.md): olsem nem bilong man i kam long pasin bilong em long givim, no long pasin bilong holim samting bilong em yet. Long wokim bikpela mumu na givim long olgeta, [bikman](persona_bikman.md) i kisim nem na holim ol lain wantaim, na olgeta i kamap wanpela long wankain kaikai.
