# Plot: independens na Melanesian We

## Taxonomy

[play_papua_new_guinea](play_papua_new_guinea.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Long ol yia bihain long bikpela pait, ol man bilong Papua Niugini i laik bosim ol yet, na ol lida i kamap i tok olsem dispela kantri i mas kisim independens. Long namel bilong ol, [Michael Somare](persona_somare.md) i sanap olsem man bilong holim ol planti lain wantaim, na [Bernard Narokobi](persona_narokobi.md) i kamapim tingting bilong rot we kantri bai bihainim.

## Action

Long 16 Septemba 1975, Papua Niugini i kisim independens, na [John Guise](persona_guise.md) i sanap olsem namba wan Gavna Jeneral na holim aut nem bilong nupela kantri, na ol i lusim flag bilong narapela ples na apim flag bilong ol yet. Somare i kamap namba wan Praim Minista na holim ol planti lain wantaim long wanpela kantri. Narokobi i kamapim tingting bilong "Melanesian We", na em i helpim long wokim konstitusen we i bihainim pasin bilong ol Melanesian, no rot bilong narapela ples tasol. Nupela kantri i sanap, em i no kamap long pait na blut, tasol long toktok na long wanbel.

## Stage

- [Michael Somare](persona_somare.md): namba wan Praim Minista i holim ol planti lain wantaim long wanpela kantri.
- [Bernard Narokobi](persona_narokobi.md): man i kamapim tingting bilong Melanesian We na helpim wokim konstitusen.
- [John Guise](persona_guise.md): namba wan Gavna Jeneral i holim aut nem bilong nupela kantri.
- [the Melanesian Way](position_melanesian_we.md): rot we nupela kantri i bihainim, em i bihainim pasin bilong tumbuna.

## Tension

Nupela kantri i sanap, na em i no kamap long pait, tasol long toktok na wanbel. Tasol hevi i stap yet: olsem wanem dispela kantri i gat planti tok ples na planti lain inap holim han wantaim na sanap olsem wanpela, na olsem wanem em i bihainim rot bilong Melanesian We na no lusim kastom, taim nupela taim na nupela samting i kamap.
