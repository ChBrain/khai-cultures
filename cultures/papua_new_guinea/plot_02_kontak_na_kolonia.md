# Plot: kontak na kolonia

## Owner

## Cue

Long arere bilong nambis, ol waitman i bin kamap pastaim, na ol Siaman i kisim hap not, ol Inglis i kisim hap saut, na bihain ol Australia i bosim. Tasol long namel bilong bikpela ailan, long ol bikpela ples antap, planti manmeri i stap na ol waitman i no save long ol.

## Action

Long ol yia bilong 1930s, ol waitman i go insait long ol Hailans na painim planti manmeri ol i no bin save long ol, na ol manmeri bilong ples i lukim waitman pastaim long laif bilong ol. Long bikpela pait, ol soldia i kam long dispela hap, na ol man bilong Papua Niugini i bin helpim long karim ol samting na ol man i bagarap long rot bilong Kokoda, na ol pait i go strong long namel bilong ol bus na maunten. Bihain long pait, ol Australia i bosim dispela hap olsem wanpela teritori inap long taim independens i kamap. Long olgeta dispela taim, Tok Pisin i kamap strong olsem tok we ol man bilong planti tok ples inap toktok wantaim, na em i kamap brij namel long ol lain.

## Stage

- [ol Hailans](place_ol_hailans.md): ol bikpela ples antap ol waitman i go insait long ol long 1930s.
- [Tok Pisin](position_language_tpi.md): long taim bilong kontak na kolonia, askim i kamap olsem wanem dispela planti lain inap kamap wanpela.

## Tension

Long dispela taim, ol man bilong planti tok ples na planti lain i bung aninit long wanpela gavman i kam long narapela ples. Dispela i kamapim hatpela samting: long wanpela sait, ol waitman i kam na senisim sindaun bilong ol; long narapela sait, dispela bung aninit long wanpela gavman i kamapim as bilong wanpela kantri, na Tok Pisin i kamap tok we ol planti lain inap bung wantaim long en.
