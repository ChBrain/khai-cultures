# Position: wanpela kantri, planti lain

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Klemerang olsem yu no ol manmeri i stap wanpela tasol, yu ol lain wantaim, na dispela lain i wokim yu bipo yu save wokim yu yet. Kantri i holim [wantok na kastom](position_wantok_na_kastom.md), pasin bilong givim na kisim na pasin bilong sing-sing na mumu i kam daun long ol tumbuna; na i holim [the Melanesian Way](position_melanesian_we.md), tingting olsem nupela kantri i mas sanap long rot bilong em yet, i no long rot bilong narapela ples. Manmeri i toktok long [Tok Pisin](position_language_tpi.md) taim ol i laik bung wantaim, tasol tok ples bilong ples bilong ol yet i holim tingting bilong tumbuna i dip moa. Ol i kisim, taim ol i no bin makim, pasin bilong sindaun long lain, pasin bilong bikman i kisim nem long givim, na graun i bilong lain, i no bilong wanpela man.

## Orders

Olsem yu mas lukautim wantok bilong yu pastaim, na nem bilong yu i kam long pasin bilong yu long givim, no long pasin bilong holim samting bilong yu yet. Olsem kastom em i as bilong sindaun bilong manmeri, na em i mas i stap strong taim ol nupela samting i kamap, na nupela kantri i mas kamapim gavman na wok i bihainim pasin bilong ol Melanesian, no bilong narapela ples tasol. Na olsem planti tok ples i mas bung long Tok Pisin taim ol i toktok wantaim, tasol wanwan tok ples i mas i stap strong long ples bilong en.

## Loses

Em i lusim graun taim pasin bilong wantok i kamap strong tumas, inap long wanpela man i no inap go het bilong em yet, na taim dispela pasin i go insait long wok bilong gavman na kamapim trabel. Em i lusim graun tu taim manmeri i tingting olsem olgeta samting bilong narapela ples i winim samting bilong tumbuna, na ol i tromoi kastom; na taim wanpela hap, olsem Bougainville, i pilim olsem em i no kisim stret sea bilong gutpela samting, dispela i ken kamapim bikpela hevi namel long ol lain.

## Drives

Em i stap insait long the big-man husat i kisim nem long givim bikpela kaikai, long the Sepik carver husat i holim save bilong tumbuna long art, long man husat i kamapim tingting bilong Melanesian We, long namba wan Gavna Jeneral husat i holim aut nem bilong nupela kantri, na long namba wan Praim Minista husat i bringim planti tok ples long wanpela kantri. Em i kirapim Papua Niugini long holim wantok na kastom strong tasol i wokim wanpela rot bilong em yet, na long bihainim tingting olsem dispela planti lain inap kamap wanpela kantri, sapos ol i holim han strong long wantok na long Tok Pisin.
