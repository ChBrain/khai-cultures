# Plan: wanpela kantri, planti tok

## Taxonomy

Em i bikpela wok dispela kantri i putim long em yet: long holim ol planti tok ples wantaim olsem [planti tok, wanpela kantri](position_planti_tok_wanpela_kantri.md), long holim wantok na kastom strong olsem [wantok na kastom](position_wantok_na_kastom.md) i tok, na long bihainim rot bilong [Melanesian We](position_melanesian_we.md) taim em i kamap bikpela na gat moa mani, na long bekim gut askim bilong Bougainville.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Holim dispela kantri wantaim, taim em i gat planti tok ples na planti lain, long larim Tok Pisin na wantok holim olgeta wantaim; mekim kantri kamap bikpela na gat moa mani long graun i gat planti samting insait, tasol long rot we i no brukim kastom bilong ol; bekim gut askim bilong Bougainville long pasin bilong wanbel na toktok, no long pait; na holim rot bilong Melanesian We, olsem kantri i bihainim pasin bilong tumbuna na no tromoi em long baksait.

## Orders

- Long [the big-man](persona_bikman.md): holim wantok na kastom strong long ples, tasol no larim pasin bilong wantok go insait long gavman na kamapim trabel.
- Long [Bernard Narokobi](persona_narokobi.md): holim tingting bilong Melanesian We strong, na no larim mani na pawa senisim em long tok nating.
- Long kantri: holim ol planti tok ples wantaim long Tok Pisin na long wantok, na bekim gut askim bilong Bougainville long pasin bilong wanbel.

## Implementation

Wanpela kantri i kamap long 1975 long toktok na wanbel, no long pait na blut, na em i sanap inap nau olsem wanpela kantri i gat planti tok ples moa long olgeta arapela. Em i holim ol planti lain wantaim long Tok Pisin na long wantok, na em i bin sindaun na toktok long Bougainville long pasin bilong wanbel. Tasol wok i stap yet: pait namel long ol lain i kamap, graun i gat planti samting insait tasol moa mani i no kamap long olgeta, na ol yangpela i lusim ples na lusim kastom taim taun na mani i pulim ol.

## Targets

- [x] Wanpela kantri i kamap long 1975 long toktok na wanbel, no long pait.
- [x] Tok Pisin i kamap tok bilong olgeta lain na holim ol planti tok ples wantaim.
- [x] Bougainville i sindaun na toktok long gavman long pasin bilong wanbel na long askim bilong bosim em yet.
- [W] Pait namel long ol lain i mas i go daun, na kantri i mas holim olgeta wantaim.
- [W] Graun i gat planti samting insait, tasol moa mani i mas kamap long olgeta lain, no long sampela tasol.
- [ ] Holim kastom bilong ol taim kantri i kamap bikpela na nupela taim i kamap, na no larim ol yangpela lusim tok ples bilong tumbuna.
- [ ] Bekim olgeta askim bilong Bougainville long pasin bilong wanbel na toktok.
