# Place: ol Hailans

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Ol bikpela ples antap long namel bilong bikpela ailan Niugini, namel long ol maunten, we ol bikpela ples i pulap long manmeri na graun i gutpela long planim kaikai. Em i hap we planti manmeri i sindaun, na ol bikpela sing-sing i bung planti lain wantaim, taim ol man i bilas long pen na long gras bilong pisin na danis na singsing. Em i hap bilong ol bikman, na hap we ol waitman i no bin save inap long 1930s.

## Holds

- [contact and colony](plot_02_kontak_na_kolonia.md): ol bikpela ples antap we ol waitman i go insait long ol long 1930s na painim planti manmeri.
- [a thousand tongues today](plot_04_planti_tok_tude.md): ol bikpela ples i pulap long manmeri na long sing-sing long taim bilong tude, hap bilong ol bikman.

## Offers

Hap we manmeri i sindaun long lain bilong ol na holim kastom strong, we sing-sing i bung planti lain wantaim long pen na long danis, na we pasin bilong bikman na bilong wantok i stap as bilong sindaun. Em i hap we yu inap lukim strong bilong kastom na strong bilong wantok long ai bilong yu.

## Withheld

Pasin bilong sindaun isi tasol. Ol Hailans i holim hevi tu: hap we planti lain i sindaun klostuklostu i ken kamapim pait namel long ol, na we nupela samting bilong taun na mani i pulim ol yangpela lusim ples na lusim kastom. Em i kantri i strong long kastom, tasol em i pas long ol askim bilong holim olgeta wantaim.
