# Process: singsing

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Ol lain bilong ol arapela ples i bung wantaim long wanpela bikpela bung, na olgeta lain i kam wantaim danis na singsing bilong ol yet. Ol man i bilas long pen, long gras bilong pisin, long bilas bilong ol tumbuna, na ol i karim kundu na danis wantaim. Em i kamap long bikpela bung, long taim bilong amamas, na taim ol lain i laik soim pasin na strong bilong ol.

## Direction

I go long pasin bilong soim lain na bilong bungim planti lain wantaim: long sing-sing, wan wan lain i soim danis na singsing na bilas bilong ol yet, na long wankain taim, ol planti lain i bung long wanpela ples na lukim narapela narapela. Em i no danis tasol, em i wanpela rot we ol lain i soim hu ol i stap, na we ol planti lain i bung olsem wanpela.

## Lever

Em i ran long strong bilong kastom na long laik bilong ol lain long soim pasin bilong tumbuna na strong bilong ol yet. Em i sanap long taim ol lain i laik amamas wantaim na soim wanem ol i gat. Tasol em i pas tu long hevi: sing-sing i bung planti lain klostuklostu, na em i mas kamap long pasin bilong wanbel, no long pasin bilong pait namel long ol lain.

## Echo

Sing-sing i mekim olsem manmeri inap harim wanem i stap aninit long [the wantok and kastom](position_wantok_na_kastom.md): olsem manmeri i bilong lain bilong ol, na olsem kastom bilong tumbuna i stap as bilong sindaun bilong ol. Long dispela bikpela bung, [the big-man](persona_bikman.md) i sanap na soim strong na nem bilong ples bilong em, na ol planti lain i bung olsem wanpela.
