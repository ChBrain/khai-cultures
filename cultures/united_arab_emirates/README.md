# the United Arab Emirates: a khai culture

The United Arab Emirates staged as a full khai play, ISO AE, language ar
(Modern Standard Arabic in an Emirati voice). A coast that lived on the pearl and
a desert that lived on the date palm, gathered by Sheikh Zayed into one union, as
a theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its
language and faith encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects,
all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_united_arab_emirates.md](play_united_arab_emirates.md), the
  culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_ar.md](pitch_ar.md), the Hofstede layer, written from the
  Emirati profile (the group and the family before the individual, respect for
  age and rank, restraint and modesty, a deep code of hospitality, and the pull
  between guarding the old and racing into the new) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the pearling coast, the desert and the Bedouin, the union
  of 1971, and the transformation - each casting the elements it needs, and each
  staged soberly from within Emirati self-understanding.
- **Personas:** Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan (the founding father), Al
  Majidi ibn Daher (the Nabati poet), Mubarak bin London (the pearling nokhada),
  and Sheikha Fatima bint Mubarak (the Mother of the Nation) as the cast, plus the
  pearl diver (the ghaus) as the defining archetype. Each is wired to the language
  engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** hospitality and the majlis (the open door and the full cup as a
  code of honour), the sea and the pearl (the rizq drawn from the depths before
  oil), the union (the seven emirates made one).
- **Places:** Liwa and Abu Dhabi (the oasis heartland and the seat of rule),
  Dubai Creek and the coast (the pearling and trading harbour).
- **Processes:** pearl diving (the great season that ordered the year), the
  majlis welcome (the coffee, the date, and the open door).
- **Pieces:** the dhow (the wooden ship that carried the divers and the trade),
  the date palm (the tree of life that made the desert livable).

## Provenance

The Hofstede scores behind the pitch and the public-domain sources are recorded
in [REFERENCES.md](REFERENCES.md). The data never enters the prose; the pitch is
its invisible hand. Sources in the public domain are credited, never claimed; the
