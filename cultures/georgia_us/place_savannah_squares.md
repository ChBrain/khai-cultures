# Place: Savannah Squares

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The historic geometric public squares in Savannah, lined with Spanish-moss-draped live oak trees, historic brick mansions, and monuments.

## Holds

- the early town planning in the plot [Savannah Founding 1733](plot_01_savannah_founding.md) : the municipal blueprint.

## Offers

The walking tours and public seating areas where [Carter](persona_carter.md) conducts agricultural shipping meetings.

## Withheld

The military encampments during the Civil War. While celebrated today as public gardens, the squares were used as encampments for occupying Union forces following the March to the Sea, a history omitted from tourism brochures.
