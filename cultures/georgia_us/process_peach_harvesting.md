# Process: peach harvesting

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Georgia peach orchard managers and seasonal agricultural laborers, conducted annually during the summer months.

## Direction

In the peach orchards of central Georgia. Selecting ripe peaches, carefully picking them by hand to prevent bruising, placing them in padded baskets, and transporting them to packing facilities.

## Lever

The specialized agricultural process that defines the state's fruit farming identity. It supports local farming families and trade, though it is highly vulnerable to early spring freezes and insect damage.
