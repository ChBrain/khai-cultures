# Plan: the Atlanta transit plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and civil engineering designed to expand the regional public transportation network in metropolitan Atlanta.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Foster transit expansion by constructing rail lines, extending bus corridors, implementing transit-oriented zoning, and securing funding.

## Orders

- To the Rapid Transit Authority: Coordinate engineering studies for proposed rail extensions.
- To urban planners like [Evelyn](persona_evelyn.md): Analyze public feedback and historic neighborhood zoning documents.
- To land managers like [Carter](persona_carter.md): Advise on transit corridors to minimize impact on surrounding agricultural zones.

## Implementation

The regional transit audits; the construction of bus rapid transit lanes; and the acquisition of right-of-way easements.
