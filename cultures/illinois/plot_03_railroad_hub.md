# Plot: The Railroad Hub of the Nation 1880

## Taxonomy

[play_illinois](play_illinois.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

By 1880, Chicago becomes the convergence point for major transcontinental railroads, dominating agricultural and industrial shipping.

## Action

The massive transit network establishes the state's commercial dominance, linking the prairies to global markets. The [rail spike](piece_rail_spike.md) represents this transport expansion. Robert's firm designs rail terminals and warehouses, while Mary ships grain along these rail links.

## Stage

- [the Chicago Loop](place_chicago_loop.md) : the elevated rail loop that defined the central commercial district.
- [rail spike](piece_rail_spike.md) : the metal spike used to anchor the vast rail networks of the Midwest.
- [corn harvesting](process_corn_harvesting.md) : the agricultural volume that was transported through the Chicago rail hub.

## Tension

The concentration of transport and meatpacking industries leads to severe labor disputes, culminating in the Haymarket Riot of 1886, and causes massive pollution in the Chicago River.
