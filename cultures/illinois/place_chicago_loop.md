## Owner

## Shown

The commercial downtown district of Chicago, defined by elevated railway tracks, streets lined with early skyscrapers, and lakeside public parks.

## Holds

- the architectural rebuilding history in the plot [The Great Chicago Fire 1871](plot_01_chicago_fire.md) : the core of the metropolis.

## Offers

The drafting studios and building lobbies where [Robert](persona_robert.md) coordinates skyscraper structural blueprints.

## Withheld

The environmental damage caused by surrounding industries. While celebrated as a center of trade, the Chicago River running through the Loop was used as an open sewer for meatpacking waste, a history omitted from architectural tours.
