# Plan: the Chicago River plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and water infrastructure management designed to control the flow and prevent lake pollution.

## Owner

## Direction

Control water flow by regulating locks, diverting municipal effluent away from Lake Michigan, and maintaining the Sanitary and Ship Canal.

## Orders
