# France: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind
the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_fr.md](pitch_fr.md) is written from France's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no
number. The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the
record without entering the prose.

The two defining personas, [Colette](persona_colette.md) and
[Yasmine](persona_yasmine.md), are archetypes, not historical individuals:
composites that carry the tension between the older France of the terroir and the
art de vivre and the younger, diverse, urban Republic. The name ' Colette ' is
used here as an ordinary given name for an everywoman, not as a reference to any
real person.
