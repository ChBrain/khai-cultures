# Plot: The Motown Sound 1959

## Taxonomy

[play_michigan](play_michigan.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In January 1959, Berry Gordy Jr. founds Motown Records in Detroit, establishing a music company that would dominate national charts.

## Action

The label establishes a unique musical style that integrates soul, R&B, and pop, crossing racial boundaries in American entertainment. The [Motown record](piece_motown_record.md) represents this cultural export. Marcus collects vintage pressings, while Clara references the Motown museum tours on her travel guides.

## Stage

- [Detroit Assembly](place_detroit_assembly.md) : the urban neighborhood where Gordy established the Hitsville U.S.A. recording studio.
- [Motown record](piece_motown_record.md) : the vinyl record representing the global success of Detroit artists.
- [Motor City](position_motor_city.md) : the commercial ambition and production-line efficiency Gordy applied to music creation.

## Tension

Despite its massive crossover success and racial Integration, the label faces internal disputes over artist compensation and control, leading to its relocation to Los Angeles.
