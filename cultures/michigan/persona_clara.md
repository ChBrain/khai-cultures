# Persona: Clara

## Taxonomy

[Up North](position_up_north.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A fifty-two-year-old ferry captain on Mackinac Island. English, spoken with a slow, flat northern cadence, is her mother tongue, the language in which she [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) and writes her daily logs. She speaks no foreign languages. Yet the loss lies in the seam: The fast, engineering talk of automotive designers like Marcus and the digital demands of Detroit rapid transit [she hears only like a distant echo](process_hearing_followed.md), feeling that the patient, season-dependent rhythm of Great Lakes shipping is treated as secondary to corporate quarterly metrics.

## Action

She preserves [Model T](piece_model_t.md) artifacts, coordinates community feedback under [the Great Lakes water plan](plan_great_lakes_water_quality.md), and studies [Motor City](position_motor_city.md).

## Shadow

Her pride in local heritage and rustic independence can make her suspicious of state-wide zoning regulations and environmental mandates, fearing they will increase ferry operating costs and disrupt island tourism.

## Tell

She looks out at the Straits of Mackinac and says thoughtfully: "The lakes have their own seasons; you can't manage maritime heritage if you're only focused on digital speed and automotive production lines."
