# Michigan: a khai culture

Michigan staged as a full khai play, ISO US-MI, language en. The Great Lakes State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its automotive assembly line, labor movement, and Great Lakes maritime life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_michigan.md](play_michigan.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_michigan.md](pitch_michigan.md), the Michigan key.
- **Plots (history):** the automotive assembly line founding in 1913, the Motown Records founding in 1959, and the Flint sit-down strike of 1936.
- **Personas:** Marcus (Detroit automotive engineer) and Clara (Mackinac Island ferry captain), written in English.
- **Positions:** Motor City (industrial innovation), Up North (rustic wilderness retreat).
- **Places:** Detroit Assembly, Mackinac Island.
- **Processes:** automotive assembly (industrial manufacturing), lake shipping (Great Lakes transport).
- **Pieces:** a Model T, a Motown record.
- **Plan:** the Great Lakes water plan.
