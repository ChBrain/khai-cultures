# Plot: Automotive Assembly Line 1913

## Taxonomy

[play_michigan](play_michigan.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In December 1913, Henry Ford installs the first moving assembly line at the Highland Park plant, reducing Model T assembly to 93 minutes.

## Action

The innovation establishes [Motor City](position_motor_city.md) industrial processes, accelerating national consumer transportation and manufacturing scale. The [Model T](piece_model_t.md) represents this manufacturing milestone. Marcus studies these early robotic assembly configurations in Detroit, while Clara presents vintage industrial histories to visitors.

## Stage

- [Motor City](position_motor_city.md) : the industrial innovation and engineering focus that defined the state's economy.
- [Model T](piece_model_t.md) : the black passenger car built through standardized mass assembly.
- [automotive assembly](process_automotive_assembly.md) : the moving line assembly method that revolutionized factory floor labor.

## Tension

While doubling worker wages to five dollars a day, the line forces an intense, monotonous physical pace that reduces individual worker autonomy.
