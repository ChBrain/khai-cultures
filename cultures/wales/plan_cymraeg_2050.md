# Plan: Cymraeg 2050

## Taxonomy

A mandate: the kind of plan a [position](position_yr_iaith.md) carries. The deliberate, public scheme to reverse the slow loss of [the language](position_yr_iaith.md) that the cast lives out, so that what reaches the young as an echo reaches them again as a tongue.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

To make Welsh a living, everyday language again rather than a heritage kept behind glass. The aim is a country where the channels a speaker loses first, writing the official world, reading for work, thinking in the tongue, are won back, so that the door [Rhys](persona_rhys.md) finds closed at the eisteddfod opens, the language [Gwen](persona_gwen.md) prays in is not buried with her, and the institutions [Owain Glyndwr](persona_glyndwr.md) dreamed of, in Welsh, finally stand.

## Orders

- To the schools: carry the language across every channel, not speech alone, so a child leaves able to read, write, and think in Welsh, not only recite it; this is the key [Rhys](persona_rhys.md) was never handed.
- To the public bodies and broadcasters: make Welsh the ordinary language of forms, signs, work, and screen, so the written world stops being English by default, as it was for [Gwen](persona_gwen.md).
- To the heartland communities and [the chapel's](position_y_capel.md) successors: hold the language where it still lives daily, in the home and on the street, not only in the [eisteddfod](process_eisteddfod.md).

## Implementation

Welsh-medium and bilingual education as the spine; the Welsh Language Measure and the Commissioner as the floor of rights; S4C and Welsh-language media as the daily voice; and a focus on daily use and on the milltir sgwar of [the heartlands](place_eryri.md), since a count of speakers is not the same as a living tongue.

## Targets

- [x] Welsh secured official status in Wales (the Welsh Language Measure, 2011)
- [x] A national strategy adopted for a million speakers by 2050 (Cymraeg 2050, 2017)
- [x] Welsh-medium education and Welsh-language broadcasting established and growing
- [F] The 2021 census recorded a fall in the proportion of Welsh speakers, not a rise
- [ ] One million Welsh speakers reached by 2050
- [ ] Daily use raised across every generation, not only taught in school
- [ ] The decline of the Welsh-speaking heartlands (y Fro Gymraeg) reversed
