# Position: solidariaeth

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The power of working-class solidarity forged in the coalfield, in the pit, the lodge, and the miners' federation. Where every shift underground depended on the man beside you, solidarity was not a slogan but a survival: the strong owed the weak, the community came before the man, and no one was left to fall alone.

## Orders

That working people stand together, share what they have, and look after their own. It asks loyalty to the lodge and the town, suspicion of those who would divide labour against itself, and the conviction that what is won is won collectively and held in common.

## Loses

Ground where loyalty turns tribal and inward, suspicious of anyone outside the fold or anyone who rises out. Solidariaeth loses when it curdles into grievance and fatalism, nursing old wounds after the work that gave it meaning is gone.

## Drives

It lives in [Aneurin Bevan](persona_bevan.md), who carried the federation's creed from the pit to the national stage, and in [Rhys](persona_rhys.md), who keeps the belonging of the coalfield long after the coal, and it is the temper the southern Valleys gave to the whole nation.
