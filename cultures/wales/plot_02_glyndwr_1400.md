# Plot: The Rising of Glyndwr 1400

## Taxonomy

[play_wales](play_wales.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

More than a century after the conquest, the Welsh chafe under English law and English lords. In 1400 [Owain Glyndwr](persona_glyndwr.md) is proclaimed Prince of Wales, and the country rises behind him from the fastness of [Eryri](place_eryri.md).

## Action

Glyndwr leads the last great Welsh rebellion. He takes castles, holds a parliament at Machynlleth, is crowned Prince of Wales, and dreams of Welsh law, Welsh churches, and Welsh universities. For a few years a free Wales seems possible again. Then the rising is ground down by the weight of the English crown; Glyndwr is never captured and never betrayed, and simply vanishes into the hills and into legend.

## Stage

- [Owain Glyndwr](persona_glyndwr.md): the last Welshman to hold the title of Prince of Wales in arms.
- [Eryri](place_eryri.md): the mountain country that shelters the rising and never gives him up.

## Tension

The rebellion fails, and native rule is not restored. But Glyndwr leaves something the conquest could not: the proof that the nation can rise as one, and a vanished hero who is never taken, around whom the longing for a free Wales keeps its shape.
