# Plot: the Nature Isle and the Storm

## Taxonomy

[play_dominica](play_dominica.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

The sovereign nation looks for a way to make her living from what makes her herself. Her slopes are too steep and her bananas too small a crop to compete forever, but her rainforest, her three hundred and sixty-five rivers, and her rare life are like nowhere else on God's earth, and the island stakes her future on being the Nature Isle.

## Action

Dominica turns to eco-tourism and the wild as her calling card, opening the rainforest of Morne Trois Pitons and the long Waitukubuli trail to those who come for the green, and keeping the Kalinago Territory as the living homeland of the island's first people. Then in 2017 Hurricane Maria crosses the island in a single night and strips the green Nature Isle bare, taking the forest, the roofs, the crops, and the roads in a matter of hours. Out of the ruin the nation sets herself the boldest charge in the region: not merely to rebuild, but to rebuild stronger, to become the world's first climate-resilient nation, and to plant the green back again. The Nature Isle answers the storm with the oldest words she has - apres Bondie c'est la te, after God, the Earth - and begins again.

## Stage

- [Morne Trois Pitons](place_morne_trois_pitons.md): the rainforest national park of the Boiling Lake, the Nature Isle's heart and its draw, stripped and regrowing.
- [the Kalinago Territory](place_the_kalinago_territory.md): the east-coast homeland of the surviving first people, struck by the storm like all the island.
- [the Nature Isle](position_the_nature_isle.md): the wild land the nation stakes its future on, and the trust the storm tests.

## Tension

The wild that is the island's pride and her living is also her exposure: the same green slopes that draw the world are the first thing the hurricane takes, and one night of storm can undo a year of growth and a generation of building. The Nature Isle must hold the wild against a sky that grows more violent, rebuild stronger after each blow rather than be emptied by it, and keep her people and her first people rooted on the steep, beautiful, dangerous ground - betting that a small, poor nation can become the very model the warming world will need. Apre lapli, bon tan, after the rain, good weather.
