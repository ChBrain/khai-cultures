# Position: the labour nation

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The conviction that the nation was made by working people standing organised together. Emancipation in 1834 ended the bondage but left the freed people on low wages and hard ground, the old power still in the planters' hands; what changed that was the trade union. In the twentieth century the labour movement rose up out of the cane fields and the waterfront, the Antigua Trades and Labour Union giving ordinary people their first real power, and from it came the political leadership that carried the islands to self-government and to independence in 1981. It holds that freedom was won twice over - once from the chains, and again from the want and the powerlessness that outlived them - and that the second freedom was the work of the organised many.

## Orders

That the dignity and the power of the working person is the founding value of the free nation, the union the instrument that turned emancipated subjects into citizens; that leadership belongs to those who came up from the people and still answer to them; and that the labour and the emancipation that made the country are to be honoured in fact - in the wages, the schools, and the share of the wealth - and not only in the holiday and the song.

## Loses

Ground when the labour movement's victory hardens into a power that forgets the people it rose from, the big man become a fixture and the union a machine; and ground when the wealth of the modern nation flows right past the working people whose forebears won the country, so that the freedom from want the movement promised is celebrated loud in name while the inequality it fought comes back in new dress.

## Drives

Lives in the labour leader the people called the father of the nation, the man who came up through the union to carry Antigua and Barbuda all the way to independence. It drives the nation to date its freedom from the people who organised for it, and to measure itself by whether the working many who won the country also share in it.
