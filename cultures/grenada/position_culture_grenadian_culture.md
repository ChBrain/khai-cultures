# Position: Grenadian culture

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The belonging to a small green island that carry a big big history: [the Spice Isle](position_the_spice_isle.md), the land itself and what it give, nutmeg and mace climbing the wet hills and put right there on the flag; [the rebel tradition](position_the_rebel_tradition.md), the long line of refusal running from the Kalinago last stand at Sauteurs through Fedon's 1795 rising to the federation dream that teach a small island to count itself part of a region; and [the revolution and the reckoning](position_the_revolution_and_the_reckoning.md), the chapter of 1979 to 1983 the nation still carry soft and sober, held now in the work of memory rather than silence. It has [Grenadian English](position_language_en_gd.md) sweet over every inch of it, the tongue of Spicemas and the calypso and the Big Drum on Carriacou. It carries, without ever choosing it, a warm, God-fearing, hardback pride, three small islands, Grenada and Carriacou and Petite Martinique, held as one people, and the sweetness of the spice carried right alongside a memory not yet wholly mend.

## Orders

That the island be known first by its land and its spice, the nutmeg not just a crop but the nation's own name and signature; that resistance sits in the marrow, so a small place refuse the terms handed to it and imagine a bigger belonging instead of keeping its head down; that the years 1979 to 1983 stay named on the public record, told with truth and without rancour, neither denied nor reopened to set the people against one another again; and that hurricane or empire or hard chapter, the island build back and plant the grove again, one people across three small shores.

## Loses

Ground when the nutmeg is sold too cheap and the world price leave the grower unable to live off the hill; ground when the long tradition of refusal thins from principle into strife alone, the rising remembered but the vision it once carried forgotten; ground to silence, when the years of the revolution go unspoken and the grief hardens unhealed, or to the opposite fray, when the chapter is taken up to divide the people rather than to mend them; and ground, always, to the discouragement of the small, the feeling that so little an island cannot stand in a wide world and had best only take what it is given.

## Drives

Drives whoever is raised in it to know the island first by its land, to come back green from every blow the world and the weather deal it, the way the spice grower plant the grove back after every storm. Drives it to carry both motions of the one same tradition, the rising and the vision, from Fedon's rebellion to Marryshow's federation dream. Drives the nation to hold the chapter of 1979 to 1983 with truth rather than silence, doing the long patient work of memory and reconciliation as one people across three small islands. And it drives the Grenadian, hardback and soft-spoken both, to carry a small place forward on its own terms in a wide world, proud of the sweetness the island give even when the chapter behind it is not yet fully mend.
