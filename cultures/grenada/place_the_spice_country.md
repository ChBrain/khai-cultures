# Place: the Spice Country

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The wet green interior and the nutmeg coast: Gouyave, the fishing and spice town on the west shore where the nutmeg station stand and the fallen crop is graded and dried, the whole place smelling of nutmeg and mace and the cocoa drying for chocolate, the hill estates climbing the volcanic slopes in groves of nutmeg, mace, cocoa, cinnamon, and clove, and high up in the centre the Grand Etang rainforest around its crater lake, wrap up in cloud and loud with the mona monkeys and the rush of waterfalls, the rain coming down soft on the leaves. The land that grow the spice and name the nation, the green heart of the Spice Isle.

## Offers

A place where the island is most itself, the land that give the nutmeg and the mace, the cocoa and the cinnamon, the ground the grower tend and the rainforest that hold the island water and its wild; the green country the Spice Isle is grown in season on season and planted back after every storm.

## Withheld

The promise of a harvest you can count on. The Spice Country carry the exposure underneath all its plenty, the groves one single hurricane can strip to bare ground and the years it take the nutmeg to bear again, the low price the world can set on the spice, and the long patience the land demand of those who live off it, a green country generous and fragile at the one same time.
