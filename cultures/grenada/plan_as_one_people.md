# Plan: Build and Advance as One People

## Taxonomy

The open mandate the nation sets itself: to heal the memory of its revolution that [the revolution and the reckoning](position_the_revolution_and_the_reckoning.md) holds tender, to build on the spice and the resilience that [the Spice Isle](position_the_spice_isle.md) has always carried, and to carry forward the long spirit of refusal and the dream of a larger belonging that [the rebel tradition](position_the_rebel_tradition.md) sets down, so that a small nation across three islands advances in the wide world as one people.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Heal the memory of the years 1979 to 1983, carrying the chapter with truth and reconciliation rather than silence or division; build on the spice and the resilience, making the land pay for those who tend it and bringing the groves back stronger after each storm; hold the people of the three islands together as one across the sea between them; and advance a small nation on its own terms in a wide world, on its spice, its faith, and its people.

## Orders

- To the grower like [the spice grower](persona_the_spice_grower.md): tend the spice and replant the grove after the storm, and be paid a fair price for the land you carry the nation on.
- To the voice like [Merle Collins](persona_merle_collins.md): keep giving the memory its language, so the chapter is mourned and held in the open, not buried in silence.
- To the nation: heal the memory of the revolution as one people, and carry the small nation forward together across its three islands.

## Implementation

A small independent nation since 1974, come through its hardest chapter and rebuilt after Hurricane Ivan, that has made real strides, the groves replanted, Spicemas and the Big Drum kept alive, the memory of the revolution carried on the public record and the work of reconciliation begun, and that carries forward still, the chapter not yet wholly healed, the spice still priced low by the wide world, and the means of a small and storm-exposed nation always slender.
