# Plan: Build and Advance as One People

## Taxonomy

The open mandate the nation set itself: to mend the memory of its revolution that [the revolution and the reckoning](position_the_revolution_and_the_reckoning.md) hold tender, to build on the spice and the resilience that [the Spice Isle](position_the_spice_isle.md) always carry, and to carry forward the long spirit of refusal and the dream of a bigger belonging that [the rebel tradition](position_the_rebel_tradition.md) set down, so that a small nation across three islands move forward in the wide world as one people.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Mend the memory of the years 1979 to 1983, carrying the chapter with truth and reconciliation instead of silence or division; build on the spice and the resilience, making the land pay for those who tend it and bringing the groves back stronger after each storm; hold the people of the three islands together as one across the sea between them; and carry a small nation forward on its own terms in a wide world, on its spice, its faith, and its people.

## Orders

- To the grower like [the spice grower](persona_the_spice_grower.md): tend the spice and plant back the grove after the storm, and get a fair price for the land you carry the whole nation on.
- To the voice like [Merle Collins](persona_merle_collins.md): keep giving the memory its language, so the chapter is mourned and held in the open, not buried down in silence.
- To the nation: mend the memory of the revolution as one people, and carry the small nation forward together across its three islands.

## Implementation

A small independent nation since 1974, come through its hardest chapter and build back after Hurricane Ivan, that make real strides, the groves planted back, Spicemas and the Big Drum kept alive, the memory of the revolution carried on the public record and the work of reconciliation begun, and that carry forward still, the chapter not yet wholly mend, the spice still priced low by the wide world, and the means of a small storm-exposed nation always slender.
