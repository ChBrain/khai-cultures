## Place

## Load Bearing

It stand for the island its own self, the green land and the people who tend it, the hardback resilience that come back after the storm. Grenada is the one nation on God earth that carry a nutmeg on its flag, the spice set in the red disc at the hoist, and the seed is the nation name made plain to see, [the Spice Isle](position_the_spice_isle.md) grown into an emblem. ## Apparent

A hard, oval brown seed the size of a thumbnail, wrap while it fresh in a lacy red web of mace inside a fat yellow fruit, drying down to a deep aroma that warm and sweeten the air the minute you grate it. In itself it is only a seed and a spice; its weight is in what the island make of it, the name it give the nation and the place it hold right there on the flag.
