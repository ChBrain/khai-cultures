# Position: the revolution and the reckoning

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The chapter the nation carries soberly: the revolution of 1979 to 1983, when a young independent country took a different road, and the events at its end in 1983 and the intervention that followed, named here on the public record without taking a side and without dwelling on the hurt. It holds, too, the long work that came after, the reckoning, the memory and the reconciliation that a people undertake when a chapter has cost them and they choose to carry it together rather than bury it. It is the most recent and the most tender layer of the island's story, the one that the warmth of the welcome holds just beneath the surface.

## Orders

That the years 1979 to 1983 belong to the nation's history and are not to be erased or denied; that they are carried on the public record, with truth and without rancour; that the work of memory and reconciliation is itself a task the nation owes itself; and that the chapter be held in such a way that it draws the people together as one rather than dividing them again.

## Loses

Ground to silence, when the years are not spoken of and the grief is left to harden unhealed; and ground, equally, to the reopening of old wounds, when the chapter is taken up to divide rather than to mend. Between forgetting and refighting lies the narrow road of reckoning, and the position loses whenever the nation slips off it to either side.

## Drives

Lives in [Maurice Bishop](persona_maurice_bishop.md), the central figure of the revolution, carried here soberly as part of the nation's history, and in [Merle Collins](persona_merle_collins.md), the poet and novelist who gave the people's voice and the memory of those years their language. It drives Grenada to carry the chapter with truth and to do the long work of healing it as one people.
