## Cue

In March 1916, New Hampshire voters cast ballots in the first early presidential primary, launching the state's political tradition.

## Action

The primary establishes a legacy of voter engagement, developing [granite independence](position_granite_independence.md). The [maple bucket](piece_maple_bucket.md) represents traditional arts. Alice coordinates primary ballot records, while Robert records oral histories of early town moderators along the mountain valley.

## Stage

- [maple bucket](piece_maple_bucket.md) : the wooden sap gathering bucket representing forest harvest.

## Tension
