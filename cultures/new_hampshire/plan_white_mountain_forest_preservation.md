# Plan: the White Mountain plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and forest hydrology designed to regulate logging and protect alpine watersheds in the White Mountains.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Preserve the watershed by restricting clear-cutting logging, protecting alpine conifer zones, establishing soil runoff barriers on ski trails, and monitoring mountain stream acidity.

## Orders

- To the Division of Forests and Lands: Enforce logging compliance rules and audit commercial cutting permits.
- To railway engineers like [Robert](persona_robert.md): Support railway corridor runoff telemetry audits and maintain low-emission coal combustion standards.
- To maple producers like [Alice](persona_alice.md): Coordinate local community forest committee logs and advocate for traditional watershed protection.

## Implementation

The stream acidity water measurements; the forest soil erosion audits; and the deployment of mountain weather monitors.
