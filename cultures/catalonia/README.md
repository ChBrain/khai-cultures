# Catalonia: a khai culture

Catalonia staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-CT, language es. Catalunya: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Renaixença, Barcelona Exposition, and Statute of Autonomy encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_catalonia.md](play_catalonia.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_catalonia.md](pitch_catalonia.md), the Catalonia key.
- **Plots (history):** the Renaixença of the 19th century, the Barcelona Exposition of 1888, and the Statute of Autonomy of 1979.
- **Personas:** Jordi (modernist artisan) and Montserrat (vineyard operator), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** modernisme art (aesthetic precision/modernist pride/craft decoration), catalan identity (linguistic immersion/self-determination/regional assembly).
- **Places:** Sagrada Família, Penedès.
- **Processes:** cava making (traditional cava fermentation), castells building (building human towers).
- **Pieces:** a porro, a gralla.
- **Plan:** the Catalan immersion plan.
