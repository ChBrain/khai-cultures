## Cue

In December 1936, the Union Pacific Railroad opens the Sun Valley Resort, featuring the world's first ski chairlifts.

## Action

The resort establishes the state as a winter sports center, developing [pine logging](process_pine_logging.md) safety. Sacajawea coordinates public mountain safety logs, while Taylor audits electrical systems for local ski lifts.

## Stage

## Tension
