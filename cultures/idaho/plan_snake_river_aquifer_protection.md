# Plan: the Snake River plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and water resources science designed to manage agricultural water diversions and protect the Eastern Snake Plain Aquifer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Preserve the aquifer by regulating deep-well irrigation, restoring river wetlands, upgrading canal recharge systems, and monitoring groundwater depth levels.

## Orders

- To the Department of Water Resources: Enforce water allocations and audit agricultural canal permits.
- To irrigation technicians like [Taylor](persona_taylor.md): Support canal flow telemetry networks and implement water-efficient water recycle systems.
- To tribal coordinators like [Sacajawea](persona_sacajawea_id.md): Coordinate local community watershed logs and advocate for traditional fishing stream protection.

## Implementation

The groundwater depth measurements; the canal flow audits; and the deployment of aquifer pressure monitors.
