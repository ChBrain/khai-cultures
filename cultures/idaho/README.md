# Idaho: a khai culture

Idaho staged as a full khai play, ISO US-ID, language en. The Gem State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Nez Perce retreat, potato irrigation boom, and Sun Valley resort encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_idaho.md](play_idaho.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_idaho.md](pitch_idaho.md), the Idaho key.
- **Plots (history):** the Nez Perce Retreat of 1877, the Potato Irrigation Boom of the 1900s, and the Sun Valley Resort Opening of 1936.
- **Personas:** Taylor (irrigation technician) and Sacajawea (Nez Perce coordinator), written in English.
- **Positions:** mountain wilderness (mountain recreation/skiing pride), agrarian industry (potato farming pride/irrigation engineering).
- **Places:** Snake River Canal, Sun Valley.
- **Processes:** potato harvesting (mechanical row digging), pine logging (sustainable forest timber logging).
- **Pieces:** a potato sack, a ski lift chair.
- **Plan:** the Snake River aquifer plan.
