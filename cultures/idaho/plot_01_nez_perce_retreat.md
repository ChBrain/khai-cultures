## Cue

In June 1877, federal pressure to relocate forces Chief Joseph to lead the Nez Perce on a fighting retreat across the Pacific Northwest.

## Action

The retreat establishes the tribe's historical legacy of resistance, developing [agrarian industry](position_agrarian_industry.md) awareness. The [potato sack](piece_potato_sack.md) represents agricultural history. Sacajawea coordinates oral histories of tribal migration paths, while Taylor audits historic irrigation canal maps near reservation borders.

## Stage

- [agrarian industry](position_agrarian_industry.md) : the agricultural endurance, potato farming pride, and irrigation water resource stewardship.
- [potato sack](piece_potato_sack.md) : the burlap storage bag representing regional farm harvests.
- [the Snake River Canal](place_snake_river_canal.md) : the southern agricultural river canal system.

## Tension

Federal military pursuit displaces tribal families from ancestral lands, triggering long-term reservation boundary disputes.
