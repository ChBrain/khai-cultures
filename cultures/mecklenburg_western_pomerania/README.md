# Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania (Mecklenburg-Vorpommern): a khai culture

Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-MV, language de. The northeastern coastal state of Germany: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its maritime, Hanseatic, and ecological life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_mecklenburg_western_pomerania.md](play_mecklenburg_western_pomerania.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_mecklenburg_western_pomerania.md](pitch_mecklenburg_western_pomerania.md), the Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania key.
- **Plots (history):** the Hanseatic League in Wismar and Rostock, the founding of the University of Rostock in 1419, and the re-establishment of the state in 1990.
- **Personas:** Hinnerk (traditional fisherman) and Wiebke (modern marine biologist), written in German.
- **Positions:** der Küstenstolz (coastal pride), die Hanseatische Tradition (Hanseatic merchant heritage), die Seenruhe (lake district solitude).
- **Places:** die Kreidefelsen auf Rügen (Rügen Chalk Cliffs), der Rostocker Hafen (Rostock Port).
- **Processes:** der Heringsfang (Baltic herring fishing).
- **Pieces:** der Strandkorb (beach chair), das Gemälde der Kreidefelsen (Friedrich's chalk cliffs painting).
- **Plan:** der Ostseeschutz (Baltic Sea protection plan).
