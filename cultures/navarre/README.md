# Navarre: a khai culture

Navarre staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-NC, language es. Navarra: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Battle of Roncesvalles, San Fermin Running of the Bulls, and Irati Forest protection encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_navarre.md](play_navarre.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_navarre.md](pitch_navarre.md), the Navarre key.
- **Plots (history):** the Battle of Roncesvalles of 778, the San Fermin festival of 1926, and the Irati forest protection.
- **Personas:** Fermin (white asparagus farmer) and Ane (Irati forest ranger), written in Spanish with local Basque co-official features.
- **Positions:** navarrese fueros (historical fiscal/administrative charters), navarra montanesa (high Pyrenean forests/Basque-speaking valleys).
- **Places:** Irati Forest, Estafeta Street.
- **Processes:** asparagus peeling (white asparagus peeling), bull running (running of the bulls).
- **Pieces:** red Pamplona scarf, beechwood shepherd stick.
- **Plan:** the Irati forest conservation plan.
