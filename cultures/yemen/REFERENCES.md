# Yemen: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical and heritage
sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_ar.md](pitch_ar.md) is written from Yemen's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without
entering the prose.

| Dimension             | Yemen score (approx.) |
| --------------------- | --------------------- |
| Power Distance        | 80                    |
| Individualism         | 30                    |
| Uncertainty Avoidance | 80                    |
| Long Term Orientation | 25                    |

Source: estimates drawn from Geert Hofstede / The Culture Factor Group framework
and regional Arab-world profiles. Scores are approximate and indicative; where
Yemen has no fully published national score, regional Arab averages inform the
profile. The pitch reads the shape (collective, hierarchical, restraint-valuing,
rooted), never the numbers.

from the public historical and cultural record. They are credited here and never
claimed; the staging is original work. The culture is staged strictly on enduring
cultural and historical ground and takes no political position.
