# Balearic Islands: a khai culture

Balearic Islands staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-IB, language es. Illes Balears: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Conquest of Mallorca, Tourism Boom, and Posidonia Decree encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_balearic_islands.md](play_balearic_islands.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_balearic_islands.md](pitch_balearic_islands.md), the Balearic Islands key.
- **Plots (history):** the Conquest of Mallorca of 1229, the Tourism Boom of 1960s, and the Posidonia Meadow Protection Decree of 2018.
- **Personas:** Miquel (fisherman) and Coloma (Tramuntana agricultural worker), written in Spanish with local Mallorquín co-official features.
- **Positions:** insular bilingualism (co-official Mallorquín/Catalan, tourism vocabularies), mediterranean isolation (maritime/island reliance).
- **Places:** Serra de Tramuntana, Formentera Sea.
- **Processes:** ensaimada baking (ensaimada pastry baking), posidonia monitoring (seagrass health tracking).
- **Pieces:** ensaimada box, traditional fishing trap.
- **Plan:** the Posidonia protection plan.
