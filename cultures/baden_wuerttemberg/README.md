# Baden-Württemberg: a khai culture

Baden-Württemberg staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-BW, language de. The south-western state of Germany: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its industrious and inventive life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_baden_wuerttemberg.md](play_baden_wuerttemberg.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_baden_wuerttemberg.md](pitch_baden_wuerttemberg.md), the Baden-Württemberg key.
- **Plots (history):** the creation of the kingdom and duchy in 1806, the unification referendum of 1951, and the invention of the automobile in 1886.
- **Personas:** Karl (traditional engineer/mechanic archetype) and Frieda (modern tech founder), written in German.
- **Positions:** das Schaffen (industrious work ethic), das Tüfteln (engineering ingenuity), die Heimat (regional identity).
- **Places:** der Schwarzwald (Black Forest), das Stuttgarter Becken (automotive hub).
- **Processes:** die Kehrwoche (traditional sweeping duty).
- **Pieces:** die Kuckucksuhr (cuckoo clock), das Autopatent (automobile patent).
- **Plan:** die Mobilitätswende (sustainable mobility plan).
