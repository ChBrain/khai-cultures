(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind
the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no
number. The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the
record without entering the prose.

The two defining personas, [Kari](persona_kari.md) and [Sondre](persona_sondre.md),
are archetypes, not historical individuals: composites that carry the tension
between the older, sober generation (thrift, friluftsliv, dugnad) and the
younger, connected one (the open world, the riches taken for granted).

The term Janteloven ([position_janteloven.md](position_janteloven.md)) was coined
by the Norwegian-Danish author Aksel Sandemose in his 1933 novel "En flyktning
krysser sitt spor"; it is referenced here as a cultural concept, not claimed.
