# Plan: umbuso lophilako

## Taxonomy

Umsebenti lomkhulu sive sesibekele wona: kugcina umbuso wemandvulo uphila, hhayi nje liyindzaba. Kubamba [busatfwa benkhosi](position_busatfwa.md), lapho inkhosi neNdlovukati bametulwa ndzawonye; kubophela bantfu ndzawonye nge-[buntfu nemabutfo](position_buntfu.md); nekubamba [tinkhomo nesibaya](position_tinkhomo.md) njengemphilo yesintfu - konkhe ngesikhatsi lapho umhlaba lomusha ufuna konkhe kushintje.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Kugcina i-Ncwala noMhlanga kuphila kuze kube ngunyalo, hhayi nje imikhuba yemandvulo; kubamba siSwati njengelulwimi lolubophela sive sibe munye; kubophela emandvulo nalokusha ndzawonye, kuze labasha bafundze, batfole emandla lamasha, kepha bangashiyi lifa lesive; nekugcina sibaya nemasi nendlu yetjani kuyimphilo yesintfu, hhayi nje liyindzaba.

## Orders

- Kuloyo lomusha njenge-[intfombi](persona_intfombi.md): hlanganyela emikhubeni yesive, hlonipha lifa lebukhosi, ube yincenye yesintfu lesibambene.
- Kumbongi njenge-[J.S.M. Matsebula](persona_matsebula.md): bhala lulwimi nesintfu sesive, gcina lifa lelilotjiwe kute kungashabalali.
- Kusive sonkhe: gcina busatfwa benkhosi, i-Ncwala noMhlanga, nesibaya sesintfu kuphila, ungalahli lifa ngesikhatsi lapho umhlaba lomusha ufuna konkhe kushintje.

## Implementation

Umbuso wemandvulo loseusekhona, lapho i-Ncwala noMhlanga kusentelwa nyaka nemnyaka, lapho busatfwa benkhosi busekhona, lapho siSwati silulwimi lwesive sonkhe. Sive sesibambe lifa laso ngetandla totimbili - imikhuba isaphilako, lulwimi lwesive lubophela bonkhe, indlu yetjani isalukwa - kepha umsebenti usavulekile, ngobe labasha bayoshiya emakhaya bayofuna umsebenti emadolobheni, kantsi umhlaba lomusha usenemandla ekushintja sintfu sesive.

## Targets

- [x] Sive sahlanganiswa saba sinye ngenkhosi Mswati II, kwaze live letsiwa ngayo.
- [x] Inkhululeko yabuya ngo-1968 ngaphandle kwengati ngenkhosi Sobhuza II.
- [x] I-Ncwala noMhlanga kusachubeka kwentelwa kuze kube ngunyalo.
- [W] Busatfwa benkhosi nesintfu sesive kufuneka kuhlale kuyimphilo, hhayi nje umtsetfo.
- [W] Labasha bahlanganiswa emikhubeni yesive ngesikhatsi lapho beyoshiya emakhaya bayofuna umsebenti emadolobheni.
- [ ] Gcina siSwati nelifa lelilotjiwe luchubeke luphila etilwimini tetingane letisha.
- [ ] Bopha emandvulo nalokusha ndzawonye, kuze sive sibambe lifa laso kepha sikhone kuphila emhlabeni lomusha.
