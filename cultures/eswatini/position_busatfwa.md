# Position: busatfwa benkhosi

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Lifa lebukhosi laka-Ngwane lapho khona inkhosi neNdlovukati bametulwa ndzawonye - iNgwenyama, lokungumuntfu lokutsiwa libhubesi, neNdlovukati, lokungumuntfu lokutsiwa indlovukazi, indlovu lensikati. Lendlu yebukhosi ayisiyo yamunye, kepha yebabili: inkhosi nemake wenkhosi babamba sive ndzawonye, lendvodza nemfati babophele sive sibe munye. Lapha kuhambisana ne-Ncwala, inkhonzo yebukhosi lebaluleke kakhulu kunato tonkhe, neMhlanga, umgubho wetintfombi neNdlovukati. Busatfwa benkhosi buyimphilo yesintfu sema Swati, lapho inkhosi nesive kungenakwehlukaniswa.

## Orders

Kutsi sive nenkhosi kungenakwehlukaniswa, kutsi inkhosi neNdlovukati bagcwele sive sonkhe ndzawonye, kantsi loyo nailoyo wenta umsebenti wakhe; kutsi i-Ncwala iyinkhonzo lebaluleke kakhulu kunato tonkhe, lebopha sive senke; nekutsi busatfwa buyimphilo yesintfu, hhayi nje umtsetfo wembuso.

## Loses

Buyaphela uma busatfwa bushintjwa baba ngumtsetfo nje wembuso, hhayi sintfu sesive; nekulahleka uma bantfu labasha bayekela kuya e-Ncwala nakuMhlanga, bashiya lifa lebukhosi libe yindzaba yemandvulo nje, kungasekho yindlela yekuphila yesive.

## Drives

Iphila ebukhosini lobekwetiwe ngalo live, lobabutsa emabutfo wabandzakanisa sive sibe munye, nasebukhosini lobabusa sikhatsi lesidze, lwahlanganisa sive, lwabuya nenkhululeko. Iyaphusha eSwatini kutsi ibambe busatfwa bayo njengesintfu sesive, hhayi nje njengemtsetfo, kuze inkhosi neNdlovukati bahlale bagcwele sive sonkhe ndzawonye.
