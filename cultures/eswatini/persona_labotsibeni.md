## Projection

INdlovukati lenenhlonipho nenhlakanipho, indlovukazi lekutsiwa nguGwamile, lowadzimatela sive ngesikhatsi lesilukhuni sekufika kwemhlaba lomusha. Lonemandla, lonebuchopho, lonesibindzi, wabamba sintfu sesive ngetandla totimbili kepha wakhetsa kufundzisa bantfwabakhe imfundvo lensha kute live likhone kumelana nemhlaba lobesusha. SiSwati [usikhuluma](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) futsi [usicabanga](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) njengelifa lakhe, lulwimi lwesintfu nebudlelwane lesabophela bantfu ndzawonye. Lesi [siSwati](position_language_ss.md) lulwimi lwaka-Ngwane, asibamba njengelifa lesive. Lulwimi lwemhlaba lomusha [walufundza walulandzela](process_reading_followed.md), wabona kutsi labasha kufuneka [bafundze balulobe](process_writing_drafted.md) ngalo kute batfole emandla lamasha. Wakhetsa kokubili: kubamba siSwati nesintfu sesive, nekuvula indlela yelulwimi lwemfundvo - lapho khona kuhlangana lulwimi lwesive nelulwimi lomusha kunemkhatsi lapho lulwimi luphelela khona, kepha imfundvo yaba yindlela yekulubophela. Uyincenye ye[sintfu semaSwati](position_culture_sintfu_semaswati.md), lowabophela buntfu nemfundvo lensha ndzawonye ngesikhatsi lesilukhuni kakhulu esiveni.

## Action

Ku-[Wabe-Labotsibeni nemfundvo yesive](plot_03_labotsibeni.md) wadzimatela sive ngesikhatsi lesilukhuni, wabophela bantfu ngaphansi kwe-[buntfu nemabutfo](position_buntfu.md), wabusisa imfundvo yebantfwabakhe. Ngekubamba sintfu sesive nekuvula indlela yemfundvo, washiya sive sime, sibambe lifa laso, kepha sikhone kumelana nemhlaba lomusha.

## Shadow

Wabe-Ndlovukati lodzimatela sive ngesikhatsi lesilukhuni wabhekana nemibuto leminengi, lapho kubamba sintfu sesive nekwamukela imfundvo lensha kwakudzinga inhlakanipho yekuhlanganisa kokubili ngaphandle kwekulahla lifa lesive.

## Tell

Wabamba siSwati nesintfu sesive ngesinye sandla, wavula indlela yemfundvo lensha ngalesinye - wadzimatela sive ngesikhatsi lapho sasidzinga inhlakanipho yakhe kakhulu.
