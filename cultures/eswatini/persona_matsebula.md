## Projection

Imbongi nemlobi waka-Ngwane, lowabhala lulwimi lwesiSwati nesintfu sesive emaphepheni kute kungashabalali, lobophela emandvulo nalokusha ngembongo nemlandvo. Lonelutsandvo lwelulwimi, lohlonipha lifa lesive, lonebuchopho bekugcina sintfu sema Swati siphila. SiSwati [usikhuluma](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [usicabanga](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), futsi [usibhala](process_writing_mother_tongue.md) ngalokugcwele, walenta lulwimi lolungabamba lifa lesive sonkhe emaphepheni. Lesi [siSwati](position_language_ss.md) lulwimi lwaka-Ngwane, asibhala emaphepheni kute kungashabalali. Lulwimi lwemhlaba lomusha [walufundza walulandzela](process_reading_followed.md), kepha siSwati saba lulwimi labhala ngalo, lulwimi lwesintfu nebudlelwane. Wabhala siSwati kuze lifa lesive lingashabalali, ngobe wabona kutsi uma lulwimi lungalotjwa lungaphela - lapho khona kunemkhatsi lapho lulwimi lolungalotjwa luphelela khona, kepha kubhala kwakhe kwaluvala lowo mkhatsi. Uyincenye ye[sintfu semaSwati](position_culture_sintfu_semaswati.md), lowagcina lulwimi nemlandvo wesive ulotjiwe kute kungashabalali emhlabeni lomusha.

## Action

Ku-[Inkhululeko nembuso lophilako](plot_04_inkhululeko.md) wabhala lulwimi nesintfu sesive, wabophela bantfu ngaphansi kwe-[buntfu nemabutfo](position_buntfu.md), washiya lifa lelilotjiwe kute kungashabalali. Ngekubhala siSwati nemlandvo wesive, washiya sive sinelifa lelilotjiwe, lapho lulwimi nesintfu kungashabalali emhlabeni lomusha.

## Shadow

Imbongi lebhala lulwimi kute kungashabalali ibhekana nengoti yekutsi lokulotjiwe kungaba yindzaba yemandvulo nje, kungasekho kuphila kwesintfu emakhaya nasemikhubeni; nekutsi lulwimi lolulotjiwe kufuneka luchubeke luphila etilwimini tetingane letisha.

## Tell
