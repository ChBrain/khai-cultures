# Process: uMhlanga

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Tintfombi tesive sonkhe neNdlovukati: uMhlanga ucala ngetintfombi tesive sonkhe tibutsana, tihambe tiyogawula umhlanga emifuleni, titfwale lomhlanga tibuyise eLudzidzini. Lapha tintfombi tibutsana ngetinkhulungwane, tigide, tihlonipha iNdlovukati, tibe ngumgubho lomkhulu wesintfu sema Swati.

## Direction

Iya ekubopheleni tintfombi nesive ndzawonye, nasekuhloniphekeni kweNdlovukati: uMhlanga umgubho lobopha tintfombi tesive sonkhe ndzawonye, lapho tihlangana titfwele umhlanga tigidela iNdlovukati. Akusiyo nje umgubho, kepha yindlela yekubophela tintfombi nesive ndzawonye, yekuhlonipha iNdlovukati, yekugcina sintfu sema Swati siphila.

## Lever

Iphushwa kucina kwema Swati kwekutsi tintfombi tibophele ndzawonye, kutsi iNdlovukati ihloniphekile njengelinye liphawu lwesive ndzawonye nenkhosi, nekutsi sintfu sesive sidzinga kugcinwa siphila ngemikhuba lemikhulu. Imelana nemhlaba lomusha lobungafuna konkhe kushintje; nayo imelana nesimo sekutsi labasha bangashiya imikhuba yesive.

## Echo

UMhlanga wenta kuvakale loko lokuphushwa [tinkhomo nesibaya](position_tinkhomo.md) nesintfu sema Swati: kutsi sive sibophele ndzawonye ngemikhuba lemikhulu, nekutsi iNdlovukati ihloniphekile. Kuwo lomgubho [intfombi](persona_intfombi.md) itfwele umhlanga igidela iNdlovukati, iluphawu lwesintfu sema Swati lesisaphilako.
