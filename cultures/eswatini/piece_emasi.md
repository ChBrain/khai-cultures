# Piece: emasi nesishwala

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Ekhishini nasetafuleni lekhaya laka-Ngwane: emasi, lubisi loluvutsiwe loluphenduke lubabako, ludliwa nesishwala, liphalishi lelishukwa ngemmbila. Lapha kukudla kwasekhaya, lokudliwa ngumndeni ndzawonye, lokuvela etinkhomeni tesibaya. Emasi nesishwala kukudla kwema Swati kwemandvulo, lokuhambisana netinkhomo letifuywa emakhaya nasesibayeni sesintfu.

## Load Bearing

Kumele imphilo yasekhaya yaka-Ngwane, lapho kudla kuvela etinkhomeni tesibaya kuyimphilo yemndeni. Endaweni lapho sintfu sebudlelwane nemndeni kubalulekile, kudla kwasekhaya kuyindlela yekubophela umndeni ndzawonye: emasi avela elubisini lwetinkhomo tesibaya, asihlanganisa imfuyo yesibaya nemphilo yasekhaya. Lubisi loluvutsiwe luphawu lwekutsi tinkhomo tiyimphilo, hhayi nje imfuyo. Ngaphandle kwaloko kudla, ekhaya laka-Ngwane belingeke libe nemphilo yaso yansuku tonkhe.

## Apparent

Lubisi loluvutsiwe lolubabako nephalishi lelishukwa ngemmbila, kudliwa ndzawonye. Ngekwako nje, kukudla nje lokuvamile; bonkhe bubukhulu bako busesintfwini sema Swati lesisibekele kuko - kutsi kuvela etinkhomeni tesibaya, kantsi kudliwa ngumndeni ndzawonye njengemphilo yesintfu.

## Yearbook

- Emasi avela elubisini lwetinkhomo tesibaya, aluphawu lwekutsi tinkhomo tiyimphilo yemaSwati.
- Adliwa nesishwala, liphalishi lelishukwa ngemmbila, kudla kwasekhaya kwemandvulo.
- Kusachubeka kudliwa kuze kube ngunyalo emakhaya aka-Ngwane, kudla lokubophela umndeni nesibaya ndzawonye.
