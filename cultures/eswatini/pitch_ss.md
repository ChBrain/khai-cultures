# Pitch: umoya waka-Ngwane

## Taxonomy

Yilo licadzi lapho [eSwatini](play_eswatini.md) idlalwa khona. Ayishintji lutfo kulokwentekako - hhayi i-Ncwala, hhayi uMhlanga, hhayi inkhululeko. Ibeka kuphela kutsi konkhe lokwentekako kuvakala njani, licadzi lapho umdlalo wonkhe wetfulwa khona.

## Tenor

Kudlalwa ngekuhlonipha nangebudlelwane: sive lesibeka sive kunemuntfu lomunye, lapho umuntfu ngumuntfu ngebantfu, lapho inkhosi neNdlovukati bametulwa ndzawonye njengeluphawu lwesive. Bantfu labanesintfu, labahlonipha labadzala nebukhosi, labahlangana etibayeni nasemikhubeni, labatfola ekhaya nasesiveni kwekutsi bangoban. Umuntfu akamile yedvwa: umile ngemndeni wakhe, ngesigodzi sakhe, ngelibutfo lakhe, nangesive sakhe sonkhe. Lokuhloniphekako kuhleliwe, kantsi lobudlelwane bemndeni nesive bubaluleke kakhulu kunekuphangisa kwemuntfu yedvwana.

## Undertow

Ngaphansi kwalokuhlonipha kugijima budzima bekutsi umuntfu ngalokunye angete azitsetse yedvwana, ngobe konkhe kuhleliwe ngebukhosi nebudzala. Kunekutsindzeka kwekubamba sintfu sangempela ngesikhatsi lapho umhlaba lomusha ufuna konkhe kushintje, lapho bantfu labasha bayoshiya emakhaya bayofuna umsebenti emadolobheni, lapho lulwimi nesintfu kungashabalala uma kungabanjwa ngetandla totimbili. Naphansi kwekuhlonipha kunebudzima bekutsi loyo lomdzala angete lalele loyo lomusha, nekutsi lokuhleliwe ngalokunye kungavalela loyo lofuna kuvula indlela lensha.

## Nerve

Ishaya inhlitiyo yebudlelwane nesintfu: kucina kwekutsi awusilo wedvwa, kutsi unesive lesikubambile, kutsi inkhosi neNdlovukati banawe, kutsi sibaya nemasi nekhaya kukulindzile. Idlala etintweni letibalulekile kumaSwati - umndeni, sibaya, libutfo, nesive sonkhe - emkhubeni lobophela bantfu bonkhe ndzawonye, kuze umuntfu angabi yedvwa. Idlala futsi nasebudzimeni lobungaphansi - bekutsi loyo lofuna kuhamba kufuneka asihloniphe lesintfu lesimbambile.

## Echo

Lokusala ngumfanekiso wesive lesibambe sintfu saso ngetandla totimbili: lesihloniphako, lesinebudlelwane, lesibophele inkhosi neNdlovukati ndzawonye, lesihlangene etibayeni nasemikhubeni. Sive lesigijima ngekucina kwekutsi awusilo wedvwa, nekutsi umbuso wemandvulo ungaphila kuze kube ngunyalo uma sive sisibambile sintfu saso.
