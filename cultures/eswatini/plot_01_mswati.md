# Plot: Inkhosi Mswati II nekwetsiwa kwesive

## Taxonomy

[play_eswatini](play_eswatini.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Ngelikhulu lemashumi lamabili nesinhlanu, sive saka-Ngwane sasesehlile saphuma eshashalazini lemandvulo, sefika etinkalweni letisemkhatsini weNingizimu Afrika. Inkhosi [Mswati II](persona_mswati.md), lekwetsiwe ngayo live, yatfola sive lesisancundzwa, lesisafuna kuhlanganiswa sibe munye kuletive letehlukene letatahlangene ngaphansi kwebukhosi.

## Action

Inkhosi Mswati II yabutsa emabutfo, yabandzakanisa tive letinengi tabamunye ngaphansi kwebukhosi baka-Ngwane, yaze sive sake savutfwa savutsiwe ngendlovu. Emabutfo abophela labasha ngeminyaka yabo, abenta umsebenti wesive ndzawonye, [buntfu nemabutfo](position_buntfu.md) baba yindlela yekuhlanganisa sive sibe munye. Sive saka-Ngwane saba sive lesibambene, lesinenkhosi nesintfu sebudlelwane, kwaze live letsiwa ngayo lenkhosi, eSwatini.

## Stage

- [Mswati II](persona_mswati.md): inkhosi lebutsa emabutfo, ihlanganisa sive sibe munye, lekwetsiwa ngayo live.
- [buntfu nemabutfo](position_buntfu.md): indlela yekuhlanganisa sive ndzawonye, lebopha labasha ngaphansi kwenkhosi.
- [Lobamba](place_lobamba.md): inhloko-dolobha yebukhosi lapho khona sive sihlangana nenkhosi.

## Tension

Sive lesisancundzwa, lesehla saphuma eshashalazini lemandvulo, kufuneka sihlanganiswe sibe munye kuletive letehlukene letatahlangene. Emabutfo abandzakanisa labasha, kepha lokuhlanganisa sive ngaphansi kwebukhosi kudzinga inkhosi lenemandla nesintfu lesibophele bonkhe ndzawonye - kwaze sive saka-Ngwane saba sive sinye, lesinenkhosi nesintfu lesibambene.
