# Position: buntfu nemabutfo

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Sintfu sebudlelwane lapho khona umuntfu ngumuntfu ngebantfu - awusilo wedvwa, umile ngemndeni wakho, ngesigodzi sakho, nangesive sakho sonkhe. Lapha kuhambisana nemabutfo, emabutfo lahlanganisa bantfu labasha ngeminyaka yabo, abenta umsebenti wesive ndzawonye, abadlalela inkhosi e-Ncwala nakuMhlanga. Libutfo akusilo lihhabe nje, kepha yindlela yekuhlanganisa sive, yekufundzisa labasha kuhlonipha, nekubophela sonkhe sive ndzawonye ngaphansi kwenkhosi. Buntfu nemabutfo kuyimphilo yesintfu, lapho umuntfu atfola ekhaya nasesiveni kwekutsi ungoban.

## Orders

Kutsi umuntfu ngumuntfu ngebantfu, hhayi yedvwa; kutsi labasha bahlanganiswa emabutfweni, bafundziswe kuhlonipha nekwenta umsebenti wesive ndzawonye; nekutsi sive sonkhe sibophele ndzawonye ngebudlelwane, hhayi ngemtsetfo nje.

## Loses

Buyaphela uma labasha bashiya emakhaya bayofuna umsebenti emadolobheni, bayekele emabutfo nemikhuba yesive; nekulahleka uma sintfu sebudlelwane sishintjwa saba sintfu sekuphangisa kwemuntfu yedvwana, lapho umuntfu acabanga ngaye yedvwa, angasacabangi ngesive.
