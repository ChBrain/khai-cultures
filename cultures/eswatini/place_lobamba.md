# Place: Lobamba

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Inhloko-dolobha yebukhosi nendlu yemtsetfo, lapho khona kuhlala iNdlovukati eLudzidzini, indlu-nkhulu yesive sonkhe. Lapha kusesigodzini saseEzulwini, phakatsi kwetintsaba, lapho kwentelwa khona i-Ncwala noMhlanga, imikhuba lebaluleke kakhulu yesive. Sibaya sesive, indlu yemtsetfo, nendlu yebukhosi - bukhosi nesintfu kubekwe kanyekanye etintsabeni teEzulwini. Lapha sive sihlangana nenkhosi neNdlovukati, busatfwa benkhosi bubekwe ndzawonye.

## Holds

- [Inkhosi Mswati II nekwetsiwa kwesive](plot_01_mswati.md): inhloko-dolobha yebukhosi lapho khona sive sihlanganiswa sibe munye ngaphansi kwenkhosi.
- [Wabe-Labotsibeni nemfundvo yesive](plot_03_labotsibeni.md): indzawo yebukhosi lapho khona wabe-Ndlovukati abamba sive ndzawonye ngesikhatsi lesilukhuni.

## Offers

Indzawo lapho sive sibona busatfwa baso, lapho i-Ncwala noMhlanga kwentelwa khona, lapho inkhosi neNdlovukati bahlangana nesive sonkhe. Sibaya sesive, indlu yemtsetfo, nendlu yebukhosi kubophele ndzawonye, kuze sive sibone busatfwa baso bukanyekanye nesintfu saso.

## Withheld

Akusiyo nje indzawo yembuso. Lobamba ibamba lifa lebukhosi lapho khona busatfwa kufuneka buhlale buyimphilo yesintfu, hhayi nje umtsetfo - indzawo lekhumbula sive kutsi busatfwa buphila uma sive sisibambile, sichubeka sente i-Ncwala noMhlanga kuze kube ngunyalo.
