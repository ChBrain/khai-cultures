# Piece: indlu yetjani

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Emakhaya aka-Ngwane, etinkalweni kusukela eMaveni laphakeme kuya etigodzini letishisako: indlu yetjani lelukwa ngetjani njengeligwababa, ime njengeligamu lemvelo, ifudvumele ebusika ipholile ehlobo. Lapha kukhaya, lapho umndeni uhlala khona, lapho sibaya sisedvute, lapho i-Ndlovukati ihlala etindlini tetjani eLudzidzini. Indlu yetjani liphawu lekhaya laka-Ngwane, lelilukwa ngetandla telutfo lwemvelo lwelive.

## Load Bearing

Imele likhaya laka-Ngwane ngekwalo, lapho khona umndeni nesibaya kukhona ndzawonye. Endaweni lapho sintfu sesintfu sebudlelwane sibalulekile, indlu yetjani liphawu lebudlelwane bemndeni: ilukwa ngetandla letitsatsa tjani lwemvelo, ihlanganiswa ngabantfu labasebenta ndzawonye, ime emakhaya emoya wesintfu. Yingayo iNdlovukati ihlala etindlini tetjani eLudzidzini, kwaze indlu yetjani yaba liphawu lebukhosi nesintfu ndzawonye. Ngaphandle kwalendlu, likhaya laka-Ngwane belingeke libe nemoya welive laso.

## Apparent

Indlu lelukwa ngetjani nemise, ime njengeligwababa, inelitsala lelincane. Ngekwayo nje, ilukwa ngetjani lwemvelo nemise lwemphi; bonkhe bubukhulu bayo busekutseni sive sisibekele kuyo - emoya wekhaya, budlelwane bemndeni, nesintfu sesive lesilukwa ngetandla.

## Yearbook

- Ilukwa ngetjani lwemvelo njengeligwababa, ifudvumele ebusika ipholile ehlobo, ihambisana nesimo selive laka-Ngwane.
- Ime emakhaya nasendlini yebukhosi eLudzidzini, lapho khona iNdlovukati ihlala etindlini tetjani.
- Isalukwa ngetandla kuze kube ngunyalo, iphawu lesintfu sema Swati lesisaphilako, lapho i-Ncwala noMhlanga kwentelwa khona eduze kwayo.
