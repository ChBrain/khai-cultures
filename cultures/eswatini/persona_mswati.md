## Projection

INgwenyama lekwetsiwe ngayo live, libhubesi lelabutsa emabutfo, labandzakanisa tive letinengi tabamunye ngaphansi kwebukhosi baka-Ngwane, laze sive sake savutfwa savutsiwe ngendlovu. Lonemandla, lonebuchopho bekuhlela, lonenhlonipho yesintfu, wakha sive sinye kuletive letehlukene, wabophela labasha emabutfweni. SiSwati [usikhuluma](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) futsi [usicabanga](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) njengelifa lakhe, lulwimi lwesive lalubophela tive letehlukene tibe sive sinye. Lesi [siSwati](position_language_ss.md) lulwimi lwaka-Ngwane, lolwenta tive letinengi sibe sive sinye. Tilwimi tetive labatahlanganisako [wativa watilandzela](process_hearing_followed.md) ekuhlanganiseni sive, kepha siSwati saba lulwimi lolubophela sonkhe sive ndzawonye. Wahlanganisa tive letehlukene letatinetilwimi tato, kepha siSwati saba lulwimi lwesive sonkhe - lapho khona tilwimi letehlukene tihlangana kunemkhatsi lapho lulwimi luphelela khona, kepha siSwati saluvala lowo mkhatsi.

## Action

Ngekuhlanganisa tive letehlukene sibe sive sinye, washiya sive sinenkhosi nesintfu sebudlelwane lesibambene.
