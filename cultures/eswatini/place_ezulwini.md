# Place: Esigodzini saseEzulwini

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Sigodzi sasezulwini, sigodzi lesisemkhatsini wetintsaba, lapho litigodzi lihlangana neMaveni laphakeme. Lapha kuseduze kwaLobamba, lapho khona kuhlala bukhosi, kantsi kuduze nayo lidvwala laseSibebe, litjitje lelikhulu lemandvulo lelimile njengelibhulgu. Tinkalo letehlukile, kusukela eMaveni laphakeme aseHighveld lapho kumakhata khona, kuya etigodzini letishisako teLowveld lapho khona kufuywa tinkhomo. Lesigodzi sigcwele bukhulu bemandvulo neluhlata lwemvelo waka-Ngwane.

## Holds

- [Sive sake savutfwa ngendlovu](plot_02_sive.md): tinkalo lapho sive sakha emakhaya aso, sifuye tinkhomo etigodzini letishisako.

## Offers

Sigodzi lapho sive sibona buhle bemvelo yelive laso, kusukela etintsabeni letiphakeme kuya etigodzini letishisako, lapho khona kufuywa tinkhomo, kufuywa emakhaya etindlu tetjani. Lapha kuseduze kwebukhosi baseLobamba, kantsi lidvwala laseSibebe limile njengeluphawu lwebukhulu bemvelo nemandvulo.

## Withheld

Akusiyo nje indzawo yebuhle. Esigodzini saseEzulwini sibamba sintfu sema Swati, lapho tinkhomo nesibaya kuyimphilo, lapho emakhaya etjani amile etinkalweni - sigodzi lesikhumbula sive kutsi imvelo yelive nesintfu saso kubophele ndzawonye, hhayi nje buhle bemehlo.
