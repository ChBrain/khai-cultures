# Process: i-Ncwala

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Inkhosi nesive sonkhe ndzawonye: i-Ncwala icala nabotinyanga betfula umoya welwandle, batsatse emanti laluphawu lwekuvuselela inkhosi. Emabutfo abutsana, sive sonkhe sibutsana eLudzidzini, kwentelwa inkhonzo yebukhosi lebaluleke kakhulu kunato tonkhe, umchamo wetitselo tekucala lapho inkhosi idla titselo letisha kuqala kunesive sonkhe.

## Direction

Iya ekuvuseleleni inkhosi nesive ndzawonye: i-Ncwala ayisiyo nje umgubho, kepha yinkhonzo lebopha inkhosi nesive sibe munye, levuselela emandla enkhosi nemphilo yesive sonkhe ngalomnyaka lomusha. Yinkhonzo yebudlelwane lebopha sive sonkhe ndzawonye ngaphansi kwenkhosi, lapho inkhosi idla titselo tekucala isikhamela sive sonkhe.

## Lever

Iphushwa kucina kwema Swati kutsi inkhosi nesive kungenakwehlukaniswa, nekutsi emphilo yesive idzinga kuvuselelwa nyaka nemnyaka ngenkhonzo lengcwele. Imelana nemhlaba lomusha lobufuna konkhe kushintje; nayo imelana nesimo sekutsi labasha bangashiya emakhaya, bayekele imikhuba yesive.

## Echo

I-Ncwala yenta kuvakale loko lokuphushwa [busatfwa benkhosi](position_busatfwa.md): kutsi inkhosi nesive kungenakwehlukaniswa, nekutsi busatfwa buyimphilo yesintfu, hhayi nje umtsetfo. Ngaleyo nkhonzo [Sobhuza II](persona_sobhuza.md) wagcina busatfwa busekhona, sive sonkhe sibutsana eLudzidzini sente inkhonzo yebukhosi lebaluleke kakhulu.
