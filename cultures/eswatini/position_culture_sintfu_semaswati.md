# Position: sintfu semaSwati

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Sintfu semaSwati sihlanganisa konkhe lokwakha live: [busatfwa benkhosi](position_busatfwa.md), lapho inkhosi neNdlovukati kungenakwehlukaniswa nesive; [buntfu nemabutfo](position_buntfu.md), lapho umuntfu ngumuntfu ngebantfu futsi emabutfo abophela labasha esiveni; ne[tinkhomo nesibaya](position_tinkhomo.md), lapho imfuyo iyimphilo kantsi sibaya siyindzawo lengcwele yekhaya. Sikhulunywa nge[siSwati](position_language_ss.md), lulwimi lolubophela sive sonkhe sibe munye ngaphandle kwekwehlukanisa sive ngetigodzi. Sitfwele nemikhuba yebukhosi, i-Ncwala noMhlanga, lokungasiko nje imigcugcuteko, kepha kuyimphilo lebophela emandvulo nalokusha ndzawonye. Nguloko umuntfu waka-Ngwane ashiwo ngako angakakhetsi, indlela yekuphila lapho umuntfu, umndeni, sive nenkhosi kungenakwehlukaniswa.

## Orders

Kutsi inkhosi neNdlovukati nesive bahlale bametulwe ndzawonye, kutsi umuntfu ahloniphe labadzala nalabancane, futsi angabi yedvwa kepha abe ngumuntfu ngebantfu. Kufuna kutsi tinkhomo naletinye timfuyo tihlonishwe njengemphilo, hhayi njengemali nje, nekutsi i-Ncwala noMhlanga kugcinwe njengemikhuba lebalulekile kunato tonkhe. Kutjela kutsi sintfu sibonwe njengendlela yekuphila, hhayi njengemtsetfo lomdzala nje longasenamsebenti emhlabeni lomusha.

## Loses

Buyaphela sintfu semaSwati uma busatfwa, buntfu netinkhomo kubukwa njengemtsetfo wemandvulo nje, kungasenandzaba yesintfu lesisaphilako; nekulahleka uma labasha bashiya emakhaya bayofuna umsebenti emadolobheni, bayekele emabutfo, i-Ncwala noMhlanga, kungasekho lapho khona kuhlanganiswa sive sonkhe. Buyalahleka nalapho lulwimi nemikhuba kungabhalwa kute kungashabalali, ngobe emhlaba lomusha ufuna konkhe kushintje ngesivinini lesingakalindzeli sintfu.

## Drives

Iphila ebukhosini lobalusa sive sikhatsi lesidze, lwahlanganisa tive letahlukene sibe sive sinye kantsi lwabuya nenkhululeko ngaphandle kwengati; iphila naku-Labotsibeni lowadzimatela sive ngesikhatsi lesilukhuni wabusisa imfundvo yebantfwabakhe; iphila naku-Matsebula lobhale lulwimi nemlandvo wesive kute kungashabalali; nakuletintfombi letitfwala umhlanga tigidela iNdlovukati mnyaka ngamunye. Iyaphusha eSwatini kutsi ibambe busatfwa, buntfu netinkhomo njengemphilo yinye yesintfu, kuze inkhosi nesive bahlale bangenakwehlukaniswa emhlabeni lolushintjako.
