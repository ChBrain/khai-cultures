# Position: tinkhomo nesibaya

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Tinkhomo kuyimfuyo nemphilo yemaSwati - hhayi nje kudla, kepha bunjalo bemuntfu nemndeni. Sibaya, lapho tinkhomo tihlala khona, sisendlini-nkhulu yekhaya: lapha kuyahlatjwa kubikwe kubanini, kuyacaliswa emadlozi, kuyakhokhelwa nelobola lemfati. Tinkhomo tikhokhelwa elobola, tisetjentiselwa imikhuba yebukhosi e-Ncwala, kantsi sibaya sibalulekile njengendzawo lengcwele lapho khona kuhlangana labaphilako nalabasalele. Tinkhomo nesibaya kuyimphilo yesintfu, lapho imfuyo iyimphilo, kantsi sibaya sisendlini lengcwele yekhaya.

## Orders

Kutsi tinkhomo tiyimfuyo nemphilo, hhayi nje kudla; kutsi sibaya siyindzawo lengcwele yekhaya, lapho kuhlangana labaphilako nemadlozi; nekutsi lobola lukhokhelwa ngetinkhomo, kubophela imindeni lemibili ndzawonye.

## Loses

Buyaphela uma tinkhomo titsatfwa njengemali nje, kungasekho bunjalo bemuntfu nesintfu sazo; nekulahleka uma sibaya silahlwa, bantfu bahlala emadolobheni kungasekho sibaya lapho khona bangahlangana nemadlozi nemndeni wonkhe.

## Drives

Iphila ekhaya lapho umuntfu afuya sibaya, ahlonipha imikhuba yesibaya, nase-Ncwala lapho tinkhomo tibamba indzima lengcwele. Iyaphusha eSwatini kutsi ibambe tinkhomo nesibaya njengemphilo yesintfu, kuze imfuyo ihlale iyimphilo, kantsi sibaya sihlale siyindzawo lengcwele yekhaya.
