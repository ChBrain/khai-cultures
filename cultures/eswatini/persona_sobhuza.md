# Persona: Sobhuza II

## Taxonomy

[busatfwa benkhosi](position_busatfwa.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

INgwenyama lebusa sikhatsi lesidze kunato tonkhe etinkhosini temhlaba lacasha, lihbubesi lelahlanganisa sive sonkhe ngaphansi kwebukhosi, laze labuya nenkhululeko ngo-1968 ngaphandle kwengati. Lonenhlonipho, lohlakanipfile, lonesineke, wabamba sintfu sesive ngetandla totimbili kepha wamukela nemfundvo lensha, abophela emandvulo nalokusha ndzawonye. SiSwati [usikhuluma](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [usicabanga](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), futsi [usiva](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) njengelifa lakhe, lulwimi lwesive lalubophela bonkhe ndzawonye. SiNgisi, lulwimi lwemhlaba lobesusha, [walusebentisa njengelitsulo](process_speaking_borrowed.md) ekuhlanganiseni sive nasekubuyiseni inkhululeko, [walufundza walulandzela](process_reading_followed.md) emaphepheni embuso. Wakhuluma siSwati saka-Ngwane phambi kwemhlaba wonkhe, kepha lulwimi lwembuso lomusha lwaludzinga kulusebentisa, kuze lifa lesive lihlanganiswe nemhlaba lomusha - lapho khona kuhlangana siSwati nesiNgisi kunemkhatsi lapho lulwimi luphelela khona.

## Action

Ku-[Inkhululeko nembuso lophilako](plot_04_inkhululeko.md) wabuya nenkhululeko ngaphandle kwengati, wahlanganisa sive ngaphansi kwe-[busatfwa benkhosi](position_busatfwa.md), wagcina inkhosi neNdlovukati bametulwe ndzawonye. Ngekubamba lifa lebukhosi nekwamukela nemfundvo lensha, washiya sive sinembuso lotimele, lapho i-Ncwala noMhlanga kuchubeka kuze kube ngunyalo.

## Shadow

Inkhosi lebusa sikhatsi lesidze kangaka isengoti yekubamba emandla aze ashintje sintfu sesive sibe ngumtsetfo nje wembuso, hhayi sintfu sebudlelwane; nemhlaba lobesushintja kungaca kutsi lifa lebukhosi libhekane nemibuto lemisha yesikhatsi sanyalo.

## Tell

Wabusa sikhatsi lesidze kangaka kepha wahlala anenhlonipho yesintfu sesive, abophela emandvulo nalokusha, ashiya sive sinenkhululeko nelifa lebukhosi lelisaphilako.
