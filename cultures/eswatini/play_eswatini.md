# Play: eSwatini

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): indlu lebamba lomsebenti futsi iphendvula ngawo.

## Name

ESwatini, umbuso losemkhatsini weNingizimu Afrika neMozambiki, etinkalweni letehlukile letehla kusukela eMaveni laphakeme aseHighveld, kuya etigodzini letishisako teLowveld. Inhloko-dolobha yetembusave nguMbabane, kantsi iLobamba yinhloko-dolobha yebukhosi nendlu yemtsetfo. ISO SZ, lulwimi ngesiSwati lolukhulunywa ngumuntfu wonkhe waka-Ngwane, lolubophela sive sibe munye. Live lelincane, kodvwa umbuso wemandvulo loseusekhona, lapho khona inkhosi nesive kungenakwehlukaniswa.

## Arc

Sive saka-Ngwane, emaSwati, sehla saphuma eshashalazini lemandvulo, sahamba sehla siNgwane kuze sefike lapho sigcinwe khona ngaphansi kweMaveni laseSibebe. Inkhosi Mswati II lekwetsiwe ngayo live wabutsa emabutfo wabandzakanisa tive letinengi tabamunye, sive sake savutfwa savutsiwe ngendlovu, savutsa ngendlovu. Wabe-Labotsibeni Gwamile wadzimatela sive ngesikhatsi lesilukhuni, wakhetsa kufundzisa bantfwabakhe imfundvo kute live likhone kumelana nemhlaba lomusha. Inkhosi Sobhuza II yabusa sikhatsi lesidze kunato tonkhe etinkhosini temhlaba, yahlanganisa sive, yase ngo-1968 ibuya nenkhululeko ngaphandle kwengati. Ngaphansi kwako konkhe kugijima inhlitiyo yinye: kutsi lapha inkhosi neNdlovukati bametulwe ndzawonye, iNgwenyama neNdlovukati, libhubesi nendlovukazi; kutsi tinkhomo nesibaya kuyimfuyo nemphilo; kutsi i-Ncwala iyinkhonzo yebukhosi nemchamo wetitselo tekucala, kantsi uMhlanga ungumgubho wetintfombi neNdlovukati. Sive lesisancundza umlilo wesintfu sangempela, lesibambe sintfu saso ngetandla totimbili, sime sime emkhatsini wemhlaba lomusha.
