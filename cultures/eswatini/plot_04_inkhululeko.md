# Plot: Inkhululeko nembuso lophilako

## Taxonomy

[play_eswatini](play_eswatini.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Ngemvakwesikhatsi lesidze sesintfu sesive nemfundvo, sive saka-Ngwane safika esikhatsini lapho sasidzinga kubuya nenkhululeko, sibuse sive saso ngekwaso. NgeNkhosi [Sobhuza II](persona_sobhuza.md), lowabusa sikhatsi lesidze kunato tonkhe etinkhosini temhlaba, lowahlanganisa sive ngaphansi kwebukhosi.

## Action

Ngo-1968, inkhosi Sobhuza II yabuya nenkhululeko ngaphandle kwengati, sive saka-Ngwane saba ngumbuso lotimele. [Busatfwa benkhosi](position_busatfwa.md) bagcina busekhona, inkhosi neNdlovukati bahlala bametulwe ndzawonye njengeluphawu lwesive. I-Ncwala, inkhonzo yebukhosi lebaluleke kakhulu, noMhlanga, umgubho wetintfombi neNdlovukati, kwachubeka kuze kube ngunyalo, lifa lebukhosi nesintfu sesive sasala siphila, hhayi nje liyindzaba yemandvulo.

## Stage

- [Sobhuza II](persona_sobhuza.md): inkhosi lebuya nenkhululeko, lehlanganisa sive, lebusa sikhatsi lesidze.
- [busatfwa benkhosi](position_busatfwa.md): lifa lebukhosi lapho inkhosi neNdlovukati bametulwa ndzawonye, lebagcina busekhona.
- [i-Ncwala](process_incwala.md): inkhonzo yebukhosi lebaluleke kakhulu, lebopha sive senke, lechubeka kuze kube ngunyalo.

## Tension

Sive sidzinga kubuya nenkhululeko, sibuse sive saso ngekwaso, kepha sidzinga futsi kubamba lifa lebukhosi nesintfu sesive kute kungashabalali emhlabeni lomusha. Inkhululeko yabuya ngaphandle kwengati, kepha umsebenti lomkhulu kwaba kugcina busatfwa benkhosi, i-Ncwala, noMhlanga kuphila kuze kube ngunyalo - kwaze umbuso wemandvulo wasala uphila, hhayi nje liyindzaba.
