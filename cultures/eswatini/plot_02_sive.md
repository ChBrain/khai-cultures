# Plot: Sive sake savutfwa ngendlovu

## Taxonomy

[play_eswatini](play_eswatini.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Ngemvakwekuhlanganiswa kwesive, sive saka-Ngwane sahlangana ngaphansi kwebukhosi, sakha emakhaya etinkalweni letisukela eMaveni laphakeme kuya etigodzini letishisako. Sive sasidzinga kume, sibambe imfuyo nesintfu saso, kute sikhone kuphila etinkalweni letehlukile, sibe sive sinye.

## Action

Sive saka-Ngwane sagcwala emakhaya etindlu tetjani, indlu yetjani lelukwa ngetandla njengeligwababa, [tinkhomo nesibaya](position_tinkhomo.md) kwaba yimfuyo nemphilo yemaSwati. Tinkhomo takhokhelwa elobola, tasetjentiselwa imikhuba yebukhosi, kantsi sibaya saba yindzawo lengcwele yekhaya lapho kuhlangana labaphilako nemadlozi. Sive sema, samelana nemhlaba lobesusha lobesufikile etinkalweni teNingizimu Afrika, sabambe imfuyo nesintfu saso ngetandla totimbili.

## Stage

- [tinkhomo nesibaya](position_tinkhomo.md): imfuyo nemphilo yemaSwati, lesibaya siyindzawo lengcwele yekhaya.
- [indlu yetjani](piece_indlu_yetjani.md): indlu lelukwa ngetjani njengeligwababa, likhaya lema Swati.
- [Esigodzini saseEzulwini](place_ezulwini.md): tinkalo letisukela eMaveni laphakeme kuya etigodzini letishisako, lapho sive sakha emakhaya aso.

## Tension

Sive lesisancunda kufuneka sime, sibambe imfuyo nesintfu saso, ngesikhatsi lapho umhlaba lobesusha bufika etinkalweni teNingizimu Afrika. Tinkhomo nesibaya kwaba yimphilo yesintfu, kepha kume kwesive etinkalweni letehlukile kudzinga kubamba sintfu ngetandla totimbili - kwaze sive saka-Ngwane sema, samelana nemhlaba lobesusha, sabambe imfuyo nesintfu saso.
