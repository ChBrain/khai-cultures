# Persona: intfombi

## Taxonomy

[tinkhomo nesibaya](position_tinkhomo.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Intfombi yaka-Ngwane, lensikatana lesefika kuMhlanga ihlanganyele netinye tintfombi tesive sonkhe, itfwele umhlanga, igide phambi kweNdlovukati. Lehlanyelako, lehloniphako, lenelutsandvo lwesintfu sesive, ihamba ekhaya lapho ifuye sibaya, ifundziswa kuhlonipha imikhuba yesibaya nesintfu sesive. SiSwati [usikhuluma](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [usicabanga](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), futsi [usiva](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) ekhaya nasesiveni, lulwimi lwesintfu nebudlelwane lalubophela bonkhe ndzawonye. Lesi [siSwati](position_language_ss.md) lulwimi lwaka-Ngwane, asigide ngaso kuMhlanga. [SiNgisi](../united_kingdom/position_language_en_gb.md) sesikolwa [usifundza usilandzela](process_reading_followed.md), kepha siSwati silulwimi lwakhe lwenhlitiyo, lulwimi lwemkhuba waka-Ngwane. Itfwele umhlanga igide ngesiSwati sesintfu, kepha umhlaba lomusha wesikolwa nemadolobha ufuna lulwimi lolusha - lapho khona kuhlangana siSwati nesiNgisi kunemkhatsi lapho lulwimi luphelela khona, kepha emkhubeni weMhlanga siSwati saluvala lowo mkhatsi.

## Action

Ku-[Inkhululeko nembuso lophilako](plot_04_inkhululeko.md) iyaluphawu lwe-[tinkhomo nesibaya](position_tinkhomo.md), umgubho weMhlanga lochubeka kuze kube ngunyalo, lapho tintfombi tesive sonkhe tihlangana titfwele umhlanga tigidela iNdlovukati. Iyabophela sintfu sesive ngekutsi ibe yincenye yemkhuba lobopha tonkhe tintfombi nesive sonkhe ndzawonye.

## Shadow

Intfombi lehlanganyela kuMhlanga isengoti yekutsi labasha bayoshiya emakhaya bayofuna umsebenti emadolobheni, bayekele imikhuba yesive; kantsi kubamba sintfu sesive emhlabeni lomusha kudzinga kubophela emandvulo nalokusha ndzawonye.

## Tell

Itfwele umhlanga igide phambi kweNdlovukati netinye tintfombi tesive sonkhe, iluphawu lwesintfu sema Swati lesisaphilako, lapho intfombi yinye ibophela sive sonkhe ngemkhuba munye.
