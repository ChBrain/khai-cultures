# Plot: Wabe-Labotsibeni nemfundvo yesive

## Taxonomy

[play_eswatini](play_eswatini.md)

## Owner

## Cue

Ekucaleni kwelikhulu lemashumi lamabili, sive saka-Ngwane safika esikhatsini lesilukhuni, lapho umhlaba lomusha wawusufikile etinkalweni teNingizimu Afrika, sive sidzinga kume kute sikhone kumelana nemhlaba lobesusha. NgeNdlovukati [Labotsibeni Gwamile](persona_labotsibeni.md), wabe-mfati lonenhlonipho nenhlakanipho, lowadzimatela sive ngalesikhatsi.

## Action

Wabe-Labotsibeni Gwamile yadzimatela sive ngesikhatsi lesilukhuni, [buntfu nemabutfo](position_buntfu.md) wabophela bantfu ndzawonye, wakhetsa kufundzisa bantfwabakhe imfundvo lensha kute live likhone kumelana nemhlaba lobesusha. Wabamba sintfu sesive ngetandla totimbili, kepha wavula indlela yekutsi labasha bafundze, batfole emandla lamasha. Lokuhlanganisa sintfu sesive nemfundvo lensha kwaba yindlela yekutsi sive sime, sibambe lifa laso, kepha sikhone kumelana nemhlaba lomusha.

## Stage

- [Labotsibeni Gwamile](persona_labotsibeni.md): wabe-Ndlovukati lodzimatela sive, lobusisa imfundvo yebantfwabakhe.
- [buntfu nemabutfo](position_buntfu.md): sintfu sebudlelwane lesibophela bantfu ndzawonye, lesidzimatela sive.
- [Lobamba](place_lobamba.md): inhloko-dolobha yebukhosi lapho khona wabe-Ndlovukati abamba sive ndzawonye.

## Tension

Sive sidzinga kubamba lifa laso ngetandla totimbili, kepha sidzinga futsi kufundza, kutfola emandla lamasha kute simelane nemhlaba lobesusha. Wabe-Labotsibeni wakhetsa kokubili: kubamba sintfu sesive nekuvula indlela yemfundvo - kwaze sive sime, sibambe lifa laso, kepha sikhone kuphila emhlabeni lomusha.
