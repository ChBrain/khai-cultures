# Plan: the salt marsh plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and estuary science designed to manage shoreline development and protect coastal salt marshes from erosion.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Protect salt marshes by constructing living shorelines, planting spartina grass, regulating septic permits, and monitoring dock runoff.

## Orders

- To the Department of Health: Enforce water quality rules and audit coastal septic permits.
- To architects like [Charlotte](persona_charlotte.md): Support historic drainage audits and use eco-efficient design specifications.
- To Gullah weavers like [Elijah](persona_elijah.md): Coordinate community marsh surveys and advocate for traditional harvesting access.

## Implementation

The spartina density counts; the water nitrogen measurements; and the deployment of marsh erosion monitoring poles.
