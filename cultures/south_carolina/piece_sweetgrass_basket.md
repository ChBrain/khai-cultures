# Piece: sweetgrass basket

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Maintained in family homes and displayed at open-air basket markets in Mt. Pleasant and Charleston.

## Load Bearing

It represents the preservation of Gullah sweetgrass weaving techniques, the connection to West African basketry, and the artistic representation of coastal history.

## Apparent

A circular, hand-coiled basket constructed of light green sweetgrass, brown pine needles, and split palmetto leaf bindings.

## Yearbook

- Woven and preserved by basket maker [Elijah](persona_elijah.md) in his coastal workshop.
