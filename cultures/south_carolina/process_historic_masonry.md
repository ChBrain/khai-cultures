# Process: historic masonry

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Historic preservation masons, restoration architects, and masonry apprentices, conducted during the dry spring and summer seasons.

## Direction

At the historic building site. Scraping damaged cement mortar, mixing traditional sand and lime mortar, wetting historic handmade bricks, and repointing brick joints.

## Lever

The traditional preservation process that maintains the architectural integrity of old port cities. It supports specialized preservation jobs and heritage tourism, though it requires specialized materials and cannot be conducted during freezing weather.

## Echo

The masonry restoration requires material consistency. The process defines the daily work of [Charlotte](persona_charlotte.md) in Charleston, while basket weaver [Elijah](persona_elijah.md) respects the method as an urban counterpart to detailed coiled structures.
