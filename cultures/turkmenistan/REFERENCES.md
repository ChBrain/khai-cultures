# Turkmenistan: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical and cultural
sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_tk.md](pitch_tk.md) is written from Turkmenistan's profile on
Geert Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no
number. Published country-level data for Turkmenistan is limited; the figures
below are approximate, drawn from regional Central Asian estimates, and are
recorded here so the data is on the record without entering the prose.

| Dimension                     | Turkmenistan score (approx., regional estimate) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |

| Individualism                 | 25                                              |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 50                                              |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the six-dimensions model of national
culture), with Central Asian regional estimates where direct country data is not
published. Scores are approximate and used only to set the tone, never quoted in
the prose.
