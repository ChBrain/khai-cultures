# Rhineland-Palatinate (Rheinland-Pfalz): a khai culture

Rhineland-Palatinate staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-RP, language de. The state of wine, Roman antiquity, and democratic awakenings: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its viniculture and historical depth encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_rhineland_palatinate.md](play_rhineland_palatinate.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_rhineland_palatinate.md](pitch_rhineland_palatinate.md), the Rhineland-Palatinate key.
- **Plots (history):** the Gutenberg printing press in 1440, the Hambach Festival of 1832, and the development of the wine regions.
- **Personas:** Jakob (traditional winemaker archetype) and Clara (modern print/digital media scholar), written in German.
- **Positions:** das demokratische Erbe (democratic heritage), der Winzerstolz (winemaker pride), die Antike (Roman antiquity).
- **Places:** die Porta Nigra (Roman gate in Trier), die Weinberge (vineyards of Moselle/Rhine).
- **Processes:** die Weinlese (grape harvest).
- **Pieces:** die Gutenberg-Presse (printing press), die Hambacher Fahne (Hambach flag).
- **Plan:** der nachhaltige Weinbau (sustainable viticulture plan).
