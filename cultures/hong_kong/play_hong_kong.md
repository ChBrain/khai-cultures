# Play: Hong Kong

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the house that holds this staging and answers for it.

## Name

Hong Kong, a territory of some seven million on the south coast of China where the Pearl River meets the sea, one island, a peninsula, and a scatter of outlying islands gathered around one of the deepest harbours on earth. ISO HK, a Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China, with Chinese and English as its official languages and [Cantonese](position_language_yue_hk.md) the mother tongue of its streets. Ceded a barren rock, grown into a global port and financial centre, handed back after a century and a half as a place promised to keep its own way of life - small in land, vast in reach, a city that was always somebody's and never quite anybody's but its own.

## Arc

A place that began as what the Qing court called a barren rock and the British Empire called a free harbour: ceded island by island and lease by lease after the Opium Wars, made a colonial entrepot where the trade of China passed out to the world and the world's goods passed in. When the mainland convulsed, wave after wave of refugees crossed the border with nothing, and out of that nothing - a spare room, a borrowed sewing machine, a stall - they built a manufacturing city and then a financial one, in a single lifetime, under the [Lion Rock](position_lion_rock_spirit.md) that watched over the tenement roofs. It grew a way of life all its own: [Cantonese](position_language_yue_hk.md) in the mouth and the market, English in the courtroom and the boardroom, the [entrepot's](position_the_entrepot.md) instinct for the deal and the exit, and a refugee's knowledge that everything is borrowed and nothing is owed. In 1997 the colony was returned to China under [one country, two systems](position_one_country_two_systems.md), promised its own courts, its own money, and its own freedoms unchanged for fifty years - and the decades since have been the long, contested test of what that promise means and who gets to say. Under it all runs one nerve: the sense of a place on borrowed time and borrowed ground that answered its own precariousness by hustling, by staying nimble, by keeping one bag packed and one eye on the harbour, and that came, against every expectation of a port meant only to be passed through, to love itself as a home.

## Triggers

**[Plot 1: the cession and the free port](plot_01_cession.md)**

After the Opium Wars the barren rock is ceded to Britain island by island and lease by lease, and turned into a free harbour where the trade of China meets the world; the colonial [entrepot](position_the_entrepot.md) is founded on the water of [Victoria Harbour](place_victoria_harbour.md), the crossroads the whole later city is built upon.

**[Plot 2: the refugees and the factories](plot_02_refuge_and_industry.md)**

As the mainland convulses, refugees pour across the border with nothing and build, out of that nothing, a manufacturing city under the [Lion Rock](position_lion_rock_spirit.md); [the refugee industrialist](persona_the_refugee_industrialist.md) turns a borrowed sewing machine into a factory, and the hustle of the dispossessed becomes the character of the place.

**[Plot 3: the handover (1997)](plot_03_handover.md)**

The colony is returned to China under [one country, two systems](position_one_country_two_systems.md), promised its own courts, money, and freedoms unchanged for fifty years; [Chris Patten](persona_chris_patten.md), the last governor, hands over a city anxious about who will now answer for its way of life.

**[Plot 4: the contested autonomy](plot_04_the_contested_autonomy.md)**

Through the decades after the handover the meaning of the fifty-year promise is tested again and again, in the streets and the courts and the ballot; [the young Hongkonger](persona_the_young_hongkonger.md) comes of age inside that unresolved question of what the city is and what it is allowed to be.

## Stakes

Whether a place built by people who came with nothing and expected to stay only until the next boat can hold on to the home it made almost by accident; whether the [Cantonese](position_language_yue_hk.md) tongue, the [entrepot's](position_the_entrepot.md) nimble freedoms, and the rule of law that made it what it is survive the long test of [one country, two systems](position_one_country_two_systems.md); whether the [Lion Rock](position_lion_rock_spirit.md) grit that turned refugees into a city can outlast the ease and the doubt of the generations born after the struggle - or whether a borrowed place on borrowed time, always half-ready to leave, quietly becomes something other than itself before the fifty years are out.
