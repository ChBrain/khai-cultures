# Persona: the refugee industrialist

## Taxonomy

[the Lion Rock spirit](position_lion_rock_spirit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

The archetype of the wave after wave of refugees, Shanghainese or Cantonese, who crossed the border from a convulsing mainland with nothing to their name but the clothes they stood in and a trade half remembered from a life left behind, and who set about rebuilding that life from zero on a rock that owed them nothing. He starts with one borrowed sewing machine in a rented room or a rooftop shack, works it himself before he can pay anyone else to, and turns bolt after bolt of cheap cloth, and later a run of plastic flowers or a crate of transistor toys, into the first small factory of a city that will one day be a manufacturing power. His own tongue is the Cantonese of the tenement stairwell and the factory floor, [廣東話](position_language_yue_hk.md), which he [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) and [thinks](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) in as the only ground he never has to translate himself out of, and he picks up just enough [Hong Kong English](position_language_en_hk.md), [worn](process_speaking_worn.md) like a suit kept for visitors, to quote a price to an overseas buyer or satisfy a bank manager who will lend in no other tongue. Every crate he ships out is [the entrepot](position_the_entrepot.md) made small, the port's whole living turned into a trade: to stand in the middle between the mainland's cheap hands and the world's open wallets, and take the margin the two sides cannot reach without him. He holds [the Hongkonger](position_culture_hongkonger.md) as his own, the borrowed home he built with his hands rather than the one he was born to.

## Action

In [the refugees and the factories](plot_02_refuge_and_industry.md) he is the one who turns the flood of the dispossessed into the character of a city, staking a borrowed sewing machine against a future no one guaranteed him and hiring the next refugee off the boat before the ink on his own lease is dry. By multiplying his single machine into a workshop and his workshop into a factory, he gives the colony the manufacturing base and the working creed, endure, save, never wait to be asked, that becomes [the Lion Rock spirit](position_lion_rock_spirit.md) itself.

## Shadow

The same refusal to wait on anyone's permission that built the factory floor also runs it: the long hours he demanded of himself he demands of everyone under his roof, and a workforce paid in survival wages learns the same lesson he did, that the ladder is climbed alone. He hoards what he makes against a departure he still half expects, and a son who grew up in comfort finds a father who trusts no bank, no government, and barely his own good fortune.

## Tell

He keeps the first sewing machine long after the factory has thirty more, oiled and working in a corner of the office nobody is allowed to move, beside a suitcase packed by the door that has not been unpacked since the year he arrived.
