# Plot: the contested autonomy

## Taxonomy

[play_hong_kong](play_hong_kong.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

The handover promises fifty years of [one country, two systems](position_one_country_two_systems.md), but a promise of that length has to be read again at every turn, and different people read it differently. In late 2003, as the territory works through the aftermath of the SARS outbreak and a large public march against proposed national security legislation, [Anita Mui](persona_anita_mui.md) gives her farewell concert and dies of cancer weeks later, closing out the colonial-era generation of Cantonese pop even as a new, more openly contested chapter of the territory's politics opens. The decades that follow carry that same open question forward: what exactly the autonomy guaranteed in 1997 covers, and who has the final word on where its limits sit.

## Action

Across the years that follow, the question is tested repeatedly and through different channels: in street demonstrations of varying size and character, in court cases over the scope of the Basic Law and the rights it guarantees, and in elections and electoral reforms that reshape how the territory's legislature and chief executive are chosen. [The young Hongkonger](persona_the_young_hongkonger.md) comes of age inside this unresolved question, inheriting a city whose courts, currency, and civil liberties were promised as distinct from the mainland's, and growing up watching each side, central authorities, the territory's government, and various parts of the public, argue over what that distinctness is meant to mean in practice. No single episode in this period settles the matter; each round of protest, litigation, or legislation becomes another data point in a debate about autonomy that remains open rather than resolved.

## Stage

- [the young Hongkonger](persona_the_young_hongkonger.md): the generation that grows up inside the unresolved question of what the fifty-year promise covers.
- [Anita Mui](persona_anita_mui.md): the farewell and death that close the colonial-era generation of Cantonese pop as this contested era opens.
- [one country, two systems](position_one_country_two_systems.md): the fifty-year formula whose exact meaning and limits are the subject under test throughout this period.

## Tension

The stakes of this era are read differently by different people, and this plot does not adjudicate between them: some see a guaranteed way of life under strain, others see a necessary reassertion of sovereignty within terms always subject to interpretation, and both readings can be found, argued in good faith, among people who live in the territory. What is not in dispute is that the question itself, how much autonomy the handover actually promised and how long that promise can be expected to hold, remains open and contested well past the point anyone in 1997 might have expected it to be settled.
