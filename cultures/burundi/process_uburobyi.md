# Process: uburobyi bwo mw'ijoro

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Abarobyi bo ku [Kiyaga Tanganyika](place_tanganyika.md), abagabo bafata amato yabo iyo bwije, bakajamwo amatara. Baza bagasunika amato ku mazi, bagakwiragiza ku kiyaga, bagatega amatara ngo ndagara n'amafi ye azane ku rumuri. Bakora ijoro ryose, bagataha mu gitondo bafise amafi bafashe, ndagara ikanikwa ku zuba ku nkombe.

## Direction

Aja ku gukura ubuzima mu mazi ya kera: uburobyi ni ubuzima bw'abantu bo ku nkombe, kandi ni ubwiza bw'igihugu. Iyo bwije, amatara y'abarobyi amurika ku mazi nk'inyenyeri zimanutse, ishusho idasanzwe y'abantu bakorera ku kiyaga. Ni akazi karuhije ariko gatanga ubuzima, kandi gahuza abantu n'ikiyaga ca kera kibaha amafi.

## Lever

Bituruka ku butunzi bw'amazi ya kera y'ikiyaga kimwe mu vyimbitse cane mu isi, n'ubuhinga bwo kuroba bw'abasokuru. Bihagarara imbere y'amazi yimbitse n'ijoro rirerire; ariko hari ubwoba bw'uko amafi yogabanuka, n'uko ikiyaga gisaba kubungabungwa ngo gikomeze gutanga ubuzima.

## Echo

Uburobyi bwo mw'ijoro bugaragaza ubuzima bw'abantu bo ku [Kiyaga Tanganyika](place_tanganyika.md): amatara amurika ku mazi nk'inyenyeri, ishusho y'amahoro y'abantu bakorera ku kiyaga ca kera gitanga ubuzima ku gihugu.
