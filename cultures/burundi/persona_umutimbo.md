# Persona: umutimbo

## Taxonomy

[inka](position_inka.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Umuvuza w'ingoma wo ku musozi, uwakuze mu muco w'ingoma n'inka, uwuvuza ingoma akabungabunga umutima w'igihugu. Avuza ingoma hamwe n'abandi batimbo, bagateramira ku mutwe ingoma, bagataburuka bagavuza, bagatemba bagasimbagurika, ijwi ry'ingoma rikuzura imisozi. Akunda inka z'amahembe maremare, akazihimbira mu bivugo, kandi akubaha abakuru n'umuco w'iwabo. Ikirundi rw'iwabo ni rwo afise, [aravuga](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) kandi [aratekereza](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) mu rurimi rumwe rusangiwe na bose, ategura ibivugo by'ingoma n'iby'inka mu kanwa, kuko ubwo buhinga bwose bubikwa mu kanwa atari mu gitabo. Ubwo buhinga [aratangwa zigatangwa](process_speaking_carried.md) kuva ku batimbo ba kera gushika kuri we, akabushikiriza abana. Mugabo igihe avuza ingoma, [aragumana ikintu kitavugwa](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md): ijwi ry'ingoma rivuga ico amajambo adashobora kuvuga, kandi haba hari icara hagati y'ico umutima wiwumva n'ico ururimi rushobora gushikana.

## Action

Mu [ngoma nk'umutima w'igihugu](plot_03_ingoma_umutima.md) avuza ingoma ku misozi gushika i Gitega, ahindura [inka](position_inka.md) n'imisozi n'ingoma umutima w'igihugu ukomeza kuvuga. Mu kuvuza ingoma no guhimbira inka, abungabunga umutima w'igihugu.

## Shadow

Umuvuza w'ingoma uba ku musozi ashobora kubona urwaruka rugiye kure y'umuco w'ingoma n'inka, abana bakurikira isi yugurutse. Ubuhinga bwo kuvuza ingoma no guhimbira inka bushobora guhomba igihe abatimbo ba kera bagiye, n'umurage wabo utabashikiriza abana b'ejo.

## Tell

Avuza ingoma ku musozi, ijwi ryayo rigakuzura ikibanza cose, kandi inka ziwe akazihimbira mu bivugo, intahe y'uko umutima w'igihugu utahwema kuvuga.
