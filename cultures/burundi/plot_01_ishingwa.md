# Plot: ishingwa ry'ingoma

## Taxonomy

[play_burundi](play_burundi.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Mu bihe vya kera, imisozi y'u Burundi yari ituwe n'abantu basangiye ururimi rumwe n'umuco umwe, ariko itararakomera munsi y'ubwami bumwe. Mu nkuru zibikwa mu kanwa k'abasokuru, [Ntare Rushatsi](persona_ntare.md) araza ngo ahuze imisozi munsi y'ingoma imwe.

## Action

[Ntare Rushatsi](persona_ntare.md) ashinga ubwami bw'u Burundi, ahuza imisozi munsi y'ubutegetsi bumwe. Ashira ingoma nk'umutima w'igihugu n'ikiranga umwami: ijambo "ingoma" rikaba risobanura ingoma n'ubutegetsi icarimwe. Ingoma yera iba ikiranga ubwami, ifise abayivuza, ibikwa mu cubahiro. Igihugu kiba gifise umutima utera n'ijwi rihuza abantu bose. Aya mateka yose abikwa mu kanwa, akagenda kuva ku basokuru gushika kuri twe nk'umurage.

## Stage

- [Ntare Rushatsi](persona_ntare.md): umwami wa mbere ashinga ubwami kandi ashira ingoma nk'umutima w'igihugu.
- [ingoma](position_ingoma.md): umutima w'igihugu washizwe hagati y'ubwami, ihuza abantu kandi imenyekanisha umwami.
- [Gitega](place_gitega.md): hagati y'igihugu aho ubwami n'ingoma bizoshinga imizi.

## Tension

Igihugu kivuka gifise umutima umwe: imisozi isangiye ururimi rumwe ihurira munsi y'ingoma imwe. Ariko amateka y'iyi ntango abikwa mu kanwa atari mu vyandiko, agahindagurika, agaca aba inkuru kuruta ukuri kw'icara. Ico kitavuga uburyarya ni iki: ingoma yashizwe hagati y'igihugu mu ntango, kandi iguma ari umutima waco utera gushika uyu musi.
