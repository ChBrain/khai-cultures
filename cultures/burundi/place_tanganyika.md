# Place: Ikiyaga Tanganyika

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Ikiyaga kinini c'u Burundi, kimwe mu biyaga vya kera kandi vyimbitse cane mu isi yose. Kiri ku ruhande rw'iburengerazuba bw'igihugu, Bujumbura igisagara kinini iri ku nkombe zaco. Amazi yaco yimbitse cane abamwo amafi menshi, kandi abarobyi bafata ndagara mw'ijoro. Iyo bwije, amatara y'abarobyi amurika ku mazi nk'inyenyeri zimanutse, ishusho idasanzwe y'abantu bakorera ku kiyaga ca kera. Ni ubutunzi bw'amazi, ubuzima, n'ubwiza bw'igihugu.

## Holds

- [ingoma nk'umutima w'igihugu](plot_03_ingoma_umutima.md): ikiyaga gitanga ubuzima ku gihugu mu gihe umutima w'umuco ukomeza ku misozi.
- [Ikirundi n'umuziki mu isi](plot_04_isi.md): ubwiza bw'ikiyaga n'igihugu bujana n'ijwi ry'imisozi mu isi.

## Offers

Amazi atanga ubuzima, amafi, n'ubwiza; ahantu aho abarobyi bakora mw'ijoro, amatara yabo akamurika nk'inyenyeri; ubutunzi bw'amazi ya kera abamwo ubuzima budasanzwe; n'ubwiza buha igihugu ishusho y'amahoro hagati y'amazi n'imisozi.

## Withheld

Ubworoherane bw'amazi ata gufuhira. Tanganyika ni ikiyaga kinini gisangiwe n'ibihugu vyinshi, kandi ubutunzi bwaco busaba kubungabungwa. Ihagarara ngo yibutse igihugu ko ubuzima bw'amazi n'ubwiza bwaco ari ubutunzi busaba ko abantu bayitwararika, atari ikintu co gukoreshwa nabi gusa.
