# Place: Gitega

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Umurwa mukuru w'u Burundi, uri hagati y'igihugu ku misozi miremire. Ni ho ubwami bwategekeye, ni ho ingoma zera zabikwa mu cubahiro, kandi ni hagati y'igihugu aho abatimbo bo ku misozi bashika. Igisagara kiri hagati y'imisozi y'icayi n'ikawa, hafi y'ahantu hera h'ingoma. Ni hagati y'umutima w'igihugu, aho amateka y'ubwami n'umuco w'ingoma vyashinze imizi.

## Holds

- [ishingwa ry'ingoma](plot_01_ishingwa.md): hagati y'igihugu aho ubwami n'ingoma bishinga imizi.
- [ingoma ya Mwezi Gisabo](plot_02_gisabo.md): hagati y'igihugu aho umwami akomeye ategeka kandi ingoma zibikwa.
- [ingoma nk'umutima w'igihugu](plot_03_ingoma_umutima.md): hagati y'igihugu aho ingoma zera zabikwa kandi abatimbo bashika.

## Offers

Ahantu hagati y'igihugu aho umutima w'ubwami n'ingoma washinze imizi; ikibanza aho amateka y'igihugu, ingoma zera, n'icubahiro c'umwami vyahurira; ahantu hera aho ingoma zabikwa, abatimbo bavuza, kandi umuco w'igihugu ushinga imizi hagati y'imisozi.

## Withheld

Ubworoherane bw'ahantu hata mateka. Gitega ibika amateka y'ubwami n'ingoma, atari nk'ikintu c'ubwoba gusa ahubwo nk'umurage usaba kubungabungwa. Ihagarara ngo yibutse igihugu ko ingoma zera n'amateka y'ubwami ari ubutunzi busaba icubahiro, atari ikintu co kugurishwa gusa.
