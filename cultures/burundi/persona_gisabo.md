# Persona: Mwezi Gisabo

## Taxonomy

[ingoma](position_ingoma.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Umwami akomeye w'u Burundi mu kinjana ca cumi n'icenda, uwategetse igihe kirekire kandi yaharaniye ubumwe bw'igihugu n'icubahiro c'ingoma. Yari umunyacubahiro, umunyabwenge, kandi umwami wubahwa cane mu gihugu cose. Yategekeye hagati y'imisozi, abami n'abaganwa bamukikuje, kandi ingoma zera zibikwa mu cubahiro mu bisanzu vyiwe. [Ikirundi](position_language_rn.md), ururimi rumwe rusangiwe na bose, yari afise neza, [aravuga](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) kandi [aratekereza](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) mu rurimi rw'iwabo, ategeka n'umugani n'ijambo rifise uburemere, akoresha ubuhinga bwo kuvuga nk'igikoresho c'ubutegetsi. Inkuru ziwe [aratangwa zigatangwa](process_speaking_carried.md) mu kanwa k'abasokuru. Umwami yamenyekanira mu cubahiro c'ingoma, mugabo na we [aragumana ikintu kitavugwa](process_hearing_followed.md): mu bihe vyiwe isi yo hanze yariko irisuka, kandi haba hari icara hagati y'ico yashaka kuvuga n'ico amajambo ashobora gushikana, kuko ururimi rugira aho rugarukira.

## Action

Mu [ngoma ya Mwezi Gisabo](plot_02_gisabo.md) ategeka i Gitega abami b'u Burundi, akomeza ubumwe bw'igihugu n'icubahiro c'[ingoma](position_ingoma.md). Mu kubungabunga ubwami n'ingoma, akomeza umutima w'igihugu mu gihe kigoye c'amateka.

## Shadow

Umwami yashaka gukomeza ubumwe bw'igihugu yahuye n'ibihe bigoye, isi yo hanze yariko irisuka. Icubahiro c'umwami n'ingufu z'ubwami vyari binini, mugabo amateka y'ico gihe abikwa mu cubahiro, ata gushira imbere ububabare canke gufata uruhande.

## Tell

Akomeza ingoma n'ubumwe bw'igihugu mu bihe bigoye, asiga umurage w'umwami yaharaniye ko umutima w'igihugu utahwera.
