# Persona: Ntare Rushatsi

## Taxonomy

[ingoma](position_ingoma.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Umwami wa mbere w'u Burundi mu nkuru z'abasokuru, uwashinze ingoma y'igihugu kandi atangiza umuryango w'abami. Mu nkuru zibikwa mu kanwa, ni we yahuje imisozi munsi y'ubwami bumwe ashira ingoma nk'umutima w'igihugu. Yari umunyabwenge, umunyacubahiro, kandi umushizi w'intango. Ikirundi yari afise mu kanwa, [aravuga](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) kandi [aratekereza](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) mu rurimi rw'iwabo, ategeka n'umugani n'ijambo rifise uburemere, kuko muri ico gihe nta gitabo cariho, amategeko n'amateka vyose bibikwa mu kanwa. Inkuru ziwe [aratangwa zigatangwa](process_speaking_carried.md) zigafata urugendo mu kanwa k'abasokuru gushika kuri twe. Ni we washize intango, mugabo amateka yiwe nyene [aragumana ikintu kitavugwa](process_hearing_followed.md): inkuru zibikwa mu kanwa zihindagurika, kandi haba hari icara hagati y'ico yabaye n'ico amajambo ashobora kuvuga, kuko ururimi rugira aho rugarukira.

## Action

Mu [ishingwa ry'ingoma](plot_01_ishingwa.md) ashinga ubwami bw'u Burundi kandi ashira [ingoma](position_ingoma.md) nk'umutima w'igihugu. Mu gushira ingoma hagati y'igihugu, aha u Burundi umutima utera n'ijwi rihuza abantu bose munsi y'ubwami bumwe.

## Shadow

Umwami wa mbere uba mu nkuru atari mu vyandiko ni ikintu kigoye: amateka yiwe abikwa mu kanwa, agahindagurika, agaca aba inkuru kuruta ukuri kw'icara. Igishushanyo c'umwami uwo ari we cose kibikwa mu kanwa gishobora kuba kinini kuruta umuntu nyamukuru, kandi haba hari icara hagati y'inkuru n'ukuri kwayo.

## Tell

Ashira ingoma hagati y'igihugu, kandi ijwi ryayo riguma rivuga gushika uyu musi, intahe y'uko umutima yashize utigeze uhwera.
