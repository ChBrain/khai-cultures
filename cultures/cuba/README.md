# Cuba: a khai culture

Cuba staged as a full khai play, ISO CU, language es. An island of immense
culture and stubborn ingenuity, split between two shores, as a theatre: its
history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its music and memory
encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a
single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_cuba.md](play_cuba.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_es.md](pitch_es.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Cuba's
  profile (a warm, fast, present-facing collectivism, the choteo humour over real
  hardship, pride and resourcefulness) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Colony and Sugar, the Wars of Independence, the
  Revolution and the Century, and the Island and its Diaspora - each casting the
  elements it needs, and each staging the gap between the promise and the reality
  soberly and on the public record.
- **Personas:** Jose Marti, Antonio Maceo, Nicolas Guillen, and Celia Cruz as the
  historic cast, plus one defining archetype, el guajiro (the rural campesino of
  the tobacco and cane country, root of the son montuno). Each is wired to the
  language engine by the language-crossing it holds, all in Spanish, the island's
  mother tongue.
- **Positions:** la patria y Cuba libre (Marti's ideal of a free and sovereign
  island), el son y lo afrocubano (the Afro-Cuban rhythmic soul, son and rumba and
  mestizaje), la isla y la diaspora (the island and its diaspora, the divided
  family, and the ingenuity of "resolver").
- **Places:** La Habana / Havana (the Malecon, Habana Vieja, the weathered
  capital), Santiago de Cuba (the Afro-Caribbean east, cradle of son and of the
  nation's risings).
- **Processes:** la rumba (the Afro-Cuban drum-and-dance gathering of the solar),
  el Carnaval de Santiago (the great eastern carnival of congas and comparsas).
- **Pieces:** el habano / the cigar (the hand-rolled cigar of the Vuelta Abajo
  leaf), el almendron / the vintage car (the 1950s American car kept running for
  decades, emblem of ingenuity and time held still).

## Provenance

The Hofstede framing behind the pitch and the public-record historical sources
are recorded in [REFERENCES.md](REFERENCES.md). The data never enters the prose;
the pitch is its invisible hand. Sources on the public record are credited, never
claimed; slavery, the wars of independence, the 1959 revolution and the long
standoff, and the diaspora and divided families are treated soberly, taking no
side; the staging and the architecture are original work.
