# Persona: Sam

## Taxonomy

[tech optimism](position_innovation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A thirty-six-year-old tech entrepreneur in Palo Alto. English, the fast [Californian English](position_language_en_us_ca.md) of the tech campuses, is his mother tongue, the language in which he [speaks, hears, and thinks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), the primary code of his life. He speaks no foreign languages. Yet the loss lies in the seam: The slower, nature-bound agricultural traditions of winegrowers like Chloe and the deep connection to the land [he reads only as abstract metrics](process_reading_followed.md) on his dashboard, feeling that the experience of the physical world is being digitized into data streams.

## Action

He designs software for [the California water plan](plan_water_conservation.md), utilizes hardware built on the [silicon wafer](piece_wafer.md), and pursues the promise of [the California Dream](position_california_dream.md).

## Shadow

His belief in technological solutions can blind him to the social displacement caused by gentrification and the environmental limits of high-energy computation.

## Tell

He pitches to investors with high energy, saying: "We aren't just writing code, we are building the algorithmic tools to solve the planet's resource limits before they solve us."
