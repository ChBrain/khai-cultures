# Position: tech optimism

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The belief in disruptive innovation, digital progress, and venture-backed problem solving. It possesses the culture of failure-tolerance, rapid iteration, and high-tech efficiency.

## Orders

That one embraces technological change, challenges traditional processes, and seeks market-scale solutions for human needs.

## Loses

Its moral ground when algorithms perpetuate bias, high-tech campuses gentrify neighborhoods, or companies ignore their huge carbon footprint.

## Drives

Guides the industry in the plot [The Silicon Valley Boom 1970](plot_03_silicon_valley_boom.md) and drives the startup work of the entrepreneur who bets a venture on a disruptive idea before the market has proven it out.
