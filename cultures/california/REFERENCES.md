## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_california.md](pitch_california.md) is written from California's profile inflected by its history of the gold rush, the entertainment industry, and the Silicon Valley tech revolution, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | California base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 35                                           |
| Individualism                 | 93                                           |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for California's low hierarchy in startups, high tolerance for risk/uncertainty, and strong focus on personal wellness and self-expression).
