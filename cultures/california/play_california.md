## Name

California, the Golden State on the Pacific coast of the United States. ISO US-CA, language en.

## Arc

California has run on the same trick since 1848: strike it rich, reinvent yourself, and bet the next big one is still coming, whether that means gold, a movie deal, or a Series A. The rush at Sutter's Mill built a state out of a territory in one season; Hollywood turned the same restlessness into a global picture business; Silicon Valley turned it again into circuits and code. It treats change as the whole point and personal freedom as the terms of the deal, even while counting its dry summers and waiting, as it always has, for the next big one - earthquake or IPO.
