# California: a khai culture

California staged as a full khai play, ISO US-CA, language en. The Golden State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its pioneering, technological, and agricultural life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_california.md](play_california.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_california.md](pitch_california.md), the California key.
- **Plots (history):** the Gold Rush of 1848, the rise of Hollywood in the 1910s, and the Silicon Valley technology boom.
- **Personas:** Sam (Silicon Valley entrepreneur) and Chloe (Napa Valley organic winegrower), written in English.
- **Positions:** the California Dream (reinvention), tech optimism (disruptive innovation).
- **Places:** Hollywood (Los Angeles), Yosemite Valley.
- **Processes:** surfing (beach culture), wine making (Napa Valley).
- **Pieces:** a cinema camera (Hollywood), a silicon wafer (Silicon Valley).
- **Plan:** the California water conservation plan.
