# Plot: The Rise of Hollywood 1910

## Taxonomy

[play_california](play_california.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the early 1910s, independent filmmakers migrate from the East Coast to Southern California, drawn by the mild climate and cheap land.

## Action

The relocation establishes [Hollywood](place_hollywood.md) as the global capital of motion pictures, cementing the state's role as a generator of global myths and dreams. The industry develops new narrative forms. The iconic [cinema camera](piece_cinema_camera.md) becomes the tool of this storytelling age. Sam analyzes the media industry's transition to digital streaming to guide his tech startup investments.

## Stage

- [Hollywood](place_hollywood.md) : the district that became synonymous with the glamour and power of the film industry.
- [the California Dream](position_california_dream.md) : the promise of fame and self-reinvention through the screen.
- [cinema camera](piece_cinema_camera.md) : the physical equipment that captured the early movies and defined visual entertainment.

## Tension

The growth of the studio system establishes monopolies, creating high barriers to entry and generating rigid cultural stereotypes that independent artists must constantly fight.
