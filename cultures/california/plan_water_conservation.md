# Plan: the California water plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and water infrastructure management designed to balance agricultural and urban water needs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Enforce water conservation by restricting urban usage, recycling wastewater, recharging aquifers, and optimizing agricultural irrigation.

## Orders
