# Plot: The Silicon Valley Boom 1970

## Taxonomy

[play_california](play_california.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the 1970s, the concentration of semiconductor companies in Santa Clara Valley sparks the personal computer revolution.

## Action

The digital boom establishes [tech optimism](position_innovation.md) as the dominant philosophy of Silicon Valley, driving global technological change. The mass production of the [silicon wafer](piece_wafer.md) fuels the industry. Sam founds his tech start-up in Palo Alto, designing software that relies on these hardware foundations.

## Stage

- [tech optimism](position_innovation.md) : the belief that technological innovation is the primary driver of human progress.
- [silicon wafer](piece_wafer.md) : the thin slice of semiconductor material on which microchips are built.
- [the California water plan](plan_water_conservation.md) : the infrastructure that must balance the huge water needs of microchip fabrication and agriculture.

## Tension

The rapid growth of the tech industry causes extreme housing crises, gentrification, and severe groundwater contamination from early semiconductor manufacturing plants.
