# Position: the California Dream

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The promise of a fresh start, rapid prosperity, and personal reinvention. It possesses the lure of open land, sunshine, and celebrity, drawing millions to the Pacific edge.

## Orders

That one pursues self-fulfillment, remains open to change, and believes in the possibility of personal growth and success.

## Loses

Its promise when severe inequality, high cost of living, and homelessness lock out all but the wealthy from the dream.
