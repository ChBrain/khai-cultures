# Niger: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain sources behind the plots,
personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_fr.md](pitch_fr.md) is written from Niger's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without
entering the prose. Where a country-specific score is not published, the regional
West African profile is used as the guide.

| Dimension                     | Niger / West Africa score (approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 80                                  |
| Individualism                 | 20                                  |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 46                                  |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 54                                  |
| Long Term Orientation         | 16                                  |
| Indulgence                    | 78                                  |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the Country Comparison tool and the
six-dimensions model of national culture). Scores are approximate, as published by
Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group; where Niger has no standalone score,
the published West African regional profile (high power distance and low
individualism) is used.

## Historical and cultural sources

The plots, the historic personas they cast, the places, and the pieces are drawn
from the public record and from Niger's own cultural self-understanding. They are
credited here and never claimed; the staging is original work. Episodes of
hardship in the country's history are acknowledged soberly and without detail, and
no political side is taken.
