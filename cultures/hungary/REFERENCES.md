## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_hu.md](pitch_hu.md) is written from Hungary's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no
number. The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the
record without entering the prose.

The two defining personas, [Erzsi](persona_erzsi.md) and [Bence](persona_bence.md),
are archetypes, not historical individuals: composites that carry the tension
between the older generation (the melancholy, the kitchen, faith, the mother tongue,
the relatives beyond the border) and the younger one (the Transylvanian Magyar and
the post-2004 emigration, the pull of global English on a kinless language).
