## Shown

Point State Park in Pittsburgh, marked by a water fountain at the tip of the land where the Allegheny and Monongahela rivers join to form the Ohio River.

## Holds

- the industrial memory of steel mills and barges in the plot [The Rise of Steel and Coal 1875](plot_03_steel_and_coal.md) : the gateway to the west.

## Offers

The walking trails and historic fort outlines where [John](persona_john.md) leads industrial history tours.

## Withheld

The industrial dumping that poisoned the rivers. While celebrated today as a clean urban park, the confluence was once lined with factories and toxic discharges, a legacy that remains in the river sediments.
