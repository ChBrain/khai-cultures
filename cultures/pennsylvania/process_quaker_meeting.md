# Process: Quaker meetings

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Pennsylvania Quakers (Society of Friends) and meeting attendees, conducted weekly in meetinghouses across the state.

## Direction

In the meetinghouse. Gathering in silence, sitting in a circle without a pulpit or minister, waiting for the inner light, and speaking only when moved by the spirit.

## Lever

The spiritual practice that encourages quiet reflection, equality, and social justice. It supports consensus-driven community decisions, though it faces low participation among younger generations.

## Echo

The quiet silence offers a space for reflection. It rewards the moral focus of [Clara](persona_clara.md) in Philadelphia, while [John](persona_john.md) respects the method as an early model of peaceful community coordination.
