## Place

Displayed at the Liberty Bell Center in Independence National Historical Park, Philadelphia.

## Load Bearing

It represents the proclamation of independence, the abolitionist movement that adopted it as a symbol, and the national covenant of liberty.

## Apparent

A large bronze bell featuring a famous prominent crack, inscribed with a quote from Leviticus about proclaiming liberty throughout the land.
