# Plot: The Rise of Steel and Coal 1875

## Taxonomy

[play_pennsylvania](play_pennsylvania.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the late 19th century, the expansion of steel mills and coal mines in western Pennsylvania transforms the state into a manufacturing powerhouse.

## Action

The steel production at [Pittsburgh Point](place_pittsburgh_point.md) builds the rails and skyscrapers of the nation, establishing [industrial grit](position_industrial_grit.md) as a core working-class identity. The state becomes the center of heavy industry. The production of the [steel beam](piece_steel_beam.md) represents this industrial age. John worked for decades in the mills, witnessing both their peak and their sudden closure.

## Stage

- [Pittsburgh Point](place_pittsburgh_point.md) : the industrial rivers confluence where the massive steel mills operated.
- [steel beam](piece_steel_beam.md) : the structural steel used to build American cities.
- [steel smelting](process_steel_smelting.md) : the intense industrial process of melting ore to produce structural steel.

## Tension

The rapid industrialization leads to severe labor exploitation, culminating in the bloody Homestead Strike of 1892, and causes long-term environmental pollution in the valleys.
