# Plot: The Declaration of Independence 1776

## Taxonomy

[play_pennsylvania](play_pennsylvania.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the summer of 1776, delegates to the Second Continental Congress meet in Philadelphia to draft and sign the Declaration of Independence.

## Action

The debates in [Independence Hall](place_independence_hall.md) produce the founding text of the United States. The city becomes the political capital of the revolution. The ringing of the [Liberty Bell](piece_liberty_bell.md) announces the new declaration. Clara prepares archival exhibitions that document the draft corrections.

## Stage

- [Independence Hall](place_independence_hall.md) : the red-brick assembly room where the Declaration and Constitution were drafted.
- [Liberty Bell](piece_liberty_bell.md) : the bronze bell that became a symbol of national freedom.
- [religious tolerance](position_tolerance.md) : the legacy of pluralism that allowed delegates from diverse colonies to coordinate.

## Tension

While declaring all men equal, the congress fails to address the institution of slavery, postponing a moral reckoning that would divide the nation.
