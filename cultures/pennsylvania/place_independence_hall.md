# Place: Independence Hall

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The historic red-brick building in Philadelphia, featuring a white steeple, windows, and the Assembly Room where the nation's founding documents were signed.

## Holds

- the political debates in the plot [The Declaration of Independence 1776](plot_02_independence_1776.md) : the birthplace of the nation.

## Offers

The guided tours and archival exhibits where [Clara](persona_clara.md) explains the drafts of the Constitution.

## Withheld

The exclusion of women and enslaved people from early debates. While celebrated as a sanctuary of freedom, the delegates who met here protected the slave trade, a contradiction not emphasized in the tourist presentation.
