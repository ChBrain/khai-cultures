# Place: Birunga

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Ibirunga byo mu majyaruguru y'u Rwanda, imisozi miremire y'ibirunga ihishwa mu gihu, aho hari Pariki y'Igihugu y'Ibirunga. Munsi y'amashyamba aho hatuye ingagi zo mu misozi, izo nyamaswa zikomeye zihishwa mu gihu cy'imisozi. Ku byatsi byo munsi y'ibirunga, abantu batoza inka kandi banga imirima. Ni ahantu h'ubwiza buhambaye: imisozi y'ibirunga, gihu cyo mu gitondo, n'amashyamba abisi.

## Holds

- [Imisozi n'inka](plot_01_imisozi_ninka.md): ibirunga n'imisozi aho abantu bubaka ubuzima kandi batoza inka.
- [Ubwiyunge n'agaciro](plot_04_ubwiyunge_nagaciro.md): ingagi zo mu birunga zikurura abashyitsi igihugu cyongera kwiyubaka.

## Offers

Ahantu h'ubwiza karemano bw'u Rwanda buboneka, aho imisozi y'ibirunga ihagaze nk'ikimenyetso cy'igihugu cy'imisozi, n'aho ingagi zo mu misozi zikurura abashyitsi bo ku isi. Ni urubuga aho karemano k'igihugu kongera kuba ubukungu n'agaciro, kandi aho ubwiza bw'imisozi buhimbazwa.

## Withheld

Ubworoshye bw'ahantu hatagira inkomyi. Birunga byibutsa igihugu ko ubwiza bw'imisozi n'ingagi bugomba kurindwa, ko karemano katari impano iboneka ku buntu ahubwo ari ako kurindwa, kandi ko ubuzima bwo munsi y'ibirunga butegeka ubwitange bwo kongera kubaka buri munsi.
