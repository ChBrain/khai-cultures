# Place: Kigali

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Umurwa mukuru w'u Rwanda, wubatswe ku misozi mu mutima w'igihugu, uzwiho gusukura n'icyatsi kibisi. Imihanda isukuye, imisozi yubatsweho amazu, n'ibibuga by'icyatsi byatewe mu mujyi. Ni umujyi uzamuka uva ku musozi ujya ku wundi, aho umuganda usukura imihanda, aho agaciro k'igihugu kigaragarira mu mucyo n'isuku, kandi aho ijwi ry'u Rwanda rigezweho rivugira.

## Holds

- [Ubwigenge](plot_03_ubwigenge.md): umusozi uhinduka umurwa mukuru w'igihugu gishya cyiyobora ubwacyo.
- [Ubwiyunge n'agaciro](plot_04_ubwiyunge_nagaciro.md): umurwa usukuye kandi w'icyatsi werekana igihugu cyongera kwiyubaka.

## Offers

Ahantu igihugu cyiyoborera kandi cyiyerekana agaciro kacyo mu mucyo n'isuku; urubuga aho umuganda usukura imihanda, aho icyatsi gitewe ku misozi, n'aho ijwi ry'u Rwanda rigezweho rihurira n'umuco gakondo. Umujyi werekana ko agaciro k'igihugu kava mu kazi n'ubwitange bwa buri munsi.

## Withheld

Ubworoshye bw'umujyi udafite amateka. Kigali ikomeza icyubahiro cy'ibyahise bidukuye ku mugaragaro, umujyi uvuga byinshi mu isuku no mu mucyo aho kuvuga ibyababaye, kandi wibutsa igihugu ko isuku n'agaciro ari umurimo wo kongera gukorwa buri munsi, atari impano iboneka rimwe.
