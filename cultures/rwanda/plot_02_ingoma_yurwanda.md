# Plot: Ingoma y'u Rwanda

## Taxonomy

[play_rwanda](play_rwanda.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Mu binyejana byashize, abami b'u Rwanda bahuza imisozi mu ngoma imwe. Ku ngoma ya [Kigeli IV Rwabugiri](persona_rwabugiri.md), igihugu kigura imipaka kandi umuco wa cyami ugera ku rwego rwo hejuru: ku rukiko havugaga abasizi, intore zikabyina, ingoma zikavuga, kandi umuco w'inka uba intango y'icyubahiro.

## Action

Ku rukiko rw'umwami, [Sekarama](persona_sekarama.md) avuga ibisigo by'inka n'iby'abami, ahimbaza ubwiza bw'amahembe maremare mu mutwe nta nyandiko. [Intore](persona_intore.md) zibyina umubyino w'icyubahiro, amaboko yazo akwirakuwe nk'amahembe y'inka mu kirere, ku ijwi ry'ingoma. Imigongo y'amabara igashushanya inkike z'inzu, kandi umuco w'inka ugera ku rwego rwo hejuru. Ingoma ihuza imisozi, ururimi rumwe rukomera, kandi umuco wa cyami ushinga imizi y'ubwiza buzakomeza n'ubu.

## Stage

- [Kigeli IV Rwabugiri](persona_rwabugiri.md): umwami uhuza imisozi akageza umuco w'inka n'intore ku rwego rwo hejuru.
- [Sekarama](persona_sekarama.md): umusizi wa cyami uvuga ibisigo by'inka ku rukiko.
- [Intore](persona_intore.md): abamyinnyi babyina umubyino w'icyubahiro ku rukiko.
- [umuco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md): umuco ugera ku rwego rwo hejuru ku rukiko rw'umwami.

## Tension

Ingoma ihuza imisozi mu gihugu kimwe kandi igeza umuco ku rwego rwo hejuru, ariko ubukuru bw'ingoma bushobora kwibukwa nk'imbaraga gusa, mu gihe ubukuru nyabwo bwari mu muco: mu bisigo by'inka, mu mubyino w'intore, mu ijwi ry'ingoma, n'imigongo y'amabara. Igihugu cyubaka umuco uzakomeza no nyuma y'ingoma.
