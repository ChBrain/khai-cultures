# Persona: Jean-Paul Samputu

## Taxonomy

[agaciro](position_agaciro.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Umuhanzi n'umuririmbyi ukomeye ufite ijwi rikomeye, wagize indirimbo y'imbabazi n'agaciro ikimenyetso cye, ajyana ijwi ry'u Rwanda ku isi. Afite icyubahiro, akomeye mu mutima, kandi yiyemeje kwubaka agaciro k'umuntu binyuze mu ndirimbo, ahimbaza imbaraga zo kongera guhaguruka. [Ikinyarwanda](position_language_rw.md) agifite cyose: [aravuga](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [aratekereza](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), kandi indirimbo nyamukuru [azihimba mu rurimi rwe rw'amavuko](process_writing_mother_tongue.md), kuko ari rwo ruvugamo agaciro k'umutima. Indimi z'amahanga z'urugendo rwe rw'isi [azifata nk'igikoresho cy'akazi](process_speaking_carried.md), kandi [zimwe asoma akurikira gusa](process_reading_followed.md). Ariko iyo agiye ku munyu w'agaciro, asubira mu Kinyarwanda, kuko ijambo ry'imbabazi n'agaciro ridashobora kuvuga mu rundi rurimi nk'uko rivuga mu rwe: aho ururimi rw'amavuko rurangirira, ni naho amagambo y'agaciro y'umutima atangira kubura. Ni uw'[umuco nyarwanda](position_culture_umuco_nyarwanda.md), akawubaka agaciro binyuze mu mbabazi n'indirimbo.

## Action

Muri [Ubwiyunge n'agaciro](plot_04_ubwiyunge_nagaciro.md) abaho nk'ikimenyetso cy'[agaciro](position_agaciro.md), umuhanzi wereka isi ko umunyarwanda ashobora kongera guhaguruka akubaka agaciro ke binyuze mu mbabazi n'indirimbo. Akomeza isezerano ry'agaciro ayisubira mu ijwi rye, ajyana ubupfura bw'u Rwanda ku isi.

## Shadow

Indirimbo y'agaciro ishobora guhindurwa ijambo ridakora, ririririmbwa ngo riryohe aho gukomeza agaciro nyako. Ijwi rishobora gukundwa hanze kurusha uko rikomeza umuntu imbere, agaciro kavugwa mu ndirimbo kakabura mu buzima bwa buri munsi, ngo abumva bibuke ko agaciro gava mu kazi n'ubupfura, atari mu majwi gusa.

## Tell

Aririmba imbabazi mu rurimi rwe rw'amavuko ku buryo n'utumva Ikinyarwanda yumva ko ijwi rivuga agaciro k'umuntu, kandi ahisha mu ndirimbo imbaraga zo kongera guhaguruka.
