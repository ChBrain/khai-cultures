# Plot: Ubwiyunge n'agaciro

## Taxonomy

[play_rwanda](play_rwanda.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Mu bihe bigezweho, igihugu cyongera kwiyubaka cyishingikiriza ku ndangagaciro ebyiri: [ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md) n'[agaciro](position_agaciro.md). Abanyarwanda bahurira mu muganda buri kwezi, [Kigali](place_kigali.md) iba umurwa usukuye kandi w'icyatsi, kandi ijwi ry'u Rwanda rigera ku isi binyuze mu bahanzi.

## Action

Igihugu kiyubaka buhoro buhoro, gikorera hamwe mu muganda, gishyira agaciro k'umuntu n'ak'igihugu imbere. [Cecile Kayirebwa](persona_kayirebwa.md) ajyana Ikinyarwanda ku isi nk'impano y'ubumwe, naho [Jean-Paul Samputu](persona_samputu.md) akubaka agaciro k'umunyarwanda binyuze mu mbabazi n'indirimbo. [Kigali](place_kigali.md) iba umurwa usukuye, icyatsi kibisi ku misozi, ikawa n'icyayi bigatera imbere ku misozi, n'ingagi zo mu birunga bya [Birunga](place_birunga.md) zikurura abashyitsi. Igihugu kigaragaza ko ushobora kongera guhaguruka ahantu waguye.

## Stage

- [Cecile Kayirebwa](persona_kayirebwa.md): umuririmbyikazi ujyana Ikinyarwanda n'ubumwe ku isi.
- [Jean-Paul Samputu](persona_samputu.md): umuhanzi wubaka agaciro k'umunyarwanda binyuze mu ndirimbo.
- [ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md): ishingiro ry'igihugu cyongera kwiyubaka.
- [agaciro](position_agaciro.md): indangagaciro ituma igihugu kwiyemera no kwiyubaka.
- [Kigali](place_kigali.md): umurwa usukuye kandi w'icyatsi werekana igihugu cyongera kubaka.

## Tension

Igihugu cyongera kwiyubaka ku bumwe no ku gaciro, ariko ubumwe bushobora guhinduka ijambo ridakora niba abantu batibutse ko umubano ari uwo kubakwa buri munsi, kandi agaciro gashobora guhinduka ubwirasi niba katavuye mu kazi n'ubupfura. Igihugu gikomeza umurimo udashira: kongera guhaguruka, gukorera hamwe, no kubaka inzu y'abanyarwanda bose nta n'umwe usigaye inyuma.
