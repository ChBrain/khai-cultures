# Rwanda: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain cultural and historical
sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_rw.md](pitch_rw.md) is written from Rwanda's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without
entering the prose.

| Dimension                     | Rwanda score (approx., regional estimate) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 85                                        |
| Individualism                 | 25                                        |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 45                                        |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 55                                        |
| Long Term Orientation         | 35                                        |
| Indulgence                    | 35                                        |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the six-dimensions model of national
culture). Rwanda is not separately published in the core Hofstede dataset; the
figures above are approximate regional estimates for East Africa, used only to
orient the pitch (high orientation toward the group, respect for authority and age,
and restraint) and never quoted in the prose.

## Cultural and historical sources

The plots, the historic personas they cast, the places, and the pieces are drawn
from the public cultural and historical record. They are credited here and never
claimed; the staging is original work. The nation is staged from within its own
self-understanding, through its hills, its language, its cattle culture, its arts,
its dance, and the values of unity and dignity, taking no political side.
