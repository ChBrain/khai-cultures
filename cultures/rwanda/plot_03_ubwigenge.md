# Plot: Ubwigenge

## Taxonomy

[play_rwanda](play_rwanda.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Nyuma y'igihe igihugu kibayeho munsi y'ubutegetsi bw'amahanga, u Rwanda rubona ubwigenge mu mwaka w'igihumbi n'amagana cyenda na mirongo itandatu n'ibiri. Igihugu gitangira kwiyobora ubwacyo, [Kigali](place_kigali.md) iba umurwa mukuru, kandi abanyarwanda bagomba kongera kubaka igihugu cyabo n'imbaraga zabo.

## Action

Igihugu gishya kiyobora ubwacyo, gikomeza ururimi rumwe, Ikinyarwanda, nk'urumuri ruhuza bose. [Kigali](place_kigali.md) ikura iva ku musozi ngo ibe umurwa mukuru w'igihugu. Ariko ikibazo cy'[ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md) bw'abanyarwanda kiba ikibazo gikomeye cy'igihugu gishya: uko abantu bahuriye ku gihugu kimwe bashobora kubaka umubano umwe. Igihugu kibona amahindagurika akomeye y'amateka, ibyababaye bihabwa icyubahiro ariko ntibivugwe ku mugaragaro, kandi inzira yo kongera kubaka ubumwe iba ndende.

## Stage

- [Kigali](place_kigali.md): umusozi uhinduka umurwa mukuru w'igihugu gishya.
- [ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md): ikibazo cy'ubumwe bw'abanyarwanda kiba ikibazo gikomeye cy'igihugu.

## Tension

Igihugu gifite ubwigenge n'ururimi rumwe rwo guhuza bose, ariko gigomba kubaka ubumwe nyabwo hagati y'abantu bacyo, umurimo udashobora kurangira mu munsi umwe. Inzira yo kongera kubaka umubano iba ndende nk'umusozi wurira, ariko igihugu gikomeza ku byiringiro ko ushobora kongera guhaguruka.
