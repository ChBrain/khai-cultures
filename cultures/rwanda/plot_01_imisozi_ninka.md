# Plot: Imisozi n'inka

## Taxonomy

[play_rwanda](play_rwanda.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Mu ntangiriro, abantu batuye ku misozi miremire y'u Rwanda, igihugu cy'imisozi igihumbi aho buri musozi ufite izina. Banga imirima ku mpinga, baca amaterasi ngo ubutaka butajyana n'imvura, kandi batoza inka z'amahembe maremare ku byatsi byo ku misozi no munsi y'ibirunga bya [Birunga](place_birunga.md).

## Action

Abantu bubaka ubuzima ku misozi: imirima ku mpinga, inka mu rwuri, n'inzu zubatswe ku musozi. Inka iba ishingiro ry'ubukungu n'ubwiza, kandi cyane cyane iba impano y'ubucuti: uwakuguriye inka aba akubereye se, kandi umubano ukozwe n'inka ntuvunika. Ururimi rumwe, Ikinyarwanda, ruvugwa ku misozi yose, ruba urumuri ruhuza abatuye imisozi itandukanye. Ku gihu cyo mu gitondo gihishira impinga, umuco w'inka ushinga imizi ngo ube ishingiro ry'igihugu kizaza.

## Stage

- [umuco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md): umubano w'abantu n'inka utangira kuba ishingiro ry'ubwiza n'ubucuti.
- [Birunga](place_birunga.md): ibirunga n'imisozi aho abantu bubaka ubuzima kandi batoza inka.
- [Kigeli IV Rwabugiri](persona_rwabugiri.md): umwami uzaza uzakomeza umuco w'inka washinze imizi kuri iyi misozi.

## Tension

Igihugu kivuka ku misozi yacurwe nk'imihora itagira ingano, aho ubutaka bugomba gucwa amaterasi ngo bukomeze ubuzima, kandi aho inka itari indyo gusa ahubwo ari umubano. Ishingiro ry'igihugu rishyirwaho: ururimi rumwe ruhuza imisozi, n'inka ihuza abantu, ku buryo igihugu kizaza kizahagarara ku bumwe no ku muco w'inka.
