# Plan: u Rwanda rwiyubaka

## Taxonomy

Inshingano ifunguye igihugu cyiyemeje: umurimo udashira wo gukomeza [ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md) bw'abanyarwanda, wo kurinda no kongera [agaciro](position_agaciro.md) k'umuntu n'ak'igihugu, no kubaka ku [muco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md) n'umuco gakondo nk'ishingiro ry'ubwiza n'umubano.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Komeza ubumwe bw'abanyarwanda - urinde ururimi rumwe rwa bose kandi wubake umubano hagati y'abantu buri munsi; rinda agaciro k'umuntu n'ak'igihugu, igihugu kwiyemera kandi kwiyubaka n'imbaraga zacyo; komeza umuco gakondo - inka, intore, ingoma, n'imigongo - nk'ishingiro ry'ubwiza; kandi werekane ko ushobora kongera guhaguruka ahantu waguye, ukubaka inzu y'abanyarwanda bose nta n'umwe usigaye inyuma.

## Orders

- Ku muhanzi nka [Cecile Kayirebwa](persona_kayirebwa.md): jyana Ikinyarwanda n'umuco ku isi, ukomeze ubumwe ubinyujije mu ndirimbo.
- Ku muhanzi nka [Jean-Paul Samputu](persona_samputu.md): ubake agaciro k'umunyarwanda binyuze mu mbabazi n'indirimbo, werekane ko ushobora kongera guhaguruka.
- Ku gihugu: komeza ubumwe n'agaciro, ukorere hamwe mu muganda, kandi ntuhindure ubumwe ijambo ridakora cyangwa agaciro ubwirasi.

## Implementation

Igihugu cyongera kwiyubaka buhoro buhoro, gikorera hamwe mu muganda buri kwezi, gishyira agaciro k'igihugu imbere, kandi gifite umurwa usukuye kandi w'icyatsi. Cyakomeje umuco gakondo - inka, intore, ingoma, imigongo - ariko kigifite imbere yacyo umurimo udashira: kurinda ubumwe nyabwo hagati y'abantu, no kubaka ubukungu ku misozi binyuze mu kawa, icyayi, n'ubukerarugendo bw'ingagi, ku buryo agaciro kava mu kazi gakomeza kuvuka.

## Targets

- [x] Igihugu gifite ururimi rumwe, Ikinyarwanda, ruvugwa na bose hafi ya bose nk'urumuri ruhuza igihugu.
- [x] Umuganda ushyizweho nk'umurimo wo gukorera hamwe igihugu buri kwezi.
- [x] Kigali ihinduka umurwa usukuye kandi w'icyatsi werekana agaciro k'igihugu.
- [W] Ubumwe nyabwo hagati y'abantu bukomeza kubakwa buri munsi.
- [W] Ubukungu bukomeza kubakwa ku misozi binyuze mu kawa, icyayi, n'ubukerarugendo.
- [ ] Komeza umuco gakondo - inka, intore, ingoma, n'imigongo - utawibagiwe mu bihe bigezweho.
- [ ] Werekane ko igihugu, nk'umusozi wurira, gishobora kongera guhaguruka kitarwanyije ubukene.
