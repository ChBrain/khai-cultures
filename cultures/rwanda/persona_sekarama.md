# Persona: Sekarama

## Taxonomy

[umuco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Umusizi wa cyami uzwi cyane, wabaye nk'ububiko bw'ibisigo by'u Rwanda, uvuga ibisigo by'inka n'iby'abami ku rukiko. Afite ubwenge bwo kwibuka ibisigo byinshi mu mutwe, afite icyubahiro cy'umuvugizi wa cyami, kandi azi imvugo igoramye y'imigani n'amagambo y'icyubahiro. Ikinyarwanda agifite ku rwego rwo hejuru: [aravuga](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) kandi [aratekereza](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) mu rurimi rwe rw'amavuko n'imbaraga zituma avuga ibisigo bigoye by'inka mu mutwe, kandi [arasoma](process_reading_followed.md) imvugo y'icyubahiro y'abakurambere akayikomeza. Ibisigo bya kera by'abandi basizi [arabikomeza](process_speaking_carried.md), abikuye mu kanwa k'abamubanjirije. Ariko n'uwabitse ibisigo byose mu mutwe yumva ko hari ubwiza bw'inka budashobora kuvugwa rwose: aho ururimi rw'ibisigo rugera ku mpera yarwo, ni naho ubwiza bw'amahembe maremare butangira kurenga amagambo.

## Action

Muri [Ingoma y'u Rwanda](plot_02_ingoma_yurwanda.md) avuga ibisigo by'inka ku rukiko, akomeza [umuco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md) ku rwego rwo hejuru, ahimbaza ubwiza bw'amahembe n'amabara y'inka. Akomeza umuco ayisubira mu bisigo, ajyana ijwi ry'inka mu mateka y'igihugu.

## Shadow

Igisigo gishobora guhindurwa urwibutso rw'ubukuru gusa, civugwa ngo gisingize aho gukomeza umuco nyawo. Ubuhanga bwo kwibuka ibisigo byinshi mu mutwe bushobora gushira iyo abasizi bashize, ibisigo by'inka bikibagirana, umuco ugahagarara mu nyandiko aho kubaho mu kanwa.

## Tell

Avuga ibisigo by'inka mu mutwe nta nyandiko, agahimbaza umufuka umwe w'amahembe maremare mu magambo magana, ku buryo uwumva yumva ko inka itari indyo gusa ahubwo ari ubwiza n'icyubahiro.
