# Position: agaciro

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Igitekerezo ko umuntu n'igihugu bigira agaciro kabo, kandi ko agaciro ari ako kurindwa no kongerwa, atari ako gutangwa n'abandi. Agaciro ni icyubahiro cy'umuntu, ariko ni n'ubwigenge bwo kwiyemera, ko umunyarwanda ashobora kwiyubaka n'imbaraga ze, akubaha icyo ari cyo, kandi akarinda ko igihugu cye kigira ijwi ryacyo bwite. Ni indangagaciro ibaho, ivugwa mu mvugo ya buri munsi, ko buri muntu agira agaciro ke akagomba kukarinda.

## Orders

Ko umuntu agomba kubaha agaciro ke n'ak'undi, ko igihugu kigomba kwiyubaka kikwiringira aho kwiringira abandi gusa, ko umurimo n'ubwitange ari inzira yo kongera agaciro, kandi ko icyubahiro cy'umuntu kidashobora kugurwa cyangwa gutwarwa, ahubwo cyubakwa buhoro buhoro nk'umusozi wurira.

## Loses

Imbaraga iyo agaciro gahindurwa ubwirasi cyangwa ishyari aho kuba icyubahiro gikora; kandi imbaraga iyo umuntu yiyanga, yumva adafite agaciro, cyangwa agategereza ko abandi bamuha agaciro aho kukubaka we ubwe, ngo yibuke ko agaciro k'umuntu kava mu kazi ke no mu bantu be.

## Drives

Ibaho mu muhanzi ujyana ijwi ry'u Rwanda ku isi akubaka agaciro k'umunyarwanda binyuze mu ndirimbo n'imbabazi. Bituma u Rwanda rukomeza kwiyubaka rukirinda agaciro kacyo, rwiyemera kandi rukubaha icyo ari cyo.
