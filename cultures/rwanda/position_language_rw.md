# Position: Ikinyarwanda

## Owner

## Has

Ikinyarwanda gifite imiterere y'amoko y'amazina (noun classes) igera ku icumi n'abiri, buri rimwe rikaba rifite imigereka ihuriweho n'amazina, mu nteruro yose kuva ku nshinga kugeza ku nshinja no ku mugereka; iyi mikorere ituma ijambo rimwe ry'inshinga rishobora gusimbura interuro yose mu zindi ndimi, kubera imigereka myinshi ihurizwa hamwe: uwavuze, igihe, icyo akora, uwo abikorera, kandi n'uko abikora (bikoreshwa, bikorewe, bisubirwamo). Ijwi (ton) rigira uruhare mu gutandukanya insobanuro: amagambo abiri asa mu nyuguti arashobora gusobanura ibintu bitandukanye bitewe gusa n'uko ijwi rizamutse cyangwa rimanutse. Ururimi rufite kandi ubukungu bw'amagambo y'ibisimba, cyane cyane ay'inka: buri bara, buri gishushanyo cy'umubiri w'inka rifite izina ryihariye, naho ibisigo n'icyivugo bikoresha imvugo isubiramo (ideophone) n'imigani igufi ihishe ubwenge burebure.

## Orders

Rusaba ko buri zina rihabwa icyiciro cyaryo (umuntu, ikintu, ahantu, igihe...), kandi ko ibindi bice by'interuro (inshinga, ibisobanuro) byumvikana n'icyo cyiciro; ntawe wemerewe kuvuga izina atarishyize mu murongo w'icyiciro cyaryo. Ku nshinga, rusaba ko buri gihe hagaragara mu ijambo rimwe uwavuze, igihe (ubu, ejo, kera), niba ari ukwemeza cyangwa guhakana, kandi impamvu (ni uko abikoze ku bw'undi, cyangwa yabikoreye ubwe). Rusaba kandi ko ijwi rivugwa neza, kuko guhindura uburebure cyangwa uko ijwi rizamuka bihindura insobanuro y'ijambo rimwe na rimwe burundu.

## Loses

Ijambo rigufi ryo mu rundi rurimi ridashobora kubaho mu Kinyarwanda uko riri: rigomba kwambikwa igiciro (préfixe) cy'icyiciro cy'amazina, bikaba bitewe n'imiterere y'icyo kintu mu Kinyarwanda, ikaba idahuye buri gihe n'iyo mu rundi rurimi. Ururimi kandi ntirworohera uwatojwe indimi z'i Burayi kongera guhinga inshinga imwe irimo ibisobanuro byinshi icyarimwe (nk'uko rubigenza), maze insobanuro zimwe zigasigara zumvikana gusa mu ijwi ridashobora kwandikwa neza mu nyuguti zisanzwe. Kandi imigani migufi n'ibisigo bibitswe mu kanwa birashobora kubura iyo batabivuze kenshi, kuko ubwiza bwabyo buba mu majwi n'uburyo busubirwamo, atari mu nyandiko yonyine.

## Drives

Utekereza akoresheje Ikinyarwanda ahora yubaka interuro imwe ikubiyemo byinshi: uwavuze, igihe, uko yabigenje, n'impamvu, byose bihuriye ku nshinga imwe, bigatuma yiga guhuza ibintu byinshi mu ijambo rimwe mbere yo kuvuga. Amenya ko ijwi ari ingenzi nk'inyuguti ubwazo, bigatuma yumva neza akongera akita ku majwi mu magambo. Kongera akorana n'imigani n'ibisigo bimwibutsa ko ubwenge n'amateka bishobora kubikwa mu kanwa gusa, atari mu nyandiko, none akongera kwita cyane ku ijambo rivuzwe neza n'uburyo rivugwa, kurusha uko yakwita ku nyandiko yanditse.
