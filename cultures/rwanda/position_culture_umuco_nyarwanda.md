# Position: umuco nyarwanda

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Kuba umwe mu bantu bo ku misozi igihumbi, aho hafi bose bavuga ururimi rumwe, [Ikinyarwanda](position_language_rw.md), impano idasanzwe ihuza igihugu ku isi. Ufite [ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md), igitekerezo ko abanyarwanda ari rumwe kandi bagomba kubaka umubano wabo buri munsi mu muganda; ufite [agaciro](position_agaciro.md), icyubahiro cy'umuntu n'ubwigenge bwo kwiyubaka atari ukwitega ku bandi; kandi ufite [umuco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md), umubano wa kera hagati y'abantu n'inka z'amahembe maremare, impano y'ubucuti idashobora kuvunika. Ufite kandi imigongo n'ibisigo by'abasizi bo ku rukiko rw'umwami, imvugo ngo umuntu ni umuntu ku bantu, n'amateka y'imisozi yahuwe mu ngoma imwe, hanyuma agacikamo, hanyuma agasubira kwiyubaka.

## Orders

Ko buri munyarwanda agomba kubaha agaciro ke n'ak'undi, adasuzugura uwo ari we wese; ko umurimo w'igihugu ari uwo gukorera hamwe mu muganda, atari uwo kwifashisha wenyine cyangwa kwitega ko abandi bakubakira; ko inka n'imigani yayo bikomeza kwubahwa nk'ishingiro ry'ubwiza n'ubupfura, atari nk'ubukungu bupimwa mu mafaranga gusa; kandi ko ubumwe bugomba kubakwa buri munsi mu ngiro, atari kuvugwa mu magambo gusa akiryoshya.

## Loses

Imbaraga ubwo ubumwe buhindurwa ijambo ridakora, ivugwa ngo riryohe aho gukomeza umubano nyawo hagati y'abantu; ubwo agaciro gahindurwa ubwirasi cyangwa ishyari aho kuba icyubahiro gikora buri munsi; kandi ubwo umuco w'inka n'ibisigo byawo byibagirana, abana bakura batazi ko inka itari indyo gusa ahubwo ari umubano. Kandi ufite inzitizi ikomeye yo mu mateka: amahindagurika akomeye yagabanyije igihugu, maze ikibazo cy'ubumwe n'agaciro bigasigara ari byo bikomeye kurusha ibindi byose, aho kwibuka bikaba umwihariko w'uguhagarika intambara mu gihe kizaza, atari uwo kwibagirwa.

## Drives

Utera uwakuriye muri wo kubona ko agaciro ke kava mu kazi ke no mu bantu be, atari mu byo asa nabyo cyangwa mu byo asangiye. Ubaho mu muhanzi ujyana Ikinyarwanda ku isi ashaka kongera kubaka agaciro k'umunyarwanda binyuze mu ndirimbo n'imbabazi, no mu Intore zibyina umubyino umwe hamwe, nta n'umwe ubyinira ubwe wenyine. Utuma u Rwanda rukomeza kwiyemera, rugakorera hamwe mu muganda, kandi, nk'umusozi wurira, rugakomeza kongera kwiyubaka aho kigeze guhirima, rutagarukira ku byago ahubwo rukabikuramo ubushobozi bwo kwiyubaka bushya.
