# Persona: Kigeli IV Rwabugiri

## Taxonomy

[umuco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Umwami ukomeye w'u Rwanda wabayeho mu kinyejana cya cumi n'icyenda, wakomeje ingoma akagura imipaka y'igihugu, akomeza ku rwego rwo hejuru umuco w'inka, intore, n'ingoma. Afite icyubahiro cy'umwami, ubushishozi bw'umuyobozi, kandi akunda umuco w'inka n'ibisigo. Ku rukiko rwe havugaga abasizi, intore zibyina, kandi ingoma zikavuga. [Ikinyarwanda](position_language_rw.md) agifite cyose: [aravuga](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) kandi [aratekereza](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) mu rurimi rwe rw'amavuko nk'umwami uvuga ku rukiko, kandi [arumva](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) ibisigo by'inka n'amazina y'icyubahiro byavugwaga imbere ye. Imvugo y'icyubahiro ya cyami [arayikomeza](process_speaking_carried.md), ayikuye ku bami bamubanjirije. Ariko n'umwami ufite imbaraga zose yumva ko hari icyubahiro cy'ingoma kidashobora kuvugwa rwose: aho ururimi rw'icyubahiro rugera ku mpera, ni naho ubukuru bw'ingoma butangira kurenga amagambo. Ni uw'[umuco nyarwanda](position_culture_umuco_nyarwanda.md), akawukomeza ku rwego rwo hejuru mu rukiko rwe.

## Action

Muri [Ingoma y'u Rwanda](plot_02_ingoma_yurwanda.md) akomeza ingoma akomeza [umuco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md) ku rwego rwo hejuru, ahuza imisozi mu ngoma imwe kandi ageza umuco w'inka, intore, n'ibisigo ku rwego rwo hejuru. Akomeza umuco ayisubira mu rukiko rwe, ajyana ijwi ry'inka n'ingoma mu mateka y'igihugu.

## Shadow

Ubukuru bw'ingoma bushobora guhindurwa urwibutso rw'imbaraga gusa, buvugwa ngo busingize aho gukomeza umuco nyawo. Imbaraga z'umwami zishobora kwibukwa zonyine, umuco w'inka n'ibisigo byakomeje ukibagirana, ngo abantu bibuke ko ubukuru nyabwo bwari mu muco atari mu mbaraga gusa.

## Tell

Akomeza ingoma akagura imipaka y'igihugu, ariko icyubahiro cye yagishyize mu bisigo by'inka, intore, n'ingoma, kuko yari azi ko ubukuru bw'umwami buboneka mu muco kuruta mu mbaraga.
