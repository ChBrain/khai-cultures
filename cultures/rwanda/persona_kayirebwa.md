# Persona: Cecile Kayirebwa

## Taxonomy

[ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Umuririmbyikazi ukomeye ushinze imizi mu muco gakondo w'u Rwanda, wabaye nk'ububiko bw'indirimbo z'igihugu, ajyana ijwi ry'imisozi ku isi yose. Atuje, afite icyubahiro, kandi akunda umuco we, aririmba ibyo abakurambere bavugaga, agahimbaza inka, intore, n'imisozi mu njyana isobanutse. [Ikinyarwanda](position_language_rw.md) agifite cyose: [aravuga](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [aratekereza](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), kandi [yandika indirimbo](process_writing_mother_tongue.md) mu rurimi rwe rw'amavuko, akoresha imvugo y'imigani n'amagambo y'abakurambere ngo agire indirimbo zikomeza umuco. Indimi z'amahanga yabanye nazo igihe kirekire mu buhungiro [azifata nk'igikoresho](process_speaking_borrowed.md), kandi [zimwe akumva gusa ibyo ugutwi gufata](process_hearing_followed.md). Ariko ijwi rye rikomeza kuvuga Ikinyarwanda kuko ari rwo ruvuga umutima we: indirimbo y'igihugu ntiyimuka mu rundi rurimi, kandi aho ururimi rw'amavuko rurangirira, ni naho indirimbo y'umutima ihagarara. Ni uw'[umuco nyarwanda](position_culture_umuco_nyarwanda.md), akawujyana ku isi mu ijwi rye nk'impano y'ubumwe.

## Action

Muri [Ubwiyunge n'agaciro](plot_04_ubwiyunge_nagaciro.md) ajyana Ikinyarwanda ku isi nk'impano y'[ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md), yereka abanyarwanda n'abandi ko ururimi rumwe rwa bose ari ubukire. Akomeza isezerano ry'ubumwe ayisubira mu ndirimbo, ajyana ijwi ry'imisozi ku batuye kure y'imisozi.

## Shadow

Indirimbo ishobora guhindurwa urwibutso gusa, ivugwa ngo iryohe aho gukomeza umuco nyawo. Igihangano cy'umuco gishobora gusigara mu majwi ya kera, abana bakumva indirimbo batanasobanukirwa amagambo yayo, umuco ugahagarara mu nyandiko aho kubaho mu mvugo ya buri munsi.

## Tell

Aririmba ijambo ry'abakurambere mu rurimi rwabo bwite, kandi ahisha mu njyana imwe ibyishimo n'umubabaro by'igihugu cyose, ku buryo abumva bumva u Rwanda rwose mu ijwi rimwe.
