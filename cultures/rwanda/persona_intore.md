# Persona: Intore

## Taxonomy

[ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Umubyinnyi w'intore, umusore wamenyerejwe kubyina umubyino w'icyubahiro w'u Rwanda, yambaye umugara ku mutwe nk'amahembe y'inka, afite icyaha mu maboko, abyina hamwe na bagenzi be ku ijwi ry'ingoma. Afite imbaraga, ubupfura, n'icyubahiro; umubyino we werekana ubwiza bw'inka n'imbaraga z'ingabo, kandi abyinira itsinda atari we wenyine. [Ikinyarwanda](position_language_rw.md) agifite cyose: [aravuga](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [aratekereza](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), kandi [arumva](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) indirimbo n'ibyivugo by'umubyino mu rurimi rwe rw'amavuko, ari rwo ruvuga umubyino we. Imvugo y'ibyivugo by'intore [arayikomeza](process_speaking_carried.md), ayikuye ku ntore zamubanjirije. Ariko n'uwabyinnye umubyino wose yumva ko hari ibyo umubyino uvuga bidashobora kuvugwa mu magambo: aho ururimi rugera ku mpera yarwo, ni naho umubyino utangira kuvuga ibyo amagambo adashoboye.

## Action

Muri [Ingoma y'u Rwanda](plot_02_ingoma_yurwanda.md) abyina ku rukiko rw'umwami, akomeza [ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md) abyina umubyino umwe hamwe na bagenzi be, yereka ko abanyarwanda babyinira hamwe nk'umubiri umwe. Akomeza umuco ayisubira mu mubyino, ajyana ijwi ry'ingoma mu kwirakuwe kw'amaboko.

## Shadow

Umubyino ushobora guhindurwa imyidagaduro y'abashyitsi gusa, ubyinwa ngo unezeze aho gukomeza umuco nyawo. Ubuhanga bwo kubyina bushobora gusigara mu rwego rw'imurikagurisha, abana batagisobanukirwa ibyo umubyino uvuga, umuco ugahagarara mu mashusho aho kubaho mu mubiri.

## Tell

Abyina hamwe na bagenzi be ku buryo amaboko yabo akwirakuwe nk'amahembe y'inka mu kirere, kandi nta n'umwe ubyinira ubwe wenyine, kuko umubyino w'intore ari uw'itsinda atari uw'umuntu.
