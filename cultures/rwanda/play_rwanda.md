# Play: u Rwanda

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): inzu ibitse iyi production kandi ikayisubiza.

## Name

U Rwanda, igihugu gito cyo hagati mu Afurika, ku misozi miremire iri hagati y'ibiyaga binini, mu majyaruguru y'umupaka wa Tanzaniya no mu burasirazuba bwa Kongo. ISO RW, ururimi rw'Ikinyarwanda. Igihugu cy'imisozi igihumbi, aho buri musozi ufite izina, kandi aho abantu bose hafi ya bose bavuga ururimi rumwe rwabo bwite: Ikinyarwanda. Aho bavuga ngo umuntu ni umuntu ku bantu, kandi ko agaciro k'umuntu ari mu bantu be.

## Arc

Igihugu cy'imisozi yacurwe nk'urugendo rurerure rw'imihora itagira ingano, aho ibitaka byumbitse byambitswe imirima itambaganywe ku musozi, naho urumuri rwa nyuma rw'umwijima rugahishira mu gihu. Mu binyejana byabanjirije, abami b'u Rwanda bahuje imisozi mu ngoma imwe, bagize Ikinyarwanda ururimi rumwe rwa bose, kandi bashinga umuco w'inka uhereye ku Nyambo z'amahembe maremare, izo ngabo z'ibitiyo zikomeza umubano hagati y'abantu. Inka ntiyari indyo gusa: yari impano y'ubucuti, kuko uwakuguriye inka aba akubereye se. Ku rukiko rw'umwami havugaga abasizi bavuga ibisigo by'inka, intore zikabyina, ingoma zikavuga, kandi imigongo y'ibara ry'amabara y'amabuye igashushanya inkike z'inzu. Igihugu cyahuye n'amahindagurika akomeye y'amateka kandi cyabyihanganiye n'icyubahiro, gihagaze ku ndangagaciro ebyiri: ubumwe, ko abanyarwanda ari rumwe; n'agaciro, ko umuntu agira agaciro ke kandi akakirinda. Munsi y'ibyo byose hari umutima umwe: abantu bafite umuco ukomeye w'ubwitange, bakorera hamwe mu muganda, basenga Imana mu migisha yabo, kandi bizera ko igihugu cyabo, nk'umusozi wurira, kitarangira, ahubwo kigenda cyiyubaka buhoro buhoro.

**[Plot 1: Imisozi n'inka](plot_01_imisozi_ninka.md)**

Mu ntangiriro abantu batuye ku misozi y'u Rwanda, banga imirima ku mpinga, kandi batoza inka z'amahembe maremare, [umuco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md) ushinga imizi nk'ishingiro ry'umubano n'ubukungu ku [Birunga](place_birunga.md) no ku misozi yose.

**[Plot 2: Ingoma y'u Rwanda](plot_02_ingoma_yurwanda.md)**

Abami bahuje imisozi mu ngoma imwe; ku rukiko rwa [Kigeli IV Rwabugiri](persona_rwabugiri.md), [Sekarama](persona_sekarama.md) avuga ibisigo by'inka, intore zibyina, kandi [umuco w'inka](position_umuco_winka.md) ugera ku rwego rwo hejuru.

**[Plot 3: Ubwigenge](plot_03_ubwigenge.md)**

Igihugu kibona ubwigenge kikiyobora ubwacyo, [Kigali](place_kigali.md) iba umurwa mukuru, kandi ikibazo cy'[ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md) bw'abanyarwanda kiba ikibazo gikomeye cy'igihugu gishya.

**[Plot 4: Ubwiyunge n'agaciro](plot_04_ubwiyunge_nagaciro.md)**

Igihugu cyongera kwiyubaka cyishingikiriza ku [bumwe](position_ubumwe.md) no ku [gaciro](position_agaciro.md); [Cecile Kayirebwa](persona_kayirebwa.md) aririmba Ikinyarwanda hose ku isi, kandi [Kigali](place_kigali.md) iba umurwa usukuye kandi w'icyatsi.

## Stakes

Niba igihugu cy'imisozi igihumbi, gifite ururimi rumwe rwo guhuza bose n'umuco ukomeye w'inka, kandi cyahuye n'amahindagurika akomeye y'amateka maze kikabyihanganira, gishobora gukomeza ubumwe bwacyo n'agaciro kacyo kitarwanyije ubukene n'isi ifunguye igenda yegera, gikomeza gukorera hamwe mu muganda kandi kikubaha Imana mu migisha yacyo, gihagaze ku ijambo ngo umuntu ni umuntu ku bantu, kandi igihugu, nk'umusozi wurira, kitarangira ahubwo kigenda cyiyubaka.
