# Process: umuganda

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Abaturage bose b'umudugudu, abaturanyi bahurira hamwe ku munsi umwe wa buri kwezi ngo bakorere igihugu cyabo. Buri wese azana intoki ze: gusukura imihanda, kubaka inzu y'umukene, gufungura imiyoboro y'amazi, cyangwa gutera ibiti. Nta wuhembwa, kuko ni umurimo wo gukorera hamwe igihugu, ari bwo bumwe bukozwe n'amaboko.

## Direction

Werekeza ku bumwe no ku gukorera hamwe: umuganda ni ubwitange bukozwe na buri wese ku murimo umwe, bwerekana ko umuntu ari umuntu ku bantu kandi ko nta wubaka inzu wenyine. Si umurimo gusa ahubwo ni umuhango wo kongera kubaka umubano: abaturanyi bahurira hamwe, bavugana, kandi bubaka igihugu n'amaboko yabo.

## Lever

Ushyizwe mu bikorwa n'ubumwe bwo gukorera hamwe n'icyizere ko igihugu kibakwa na buri wese. Uhagaze imbere y'ubukene n'imirimo myinshi y'igihugu, ariko imbere yawo hari intege nke ziterwa n'uwanga kuza ku murimo, n'umurimo udashobora gukorwa n'umuntu umwe wenyine.

## Echo

Umuganda werekana ku mugaragaro ibyo [ubumwe](position_ubumwe.md) buvuga: ko igihugu cyubakwa n'amaboko ya bose hamwe, ko nta wubaka inzu wenyine, kandi ko agaciro k'igihugu kava mu kazi n'ubwitange bwa buri munsi.
