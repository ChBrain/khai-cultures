# Play: England

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the house that holds this production and answers for it.

## Name

England, the largest of the constituent nations of the United Kingdom, a green and temperate land south of Scotland and east of Wales. ISO GB-ENG, language en.

## Arc

An old country that learned, after conquest from across the Channel, to govern its feeling rather than show it. It made an island of itself in temper as well as geography: it prizes fair play and the rules of the game above the prize, the gifted amateur above the grinding professional, and the dry word above the loud one. It turned its damp, green countryside into a self-image of home, and learned to say less than it means and to meet trouble with a shrug and a 'mustn't grumble'. Beneath the calm runs an old, stubborn dissent from below and a quiet pride that asks to be felt, never stated.

## Triggers

**[Plot 1: The Norman Conquest 1066](plot_01_norman_conquest_1066.md)**

At the field by Hastings the old English order falls to an invader from across the Channel; the land is conquered, surveyed, and made over from the top down, and learns to carry its loss inward.

**[Plot 2: The Peasants' Revolt 1381](plot_02_peasants_revolt_1381.md)**

The common people march from the country to demand their freedom, and the figure of the free-born Englishman who answers back to power is set into the national grain.

**[Plot 3: The English Reformation 1534](plot_03_reformation_1534.md)**

[Henry VIII](persona_henry_viii.md) breaks the Church in England from Rome and makes the king its head, cutting the country loose to settle its own faith in its own way.

**[Plot 4: The Industrial Revolution](plot_04_industrial_revolution.md)**

In [Manchester](place_manchester.md) and the mill towns of the north, England becomes the workshop of the world, and [Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md) holds the human cost of it up to the light.

## Stakes

Whether a country that hides its feeling behind irony and understatement, that loves the rules of the game and the gifted amateur, and that keeps a green and pleasant self-image of itself, can still know what it stands for, now that the workshop has gone quiet and it must tell its own story apart from the union it once led.
