## Shown

A range of low green hills in the English heart, of honey-coloured stone villages, dry-stone walls, sheep meadows, and church towers, the very picture of the green and pleasant land. Not grand scenery but a tended, ordered countryside, the England the country keeps in its mind's eye as home.

## Offers

The pastoral self-image of England: the village green, the parish, the cricket pitch and the pub, where the quiet, ordered, understated life of the country shows itself in the landscape.

## Withheld

The smoke and the crowd of the industrial city. The Cotswolds hold to the England of the field and the village and keep the mill and the slum out of the picture, the green country set against the workshop.
