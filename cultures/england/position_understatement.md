# Position: understatement

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The power of saying less than you mean. Understatement holds that the felt thing is truer for being unstated, that the stiff upper lip keeps trouble from spreading, and that 'not bad' may be the highest praise there is. It makes the shrug, the dry aside, and the 'mustn't grumble' carry the weight that elsewhere a speech would take.

## Orders

That you keep your feeling to yourself, make no fuss, and meet disaster as you would mild inconvenience. You name the worst thing lightly or not at all, let the silence and the joke do the work, and trust the other to read what you leave unsaid.

## Loses

Ground where the reserve hardens into an inability to speak of anything that matters, so that grief, love, and trouble go unattended behind the calm. It loses when the horror of making a fuss leaves the deepest things unsaid until it is too late.

## Drives

It lives in the answer that meets real sympathy with 'mustn't grumble' and a fresh pot of tea, and lets the silence say the rest.
