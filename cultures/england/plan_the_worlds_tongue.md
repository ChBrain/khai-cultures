# Plan: the world's tongue

## Taxonomy

A mandate: the deliberate cultivation and export of English as the world's common tongue, from the empire that first carried it to the British Council that now teaches it, so that a small island keeps a voice far larger than itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

To keep English the lingua franca of the world, the language of trade, science, the screen, and the sky, and to trade on the soft power of the tongue the world already speaks, while living with the irony that a language, once spread, belongs to everyone and no longer to England alone.

## Orders

- To the British Council and the institutions: teach and spread English as soft power and as a national export.
- To the nation: trade on the advantage of speaking the world's own language from birth.
- To itself: accept that the world's English is no longer the [King's English](position_the_kings_english.md), and never will be again.

## Implementation

The spread through empire and trade; the British Council and the English-teaching industry; the exam boards; and English as the standing language of science, aviation, finance, and the internet.

## Targets

- [x] English became the most widely used language on earth, the lingua franca of trade, science, and the screen.
- [x] English-teaching and the British Council turned the tongue into soft power and a major export.
- [W] The tongue, once spread, became everyone's; the many Englishes of the world answer to no English standard.
- [ ] Keep a voice in a tongue the island no longer owns, as the world's English drifts ever further from its own.
