# Process: the pub

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Anyone who pushes open the door of the local and stands their round. The pub needs only a bar, a few familiar faces, and the unspoken rule that when it is your shout, you get them in without being asked twice.

## Direction

Toward easy, level company where rank is left at the door and the talk runs to football, the weather, and the wry complaint. It is not a party but a steady, ordinary belonging, the warm middle ground between home and the world, kept going by the ritual of the round.

## Lever

The wish for company without ceremony drives it, and the round binds it: to buy your turn is to belong, and to dodge it is the one real sin. Against it stands showing off and standing apart, and the pub quietly levels both.

## Echo

The pub makes English belonging audible in the buying of a round, and it is where [Liam](persona_liam.md) keeps the loyalty and the dry humour of the working north going over a pint, showing he cares only by who he stands beside.
