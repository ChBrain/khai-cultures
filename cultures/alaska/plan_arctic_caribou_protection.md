# Plan: the caribou plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and wildlife biology designed to manage industrial resource transit and protect caribou migration corridors.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Protect caribou by elevating pipeline sections, enforcing transit speed limits, mapping herd locations, and restricting calving ground development.

## Orders

- To the Department of Fish and Game: Coordinate caribou herd surveys and audit pipeline crossing designs.

## Implementation

The elevated pipeline crossing audits; the caribou collar tracking; and the deployment of trail migration camera monitors.
