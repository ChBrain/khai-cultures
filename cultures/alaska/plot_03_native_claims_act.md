## Cue

In December 1971, President Richard Nixon signs the Native Claims act, transferring forty-four million acres to native corporations.

## Action

The act establishes native regional corporations, developing [arctic survival](position_arctic_survival.md) rights. Siku teaches traditional subsistence methods at corporate heritage centers, while Karl audits pipeline land easements.

## Stage

- [arctic survival](position_arctic_survival.md) : the indigenous land stewardship, cold endurance, and subsistence hunting focus.
- [plan_arctic_caribou_protection](plan_arctic_caribou_protection.md) : the caribou plan designed to protect migratory corridors.
- [salmon fishing](process_salmon_fishing.md) : the traditional fishery process that secures native food supplies.

## Tension

The transition to corporate land ownership clashes with traditional communal sharing customs, creating long-term tribal identity debates.
