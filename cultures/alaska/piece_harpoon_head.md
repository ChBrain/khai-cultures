# Piece: harpoon head

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Maintained in family hunting chests and displayed in the Iñupiat Heritage Center in Barrow.

## Load Bearing

It represents the preservation of traditional subsistence hunting, the connection to arctic marine mammal migrations, and the legacy of ancestral survival skills.

## Apparent

A hand-carved walrus ivory toggling harpoon head displaying detailed blade slots, sinew line bindings, and family ownership engravings.

## Yearbook

- Utilized and maintained by native hunter [Siku](persona_siku.md) during seasonal whale hunts.
