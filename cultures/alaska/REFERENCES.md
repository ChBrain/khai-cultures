# Alaska: References

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_alaska.md](pitch_alaska.md) is written from Alaska's profile inflected by its oil fields, Inupiat hunting traditions, and gold mining districts, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Alaska base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 40                                       |
| Individualism                 | 78                                       |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 66                                       |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 44                                       |
| Long Term Orientation         | 24                                       |
| Indulgence                    | 62                                       |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Alaska's low power distance in native corporations, active local county assemblies, and low uncertainty avoidance in arctic transportation innovation).
