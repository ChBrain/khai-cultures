# Plot: The Klondike Gold Rush 1896

## Taxonomy

[play_alaska](play_alaska.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In August 1896, prospectors discover rich gold deposits in the Yukon, launching the massive Klondike gold rush.

## Action

The rush establishes frontier transit networks, developing [frontier wealth](position_frontier_wealth.md). The [harpoon head](piece_harpoon_head.md) represents traditional tools. Karl audits historic mining trail maps near pipeline stations, while Siku records oral histories of early native guides.

## Stage

- [frontier wealth](position_frontier_wealth.md) : the resource extraction, construction labor, and pipeline logistics.
- [harpoon head](piece_harpoon_head.md) : the toggling ivory harpoon representing subsistence coastal hunting.
- [the Kenai Fjords](place_kenai_fjords.md) : the glacial marine wilderness area that served as a shipping route.

## Tension

The massive influx of prospectors introduces devastating epidemic diseases, wiping out dozens of Native villages along the Yukon River.
