# Process: tree sapling planting

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Forestry conservationists, school volunteer groups, and agricultural extension agents, conducted during spring planting weeks.

## Direction

Along the prairie windbreak line. Digging sapling holes, preparing organic soil, placing bare-root tree saplings, packing soil, watering roots, and installing protective fencing.

## Lever

The traditional conservation forestry process that protects prairie topsoil. It supports local windbreak restoration and agricultural productivity, though it is limited by water availability and requires constant weeding audits.
