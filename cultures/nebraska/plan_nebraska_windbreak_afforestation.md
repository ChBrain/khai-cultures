# Plan: the Nebraska windbreak plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and agricultural science designed to plant tree windbreaks and manage wind erosion in prairie croplands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Conserve topsoil by planting tree windbreaks, promoting no-till farming, establishing cover crops, and regulating groundwater irrigation.
