# Italy: a khai culture

Italy staged as a full khai play, ISO IT, language it, over a peninsula of dialects. A
civilization before it was a state, staged as a theatre: its history as the plot line,
its people as the cast, the roles its languages encode, its places, its practices, and
its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds
this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its Estate; a culture
with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_italy.md](play_italy.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_it.md](pitch_it.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Italy's profile
  (family- and beauty-centred, expressive, with a deep need for security and a distrust
  of the distant state) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** ancient Rome, the Renaissance, the Risorgimento, and the postwar
  economic miracle - each casting the elements it needs.
- **Personas:** Dante (who gave Italy a literary tongue) and Alessandro Manzoni (who gave
  the united nation its common prose), with Garibaldi (who unified a people who could not
  understand one another) as the historic cast, plus two archetypes: Rosa (the Southern,
  dialect-speaking grandmother) and Giulia (the young Northern life lived in Italian and
  English). Each is wired to the language engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** il dialetto (the dialects beneath the national tongue), il campanilismo
  (loyalty to one's town and bell-tower), la famiglia (the family stronger than the
  state).
- **Places:** Roma (the eternal city of empire, Church, and state), Firenze (the cradle
  of the Renaissance and of the Italian language).
- **Processes:** la passeggiata (the evening stroll), la tavola (the family table).
- **Pieces:** la pasta (the national everyday, one and a thousand), la Vespa (the design
  and the dolce vita of the boom).
- **Plan:** fare gli italiani, the standing project to make a single people and a common
  language out of a peninsula of states and dialects.
