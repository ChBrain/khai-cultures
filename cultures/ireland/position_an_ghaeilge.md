# Position: an Ghaeilge

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Irish, the first official language of the Republic by its own Constitution: the tongue on every road sign and official form, taught to every child, an official language of the European Union, and revered as the true voice of the nation. Yet outside the Gaeltacht it is spoken every day by few.

## Orders

That the state carry its name and its signage in Irish, that every child sit thirteen years of it, that the Gaeltacht be supported and the language be honoured as the first even by those whose only fluent tongue is English. It asks a whole nation to claim a language as its own.

## Loses

Ground to English, the everyday tongue of nearly all. It survives as a school subject, a symbol, and a shrinking Gaeltacht more than as living speech, and the gap widens between its official primacy and its real, daily use.

## Drives

It lives in [Pearse](persona_pearse.md), who tied it to freedom; in [Bríd](persona_brid.md), who speaks it as her mother tongue; and in [Cian](persona_cian.md), who owns it only as remembered school scraps. It drives Ireland to revere and revive a language it mostly does not speak, and to keep its identity in a tongue it had all but lost.
