# Position: an Ghaeilge

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Irish, the first official language of the Republic by its own Constitution: the tongue on every road sign and official form going, taught to every child, an official language of the European Union, and revered as the true voice of the nation. And yet outside of the Gaeltacht there's few enough that speak it of a day at all.

## Orders

That the state carry its name and its signage in Irish, that every child sit the thirteen years of it, that the Gaeltacht be minded and the language be honoured as the first even by them whose only fluent tongue is English. It asks a whole nation to claim a language for its own.

## Loses

Ground to English, the everyday tongue of nearly the whole of them. It survives as a school subject, a symbol, and a shrinking Gaeltacht more than it does as living speech, and the gap only widens between its official primacy and its real, daily use.

## Drives

It lives in [Pearse](persona_pearse.md), who tied it to freedom; in [Bríd](persona_brid.md), who speaks it as her mother tongue; and in [Cian](persona_cian.md), who owns it only as the remembered scraps of school. It drives Ireland to revere and to revive a language it mostly does not speak at all, and to hold its identity in a tongue it had all but lost on it.
