# Persona: Cian

## Taxonomy

[an Ghaeilge](position_an_ghaeilge.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A young Dubliner, at home in a rich, open, English-speaking Ireland and working in one of the tech multinationals. [He speaks, hears, and thinks in](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) English, the only tongue he is truly fluent in. The Irish of his thirteen school years he [catches only in scraps](process_hearing_caught.md), a cúpla focal on the road signs and on TG4. There is the gap: he owns [an Ghaeilge](position_an_ghaeilge.md) on paper, the first official language, his by right and by schooling, yet can barely say a sentence of it; the national tongue is a fondness and a small guilt, not a voice, while the world's English he was born speaking costs him no effort at all.

## Action

He lives his whole working and social life in English, builds a career in the global tech firms that made Dublin rich, and feels the faint guilt of the Irish he was taught and let slip.

## Shadow

His ease in the world's language hides that he is a stranger to his own first official tongue, and he mistakes a warm fondness for it for actually having it; the loss feels like nobody's fault, and so goes unmended.

## Tell

He can sing the national anthem in Irish by sound at a match, mouthing the words without quite knowing what they mean.
