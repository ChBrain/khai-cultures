# Plan: athbheochan na Gaeilge

## Taxonomy

A mandate: the deliberate, century-long project to revive [an Ghaeilge](position_an_ghaeilge.md), the first official language, as a living tongue rather than a school subject and a symbol.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Toward an Ireland that can one day speak the language it reveres: to protect the Gaeltacht where Irish still lives, to grow Irish-medium schooling beyond it, to hold the language's place in the state and in Europe, and above all to make new speakers, so that Irish is used and not only honoured.

## Orders

- To the state: fund the Gaeltacht, keep the official status and the broadcasting, and mean the primacy the Constitution claims.
- To the schools: grow the Gaelscoileanna and teach for real fluency, not thirteen years of rote.
- To the people: use the cúpla focal, and claim Irish as a living tongue rather than a fond memory.

## Implementation

The Gaeltacht supports; the Gaelscoileanna spreading Irish-medium schooling into the cities; compulsory Irish in school; TG4 and Raidió na Gaeltachta; and Irish as an official language of the European Union since 2007.

## Targets

- [x] Irish is the first official language and an official EU language, with its own television and radio.
- [x] Gaelscoileanna have carried Irish-medium schooling well beyond the Gaeltacht.
- [F] After a century of revival, the number of daily speakers keeps falling and the Gaeltacht keeps shrinking.
- [ ] Make enough new speakers that Irish lives as a daily tongue, not only as a school subject and a sign.
