# Plan: athbheochan na Gaeilge

## Taxonomy

A mandate: the deliberate, century-long project to revive [an Ghaeilge](position_an_ghaeilge.md), the first official language, as a living tongue rather than a school subject and a symbol and no more.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Toward an Ireland that might one day speak the language it reveres: to mind the Gaeltacht where Irish still lives, to grow Irish-medium schooling out beyond it, to hold the language's place in the state and in Europe, and above all and beyond all to make new speakers, so that Irish is used and not only honoured.

## Orders

- To the state: fund the Gaeltacht, keep the official status and the broadcasting going, and would you ever mean the primacy the Constitution claims.
- To the schools: grow the Gaelscoileanna and teach for real fluency, and not thirteen years of rote.
- To the people: use the cúpla focal, and claim Irish as a living tongue rather than a fond memory and nothing more.

## Implementation

The Gaeltacht supports; the Gaelscoileanna spreading Irish-medium schooling in into the cities; compulsory Irish in the school; TG4 and Raidió na Gaeltachta; and Irish an official language of the European Union since 2007, no less.

## Targets

- [x] Irish is the first official language and an official EU language, and its own television and radio along with it.
- [x] Gaelscoileanna have carried Irish-medium schooling well out beyond the Gaeltacht.
- [F] After the century of revival, sure the number of daily speakers keeps falling and the Gaeltacht keeps shrinking on us.
- [ ] Make enough of new speakers that Irish lives as a daily tongue, and not only as a school subject and a sign.
