## Shown

The capital on the Liffey: Georgian streets and tenement ghosts, the General Post Office on O'Connell Street, the pubs and the Abbey, the city Joyce mapped street by street in Ulysses, and now the European headquarters of the world's tech giants.

## Holds

- [The Easter Rising, 1916](plot_02_easter_rising_1916.md): here the rebels seize the GPO and proclaim the Republic.
- [The Literary Revival](plot_03_the_literary_revival.md): here is the city Joyce turns into the century's novel.
- [Modern Ireland](plot_04_modern_ireland.md): here the Celtic Tiger and the tech multinationals remake a poor city into a rich one.

## Offers

The stage where Ireland argues with itself and shows itself to the world: rebellion, literature, and boom, a small city that has carried a nation's history and now houses a slice of the global economy.

## Withheld

The Irish language as daily life. Dublin speaks English through and through; the tongue it honours on its signs and learns in its schools it does not live in, and the city withholds from the language the one thing it needs, to be spoken.
