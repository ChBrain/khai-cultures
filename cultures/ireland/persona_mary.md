# Persona: Mary

## Taxonomy

[the Church](position_the_church.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

An older woman of rural Ireland, of the parish, the farm, and the emigration generation. [She speaks, hears, and thinks in](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) English, the tongue of the kitchen and the chapel, with the odd Irish word and the prayers she learned by heart. The Irish of school and the odd blessing she [holds only as caught fragments](process_hearing_caught.md), and the letters and the calls come in the flat English of her children [abroad](position_emigration.md). There is the gap: she keeps the faith and the ways of a rural, Catholic Ireland that is passing, while the family that might have carried them is scattered across the English-speaking world.

## Action

She keeps the faith and the rosary as [the Church](position_the_church.md) empties around her, lights the candle, sets the good room ready, and lives [emigration](position_emigration.md) as the empty chair waiting for the ones home at Christmas.

## Shadow

Her faith and her holding-on can blind her to why the young left both the Church and the country, and she reads as a falling-away what was also an escape from a narrowness she never quite saw.

## Tell

She keeps the good room for visitors who rarely come, and on the dresser the photographs of grandchildren she has mostly met on a screen.
