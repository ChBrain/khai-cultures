# Process: an seisiún

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

A corner of the pub, a few players with fiddle, flute, box, and bodhrán, and a pint set down within reach. No stage and no programme: someone lifts a tune, the others fall in, and the session begins of itself.

## Direction

Toward a music made in company and handed on by ear: reel chases reel, a song quietens the room, and the tradition is carried forward not from the page but from player to player around the one table.

## Lever

The pull of the shared tune against the noise and hurry of the world: the music holds a room together and carries an older Ireland into the present. Take away the company and it is a performance; take away the tradition and it is just songs.

## Echo

The tunes pass to another set of hands and another night, and a culture older than the state survives in the one place it is still made together, by ear, for nothing but the doing of it.
