# Process: an seisiún

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

A corner of the pub, a few players and them with fiddle, flute, box, and bodhrán, and a pint set down within reach of the hand. No stage at all and no programme: someone lifts a tune, the rest of them fall in behind, and sure the session begins of itself.

## Direction

Toward a music made in company and handed on by the ear: reel chases reel, a song quietens the whole room, and the tradition is carried forward not off the page at all but from player to player around the one table.

## Lever

The pull of the shared tune against the noise and the hurry of the world: the music holds a room the one together and carries an older Ireland in into the present. Take away the company and it's only a performance; take away the tradition and it's only songs.

## Echo

The tunes pass on to another set of hands and another night, and a culture older than the state itself survives in the one place it's still made together, by the ear, for nothing only the doing of it.
