# Plot: The Easter Rising, 1916

## Taxonomy

[play_ireland](play_ireland.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Easter 1916, with the Great War raging across Europe, a small band of republicans and Gaelic revivalists judges that England's difficulty is Ireland's opportunity.

## Action

[Pádraig Pearse](persona_pearse.md) and the rebels seize the General Post Office in [Dublin](place_dublin.md) and proclaim the Republic. The Rising is crushed in a week, but the execution of its leaders turns the country, and within a few years comes the War of Independence and the Free State.

## Stage

- [Pádraig Pearse](persona_pearse.md): the schoolmaster who reads the Proclamation and gives himself to the cause.
- [Dublin](place_dublin.md): the GPO and the streets where the Republic is declared.

## Tension

Independence is won, but the new state speaks English, and the Gaelic Ireland the rebels dreamed of stays an aspiration: the freedom is real, the revived [language](position_an_ghaeilge.md) is not, and the nation is born already speaking the tongue it set out to leave behind.
