# Position: the Church

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The Catholic Church, for a century and more the backbone of Irish identity and the shadow-power beside the state: the parish and the priest, the schools and the hospitals, the Mass that ordered the week and the doctrine that ordered the home.

## Orders

That faith and parish come first, that the priest's word carry into school, hospital, and bedroom, and that the nation know itself as Catholic before all else, devout, respectable, and obedient to Rome.

## Loses

Ground, fast and far, to the abuse scandals and to a secular, prosperous age: the pews empty, the authority breaks, and a people once defined by the Church turns from it with a mix of grief and relief.

## Drives

It lives in [Mary](persona_mary.md), who keeps the faith and the rosary as the others fall away, and it drives Ireland's long reckoning with a Church that made it and then failed it.
