# Position: the Church

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The Catholic Church, for a century and more the backbone of Irish identity and the shadow-power in beside the state: the parish and the priest, the schools and the hospitals, the Mass that ordered the week and the doctrine that ordered the home.

## Orders

That faith and parish come first of all, that the priest's word carry into the school, the hospital, and the bedroom, and that the nation know itself as Catholic before anything else at all, devout, respectable, and obedient to Rome.

## Loses

Ground, and fast and far, to the abuse scandals and to a secular, prosperous age: the pews empty out, the authority breaks, and a people once defined by the Church turns away from it with the grief and the relief mixed the one together.

## Drives

It lives in [Mary](persona_mary.md), who keeps the faith and the rosary and the others falling away around her, and it drives Ireland's long reckoning with a Church that made it and then went and failed it.
