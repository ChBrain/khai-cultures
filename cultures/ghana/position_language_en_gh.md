# Position: Ghanaian English

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The authority of English as Ghana's tongue of office, the working language laid over Akan, Ga, Ewe, Dagbani and the many mother tongues of the house, the language of the school, the court, the newspaper and the state. It is a young republic's common tongue, borrowed from the colony it broke from and turned to its own use, in which a nation of many peoples does its public business and speaks to the wider world, while the heart still beats in the tongue of the grandmother and the proverb she keeps.

## Orders

What the nation sets down and conducts its public life in: the statute, the lesson, the ledger and the printed page. It orders that the affairs of the whole country pass through one working tongue, so that peoples who carry different mother tongues at home can meet on common ground in the office, the assembly and the school, and it gives the young state a single language in which to address itself and the world at once.

## Loses

Ground to the mother tongues that live in the mouth and the market, the Akan and the Ga and the Ewe the working tongue never wholly holds, and ground to the proverb and the court speech that were never English at all. It loses most when the borrowed tongue of the school and the screen begins to crowd out the home languages, so that the children come to do their thinking in English and let the grandmother's ebe thin toward silence.

## Drives

It drives a young nation of many peoples to hold its public life and its place in the world in one shared tongue. It is carried, not born, by [Kwame Nkrumah](persona_nkrumah.md), who drafted his Pan-African vision in it; by [Kofi Annan](persona_annan.md), who carried it to the world's council; by [Ama Ata Aidoo](persona_aidoo.md), who bent it back toward the rhythms of Fante on the written page; and by [the Okyeame](persona_okyeame.md), who relays the chief's word into it when the court speaks to the wider state. It reached [Yaa Asantewaa](persona_yaa_asantewaa.md) only across an interpreter, the tongue of the empire that came for the Golden Stool and could not hear, in her own words, what the stool truly was. So the working tongue shows who carries it and whom it merely reached, and binds the nation's peoples without erasing the mother tongues they were born to.
