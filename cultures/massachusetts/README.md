# Massachusetts: a khai culture

Massachusetts staged as a full khai play, ISO US-MA, language en. The Bay State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Puritan, revolutionary, and academic life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_massachusetts.md](play_massachusetts.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_massachusetts.md](pitch_massachusetts.md), the Massachusetts key.
- **Plots (history):** the Mayflower Compact of 1620, the Boston Tea Party of 1773, and the Route 128 tech/biotech boom.
- **Personas:** Abigail (Plymouth archivist) and Liam (Cambridge biotech researcher), written in English.
- **Positions:** the Puritan legacy (moral duty/thrift), the revolutionary spirit (intellectual dissent).
- **Places:** Plymouth Rock, Harvard Yard.
- **Processes:** town meetings (direct democracy), rowing on the Charles River.
- **Pieces:** the Mayflower Compact document, a Boston Tea Party tea crate.
- **Plan:** the Boston climate resilience plan.
