## Cue

In the late 20th century, the concentration of research universities around Boston and Cambridge sparks a computing and biotechnology boom.

## Action

The research expansion establishes a global tech and biotech corridor, driving innovations in medicine and software. The labs at [Harvard Yard](place_harvard_yard.md) and MIT fuel this growth. Liam conducts genetic research in a Cambridge lab, utilizing local academic networks.

## Stage

- [Harvard Yard](place_harvard_yard.md) : the academic campus that provides the research talent and historical prestige.
- [the Boston climate plan](plan_climate_resilience.md) : the coastal planning needed to protect the low-lying labs and tech infrastructure.
- [Liam](persona_liam.md) : the biotech researcher who works in the Cambridge scientific community.

## Tension

The rapid growth of the biotech sector causes extreme housing costs, gentrification in historically working-class areas like Cambridge, and debates over genetic engineering.
