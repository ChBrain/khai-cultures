# Process: town meetings

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Massachusetts town moderators and registered voters, conducted annually in the spring, and during special sessions.

## Direction

In the town hall or local school gym. The presentation of town warrants, debate on local budgets, voting on bylaws, and direct election of local officials.

## Lever

The traditional form of direct democracy that empowers local residents to govern their communities. It ensures transparency and community consensus, though it faces low turnout and longer debates that delay updates.
