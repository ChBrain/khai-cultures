# Plan: the Boston climate plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and civil engineering designed to protect coastal Massachusetts from rising sea levels.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Enforce climate resilience by constructing seawalls, restoring coastal wetlands, and mandating flood-proofing for new construction.

## Orders

- To the Boston Planning Agency: Restrict new residential zoning in flood-prone historic districts.
- To research labs like [Liam](persona_liam.md): Develop localized flood barrier solutions for facilities near the river.
- To local town meetings led by coordinators like [Abigail](persona_abigail.md): Approve municipal funds for dune restoration projects.

## Implementation

The construction of harbor barriers; the elevation of waterfront wharves; and the restoration of salt marshes.
