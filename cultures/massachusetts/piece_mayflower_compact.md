## Place

Historic copies displayed at the Plymouth museum archive and referenced in New England town libraries.

## Load Bearing

It represents the founding social contract, the establishment of early self-governance, and the legal framework that underpins the town meeting system.

## Apparent

A single sheet of aged, handwritten parchment, containing the text of the covenant and the signatures of forty-one Pilgrim men.
