The legacy of intellectual dissent, debate, and resistance to arbitrary power. It possesses a belief in individual rights, academic freedom, and the constant questioning of authority.

That one defends individual liberty, participates in public debate, and supports the freedom of intellectual inquiry.

Its balance when dissent turns into destructive factionalism or when intellectual debate ignores the material needs of the community.
