## Owner

## Shown

The historic landing site in Plymouth, marked by a granite boulder inscribed with the year 1620, sheltered under a classical portico on the harbor shore.

## Holds

- the founding memory of colonial New England in the plot [The Mayflower Compact 1620](plot_01_mayflower_1620.md) : the anchor of regional history.

## Offers

The historic displays and harbor viewing deck where [Abigail](persona_abigail.md) studies Pilgrim genealogy.

## Withheld

The lack of documentation for the specific rock. While celebrated as the exact landing spot, the rock was not identified until 121 years after the landing, a detail omitted from the monument.
