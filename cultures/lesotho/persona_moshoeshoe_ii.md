# Persona: Moshoeshoe oa Bobeli

## Taxonomy

[borena](position_borena.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Morena ea ileng a busa ha naha e fumana boipuso, ea jereng lebitso la mothehi oa sechaba 'me a emetse borena nakong eo naha e neng e ema joaloka mmuso o ikemetseng marung. Monna oa seriti, oa thuto le oa boikokobetso, ea neng a tseba hore borena ke ba sechaba, eseng ba motho a le mong. [Sesotho](position_language_st.md) o se [bua](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) le ho se [nahana](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) e le puo ea botho ba hae, mme Senyesemane sa thuto ea hae ea kantle o se [tšoere joaloka sesebelisoa](process_speaking_carried.md), a se [bala a se latela](process_reading_followed.md), a se sebelisa ho emela naha lefatšeng le pharaletseng. Morena ea emetseng sechaba lefatšeng o ile a tseba hore ho na le seo borena bo se buoang ka Sesotho seo puo ea kantle e ke keng ea se fetisa kaofela, kanyetso e leng teng moeling oa leleme, moo le morena a lokelang ho khutlela puong ea habo ha a batla ho bua le sechaba sa habo. O jere [setso sa Basotho](position_culture_setso_sa_basotho.md) seo mothehi a se qalileng, a se boloka borena bo phahameng marung.

## Action

Ho [boipuso le mmuso oa lithaba](plot_04_boipuso.md) o emela [borena](position_borena.md) ha naha e ema hape e ikemetse, a boloka borena e le motheo o kopanyang sechaba. Ka ho jara lebitso la mothehi le ka ho hlompha borena ba sechaba, o tsoela pele ka mosebetsi oo Moshoeshoe oa Pele a o qalileng: hore borena bo emetse Basotho kaofela.

## Shadow

Morena ea emetseng sechaba o tšoere moriti oa hore borena bo lokela ho lula bo le ka holimo ho likarohano, 'me ho boloka borena bo le seriti se kopanyang ho batla boikokobetso bo boholo. Bophelo ba hae bo bontša hore borena ba naha e nyenyane marung bo batla ho boloka seriti ho feta matla.

## Tell

O lula a re borena ke ba sechaba eseng ba motho, a hlompha sechaba sa habo joalo ka ha mothehi a se hlompha - mme a boloka borena e le tšepe e kopanyang Basotho.
