# Plot: Thaba Bosiu le ho thehoa ha sechaba

## Taxonomy

[play_lesotho](play_lesotho.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Mathoasong a lekholo la bo 19 la lilemo, nakong ea lifaqane, lithaba li ne li tletse merabe e hasaneng, ha lihlopha li ne li hahlauoa ke litlala le lintoa. [Moshoeshoe oa Pele](persona_moshoeshoe_i.md), morena oa bohlale ea neng a batla khotso, o batla sebaka se sireletsehileng moo a ka bokellang batho 'me a ba sireletsa.

## Action

Moshoeshoe o nyolosa batho ba hae holim'a [Thaba Bosiu](place_thaba_bosiu.md), thaba e nang le sehlohlolo se bataletseng e leng qhobosheane ea tlhaho. Holim'a thaba ena o bokella ba hasaneng ho tsoa merabeng e fapaneng, a ba amohela le ba neng ba mo lwantše, a ba fa lijo le mobu. Ka [botho](position_botho.md) le ka kamohelo, o etsa sechaba se le seng ho tse fapaneng - Basotho - 'me a theha [borena](position_borena.md) bo emetseng bohle, eseng morabe o le mong feela. Naha e hlaha tlas'a thaba, e thehiloe ka khotso le ka kamohelo.

## Stage

- [Moshoeshoe oa Pele](persona_moshoeshoe_i.md): morena ea bokellang ba hasaneng 'me a ba etsa sechaba se le seng.
- [Thaba Bosiu](place_thaba_bosiu.md): qhobosheane ea tlhaho moo sechaba se thehiloeng teng.
- [botho](position_botho.md): kholeho ea hore motho ke motho ka batho, eo Moshoeshoe a thehang sechaba ka eona.

## Tension

Sechaba se hlaha eseng ka ntoa empa ka kamohelo le ka khotso - morena ea bokellang ba tšabileng 'me a amohela le sera. Empa thaba e sirelelitseng sechaba e bontša hape hore naha e thehiloe har'a lifaqane, har'a nako e thata, 'me tšireletso eo e tla batla ho bolokoa ka mehleng e tlang. Naha e qala e na le motheo oa kamohelo le kholeho ea hore ho boloka batho ho boima ho feta ho ba hlola.
