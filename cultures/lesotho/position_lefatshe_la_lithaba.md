# Position: lefatše la lithaba

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Naha e le 'ngoe feela lefatšeng e leng holim'a limithara tse sekete kaofela ha eona: lithaba tsa Maloti, lehloa la mariha, le lithaba tse phahameng ka ho fetisisa Afrika Boroa. Ke lefatše la balisana le lipere tsa Mosotho, la likhomo le linku, la linoka tse qalang lithabeng tsena tsa nyolosa. Lithaba ke tsona tse sireletsang naha, tse e fang seriti, le tse e etsang hore e be ea bohlokoa - empa hape ke tsona tse e etsang hore bophelo bo be boima, bo le mariha le bofuma.

## Orders

Hore Mosotho o tsoa lithabeng 'me o tseba naha ea hae ka maoto, ka pere le ka mahlo; hore lithaba li sireletsa joalo ka ha Thaba Bosiu e sirelelitse, kahoo motho o batla botšabelo holim'a thaba; le hore bophelo ba lithaba bo batla matla, ho mamella mariha le ho alosa har'a lehloa, ho etsa sechaba se tiileng.

## Loses

Bo lahleheloa ke matla ha lithaba li fetoha lefella le sa fepang batho, ha bofuma bo qobella bana ba lithaba ho siea naha ho ea sebetsa kantle; bo lahleheloa, hape, ha seriti sa lithaba se lebaloa, ha naha e ratoa feela ka botle ba eona empa bophelo ba balisana bo hlokomolohuoa.

## Drives

Bo phela ho seopeli, ea fetisang molumo oa lithaba le oa balisana lefatšeng ka pina, le ho molisana, ea bonang naha pele ho bohle a alositse a apere kobo. Bo susumetsa Lesotho ho ba motlotlo ka naha ea eona e phahameng marung, ho boloka bophelo ba lithaba le seriti sa balisana ba sona.
