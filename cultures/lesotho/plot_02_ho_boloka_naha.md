# Plot: ho boloka naha

## Taxonomy

[play_lesotho](play_lesotho.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Ha sechaba se ntse se hola holim'a thaba, matla a kantle le lintoa li aparela naha. [Moshoeshoe oa Pele](persona_moshoeshoe_i.md) o tobana le potso ea hore na a ka boloka joang naha le sechaba sa hae, mobu oa balisana le oa likhomo, ha lefatše le pharaletseng le hatella.

## Action

Moshoeshoe o boloka [lefatše la lithaba](position_lefatshe_la_lithaba.md) eseng ka marumo feela empa ka boqhetšeke le ka puo. O sebelisa qhobosheane ea [Thaba Bosiu](place_thaba_bosiu.md) ho sireletsa sechaba, 'me ha matla a kantle a tsoela pele ho hatella, o inehela tšireletsong ho pholosa naha le sechaba ho tloha tšenyong. Ka bohlale ba ho khetha khotso ho feta tšenyo, o boloka mobu o moholo le sechaba se phelang, le ha naha e ne e le har'a nako e thata. Naha e sala e le 'ngoe, e bolokiloe ke bohlale ho feta ka ntoa.

## Stage

- [Moshoeshoe oa Pele](persona_moshoeshoe_i.md): morena ea bolokang naha ka boqhetšeke le ka tšireletso.
- [Thaba Bosiu](place_thaba_bosiu.md): qhobosheane e sireletsang sechaba ha matla a kantle a hatella.
- [lefatše la lithaba](position_lefatshe_la_lithaba.md): mobu oa lithaba oo morena a o lwanetseng ho o boloka.

## Tension

Naha e bolokoa, empa theko ea ho e boloka ke ho amohela tšireletso ea kantle - khotso e bolokang naha empa e hlokang theko. Ba latelang ba lula ba botsa hore na naha e ileng ea bolokoa ka boqhetšeke e ka boloka joang tokoloho ea eona ka botlalo. Bohlale ba ho khetha khotso ho feta tšenyo bo pholosa sechaba, empa bo siea kanyetso e tla hahamalla naha ka mehleng e tlang.
