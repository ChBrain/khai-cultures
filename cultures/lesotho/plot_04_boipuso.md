# Plot: boipuso le mmuso oa lithaba

## Taxonomy

[play_lesotho](play_lesotho.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Ka mor'a nako e telele tlas'a tšireletso ea kantle, naha e fihla sebakeng sa ho ipusa hape. Sechaba se hahamalla ho ema joaloka mmuso o ikemetseng, naha e phahameng marung, e tšoereng botho ba eona, kobo ea eona le borena ba eona.

## Action

Naha e fumana boipuso hape mme ea ema joaloka [borena](position_borena.md) bo phahameng marung, Mmuso oa Lithaba, naha e le 'ngoe feela lefatšeng e leng holim'a limithara tse sekete kaofela. [Moshoeshoe oa Bobeli](persona_moshoeshoe_ii.md) o emela borena ha naha e ema e ikemetse, a jara lebitso la mothehi. Sechaba se boloka botho ba sona, kobo le mokorotlo, 'me [Puseletso Seema](persona_seema.md) o fetisa molumo oa lithaba lefatšeng ka pina ea famo. Naha e ema marung, e motlotlo ka [lefatše la lithaba](position_lefatshe_la_lithaba.md) la eona, le ha bana ba eona ba e-ea ka kantle ho batla bophelo.

## Stage

- [Moshoeshoe oa Bobeli](persona_moshoeshoe_ii.md): morena ea emelang borena ha naha e ema e ikemetse.
- [Puseletso Seema](persona_seema.md): sebini se fetisang molumo oa lithaba lefatšeng ka famo.
- [borena](position_borena.md): motheo o kopanyang sechaba ha naha e ipusa hape.

## Tension

Naha e ema e ikemetse, e motlotlo ka botho le seriti sa eona, empa lefatše le pharaletseng le hatella: bofuma bo qobella bana ba lithaba ho siea naha ho ea sebetsa kantle, 'me thaba e sala. Naha e nyenyane marung e tobane le potso ea hore na e ka boloka joang botho ba eona, kobo ea eona le borena ba eona ha lefatše le potlakang le tsebang motho a le mong le hatella. Empa ha lithaba li ntse li eme, le Basotho ba tšepa ho ema.
