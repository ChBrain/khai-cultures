# Position: borena

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Kholeho ea hore sechaba se kopantsoe ke borena, eseng ke morabe o le mong feela: hore morena o emetse Basotho kaofela, 'me matla a hae a tsoa sechabeng le pitsong moo batho ba buang. Ho tloha ho Moshoeshoe oa Pele ho fihlela kajeno, borena ke tšepe e kopanyang lithaba le merabe ho ba sechaba se le seng. Ke borena bo theiloeng holim'a khotso le kamohelo: morena ea bokellang ba hasaneng, ea amohelang moeti le ea balehetseng, eseng ea hahamallang feela.

## Orders

Hore naha e kopantsoe ke borena bo emetseng bohle, kahoo motho oa morabe ofe kapa ofe ke Mosotho ka ho lekana; hore morena o lokela ho mamela sechaba pitsong, hobane ng'oan'a morena o busoa ke molao; le hore mosebetsi oa borena ke ho boloka khotso le kopano, eseng ho phahamisa motho a le mong holim'a sechaba.

## Loses

Bo lahleheloa ke matla ha borena bo lebala hore ke ba sechaba 'me bo fetoha ba motho; bo lahleheloa, hape, ha ho mameloa pitso, ha morena a busa ntle le sechaba, kapa ha borena bo sebelisoa ho arola Basotho ho fapana le ho ba kopanya joalo ka ha bo thehiloe.

## Drives

Bo phela ho [Moshoeshoe oa Pele](persona_moshoeshoe_i.md), ea thehileng sechaba ka ho bokella ba hasaneng le ka ho ba sireletsa, le ho [Moshoeshoe oa Bobeli](persona_moshoeshoe_ii.md), ea emetseng borena ha naha e ema hape e ikemetse. Bo susumetsa Lesotho ho boloka kopano ea sechaba sa eona ka borena bo emetseng bohle, hore Basotho ba lule e le motho a le mong.
