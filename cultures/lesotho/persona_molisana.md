# Persona: Molisana

## Taxonomy

[lefatše la lithaba](position_lefatshe_la_lithaba.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Moshanyana ea apereng kobo, a tsamaeang lithabeng a alositse likhomo le linku, a palame pere ea Mosotho har'a lehloa le moea o batang. Ke eena ea bonang naha pele ho bohle, ea tsebang litsela tsa lithaba, mafika le linoka, ea binang lesiba ha a le mong holim'a thaba. Sesotho o se [bua](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) le ho se [nahana](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) e le puo ea botho ba hae, mme maele a baholo o a [utloile](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) a be a [a latela](process_hearing_followed.md) molomong oa banna ba sebakeng. Senyesemane sa sekolo o se [tšoere ka lehlakoreng](process_speaking_borrowed.md), ha a se eang sekolong, hobane lithaba li mo bitsa pele. Moshanyana ea bonang naha pele ho bohle o tseba hore ho na le seo lithaba li se buang seo puo e ke keng ea se fetisa, kanyetso e leng teng moeling oa leleme, moo le molisana a tlohelang puo 'me a bua le likhomo ka molumo o sa ngoloeng.

## Action

Ho [ho boloka naha](plot_02_ho_boloka_naha.md) o emela [lefatše la lithaba](position_lefatshe_la_lithaba.md), e le eena ea phelang naha letsatsi le letsatsi, ea bolokang likhomo le mobu oo baholo ba o lwanetseng. Ka ho alosa lithabeng le ka ho apara kobo, o boloka botho ba ho ba Mosotho oa lithaba bo phela ho tloha molokong ho ea molokong.

## Shadow

Moshanyana ea bonang naha pele ho bohle o tšoere moriti oa hore bophelo ba ho alosa bo boima, bo le mariha le lehloa, hangata bo siea sekolo morao. 'Me lithaba tseo a li ratang ke tsona li mo arolang le bophelo bo bong boo a ka bo batlang.

## Tell

O bina lesiba a le mong holim'a thaba, kobo lehetleng, a alositse likhomo - mme molumo oa hae o pheta naha eo a sa kang a tloha ho eona.
