# Position: botho

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Kholeho ea hore motho ke motho ka batho ba bang: hore bohlokoa ba motho bo tsoa sechabeng, le hore motho a le mong ha a phele ka boeena. Ke eona kholeho e ileng ea etsa hore Moshoeshoe a bokelle ba hasaneng 'me a ba amohele, hobane motho ea hlokang lehae ke oa ka, eseng sera. Botho bo behang kamohelo, ho hlomphana, ho thusana le ho amohela moeti pele ho tse ling, 'me bo bonahala litabeng tsohle tsa bophelo ba Mosotho - ho tsoa pitsong ho ea kobo ea moeti.

## Orders

Hore motho o lokela ho amohela ea balehetseng le moeti, hobane le eena ke motho; hore litaba li raroloa 'moho, ka puo le ka boikokobetso, eseng ka motho a le mong; le hore ho hlompha ba bang le ho ba thusa ke tsona tse etsang motho hore e be motho oa nnete har'a sechaba.

## Loses

Bo lahleheloa ke matla ha motho a inahanela eena feela, ha kamohelo le thuso li nyamela; bo lahleheloa, hape, ha lefatše le potlakang le tsebang motho a le mong le hatella botho, ho fihlela motho a lebala hore o motho ka batho, eseng ka boeena.

## Drives

Bo phela ho [Thomas Mofolo](persona_mofolo.md), ea ngotseng botho le bohloko ba motho ka Sesotho 'me a bo boloka ka mongolo. Bo susumetsa Lesotho ho boloka kamohelo, ho hlomphana le thuso e leng motheo oa ho ba Mosotho, hore motho a lule e le motho ka batho ba bang.
