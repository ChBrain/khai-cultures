# Position: setso sa Basotho

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Kamano ea ho ba Mosotho, e hlahileng ha Moshoeshoe oa Pele a bokella ba hasaneng nakong ea lifaqane 'me a ba etsa sechaba se le seng holim'a Thaba Bosiu. E na le [botho](position_botho.md) e le motheo, tumelo ea hore motho ke motho ka batho ba bang; e na le [borena](position_borena.md) bo emetseng Basotho kaofela ho tloha ho Moshoeshoe ho fihlela borenaneng ba kajeno; 'me e na le [lefatše la lithaba](position_lefatshe_la_lithaba.md), naha e le 'ngoe feela e leng holim'a limithara tse sekete kaofela ha eona. E bua [Sesotho](position_language_st.md), puo e nang le mekhahlelo e mengata, 'me e apara kobo lehetleng le mokorotlo hloohong, litšoantšo tseo motho e mong le e mong a li tsebang ho tloha bongoaneng. E ts'oaretse pere, molisa le liphoofolo tsa lithaba, le pina ea famo e phetisang bohloko le thabo ea sechaba lefatšeng ka kantle.

## Orders

E laela hore moeti le ea balehetseng ba amoheloe pele ho tse ling, hobane le bona ke batho; e laela hore litaba li rarolloe pitsong, ka puisano le ka boikokobetso, eseng ka taelo ea motho a le mong; e laela hore kobo e aparoe ka tlhompho, e le pontšo ea boemo le ea bophelo ba Mosotho; le hore lithaba li phelisoe ka bopelo-telele, molisa a alosa a se a khathale, joalo ka ha baholo ba entse khoeling e fetileng.

## Loses

Setso sena se lahleheloa ke matla ha bofuma bo qobella banna le basali ba lithaba ho ea sebetsa mecheng le litoropong tsa Afrika Boroa, ba siea thaba e tletse basali, banana le balisana feela. Se lahleheloa hape ke moeli o tsitsitseng, hobane Lesotho le potapotiloe ke Afrika Boroa ka lehlakore le leng le le leng, 'me Basotho ba bang ba lula ka thoko ho moeli, Foreisetata, ba arohane le naha ea bo-ntat'a bona. Se ripitloa hape ke lefatše le potlakang le tsebang motho a le mong, le ka qhekellang botho ho fihlela motho e se e le motho ho feta ka batho ba bang.

## Drives

Setso sena se susumetsa Mosotho ho itšoara ka batho ba bang, ho tseba boemo ba hae ka moo a phelang le baholo, 'me ho mamella mariha le lehloa a sa lahle seriti. Se etsa hore sengoli se ngole botho ba sechaba ka mongolo, hore molisana a boloke mohlala oa bophelo ba lithaba, le hore sebini se tsamaise molumo oa lithaba lefatšeng ka kantle. Se susumetsa Lesotho ho lula e le naha e nyenyane e phahameng marung, e itšoereng ka botho le ka borena bo emetseng bohle, ha lefatše le fetoha ka lebelo 'me bana ba eona ba e-ea ka kantle ho batla bophelo.
