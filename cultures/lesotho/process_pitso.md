# Process: pitso

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Morena kapa morena oa motse, ha a bitsa sechaba lebaleng ho buisana ka litaba tsa naha kapa tsa motse: banna ba bokana, ba lula fatše, 'me e mong le e mong ea nang le seo a se buang oa fuoa monyetla. Ho hloka feela morena, lebala le batho, 'me sechaba se ka kopana ho buisana le ho rarolla litaba 'moho.

## Direction

Ho ea ho ho buisana le ho rarolla litaba ka sechaba: pitso ke sebaka seo morena a mamelang sechaba 'me sechaba se buang teng, moo ng'oan'a morena a busoang ke molao. Ha se feela kopano empa ke tsela ea ho busa moo matla a morena a tsoang sechabeng, le moo botho bo bonahalang ka hore litaba li raroloa 'moho, eseng ke motho a le mong.

## Lever

E susumetsoa ke kholeho ea hore borena ke ba sechaba, le hore litaba tsa bohle li lokela ho buisanoa ke bohle. E thibella likarohano le pheliso ea litaba ka matla; khahlanong le eona ho ema kotsi ea hore ha sechaba se sa mameloe, kapa ha pitso e fetoha lentsoe la motho a le mong feela, e ka lahleheloa ke matla a eona.

## Echo

Pitso e hlahisa seo [borena](position_borena.md) bo se hlokang: tsela eo sechaba se kopanang ho buisana le ho rarolla litaba ka khotso. Lebaleng lena [Moshoeshoe oa Pele](persona_moshoeshoe_i.md) o mamela sechaba sa hae, a busa sechaba se buang, eseng se khutsitseng, 'me sechaba se tseba hore le sona se na le lentsoe naheng ea sona.
