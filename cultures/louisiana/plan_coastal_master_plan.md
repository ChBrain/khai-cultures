# Plan: the coastal master plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and water engineering designed to protect and restore the disappearing coastal wetlands of the Mississippi River Delta.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Restore wetlands by constructing sediment diversions, rebuilding barrier islands, restoring marsh vegetation, and enforcing flood risk reduction.

## Orders

- To the Coastal Restoration Authority: Coordinate engineering studies for proposed sediment diversion gates.
- To swamp guides like [Jean](persona_jean.md): Advise on local bayou water flow changes to minimize impact on fishing grounds.
- To historic organizations led by members like [Yvette](persona_yvette.md): Document the cultural heritage of communities threatened by relocation.

## Implementation

The sediment diversion gates; the barrier island sand nourishment; and the deployment of wetland delta monitors.
