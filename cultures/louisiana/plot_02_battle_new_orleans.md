# Plot: The Battle of New Orleans 1815

## Taxonomy

[play_louisiana](play_louisiana.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In January 1815, General Andrew Jackson leads a diverse coalition to defend New Orleans from a massive British invasion force.

## Action

The defense unites Creoles, free Black soldiers, Choctaw warriors, and Baratarian pirates under Jackson's command, securing a victory. The [trumpet](piece_trumpet.md) represents the musical and military signaling of the era. Yvette curates military logs from the battle, while Jean guides visitors through the historic bayou defense channels.

## Stage

- [Creole pluralism](position_creole_pluralism.md) : the diverse coalition that united to defend the city.
- [trumpet](piece_trumpet.md) : the brass horn used for battlefield signaling and later integrated into jazz.
- [bayou resilience](position_bayou_resilience.md) : the local knowledge of the swamp channels that aided the defensive strategy.

## Tension

The battle occurs after the signing of the Treaty of Ghent, meaning the massive casualties did not alter the formal peace terms of the war.
