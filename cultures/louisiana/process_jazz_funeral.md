# Process: jazz funerals

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

New Orleans brass bands, social aid clubs, and families, conducted as a funeral procession to honor deceased musicians or community members.

## Direction

From the church or funeral home to the cemetery. Marching slowly with somber hymns (the first line), and transitioning after the burial to upbeat, celebratory jazz songs and dancing (the second line).

## Lever

The unique musical commemoration that balances grief with a celebration of life and community solidarity. It supports local brass bands and cultural clubs, though it faces noise ordinance conflicts in residential areas.

## Echo

The syncopated beat offers a celebratory release. It drives the historical research of [Yvette](persona_yvette.md) in New Orleans, while Cajun guide [Jean](persona_jean.md) respects the practice as a vital expression of Creole resilience.
