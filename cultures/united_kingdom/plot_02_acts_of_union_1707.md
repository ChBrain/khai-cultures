# Plot: the Acts of Union 1707

## Taxonomy

[play_united_kingdom](play_united_kingdom.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

England and Scotland have shared one crown for a century but kept two parliaments and two paths. Debt, trade, and the question of the succession press both kingdoms toward a single state.

## Action

The two parliaments pass matching Acts of Union, and on the first of May they become one: the kingdom of Great Britain, governed from a single parliament at [Westminster](position_westminster.md) in [London](place_london.md). Scotland keeps its own church, law, and schools; the union is a joining of states, not an erasing of nations, and from it the British identity that will hold four nations together is born.

## Stage

- [London](place_london.md): the capital where the united parliament sits.
- [Westminster](position_westminster.md): the single sovereign parliament of the new union state.

## Tension

A union made by treaty and vote, not by conquest, and never wholly settled: it joins the nations under one roof while leaving each its own institutions, and the long question of the British state will be how much union the union can ask of its parts.
