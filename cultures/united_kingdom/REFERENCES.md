# United Kingdom: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind
the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_uk.md](pitch_uk.md) is written from the United Kingdom's profile
on Geert Hofstede's model of national culture. Hofstede publishes a single
profile for Great Britain, and this is the national profile of the UK as a whole;
the constituent nation cultures (England, Scotland, Wales, Northern Ireland) that
follow inherit it as their documented base, since no separate Hofstede sub-scores
are published. The pitch names no dimension and carries no number. The scores and
their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without entering the
prose.

| Dimension                     | Great Britain score (approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 35                            |
| Individualism                 | 89                            |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 66                            |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 35                            |
| Long Term Orientation         | 51                            |
| Indulgence                    | 69                            |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the Country Comparison tool and the
six-dimensions model of national culture). Scores are approximate, as published
by Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group. This is the national profile for
Great Britain; no separate sub-scores for the constituent nations are claimed.

Britain (English worn as the shared public tongue over a heritage language).
