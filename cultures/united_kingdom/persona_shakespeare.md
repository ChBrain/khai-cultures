# Persona: William Shakespeare

## Taxonomy

[class](position_class.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

The playwright and poet whose work became the high-water mark of the English language and, through the union and its reach, the language the world borrowed. A glover's son who wrote for kings and groundlings alike, he made the tongue supple enough for any rank and any feeling. He [writes, reads, and thinks in](process_writing_mother_tongue.md) his mother tongue and shapes it into forms that have framed how English speaks ever since, supple enough for any rank and any feeling, from the throne room to the street. Through the union and its reach, that [common tongue](position_the_common_tongue.md) became the language the world borrowed: there is the gap, a glover's son's English is now spoken and bent by peoples and places he never dreamed of, his words alive on lips that have long forgotten the man who first set them down.

## Action

His plays cross every line of [class](position_class.md), set in the same theatre before lord and labourer, and his coinages and phrases pass into common speech until the language the union carried abroad is half his making.

## Shadow

To crown one figure as the national tongue can flatten the many Englishes the islands actually speak and turn a living language into a monument to be revered rather than used.

## Tell

He reaches the noble and the common with the same lines, trusting that the right words need no rank to be understood.
