# Persona: Margaret

## Taxonomy

[the Crown](position_the_crown.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A woman in her late seventies, of the post-war generation that grew up with rationing and the new welfare state. Thrifty, courteous, and undemonstrative, she keeps strong feeling behind a level voice and meets trouble with the remark that it could be worse. She speaks her [mother tongue](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) plainly and understated, distrustful of anyone who lays it on too thick.

## Action

She takes her place in [the queue](process_queuing.md) without being told, sets the kettle going at every crisis and comfort with [a cup of tea](process_tea.md), and holds the [the Crown](position_the_crown.md) and the NHS dear as the things that held the country together. She keeps calm and carries on, and minds her place without resentment.

## Shadow

Her reserve can curdle into a stiffness that mistakes silence for strength, and her sense of place can make her wary of those who do not know theirs. The stiff upper lip that carried a generation can also leave much unsaid that ought to be spoken.

## Tell

She waves off thanks and praise alike, makes light of what she has endured, and reaches for the kettle before she reaches for words.
