# Process: the football match

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Any pack of boys with a ball, or a ball made of rags and string, on any dusty lot in Monrovia or any clearing up country: the barefoot game after school, the big match on the radio with the whole quarter listening, the day the Lone Star team plays and the country stops to pull for it. It takes only a ball and an open piece of ground, ya hear, and the game is on.

## Direction

Toward pride, joy, and the one people pulling together: the football field is where it does not matter whose grandfather came on the ship and whose was always here, where the boy from the waterside and the boy from the interior play on the same side and the whole nation cheers as one. It is the great common joy of the country, the dream that a poor boy can rise to the top of the world, and the thing that makes the whole of Liberia, settler and son of the soil, one crowd with one heart.

## Lever

It is driven by the love of the game that needs no money, only a ball and a will, and by a country that has known hard times and finds in the match a pride and a joy that cost nothing and lift everyone. It holds against poverty and division, the field open to all; against it stand the danger of waiting on one great star to lift the whole nation, and the long road from the dusty lot to the wide world.

## Echo

The football match makes audible what [the love of liberty](position_love_of_liberty.md) needs: a common ground where every son of the country, of whatever heritage, stands equal and the whole nation pulls as one. Onto this field [George Weah](persona_weah.md) once stepped as a poor boy from the waterside and carried the name of Liberia to the top of the world.
