## Cue

In July 1945, the Manhattan Project detonates the first nuclear weapon at the Alamogordo bombing range.

## Action

The detonation establishes the state as a nuclear physics hub, developing [nuclear modeling](process_nuclear_modeling.md). The [trinitite glass](piece_trinitite_glass.md) specimen represents this nuclear era. Mateo calibrates laboratory spectrometer instrumentation, while Lupita coordinates regional radiation health archives.

## Stage

- [trinitite glass](piece_trinitite_glass.md) : the green glass specimen formed by the atomic heat.

## Tension
