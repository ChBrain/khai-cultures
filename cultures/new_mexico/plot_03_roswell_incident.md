## Cue

In July 1947, a high-altitude military balloon crashes near Roswell, launching global UFO speculations.

## Action

The incident establishes regional aerospace and defense research, developing [the Rio Grande plan](plan_rio_grande_water_conservation.md). Mateo audits weather balloon sensor systems, while Lupita documents traditional village astronomy records.

## Stage

- [desert science](position_desert_science.md) : the scientific research, balloon telemetry, and aerospace safety.
- [Taos Pueblo](place_taos_pueblo.md) : the historic pueblo site that served as a cultural preservation anchor.
- [plan_rio_grande_water_conservation](plan_rio_grande_water_conservation.md) : the water plan designed to regulate regional river irrigation.

## Tension

Military secrecy over the crash fuels persistent public suspicion of federal surveillance and space research operations.
