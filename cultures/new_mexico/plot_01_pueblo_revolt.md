# Plot: The Pueblo Revolt 1680

## Owner

## Cue

In August 1680, Popé organizes a coordinated revolt among Pueblo villages, successfully displacing Spanish colonial rule.

## Action

The revolt secures territorial control for native villages, developing [pueblo heritage](position_pueblo_heritage.md). The [pottery vessel](piece_pottery_vessel.md) represents traditional arts. Lupita teaches traditional pottery firing at community centers, while Mateo records historical architectural plans of early adobe structures.

## Stage

- [pueblo heritage](position_pueblo_heritage.md) : the indigenous resistance, family resourcefulness, and adobe architectural pride.
- [pottery vessel](piece_pottery_vessel.md) : the coiled clay jar representing traditional artistic craft.
- [Taos Pueblo](place_taos_pueblo.md) : the historic multi-story adobe dwelling of the Pueblo people.

## Tension

Spanish forces violently reclaim the territory decades later, initiating long-term land control and religious polarization.
