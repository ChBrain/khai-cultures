# Plan: the Rio Grande plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and watershed science designed to manage agricultural irrigation and protect the Rio Grande Basin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Conserve basin flows by regulating irrigation diversions, restoring riparian wetlands, upgrading water recycling systems, and restricting new water permits.

## Orders

- To the Office of the State Engineer: Enforce river flow allocations and audit irrigation claims.
- To solar lab technicians like [Mateo](persona_mateo.md): Support digital water telemetry networks and implement water-efficient laboratory cooling systems.
- To Pueblo land managers like [Lupita](persona_lupita.md): Coordinate community wetland surveys and maintain traditional conservation methods.

## Implementation

The river flow measurements; the riparian wetland surveys; and the deployment of aquifer water quality monitors.
