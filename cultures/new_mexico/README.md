# New Mexico: a khai culture

New Mexico staged as a full khai play, ISO US-NM, language en. The Land of Enchantment State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Pueblo revolt, nuclear Trinity test, and Roswell incident encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_new_mexico.md](play_new_mexico.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_new_mexico.md](pitch_new_mexico.md), the New Mexico key.
- **Plots (history):** the Pueblo Revolt of 1680, the Trinity Test of 1945, and the Roswell Incident of 1947.
- **Personas:** Mateo (solar lab technician) and Lupita (Pueblo potter), written in English.
- **Positions:** pueblo heritage (indigenous resistance/adobe architectural tradition), desert science (advanced scientific research/aerospace technology).
- **Places:** Los Alamos, Taos Pueblo.
- **Processes:** pottery firing (outdoor clay baking), nuclear modeling (scientific lab simulation).
- **Pieces:** a pottery vessel, a trinitite glass specimen.
- **Plan:** the Rio Grande conservation plan.
