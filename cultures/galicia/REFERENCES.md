# Galicia: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch (kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_galicia.md](pitch_galicia.md) is written from Galicia's profile inflected by its Atlantic trade history, fishing, and rural micro-parish isolation, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (Spain national base adjusted for Galicia's family networks, mini-parish structures, coastal cofradías, and maritime frugality).
