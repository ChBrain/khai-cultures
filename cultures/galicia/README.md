# Galicia: a khai culture

Galicia staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-GA, language es. Galiza: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Rexurdimento, Great Emigration, and Statute of Autonomy encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_galicia.md](play_galicia.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_galicia.md](pitch_galicia.md), the Galicia key.
- **Plots (history):** the Rexurdimento revival of 1863, the Great Emigration of the late 19th century, and the Statute of Autonomy of 1981.
- **Personas:** Xoán (Atlantic fisherman) and Rosalía (bagpiper and artisan), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** atlantic maritime (ocean reliance/maritime resource management/fishing ports), galician identity (melancholy/morriña/oral poetry/Galician language).
- **Places:** Rías Baixas, Santiago Cathedral.
- **Processes:** octopus cooking (traditional octopus boiling), shellfishing (shellfish gathering).
- **Pieces:** a gaita, a pote.
- **Plan:** the Rías estuary protection plan.
