# Azerbaijan: a khai culture

Azerbaijan staged as a full khai play, ISO AZ, language az. The Land of Fire
(Odlar Yurdu), where an ancient flame, the deep music of mugham, the heritage of
poetry, the loom, and the open table keep an old culture alive, presented as a
theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its
language and faith encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects,
all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_azerbaijan.md](play_azerbaijan.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_az.md](pitch_az.md), the Hofstede layer, written from
  Azerbaijan's profile (strong hospitality and the bond of family and elders, a
  long word and an unhurried table, the weight of the group and of what people
  will say, and a quiet endurance under a warm surface) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Land of Fire (the ancient sacred flame and the
  Zoroastrian heritage), Nizami and the Age of the Word (the twelfth-century poet
  of the Khamsa), Where the Old Met the New (the modern rebirth of drama and
  music), and the Living Mugham (the art carried on from master to pupil to the
  world's stages).
- **Personas:** Nizami Ganjavi, Uzeyir Hajibeyli, Mirza Fatali Akhundov, and
  Alim Qasimov as the historic cast, plus one archetype, the Ashiq (the bardic
  minstrel of the saz who carries the spoken word). Each is wired to the language
  engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** the Land of Fire (the ancient flame as the soul of the land,
  from the Zoroastrian fire to the lamp of oil), mugham and the word (the two
  strings of the people's heart, the deep classical music and the heights of
  poetry), and hospitality (the guest as a gift of God and the open table).
- **Places:** Baku (the walled old city of Icherisheher and the Maiden Tower on
  the Caspian) and Ganja (the city of Nizami and the birthplace of the word).
- **Processes:** mugham (the deep classical music handed from master to pupil)
  and Novruz (the spring new year of fire and renewal).
- **Pieces:** the carpet (a national art woven over generations) and the tar (the
  beloved stringed heart of mugham).
