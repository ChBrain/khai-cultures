# Azerbaijan: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain cultural and historical
sources behind the plots, personas, places, processes, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_az.md](pitch_az.md) is written from Azerbaijan's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
Azerbaijan is not always listed individually in the published comparison; where a
direct national score is not published, the profile is informed by the regional
and neighbouring data and by the documented cultural traits (strong collectivism
and family bonds, high power distance and respect for elders and authority, and a
relatively high uncertainty avoidance). The orientation is recorded here so the
basis is on the record without entering the prose.

| Dimension             | Azerbaijan orientation (approx.) |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Power Distance        | high                             |
| Individualism         | low (collectivist)               |
| Motivation            | moderate                         |
| Uncertainty Avoidance | high                             |
| Long Term Orientation | moderate                         |
| Indulgence            | low to moderate (restrained)     |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the six-dimensions model of national
culture and the Country Comparison tool). The orientation is approximate and, for
Azerbaijan, informed by regional data and documented cultural traits as published
by Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group.
