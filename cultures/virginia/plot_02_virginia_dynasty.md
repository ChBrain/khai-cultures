## Cue

Starting in 1789, a succession of Virginian leaders dominates the early presidency, shaping the nation's constitutional structure.

## Action

The leadership establishes [Cradle of Presidents](position_cradle_of_presidents.md) principles of constitutional governance and public service. The [draft constitution](piece_draft_constitution.md) represents this legislative legacy. Thomas presents early drafts of the Bill of Rights, while Sasha analyzes the modern policy structures derived from these founding frameworks.

## Stage

- [Cradle of Presidents](position_cradle_of_presidents.md) : the legacy of constitutional design, public service, and political leadership.
- [draft constitution](piece_draft_constitution.md) : the constitutional documents drafted and debated by Virginian statesmen.
- [Colonial Williamsburg](place_colonial_williamsburg.md) : the historic capital where early legislative debates took place.

## Tension

While designing a framework of liberty, the Virginian presidents remain slaveholders, exposing a profound moral contradiction that would haunt the republic.
