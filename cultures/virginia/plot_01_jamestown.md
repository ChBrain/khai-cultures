# Plot: Jamestown Founding 1607

## Taxonomy

[play_virginia](play_virginia.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In May 1607, English colonists land on the James River and establish Jamestown, the first permanent English settlement in North America.

## Action

The settlement establishes [Cavalier heritage](position_cavalier_heritage.md) trade patterns, transitioning from a desperate search for gold to a lucrative tobacco export economy. The [tobacco pipe](piece_tobacco_pipe.md) becomes a symbol of this trade. Thomas curates early agricultural artifacts at Williamsburg, while Sasha studies the historical logistics of early Atlantic shipping.

## Stage

- [Cavalier heritage](position_cavalier_heritage.md) : the traditional agrarian social structure and connection to English trade.
- [tobacco curing](process_tobacco_curing.md) : the agricultural process of drying leaves that saved the colony from bankruptcy.
- [Thomas](persona_thomas.md) : the historic interpreter who explains the early colonial economy to visitors.

## Tension

The growth of the tobacco plantations leads to the displacement of the Powhatan confederacy and the introduction of enslaved Africans in 1619, setting a tragic labor precedent.
