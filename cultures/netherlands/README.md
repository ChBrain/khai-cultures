# Netherlands: a khai culture

The Netherlands staged as a full khai play, ISO NL, languages nl and fy. A low country
won from the sea, staged as a theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as the
cast, the roles its languages encode, its places, its practices, and its defining
objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that
holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its Estate; a
culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_netherlands.md](play_netherlands.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_nl.md](pitch_nl.md), the Hofstede layer, written from the Dutch
  profile (strongly egalitarian and consensual, individualist, sober and caring rather
  than competitive) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Revolt against Spain, the Golden Age, the North Sea flood of
  1953 and the Delta Works, and the tolerant turn of the 1960s - each casting the
  elements it needs.
- **Personas:** Willem van Oranje (father of the fatherland) and Erasmus (the humanist
  who wrote Europe in Latin) as the historic cast, plus three archetypes: Riet (the
  sober, consensual older generation), Daan (the young Dutch life lived half in
  English), and Baukje (a Frisian speaker of the second official tongue). Each is wired
  to the language engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** gedogen (pragmatic tolerance), het poldermodel (consensus by overleg),
  it Frysk (the Frisian tongue, the minority language).
- **Places:** Amsterdam (the merchant city of the Golden Age and the sixties), Zeeland
  (the drowned delta and the Delta Works).
- **Processes:** de borrel (the convivial drink), fietsen (the everyday equality of the
  bicycle).
- **Pieces:** de tulp (the flower and the first speculative bubble), de molen (the
  windmill that drained the land from the sea).
- **Plan:** leven met het water, the standing mandate to live with the sea rather than
  defeat it.
