## Cue

In May 1911, Ray Harroun wins the first 500-mile race at the newly opened Indianapolis Motor Speedway.

## Action

The race establishes the speedway as the global center of motor sports, developing [motor speed](position_motor_speed.md) standards. The [checkered flag](piece_checkered_flag.md) represents this racing era. Ray tunes performance engines for speedway runs, while Helen coordinates local tourism logistics during race weekends.

## Stage

- [motor speed](position_motor_speed.md) : the engineering ambition, mechanical skill, and competitive speedway drive.
- [checkered flag](piece_checkered_flag.md) : the silk checkered flag used to signal race completion.
- [Indianapolis Speedway](place_indianapolis_speedway.md) : the historic brick-paved speedway.

## Tension

Early races feature high driver and spectator injury rates, prompting severe safety debates and the development of modern safety barriers.
