## Initiated by

Quarry stonemasons, mechanical saw operators, and block carvers, conducted year-round in Lawrence County quarries and mills.

## Direction

At the quarry mill. Aligning diamond-edge saw blades, spraying water cooling jets, cutting extracted limestone blocks, and hand-chiseling decorative building details.

## Lever

The traditional industrial extraction process that built the state's quarry economy. It supplies architectural stone to national building projects, though it is limited by seasonal weather changes and produces heavy quarry dust.
