# Plot: The Limestone Quarrying Boom 1880

## Taxonomy

[play_indiana](play_indiana.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the 1880s, Lawrence and Monroe county quarries adopt steam-powered channelers, launching massive stone extraction.

## Action

The boom establishes Bedford limestone as the nation's premier building material, developing [limestone cutting](process_limestone_cutting.md). Ray audits mechanical quarry saws, while Helen coordinates agricultural soil protection around quarry boundaries.

## Stage

- [engine tuning](process_engine_tuning.md) : the mechanical calibration used to maintain quarry extraction saws.
- [limestone cutting](process_limestone_cutting.md) : the traditional splitting and carving of stone blocks.
- [plan_agricultural_soil_conservation](plan_agricultural_soil_conservation.md) : the conservation plan developed to mitigate regional soil erosion.

## Tension

The open-pit quarries permanently alter local forest habitats and contaminate shallow limestone groundwater aquifers with silt.
