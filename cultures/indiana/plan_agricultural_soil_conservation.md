# Plan: the soil plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and agricultural science designed to manage soil erosion and reduce nutrient runoff in crop fields.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Conserve agricultural soil by mandating winter cover crops, establishing grassed waterways, promoting no-till planting, and auditing tile drainage water.
