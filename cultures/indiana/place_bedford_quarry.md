# Place: Bedford Quarry

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The deep open-pit limestone quarries in Lawrence County, featuring high vertical stone walls, massive derrick cranes, and block storage yards.

## Holds

- the extraction history in the plot [The Limestone Quarrying Boom 1880](plot_03_limestone_boom.md) : the limestone capital.

## Offers

The quarry tours and historical stone carving archives where [Helen](persona_helen.md) monitors agricultural conservation boundaries.

## Withheld

The immigrant labor exploitation. While celebrated for supplying stone to national monuments, the quarries historically relied on low-wage immigrant stonecutters who faced severe workplace injuries and silicosis diseases, histories omitted from corporate quarry archives.
