# Saxony (Sachsen): a khai culture

Saxony staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-SN, language de. The eastern state of Germany: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its artistic, industrial, and democratic life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_saxony.md](play_saxony.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_saxony.md](pitch_saxony.md), the Saxony key.
- **Plots (history):** the reign of Augustus the Strong, Saxony's rise as an industrial pioneer, and the Peaceful Revolution of 1989.
- **Personas:** Günter (traditional porcelain craftsman) and Peggy (modern microelectronics engineer), written in German.
- **Positions:** der sächsische Erfindergeist (ingenuity), der Barockstolz (baroque cultural pride), der Bürgerprotest (protest legacy).
- **Places:** die Frauenkirche in Dresden, das Silicon Saxony (semiconductor hub).
- **Processes:** die Porzellanherstellung (making Meissen porcelain).
- **Pieces:** das Meissener Porzellan (porcelain), der Leipziger Aufruf (non-violence declaration of Oct 9, 1989).
- **Plan:** der Ausbau von Silicon Saxony (semiconductor expansion plan).
