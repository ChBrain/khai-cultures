# Piece: jollof rice

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

In the big black pot at every owambe, on the plate at Christmas and Sallah, at the buka and the bukateria and the canteen, and in the heart of every Nigerian abroad who misses home: rice cooked deep in a rich tomato and pepper stew until each grain is red and full of flavour, with the prized smoky bottom, the party jollof that no celebration is complete without. The dish a Nigerian will defend to the last breath, sure beyond all argument that naija jollof is the best on the whole continent, no contest, end of discussion.

## Load Bearing

It stands for the pride and the warmth of the Nigerian table and the joy of the gathering. In a country of many peoples, jollof is one thing the whole giant agrees on, the dish that crowns every owambe, every wedding, every festival, the food of celebration and of welcome. To be served a good plate of party jollof, smoky from the firewood pot, is to be told you are family and the day is special. And it is national swagger on a plate, the friendly jollof war with the neighbours that every Nigerian fights with their whole chest, because pride in the pot is pride in the country. Without this dish the Nigerian feast would lose its very centre.

## Apparent

A plate of orange-red rice, grains cooked in tomato, pepper, onion, and spice, often served with chicken or beef, fried plantain, and a little coleslaw on the side. Plain enough on the surface, just rice and stew; but its whole weight is in the gathering it crowns, the smoky bottom that marks the cook's skill, and the fierce love and pride a whole nation has poured into one pot.

## Yearbook

- Carried across West Africa from the old empires of the region and made, in the Nigerian hand, the unmissable centre of every celebration.
- Cooked in the great party pot with the prized smoky base, the mark of the owambe and the festival table.
- Sent around the world with the Nigerian diaspora and defended, in the friendly jollof war, as simply the best there is.
