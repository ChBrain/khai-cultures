# Plan: naija no dey carry last

## Taxonomy

The standing charge the giant of Africa has laid on itself, the thing the whole country swears with its full chest: that a land sewn from over two hundred and fifty nations will hold itself together as one and rise to the greatness its people already carry in their bones. It is the unfinished work of keeping [the peoples](position_the_peoples.md) one Nigeria while each remains fully what it is, of carrying every person inside [the extended family](position_the_extended_family.md) so that nobody dances alone, and of holding [faith](position_faith.md) as the blessing that joins the giant rather than the wound that divides it. Na one country we get, and one country we go keep.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

To turn the giant's enormous energy and its bigger swagger into the greatness that matches it, so that the abundance of a blessed land reaches the table of the ordinary person and not only the headline. To keep the union whole and the peoples one, holding the many together when the question of who gets what is asked along the lines of blood instead of the common good. To carry the sober chapter the country grieves with dignity, never reopening the wound and never courting such sorrow again, but keeping the union that was kept. And to lift up the one who carries the many, the breadwinner and the market woman who feed a whole compound, so that the back that bears the load does not break under it. Slow and steady, with God and small-small patience, the country swears that tomorrow go better, that naija no dey carry last.

## Orders

- To the one who rises, like [Mama Market](persona_mama_market.md): lift the others as you climb and never eat alone, but do not let the many hands held out grind you to the ground; the one who eats alone dies alone, but the one who carries everybody must be carried too.
- To the artist and the conscience, like [Wole Soyinka](persona_soyinka.md) and [Chinua Achebe](persona_achebe.md): tell the country's story whole, the glory and the grief both, neither flattering the giant nor abandoning it, for the story of Africa must no longer be told only by the hunter.
- To the giant itself: hold the many as one with care, keep the church and the mosque standing side by side, and turn the swagger into the substance, so that what the people feel in their bones becomes what they eat at their table.

## Implementation

One of the youngest and fastest-growing nations on earth, the most populous on the whole continent, that has already proven the giant can roar, Nollywood and Afrobeats and the literature carrying the country's voice to every corner of the world on the road [Fela Anikulapo Kuti](persona_fela.md) cut, the markets and the hustle that never sleep, and that has come through its sober chapter and held itself together. The roar is true and earned; the work is not finished. The giant aches still at the gap between the greatness it knows it carries and the ordinary table it has not yet fully reached, and carries forward the long, central business of being one out of so very many.
