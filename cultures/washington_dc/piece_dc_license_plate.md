# Piece: license plate

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Mounted on all registered passenger vehicles in the District of Columbia.

## Load Bearing

It represents the District's ongoing protest against lack of voting representation in Congress, making every vehicle a mobile political message.

## Apparent

A rectangular metal vehicle plate, white with red lettering, featuring the city's flag design and the slogan "Taxation Without Representation".
