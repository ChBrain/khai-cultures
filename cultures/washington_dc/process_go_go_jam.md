# Process: go-go jams

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Local D.C. go-go bands and percussionists, performed at community centers, street festivals, and clubs across the District.

## Direction

On the stage and street corners. Playing polyrhythmic drum beats, using cowbells and congas to maintain a continuous, non-stop dance groove, and calling out to the audience.

## Lever

The distinct municipal musical style that defines local Black D.C. culture and solidarity. It creates a strong sense of community belonging, though it faces noise complaints and venue closures due to gentrification.

## Echo

The polyrhythmic beat represents local identity. It drives the musical performances of [Marcus](persona_marcus.md) on U Street, while [Eleanor](persona_eleanor.md) hears the sound as a vital part of the city's living culture.
