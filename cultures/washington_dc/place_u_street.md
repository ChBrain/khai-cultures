## Owner

## Shown

The historic U Street corridor in Northwest D.C., lined with storefronts, jazz clubs, and local restaurants.

## Holds

- the local civil rights history in the plot [The Home Rule Act 1973](plot_03_home_rule_act.md) : the Black Broadway of D.C.

## Offers

The music clubs and community spaces where [Marcus](persona_marcus.md) organizes go-go performances.

## Withheld

The displacement caused by gentrification. While celebrated today as a center of culture, rising property prices have pushed out many historic Black-owned businesses, a change ignored by tourist guides.
