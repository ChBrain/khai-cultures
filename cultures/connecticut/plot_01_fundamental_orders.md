# Plot: The Fundamental Orders 1639

## Owner

## Cue

In January 1639, representative settlers from Hartford, Windsor, and Wethersfield adopt the Fundamental Orders.

## Action

The orders establish representative self-government, developing [constitutional order](position_constitutional_order.md) rules. The [Yale charter](piece_yale_charter.md) represents early legal records. Abigail audits historic colonial town documents, while Charles records oral histories of early shipbuilding families.

## Stage

- [constitutional order](position_constitutional_order.md) : the rule of law, institutional heritage, and representative assembly.
- [Yale charter](piece_yale_charter.md) : the collegiate charter representing early institutional structure.
- [place_yale_quadrangle](place_yale_quadrangle.md) : the historic gothic university campus.

## Tension

The early constitution restricts voting rights to a limited group of puritan church members, excluding native and non-conforming residents.
