# Plan: the little red dot

## Taxonomy

The open mandate the nation set itself the day it stood alone: to make a tiny, resource-poor, multiracial island survive and thrive in a world that owes it nothing. It is the work of keeping [multiracial harmony](position_multiracial_harmony.md) across race, language, and religion; of governing by the [pragmatism and meritocracy](position_pragmatism.md) that turns scarcity into strength; and of staying the [global port-city](position_the_port_city.md) the world has reason to route its trade and trust through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Stay indispensable - keep the port, the airport, and the institutions so reliable that the world chooses to pass through them; keep the peace among the communities, guarding the trust across race and faith as the first condition of survival; house, school, and provide for the people so that all have a stake and a fair chance to rise by merit; defend the small nation through the vigilance of Total Defence, the whole society ready; and renew the discipline and the openness in each new generation, so the success does not breed the complacency that ends it.

## Orders

- To the leader like [Lee Kuan Yew](persona_lee_kuan_yew.md): govern by can or cannot, by what lasts, keep the system clean and the law even, and never let the discipline forget the people it serves lah.
- To the citizen like [the hawker](persona_the_hawker.md): keep the common table set and the craft alive, and meet every neighbour at it across race and tongue, makan together first, talk later.
- To the nation: stay useful to the world, keep faith among the communities, and renew the vigilance of the small in every generation, never assume safe is forever.

## Implementation

A nation a single generation from a developing port, now among the wealthiest and most ordered cities on earth, that turned no resources into a global crossroads, housed almost all its people, and kept a peace across communities that many larger places could not - all this from one little red dot, you know. Steady, but the work is unfinished still: in the cost of living and the pressure of the meritocracy, in keeping the home languages alive alongside the common English, in the ageing of the pioneer generation and the hawker trades, and in the standing question of whether a people born into security can keep the watchfulness that won it.

## Targets

- [x] Survived the separation of 1965 and built a sovereign nation from a port with no resources.
- [x] Housed almost the whole population in the HDB heartlands, with the communities living mixed and most owning their homes.
- [x] Made the National Pledge and the multiracial model the daily creed of one people regardless of race, language, or religion.
- [x] Built one of the world's busiest ports and air hubs and rose from a developing port to a global city.
- [W] Keep the home languages and the hawker and arts heritage alive alongside the common working English.
- [W] Ease the pressure of cost and meritocracy so that no community feels left behind on the ladder.
- [ ] Pass the vigilance and discipline of the small nation to a generation that has only known success.
