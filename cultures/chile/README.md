# Chile: a khai culture

Chile staged as a full khai play, ISO CL, language es. A long sober land of poets
between the Andes and the sea, reckoning with its memory, as a theatre: its
history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its language and its
restraint encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned
by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_chile.md](play_chile.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_es.md](pitch_es.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Chile's
  profile (a sober restraint and reserve, a strong attachment to order and the
  law, a modesty where neighbors are grand, and a patient resilience to
  catastrophe) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Mapuche and the Conquest, Independence (1818),
  Saltpeter and Copper, and the Coup and the Memory - each casting the elements it
  needs, and each staging the country's order and its wound honestly and on the
  public record.
- **Personas:** Bernardo O'Higgins, Pablo Neruda, Gabriela Mistral, and Violeta
  Parra as the historic cast, plus one defining archetype, el minero (the miner of
  the north, the backbone of the mining nation, resilient in the dark). Each is
  wired to the language engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** the country of poets (verse as the national voice and the two
  Nobel laureates), order and restraint (the sober stable legalist republic), and
  memory and the wound (the long reckoning with 1973 and the resilience of the
  people).
- **Places:** Santiago (the capital in the central valley, La Moneda and the
  republic's history) and the Atacama Desert (the driest desert, the nitrate and
  copper north, the clearest skies on earth).
- **Processes:** the Fiestas Patrias (the September dieciocho, the fonda, the
  cueca, and the country's one season of open joy) and the once (the daily
  late-afternoon tea, the modest constant rite of the household).
- **Pieces:** copper (the wage of Chile, the red metal that funds the nation) and
  the empanada (the empanada de pino, the homely food of the festival and the
  family table).

## Provenance

The Hofstede scores behind the pitch and the public-domain historical sources are
recorded in [REFERENCES.md](REFERENCES.md). The data never enters the prose; the
pitch is its invisible hand. Sources in the public domain are credited, never
claimed; the conquest, the 1973 coup, the dictatorship, and the human-rights
reckoning are treated soberly on the public record, naming the facts and the
nation's memory without graphic detail and taking no political side; the staging
and the architecture are original work.
