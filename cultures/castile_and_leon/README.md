# Castile and León: a khai culture

Castile and León staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-CL, language es. Castilla y León: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Union of 1230, Comuneros Revolt, and Duero Treaty encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_castile_and_leon.md](play_castile_and_leon.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_castile_and_leon.md](pitch_castile_and_leon.md), the Castile and León key.
- **Plots (history):** the Union of Castile and León of 1230, the Comuneros Revolt of 1520, and the Duero river water treaty.
- **Personas:** Mateo (dryland wheat farmer) and Teresa (sheep dairy artisan), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** meseta reliance (agricultural plateau/traditional farming/sobriety), pasture rights (historical grazing/mesta charters).
- **Places:** Aqueduct of Segovia, Duero Valley.
- **Processes:** roast pig (wood-fired cochinillo roasting), grain harvesting (dryland cereal harvest).
- **Pieces:** roasting clay dish, shepherd leather bag.
- **Plan:** the Duero water conservation plan.
