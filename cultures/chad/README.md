# Chad: a khai culture

Chad staged as a full khai play, ISO TD, language ar (Chadian Arabic over a
Modern Standard matrix). A landlocked nation that is not one people but a weave,
as a theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its
language and faith encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects,
all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the house that holds
this production and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its Estate; a
culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_chad.md](play_chad.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_ar.md](pitch_ar.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Chad's
  profile (the group before the individual, respect for elders and the spoken
  word, patience under hardship, dignity and reserve, and above all the manners
  of the neighbour that let many peoples live side by side) and naming no
  dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Green Sahara and its rock art, the Sao and their
  terracottas, the empire of Kanem-Bornu, and the modern weave of peoples on the
  shrinking shore - each casting the elements it needs.
- **Personas:** Idris Alooma, Joseph Brahim Seid, Baba Moustapha, and Clement
  Masdongar as the historic cast, plus one archetype, the Herder (the herder who
  crosses the land behind the rain as his forebears did). Each is wired to the
  language engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** the Weave (a nation held not by blood but by shared land, a
  shared tongue, and the manners of the neighbour), the Lake (the eye of water
  that feeds all and recedes year by year), the Long Pasture (the whole land as
  one long pasture, the herder older than any kingdom).
- **Places:** Lake Chad (the eye of water the country is named for) and the
  Ennedi (the sandstone plateau whose rock art remembers a green world).
- **Processes:** the transhumance (the herder's long crossing behind the rain),
  the three teas (the desert hospitality of atay, the cup as a pact between
  neighbours).
- **Pieces:** the Sao terracotta (the oldest human face and the oldest art known
  from this land) and the balafon (the voice of the green south that crosses the
  line language cannot).

## Provenance

The Hofstede scores behind the pitch and the public-domain historical sources are
recorded in [REFERENCES.md](REFERENCES.md). The data never enters the prose; the
pitch is its invisible hand. Sources in the public domain are credited, never
claimed; the country's many peoples and faiths are staged with equal dignity, and
its modern conflicts are out of scope; the staging and the architecture are
original work.
