# Brandenburg: a khai culture

Brandenburg staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-BB, language de. The state surrounding Berlin: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Prussian, agricultural, and modern energy life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_brandenburg.md](play_brandenburg.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_brandenburg.md](pitch_brandenburg.md), the Brandenburg key.
- **Plots (history):** the rise of Brandenburg-Prussia, the building of Sanssouci, and the re-establishment of the state in 1990.
- **Personas:** Ulrich (traditional forester/nature warden) and Nadja (modern solar park manager), written in German.
- **Positions:** die märkische Ruhe (quiet simplicity), preußische Tugenden (Prussian virtues), die Wende-Erfahrung (reunification adaptation).
- **Places:** Schloss Sanssouci (Sanssouci Palace), der Spreewald (biosphere canals).
- **Processes:** die Spreewälder Kahnfahrt (punting boat tours).
- **Pieces:** die Spreewälder Gurke (gherkin), der Kartoffelbefehl (potato decree).
- **Plan:** die Solarenergieoffensive (solar energy plan).
