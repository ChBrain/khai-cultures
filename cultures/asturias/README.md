# Asturias: a khai culture

Asturias staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-AS, language es. Asturias: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Battle of Covadonga, Asturian Miners Strike, and Picos de Europa Park encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_asturias.md](play_asturias.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_asturias.md](pitch_asturias.md), the Asturias key.
- **Plots (history):** the Battle of Covadonga of 722, the Asturian Miners' Strike of 1934, and the Picos de Europa National Park of 1995.
- **Personas:** Covadonga (Cabrales cheese artisan) and Xuan (coal miner and unionist), written in Spanish with local Asturian co-official features.
- **Positions:** cantabrian mountain (high limestone peaks/isolation/miners' solidarity), asturian language (astur-leonese identity/linguistic pride).
- **Places:** Covadonga Sanctuary, Picos de Europa.
- **Processes:** cider pouring (pouring cider from height), cabrales aging (cave cheese aging).
- **Pieces:** cider glass, miner lamp.
- **Plan:** the Cantabrian bear conservation plan.
