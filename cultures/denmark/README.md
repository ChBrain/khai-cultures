# Denmark: a khai culture

Denmark staged as a full khai play, ISO DK, language da. A small, flat, trusting
country as a theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the
roles its language encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects,
all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_denmark.md](play_denmark.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_dk.md](pitch_dk.md), the Hofstede layer, written from
  Denmark's profile (flat, trusting, ironic, the good-enough over the grand) and
  naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Jelling stones (~965), the Constitution of 1849, the
  defeat of 1864, and the occupation and rescue of the Jews (1943) - each casting
  the elements it needs (the rescue casts a place and a piece, no persona).
- **Personas:** Harald Bluetooth and N.F.S. Grundtvig as the historic cast, plus
  two defining archetypes, Inger (older, the welfare-state modesty) and Mads
  (younger, cycling, design, the open world). Each is wired to the language
  engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** Janteloven (the law of modesty), du-formen (the universal du),
  kongehuset (the crown - the world's oldest).
- **Places:** København (the capital), Jelling (the nation's birthplace).
- **Processes:** hyggen, pyt.
- **Pieces:** Dannebrog (the flag), rugbrødet (rye bread).
