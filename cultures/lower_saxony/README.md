# Lower Saxony (Niedersachsen): a khai culture

Lower Saxony staged as a full khai play, ISO DE-NI, language de. The second largest state of Germany by land area: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its agricultural and industrial life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_lower_saxony.md](play_lower_saxony.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_lower_saxony.md](pitch_lower_saxony.md), the Lower Saxony key.
- **Plots (history):** the Prussian annexation of Hanover in 1866, the creation of the modern state in 1946, and the rise of Volkswagen.
- **Personas:** Gerhard (traditional East Frisian farmer) and Antje (modern VW production planner), written in German.
- **Positions:** die Bodenständigkeit (down-to-earth pragmatism), die Industrie (industrial size), die Küstenkultur (coastal resilience).
- **Places:** Ostfriesland (East Frisia), Wolfsburg (Volkswagen factory).
- **Processes:** die Ostfriesische Teezeremonie (tea ceremony).
- **Pieces:** das Teegeschirr (tea set), der VW Käfer (Volkswagen Beetle).
- **Plan:** der Windenergieausbau (offshore wind energy plan).
