# Minnesota: a khai culture

Minnesota staged as a full khai play, ISO US-MN, language en. The Land of 10,000 Lakes: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its iron range mining, wilderness lake protections, and Minneapolis sound music innovations encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_minnesota.md](play_minnesota.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_minnesota.md](pitch_minnesota.md), the Minnesota key.
- **Plots (history):** the Mesabi Iron Range discovery of 1890, the Boundary Waters Canoe Area Wilderness Act of 1978, and the Minneapolis sound recording boom in the 1980s.
- **Personas:** Lars (Boundary Waters wilderness guide) and Samantha (Twin Cities medical device engineer), written in English.
- **Positions:** Minnesota nice (civic pride/social cooperation), northwoods resilience (winter endurance/wilderness survival).
- **Places:** Boundary Waters, Mesabi Range.
- **Processes:** medical device assembly (cleanroom engineering), wilderness portage (canoe carrying).
- **Pieces:** a canvas canoe, a pacemaker.
- **Plan:** the Minnesota River agricultural plan.
