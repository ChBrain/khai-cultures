# Process: wilderness portage

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Boundary Waters canoeists, wilderness guides, and outdoor recreation groups, conducted during the ice-free spring and summer seasons.

## Direction

Along remote wilderness trails. Lifting the canvas canoe onto shoulders, carrying heavy gear packs over rocky forest terrain, and launching the canoe into the next lake.

## Lever

The traditional transportation process that enables motor-free wilderness exploration. It supports the regional recreation economy and preserves historic routes, though it demands high physical stamina and increases injury risks.

## Echo

The portage trails require careful footing. The process defines the daily guiding work of [Lars](persona_lars.md) in the north, while engineer [Samantha](persona_samantha.md) values the method as an early model of structured sequence.
