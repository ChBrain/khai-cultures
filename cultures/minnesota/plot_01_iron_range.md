# Plot: The Iron Range Boom 1890

## Taxonomy

[play_minnesota](play_minnesota.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the 1890s, mining prospectors discover massive iron ore deposits in the Mesabi Range, launching industrial mining.

## Action

The discovery establishes [northwoods resilience](position_northwoods_resilience.md) mining settlements, supplying raw materials to national steel mills. Samantha studies the historical metallurgy of the Range, while Lars guides tourists through historic mining towns.

## Stage

- [northwoods resilience](position_northwoods_resilience.md) : the winter endurance, wilderness survival, and resource labor of the north.
- [the Mesabi Range](place_mesabi_range.md) : the open-pit iron ore mines and shipping rail terminals.
- [canvas canoe](piece_canvas_canoe.md) : the early wood-and-canvas watercraft that co-existed with industrial lake freighters.

## Tension

The rapid growth of the mining pits strips forest lands and pollutes local streams, creating long-term conflicts between mining corporations and conservationists.
