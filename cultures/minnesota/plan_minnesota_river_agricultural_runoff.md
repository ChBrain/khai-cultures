## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and agricultural science designed to reduce fertilizer runoff into the Minnesota River Basin.

Manage agricultural runoff by planting perennial buffer strips, establishing cover crops, restoring wetland retention, and auditing fertilizer rates.
