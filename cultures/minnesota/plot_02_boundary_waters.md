# Plot: The Boundary Waters Protection 1978

## Taxonomy

[play_minnesota](play_minnesota.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In October 1978, Congress passes the Boundary Waters Canoe Area Wilderness Act, establishing strict protections for the northern lakes.

## Action

The act establishes federal wilderness conservation, developing [wilderness portage](process_wilderness_portage.md) customs. Lars guides canoe trips through the protected lakes, while Samantha designs clean-energy solutions to reduce carbon emissions near the reserve.

## Stage

- [the Boundary Waters](place_boundary_waters.md) : the protected wilderness canoe lakeland along the Canadian border.
- [wilderness portage](process_wilderness_portage.md) : the manual carrying of canoes and gear between remote lakes.
- [the Minnesota River plan](plan_minnesota_river_agricultural_runoff.md) : the watershed plan developed to protect regional lakes and streams.

## Tension

The act bans logging and motorized boats, sparking intense local protests from residents who feel conservation laws threaten their resource livelihood.
