# the Maldives: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind
the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_div.md](pitch_div.md) is written from the Maldives' profile on
Geert Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no
number. Comprehensive published scores for the Maldives are limited; where the
Maldives is not separately published, the profile is read from the regional South
Asian pattern and the country's own self-understanding as a small, collectivist,
faith-centred island community. The orientation used is recorded here so the
reading is on the record without entering the prose.

| Dimension                     | Maldives orientation (approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Power Distance                | high                           |
| Individualism                 | low (collectivist)             |
| Motivation toward Achievement | moderate                       |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | moderate                       |
| Long Term Orientation         | moderate                       |
| Indulgence                    | moderate to restrained         |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the six-dimensions model of national
culture and the Country Comparison tool). Where the Maldives is not separately
published, the orientation is inferred from the South Asian regional pattern and is
marked as approximate; no numeric score is claimed as published for the Maldives.
