# Persona: Davy

## Taxonomy

[unionism](position_unionism.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

An aulder man o the unionist, Protestant fowk, brocht up wi the Ulster-Scotch tongue an the marchin tradeetion. He's prood o his Breetish identity an o the union, but he houlds nae ill wull tae his nichbours o the ither tradeetion. He mynds the auld weys, the kirk on the Sabbath an the Twalfth in the simmer, an he believes the twa fowk can bide thegither on the ae grun wi respeck. He [spaeks, hears an thinks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) in his ain mither tongue, the [braid Ulster-Scotch](position_language_sco_gb_nir.md), as the grun unner aathing else, an he hauds it for the [merk o his ain fowk](position_the_two_tongues.md). The ilkaday English he shares wi his nichbours o the ither side, but the Erse o the Catholic fowk he [hears as a fremmit flag](process_hearing_caught.md), no as his ain: there's the rift, the twa o thaim speak the ae English, but ilk hauds a different auld tongue for a badge, an the leid itsel says whilk side ye're on. He belangs tae [Northern Irish culture](position_culture_northern_irish_culture.md) as ae hauf o the twa traditions that maun share this wee grun thegither.

## Action

He cairries the cultur o [unionism](position_unionism.md) frae yin generation tae the neist, an ilka year he taks his pairt in [the Twalfth](process_the_twelfth.md), the Orange parade an the bonfire, that gaithers his ain fowk thegither. He's seen the warst o the Troubles an the lang sair years, an for aa that he staunds by the peace that wis sattelt in nineteen ninety-echt, kennin there's nae gaun back tae whit went afore.

## Shadow

His luve o his ain tradeetion can herden intae a thrawn suspeecion o the ither, an he's whiles ower swippert tae read a slicht whaur nane wis ettelt. There's a danger he forgets that baith fowk tholed in the Troubles, an that he taks the merchin saison as a thing tae lord ower his nichbours raither nor a thing tae be shared wi respeck.

## Tell

He'll tell ye plain that he's Breetish an prood o it, but he'll pit the kettle on for ony nichbour that comes tae his door, whitiver fit they kick wi.
