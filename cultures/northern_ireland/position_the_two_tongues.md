# Position: the two tongues

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Two heritage tongues, Irish and Ulster-Scots, each claimed by one community as its own badge, in a place where both communities actually live their daily lives in the same English. The tongues carry less weight as speech than as colours.

## Orders

That which tongue you nod to declares which side you are on: a name, a street sign, a greeting becomes a flag, and language is read as allegiance before it is heard as speech. Even the name of the second city cannot be said without choosing a side.

## Loses

Ground as both heritage tongues stay minority and symbolic while the shared English carries real life, and as the tongues become political footballs, the stalled Irish Language Act, the rival fundings, more than living speech.

## Drives

It lives in the Irish spoken as one community's badge of belonging and the Ulster-Scots spoken as the other's, each held with pride and no ill will, and it drives the province to read a side into every word, so that even at [Derry/Londonderry](place_derry_londonderry.md) the name itself is a choosing.
