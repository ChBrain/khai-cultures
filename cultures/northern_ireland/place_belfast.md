## Shown

The capital at the head of the lough, a city of red brick and grey water, of the great cranes on the skyline and the hills behind. Once one of the workshops of the world, it is a working city of two communities, with its own neighbourhoods, its own murals, and its own quick, dark wit.

## Holds

## Offers

The place where the region is governed and where its industry, its civic pride, and its two traditions meet most closely, the everyday city in which both communities make their lives side by side.

## Withheld

The settled calm of a city at ease with itself. Belfast carries the memory of its divisions in its very map, and it holds back any pretence that the line between its communities has simply gone away.
