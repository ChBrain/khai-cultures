# Andalusia: a khai culture

Andalusia staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-AN, language es. Andalucía: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Seville Exposition, Land Reform, and Autonomy Referendum encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_andalusia.md](play_andalusia.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_andalusia.md](pitch_andalusia.md), the Andalusia key.
- **Plots (history):** the Seville Ibero-American Exposition of 1929, the Land Reform standoffs of the 1930s, and the Autonomy Referendum of 1980.
- **Personas:** Manuel (olive grove harvester) and Rocío (flamenco singer and guitar builder), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** jornalero solidarity (agricultural labor rights/land reform/cooperative olive mills), flamenco art (flamenco expression/cante jondo/guitar craftsmanship).
- **Places:** Olive Grove, Tablao.
- **Processes:** olive pressing (traditional olive oil cold pressing), cante jondo (deep flamenco singing).
- **Pieces:** castanets, olive jar.
- **Plan:** the Guadalquivir irrigation control plan.
