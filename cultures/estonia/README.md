# Estonia: a khai culture

Estonia staged as a full khai play, ISO EE, language et. A small Finnic nation,
long under others and reborn through song and code, as a theatre: its history as
the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its language and orientation
encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a
single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_estonia.md](play_estonia.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_et.md](pitch_et.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Estonia's
  profile (cool and reserved, sparing with words, pragmatic and modest, very
  forward-looking and long-headed, secular, nature-loving, quietly stubborn) and
  naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the National Awakening (1860s-80s), the War of Independence
  (1918-1920), the Occupations (1940-1991), and the Singing Revolution (1988-91) -
  each casting the elements it needs.
- **Personas:** Lydia Koidula, Lennart Meri, and Toomas Hendrik Ilves as the
  historic cast, plus two defining archetypes, Aino (older, the Soviet decades and
  the mother tongue kept whole) and Kristjan (younger, working in Finland across
  the gulf and living online in English). Each is wired to the language engine by
  the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** eesti keel (the Finnic tongue whose only kin is Finnish across the
  gulf, the peasant language under German and Russian rule), e-Eesti (the world's
  most digital state, a small nation reinvented through technology), Soome sild (the
  Finnish bridge, Estonia looking north to the Nordic family, not the Baltic).
- **Places:** Tallinn (the Hanseatic capital and the Song Grounds), Tartu (the
  university town and cradle of the Awakening, where the Tartu Peace was signed).
- **Processes:** laulupidu (the Song Festival of massed choirs at the lauluväljak),
  metsas käimine (going to the forest, the deep secular nature-bond).
- **Pieces:** kannel (the Finnic zither of the ancient runo-song, the old pole),
  ID-kaart (the digital ID card that makes e-Estonia real, the modern pole).
