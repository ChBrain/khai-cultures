# Murcia: a khai culture

Murcia staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-MC, language es. Murcia: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Moorish Irrigation System, Canton of Cartagena, and Mar Menor Protection Law encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_murcia.md](play_murcia.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_murcia.md](pitch_murcia.md), the Murcia key.
- **Plots (history):** the Moorish irrigation system of the 10th century, the Canton of Cartagena uprising of 1873, and the Mar Menor protection law of 2020.
- **Personas:** Gines (citrus orchard farmer) and Encarna (salt flat worker and fish salter), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** huerta murciana (citrus orchard gravity irrigation/water tribunal), secano costero (arid coastal steppes/salt flats/mining).
- **Places:** Mar Menor, Cartagena Roman Theater.
- **Processes:** lemon harvesting (citrus picking), fish salting (mullet roe salting).
- **Pieces:** water wheel model, fish salting press.
- **Plan:** the Mar Menor restoration plan.
