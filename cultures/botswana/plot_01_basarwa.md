# Plot: Basarwa le Kgalagadi

## Taxonomy

[play_botswana](play_botswana.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Kwa bogologolong jo bo sa lekanyweng, pele ga merafe yotlhe e tla mo nageng, Basarwa, batho ba ntlha, ba ne ba tshela le sekaka sa Kgalagadi le metsi a [Okavango](place_okavango.md). Ba ne ba itse sekaka jaaka ntlo ya bone, ba itse fa metsi a ka fitlhelwang teng le fa diphologolo di phuthegang teng.

## Action

Basarwa ba tshela le naga sebaka se se telele go gaisa botlhe, ba tsoma le go phutha dijo tsa naga, ba bala marang a letsatsi le mebala ya legodimo. Ba bolokela naga boswa jwa yona jwa kgale: ditshwantsho tse di tshetsweng mo mafikeng, kitso ya ditlhare le diphologolo, le mošwa wa go tshela le sekaka eseng go se gapeletsa. Le fa nako e ne ya ba tlisetsa diphetogo tse di boima fa merafe e mengwe e tla, boswa jwa bone jwa bogologolgo bo santse bo tshelwa ka tlotlo, e le motheo wa botho jwa naga.

## Stage

- [Okavango](place_okavango.md): metsi le sekaka tse Basarwa ba neng ba di itse jaaka ntlo ya bone.
- [Modisa](persona_modisa.md): modisi yo o tshelang le naga jaaka Basarwa ba ne ba dira, a itse metsi le bojang.
- [dikgomo](position_dikgomo.md): mošwa wa go tshela le naga o o tlileng morago wa fetoga go disa dikgomo.

## Tension

Naga e na le batho ba ba neng ba e itse pele ga botlhe, mme boswa jwa bone jwa kgale ke jone bo bo dirang gore Botswana e itse gore e tswa kae. Go tshola boswa joo ka tlotlo, go se bo lebale fa naga e tswelela pele, ke tiro e e tsamayang le naga go tswa tshimologong go fitlha gompieno.
