# Piece: mokoro

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Mo metsing a Okavango, fa gare ga lehlaka le bojang jwa metsi: mokoro, mokorwana o o betlilweng go tswa mo kutung ya setlhare, o o kgweediwang ka thobane. Ke sediriswa sa go tsamaya mo metsing a delta, se batho ba a tshelang gaufi le metsi ba se dirisang go tshela noka, go tshwara ditlhapi, le go isa baeng go bona diphologolo. Ke sediriswa se se tsamayang le metsi a Okavango go tswa bogologolong.

## Load Bearing

Se emela tsela e batho ba tshelang ka yona le metsi a Okavango ka tlotlo le ka bokgoni. Mo lefatsheng la metsi le le mo gare ga sekaka, mokoro ke tsela e motho a tsamayang ka yona mo metsing a a boima, a kgweediwa ka thobane fela ntle le modumo. Ke sediriswa se se bontshang gore batho le tlhago ba ka tshela mmogo: mokoro o tsamaya mo metsing o sa a senye, o isa baeng go bona kgakgamalo ya delta. Ntle le mokoro, batho ba ka tlhoka tsela ya go tshela le metsi a Okavango.

## Apparent

Mokorwana o o betlilweng go tswa mo kutung ya setlhare kgotsa o dirilwe ka dilo tse dingwe gompieno, o kgweediwa ka thobane. Mo go one ke mokorwana fela; bokete jwa one botlhe bo mo go tsela e o tsamayang le metsi a Okavango ka yona, le mo bokgoning jwa yo o o kgweetsang.

## Yearbook

- O betliwa go tswa mo kutung ya setlhare, o kgweediwa ka thobane mo metsing a a boima a delta.
- O dirisiwa go tshwara ditlhapi, go tshela noka, le go isa baeng go bona diphologolo tsa Okavango.
- O nna sesupo sa tsela e batho ba tshelang ka yona le metsi a tlhago ka tlotlo, e e tlisitseng baeng ba lefatshe go bona delta.
