# Botswana: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind
the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_tn.md](pitch_tn.md) is written from Botswana's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without
entering the prose. Botswana is not always reported individually in the published
data; where a national score is not separately published, the profile is read from
the regional and comparative literature on Southern African values (the collective
over the individual, respect for elders and hierarchy held gently, and a calm,
consensus-seeking register).

| Dimension                     | Botswana profile (approx.)   |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | high                         |
| Individualism                 | low (collective)             |
| Motivation toward Achievement | low (consensus over contest) |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | moderate                     |
| Long Term Orientation         | moderate                     |
| Indulgence                    | moderate (restrained)        |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the six-dimensions model of national
culture and the Country Comparison tool), read together with the comparative
literature on Southern African and Tswana values. Figures are approximate and, for
Botswana specifically, partly inferred from regional data; they are recorded here
only to make the basis of the pitch transparent and never enter the prose.
