# Position: kgotla

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Lekgotla la morafe ka fa tlase ga setlhare, fa batho ba phuthegang teng go buisana le go dumalana mmogo. Kgotla ke felo fa lentswe la mongwe le mongwe le utlwiwang teng, fa molato o sekiwang teng, le fa tshwetso e tsewang teng e seng ke kgosi a le esi mme ke morafe otlhe morago ga go buisana. Ke motheo wa puso ya batho mo Botswana, fa go thwe mafoko a kgosi a agelwa ke morafe, mme kgosi ke kgosi ka batho jaaka motho e le motho ka batho.

## Orders

Gore tshwetso e tsewe morago ga puisano e seng pele ga yona, mme mongwe le mongwe a newe nako ya go bua; gore kgosi a reetse morafe mme a se gapeletse thato ya gagwe; le gore molato o sekiwe fa pele ga botlhe ka tshiamo, mme go buisanwe go fitlha go fitlhelelwa tumalano. Kgotla e laela gore puso e nne ya batho, e bo e nne pepeneng.

## Loses

E latlhegelwa ke tsela fa puisano e fetoga lefela mme tshwetso e setse e tserwe pele; fa mafoko a babui ba bangwe a sa reediwe ka ntlha ya maemo a bone; le fa kgotla e dirisiwa go tlhomamisa thato ya yo mongwe go na le go batla tumalano ya morafe. E latlhegelwa, gape, fa diphetogo tsa nako di e tlogela kwa morago, mme mantswe mangwe a sa newe felo mo kgotleng.

## Drives

E tshela mo go [Khama III](persona_khama_iii.md), kgosi e e gogetseng morafe wa yona ka molao le ka kgotla, e bo e sireletsa naga ka bohlale. E gogela Botswana kwa pusong ya batho, kwa go reediseng mongwe le mongwe, le kwa go tseeng tshwetso ka tumalano e seng ka pateletso, mošwa o naga e o tsereng go tswa kwa setlhareng sa kgotla ya yona.
