# Position: botho

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Mošwa o mogolo wa Setswana: motho ke motho ka batho. Botho ke gore botho jwa motho bo bonala mo ka fa a tshelang le ba bangwe ka teng, mo tlotlong, mo bopelotlhomogi, le mo go tshola yo o tlhokang ka boikobo. Ga go a bopiwa ke khumo le fa e le maemo, mme bo bopiwa ke maitsholo a a siameng le tlotlo ya botho jwa yo mongwe. Botho ke motheo o naga e ikemeng mo go one: gore re tshele mmogo, re thusane, re tlotlane, mme re seke ra itlhokomologe ope yo o mo gare ga rona.

## Orders

Gore motho a tlotle yo mongwe le fa a le mannye go le kana kang, gore moeng a amogelwe mme yo o tlhokang a thusiwe; gore o sa itse botho ga se motho yo o tletseng; le gore khumo le maemo ga di tlose tlhwaafalo ya gore motho a nne le botho mo go ba bangwe. Botho bo laela gore re ipone mo go ba bangwe, mme re itse gore re tshela ka bone.

## Loses

Bo latlhegelwa ke tsela fa motho a ikgantsha ka khumo le maemo a bo a nyatsa yo o ka fa tlase; fa go iketla ga morafe go fetoga go itlhokomologa yo o gateletsweng; le fa tlotlo ya bagolo e dirisiwa go didimatsa lentswe le le tshwanetseng go utlwiwa. Bo latlhegelwa ke tsela, gape, fa go ratwa ga khutso go fetoga boikutlo jwa gore a re seke ra ama sepe, le fa botho bo lebalwa mo lobelong lwa khumo.

## Drives

Bo tshela mo go [Seretse Khama](persona_seretse.md), yo o gogetseng naga kwa boipuso a sa busolosetse bosula ka bosula mme a aga kagiso, le mo go [Bessie Head](persona_bessie_head.md), yo o tshotsweng ke morafe wa Serowe a le moeng a bo a kwala botho jwa batho ba a neng a tshela le bone. Bo gogela Botswana kwa kagisong, kwa go amogeleng moeng, le kwa go reng motho ke motho ka batho.
