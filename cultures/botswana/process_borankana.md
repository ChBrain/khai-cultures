# Process: borankana

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Morafe o o phuthegileng mo moletlong, mo lonyalong, kgotsa mo letsatsing la naga: baopedi ba opela, batshameki ba bina ka maoto le ka diatla, mme bagologolo le bana ba phuthega go reetsa le go bina. Go tlhoka fela morafe o o phuthegileng le molodi o o tswang mo pelong ya setso, mme borankana bo bo simologa.

## Direction

Go ya kwa go kopaneng le go itumeleng mmogo: borankana ke pina le motshameko tsa Setswana tse di kopanyang morafe, di anela botshelo jwa one, dikgomo tsa one, le maitsholo a one. Ga se motshameko fela mme ke tsela ya go boloka mošwa, diane, le ngwao ya morafe gore di fetelwe go bana, le ya go itumelela botshelo mmogo jaaka morafe o le mongwe.

## Lever

Bo tsamaisiwa ke tlhokego ya morafe ya go kopana le go itumela mmogo, le ke molodi le motshameko tse di tshotseng mošwa wa setso. Bo ema kgatlhanong le go lebala ngwao fa banana ba ya ditoropong; kgatlhanong le jone go ema diphetogo tsa nako tse di ka latlhang molodi wa setso fa o sa fetelwe go bana.

## Echo

Borankana bo bonatsha se se gogang [botho](position_botho.md): gore morafe o agilwe mo go kopaneng le mo go itumeleng mmogo. Mo moloding o, [Ratsie Setlhako](persona_ratsie.md) o opela botshelo jwa naga ka segaba, a boloka mošwa le diane tsa morafe.
