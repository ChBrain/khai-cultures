# the Republic of the Congo: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain cultural and historical
sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_fr.md](pitch_fr.md) is written from the Republic of the Congo's
regional profile on Geert Hofstede's model of national culture and names no
dimension and carries no number. The scores and their source are recorded here so
the data is on the record without entering the prose. Country-specific data for the
Republic of the Congo is limited; the values below follow the West and Central
African regional profile in Hofstede's published work and are approximate.

| Dimension                     | Republic of the Congo score (approx., regional) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 75                                              |
| Individualism                 | 20                                              |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 45                                              |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 50                                              |
| Long Term Orientation         | 20                                              |
| Indulgence                    | 60                                              |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the Country Comparison tool and the
six-dimensions model of national culture), drawing on the West and Central African
regional estimates. Scores are approximate, as published by Hofstede Insights /
The Culture Factor Group, and are used only to set the register of the pitch.

philosophy of la SAPE into ordinary life.
