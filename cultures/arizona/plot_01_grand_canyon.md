## Cue

In February 1919, President Woodrow Wilson signs the bill establishing Grand Canyon National Park, protecting the canyon from private exploitation.

## Action

The act establishes federal wilderness conservation, developing [desert resilience](position_desert_resilience.md) principles. Yazhi guides visitors along the scenic park trails, while Ramon monitors the geological structures bounding the protected areas.

## Stage

- [desert resilience](position_desert_resilience.md) : the ecological stewardship and adaptation to the arid canyon landscapes.
- [the Grand Canyon](place_grand_canyon.md) : the protected geological wonder and ancestral tribal homeland.
- [copper ore](piece_copper_ore.md) : the mineral deposits that were barred from extraction within the park borders.

## Tension

The park's creation restricts traditional hunting and grazing rights for the Havasupai and Navajo, prioritizing tourism over native access.
