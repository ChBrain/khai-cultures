# Process: copper smelting

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

## Initiated by

Southwest metallurgical engineers, furnace operators, and mining managers, conducted year-round in regional smelting facilities.

## Direction

In the smelting plant. Heating crushed copper concentrate in reverberatory furnaces, separating slag, refining copper matte, and casting pure copper anodes.

## Lever

The traditional industrial extraction process that built the state's pioneer economy. It supports high-wage mining jobs and global electrical wire supplies, though it releases heavy sulfur emissions and demands massive water volumes.
