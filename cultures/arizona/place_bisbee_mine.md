## Withheld

The Bisbee Deportation of 1917. While today celebrated for its historic charm, the town was the site of a violent corporate deportation where over a thousand striking miners were kidnapped, loaded onto cattle cars, and dumped in the New Mexico desert, a history excluded from tourist guidebooks.
