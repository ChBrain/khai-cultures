## Place

## Load Bearing

It represents the mineral foundation of Southwest industrial growth, the legacy of copper mining labor, and the state's extraction economy.

## Apparent

A heavy, irregular mineral specimen displaying vibrant green malachite and blue azurite crusts embedded in a brown rock matrix.
