# Plot: The Central Arizona Project 1968

## Taxonomy

[play_arizona](play_arizona.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In September 1968, the Colorado River Basin Project Act authorizes construction of the Central Arizona Project aqueduct system.

## Action

The project establishes water security for desert cities, developing [the groundwater plan](plan_groundwater_management.md) to manage aquifers. Ramon coordinates hydrological surveys along the aqueduct routes, while Yazhi monitors the water rights of reservation lands.

## Stage

- [southwest frontier](position_southwest_frontier.md) : the engineering ambition and desert development boom enabled by water imports.
- [Bisbee Mine](place_bisbee_mine.md) : the copper mining district that historically competed for water resources.
- [the groundwater plan](plan_groundwater_management.md) : the environmental water management plan designed to regulate aquifer withdrawal.

## Tension

The massive diversion of water from the Colorado River increases downstream ecological damage, contributing to water shortages in neighboring states and Mexico.
