# Plan: the groundwater plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and water resource engineering designed to regulate groundwater pumping and protect desert aquifers.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Manage groundwater by establishing Active Management Areas, monitoring well withdrawal rates, requiring 100-year water proofs, and expanding recharge basins.

## Orders

- To the Department of Water Resources: Enforce pumping limits and audit groundwater recharge operations.
- To geologists like [Ramon](persona_ramon.md): Support hydrological modeling and analyze groundwater chemistry near extraction shafts.
- To tribal guides like [Yazhi](persona_yazhi.md): Coordinate water-use consultations and advocate for watershed protection.

## Implementation

The aquifer level measurements; the trans-basin canal water audits; and the deployment of aquifer monitoring wells.

- [x] The Groundwater Management Act boundaries were established.
