# Burkina Faso: References

The provenance behind this culture: the public, widely documented historical and
cultural record behind the plots, the personas they cast, the places, the
processes, and the pieces. These are credited here and never claimed; the staging
is original work. The colonial period, the 1984 renaming, and the national memory
of Thomas Sankara are staged soberly on the public record, taking no political
side.
