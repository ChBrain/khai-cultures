# Burkina Faso: a khai culture

Burkina Faso staged as a full khai play, ISO BF, language fr. A landlocked Sahel
nation that gave itself a name meaning the land of upright people, as a theatre:
its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its languages and
its sense of honour encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects,
all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the house that holds

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_burkina_faso.md](play_burkina_faso.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_fr.md](pitch_fr.md), the tone the whole play is keyed to,
  written from the nation's own register (sober, upright, hospitable, eye to eye,
  the group before the self, dignity measured by the word kept and the head held
  high rather than by wealth) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Mossi kingdoms and the court of the Mogho Naba, the
  colonial Upper Volta, the 1984 renaming to Burkina Faso, and Ouagadougou as the
  capital of African cinema. Each casts the elements it needs and is staged
  soberly on the public record.
- **Personas:** the Mogho Naba (the customary Mossi sovereign), Thomas Sankara
  (held soberly as national memory of austerity and self-reliance), the filmmaker
  Gaston Kabore, and the singer Victor Deme, plus one archetype, the balafon
  player (the griot, keeper of the word). Each is wired to the language engine by
  the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** integrity (the meaning written into the country's own name), the
  Mossi chieftaincy (the customary order and the keeping of the word), and cinema
  (the wager to be the home of African film).
- **Places:** Ouagadougou (the capital, the Mogho Naba's court, and the festival
  city) and Tiebele (the painted royal houses of the Gourounsi).
- **Processes:** the FESPACO (the biennial African film festival) and the dolo
  tavern (the millet-beer gathering that holds a neighbourhood together).
- **Pieces:** the balafon (the wooden keys that speak where the word stops) and
  the lost-wax bronze (the art of the founder's hand, the other gold of the
  country).
- **Plan:** [plan_compter_sur_ses_bras.md](plan_compter_sur_ses_bras.md), to count
  on its own arms, the forward-looking national aim built on the integrity the
  name proclaims.
