# Process: ore shipping

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

## Initiated by

Great Lakes shipping crews, cargo vessel captains, and port dock workers, conducted during the ice-free navigation season.

## Direction

Across the Great Lakes shipping lanes. Loading bulk iron ore at northern port pockets, navigating lake channels, and unloading at industrial steel plants.

## Lever

The maritime transportation process that supplies raw materials to national heavy industries. It supports regional trade and jobs, though it is limited by winter ice shutdowns and strict maritime safety rules.

## Echo
