# Plan: the Brule River plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and forestry conservation designed to protect the water quality of the Bois Brule River Basin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Restore water quality by constructing logging buffers, planting native white pine, restricting building, and monitoring trout spawning habitats.

## Orders

- To the Department of Natural Resources: Monitor and regulate logging practices and shoreline zoning along the river.
- To captains like [Ingrid](persona_ingrid.md): Support water quality monitoring and comply with Great Lakes ballast standards.
- To cheese makers like [Dietrich](persona_dietrich.md): Implement filtration practices and manage farm waste runoff in watersheds.

## Implementation

The shoreline logging audits; the trout spawning habitat surveys; and the deployment of river sediment monitors.
