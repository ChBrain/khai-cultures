# Place: Door County

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The scenic peninsula between Green Bay and Lake Michigan, featuring limestone cliffs, cherry orchards, sandy beaches, and small fishing harbors.

## Holds

- the conservation planning in the plot [Progressive Era Reforms 1901](plot_03_progressive_reforms.md) : the fruit peninsula.

## Offers

The farm markets and shoreline parks where [Dietrich](persona_dietrich.md) coordinates cheese sales.

## Withheld

The exclusion of the Potawatomi. While promoted as a vacation destination, the peninsula was the site of the forced displacement of the Potawatomi and Menominee nations under the 1830s treaties, a displacement not discussed in tourist brochure histories.
