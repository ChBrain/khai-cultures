# Place: Milwaukee Brewery

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The historic cream-colored brick industrial complexes along the Milwaukee River, featuring towering chimneys, copper brew kettles, and large lagering cellars.

## Holds

- the industrial brewing history in the plot [The Milwaukee Brewing Boom 1880](plot_02_brewing_boom.md) : the beer capital.

## Offers

The brewery tours and tasting halls where [Ingrid](persona_ingrid.md) coordinates transport shipping logs.

## Withheld

The working class labor strikes. While celebrated today for its historic architecture and corporate names, the breweries faced violent labor conflicts, including the 1886 Bay View Massacre where state militia fired on striking workers demanding an eight-hour day, a history left out of promotional brewery tours.
