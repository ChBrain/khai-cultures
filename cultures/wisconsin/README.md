# Wisconsin: a khai culture

Wisconsin staged as a full khai play, ISO US-WI, language en. America's Dairyland: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its dairy cooperatives, Milwaukee breweries, and progressive Wisconsin Idea reforms encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_wisconsin.md](play_wisconsin.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_wisconsin.md](pitch_wisconsin.md), the Wisconsin key.
- **Plots (history):** the Dairy Cooperative Movement of the 1890s, the Milwaukee brewing boom of the 1880s, and the Progressive Era reforms (1900-1915).
- **Personas:** Dietrich (Green County cheese maker) and Ingrid (Great Lakes ore shipping captain), written in English.
- **Positions:** dairyland (agricultural labor/cooperatives), progressive idea (education/clean government).
- **Places:** Milwaukee Brewery, Door County.
- **Processes:** cheese making (dairy fermentation), ore shipping (lake transportation).
- **Pieces:** a cheese press, a beer stein.
- **Plan:** the Brule River plan.
