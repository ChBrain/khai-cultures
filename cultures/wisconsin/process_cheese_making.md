# Process: cheese making

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Wisconsin cheese makers, dairy technicians, and creamery managers, conducted year-round in local cooperative cheese plants.

## Direction

On the cheese plant floor. Heating fresh cow milk, adding bacterial cultures and rennet, cutting the curds, draining the whey, pressing the curds into molds, and aging the cheese wheels.

## Lever

The traditional agricultural fermentation process that defines the state's dairy brand. It supports thousands of rural family farms and cheese shops, though it is highly dependent on milk price stability and strict sanitation rules.
