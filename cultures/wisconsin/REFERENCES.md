## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_wisconsin.md](pitch_wisconsin.md) is written from Wisconsin's profile inflected by its dairy cooperatives, brewing heritage, progressive reforms, and Great Lakes shipping, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Wisconsin base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 34                                          |
| Individualism                 | 78                                          |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 58                                          |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 48                                          |
| Long Term Orientation         | 28                                          |
| Indulgence                    | 66                                          |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Wisconsin's low power distance in cooperative boards, progressive civic participation, and higher uncertainty avoidance in product safety/education).
