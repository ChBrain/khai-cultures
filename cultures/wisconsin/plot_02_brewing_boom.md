# Plot: The Milwaukee Brewing Boom 1880

## Taxonomy

[play_wisconsin](play_wisconsin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the 1880s, Milwaukee breweries expand into massive industrial complexes, shipping lager beer across the nation.

## Action

The expansion establishes Milwaukee as the nation's brewing capital, developing large-scale industrial bottling methods. The [beer stein](piece_beer_stein.md) represents this German-American heritage. Dietrich preserves historic brewing recipes, while Ingrid navigates coal and grain ships supplying the industrial breweries.

## Stage

- [place_milwaukee_brewery](place_milwaukee_brewery.md) : the historic brick industrial complexes housing the major breweries.
- [beer stein](piece_beer_stein.md) : the earthenware stein representing local German-American heritage.
- [ore shipping](process_ore_shipping.md) : the bulk cargo shipping that shared the lake routes with beer transport.

## Tension

The growth of industrial breweries prompts intense temperance campaigns, leading to local conflicts over saloon licensing and liquor sales.
