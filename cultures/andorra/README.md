# Andorra (Principat d'Andorra): a khai culture

Andorra staged as a full khai play, ISO AD, language ca. The co-principality of the Pyrenees: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its mountain and financial life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_andorra.md](play_andorra.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_andorra.md](pitch_andorra.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Andorra's profile inflected by its co-principality heritage and Pyrenean valleys, naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Pareatges of 1278, the 1993 Constitution, and the transition to a modern tourist/financial economy.
- **Personas:** Jordi (traditional shepherd archetype) and Laia (modern banking/tourism youth), written with Catalan language reads.
- **Positions:** el coprincipat (the co-principality), el Pirineu (the Pyrenees), la transhumància (the pastoral tradition).
- **Places:** Andorra la Vella (the capital), Vall del Madriu-Perafita-Claror (the cultural landscape).
- **Processes:** la transhumància (sheep moving).
- **Pieces:** els Pareatges (the medieval charters), Grandvalira (the ski resort).
