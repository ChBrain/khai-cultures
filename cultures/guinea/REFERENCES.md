# Guinea: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind
the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_fr.md](pitch_fr.md) is written from Guinea's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
Guinea is not always published as a single country score; the profile used here
follows the West African regional pattern in Hofstede's data. The approximate
values and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without
entering the prose.

| Dimension                     | Guinea / West Africa score (approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Power Distance                | 77                                   |
| Individualism                 | 20                                   |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 46                                   |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 54                                   |
| Long Term Orientation         | 16                                   |
| Indulgence                    | 40                                   |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the Country Comparison tool and the
six-dimensions model of national culture). Scores are approximate, as published by
Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group for West Africa, the regional cluster
to which Guinea belongs; the low Individualism and high Power Distance are the
defining features of the profile.
