## Place

Historic units displayed at the Museum of American Finance near Wall Street in Manhattan.

## Load Bearing

It represents the mechanization of financial information, the rise of modern stock trading networks, and the speed of capital flows in the Empire State.

## Apparent

A brass telegraphic printing machine housed under a glass dome, typing out abbreviated stock symbols and prices on a continuous thin strip of paper tape.
