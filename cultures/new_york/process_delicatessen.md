Deli owners and patrons, practiced daily, particularly in Jewish and Italian neighborhood establishments across New York City.

## Direction

At the deli counter and dining tables. Curing pastrami and corned beef, slicing meats, preparing bagels and knishes, and serving towering sandwiches to customers.

## Lever

The culinary tradition that represents the immigrant heritage of the city. It provides a casual social space for diverse communities, though it faces pressure from rising rent prices and corporate chain restaurants.
