# Position: the melting pot

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The integration of diverse immigrant cultures, languages, and heritages into a shared community. It possesses a tolerance for differences and a belief that diversity is a source of strength.

## Orders

That one welcomes newcomers, respects cultural differences, and supports the integration of diverse traditions in urban life.

## Loses

Its balance when cultural differences result in systemic discrimination, segregation, or xenophobia in local neighborhoods.

## Drives

Influences the immigrant communities in the plot [Ellis Island 1892](plot_02_ellis_island_1892.md) and guides the collaborative theater work of [Sarah](persona_sarah.md).
