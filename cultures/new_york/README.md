# New York: a khai culture

New York staged as a full khai play, ISO US-NY, language en. The Empire State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its financial, industrial, and artistic life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_new_york.md](play_new_york.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_new_york.md](pitch_new_york.md), the New York key.
- **Plots (history):** the opening of the Erie Canal in 1825, the mass immigration through Ellis Island in 1892, and the Wall Street Crash of 1929.
- **Personas:** Tony (Wall Street broker) and Sarah (Broadway stage manager), written in English.
- **Positions:** the Empire State (imperial commerce), the melting pot (diversity as strength).
- **Places:** Times Square (New York City), Niagara Falls.
- **Processes:** Broadway shows (theater staging), delicatessen dining (food culture).
- **Pieces:** a Broadway billboard, a Wall Street stock ticker.
- **Plan:** the New York subway modernization plan.
