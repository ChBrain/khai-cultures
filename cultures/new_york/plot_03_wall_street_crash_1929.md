# Plot: The Wall Street Crash 1929

## Taxonomy

[play_new_york](play_new_york.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In late October 1929, the New York Stock Exchange on Wall Street suffers a devastating collapse, ending the Roaring Twenties.

## Action

The crash destroys billions in wealth, highlighting the power of [the Empire State](position_empire_state.md) over the national economy and ushering in the Great Depression. The event forces a structural overhaul of financial systems. Tony manages investments under modern compliance rules designed to prevent such systemic failures, while Sarah works in theater networks that rely on private philanthropy.

## Stage

- [the Empire State](position_empire_state.md) : the financial and economic center that controls national capital.
- [stock ticker](piece_stock_ticker.md) : the mechanical machine that typed out the crashing stock prices.
- [the New York subway plan](plan_subway_modernization.md) : the public infrastructure project that expanded during the post-crash recovery.

## Tension

The collapse leads to massive unemployment, banking failures, and widespread poverty, exposing the vulnerability of unregulated speculation.
