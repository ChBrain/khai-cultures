# Plot: The Erie Canal 1825

## Taxonomy

[play_new_york](play_new_york.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In October 1825, Governor DeWitt Clinton opens the completed Erie Canal, connecting the Hudson River to the Great Lakes.

## Action

The new waterway reduces shipping costs, establishing [the Empire State](position_empire_state.md) as the dominant commercial route and launching New York City's growth. Upstate towns along the canal boom with trade. The canal is celebrated in local songs and stories. Tony's family history traces back to merchants who shipped Midwest wheat along the canal to Manhattan docks.

## Stage

- [the Empire State](position_empire_state.md) : the ambition, wealth, and commercial dominance of the state.
- [Niagara Falls](place_niagara_falls.md) : the natural landmark that powered early factories along the canal zone.
- [stock ticker](piece_stock_ticker.md) : the financial trading machinery that grew out of the canal's trade volume.

## Tension

The construction relies on the hard labor of Irish immigrant workers who face severe disease, dangerous working conditions, and social hostility.
