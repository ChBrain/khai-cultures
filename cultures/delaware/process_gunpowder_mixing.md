# Process: gunpowder mixing

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Black powder specialists, historic mill operators, and chemistry safety monitors, conducted during scheduled museum runs.

## Direction

On the mill grinding table. Grinding charcoal and sulfur using heavy stone wheels, adding saltpeter, moistening the mixture, pressing into cakes, and drying.

## Lever

The traditional chemical mixing process that defined the early industrial economy. It supports historical heritage demonstrations and black powder science, though it requires strict friction controls and faces high explosion risks.
