# Plan: the Brandywine plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and river hydrology designed to regulate industrial runoff and protect historical river valleys in the Brandywine Basin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Conserve the river watershed by restricting industrial chemical discharges, restoring native wetland buffers, upgrading municipal storm drainage, and monitoring stream chemical acidity.

## Orders

- To the Delaware River Basin Commission: Enforce discharge standards and audit industrial watershed permits.
- To historic coordinators like [Pierre](persona_pierre.md): Support historic mill runoff telemetry audits and maintain safe black powder demonstration limits.
- To legal archivists like [Irene](persona_irene.md): Coordinate local community conservation committee logs and advocate for traditional valley preservation.

## Implementation

The stream chemical measurements; the forest soil erosion audits; and the deployment of field water monitors.
