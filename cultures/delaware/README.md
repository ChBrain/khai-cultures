# Delaware: a khai culture

Delaware staged as a full khai play, ISO US-DE, language en. The First State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its First State Constitution ratification, DuPont gunpowder mills, and corporate Court of Chancery boom encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_delaware.md](play_delaware.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_delaware.md](pitch_delaware.md), the Delaware key.
- **Plots (history):** the First State Ratification of 1787, the DuPont Gunpowder Boom of 1802, and the Corporate Chancery Court Boom of 1899.
- **Personas:** Pierre (gunpowder specialist) and Irene (Chancery Court archivist), written in English.
- **Positions:** first state charter (constitutional pride/colonial navigation/legal debate), corporate chancery (business law precision/incorporation efficiency).
- **Places:** DuPont Mills, Chancery Court.
- **Processes:** gunpowder mixing (traditional charcoal and sulfur mixing), incorporation filing (business registration processing).
- **Pieces:** a powder keg, a corporate seal.
- **Plan:** the Brandywine River protection plan.
