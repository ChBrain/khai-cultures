## Load Bearing

It represents the breakthrough of industrial research labs, the development of electrical utilities, and the invention legacy of Menlo Park.

## Apparent

A pear-shaped glass bulb containing a carbonized bamboo filament, supported by copper wires inside a vacuum, fitted with a brass screw base.
