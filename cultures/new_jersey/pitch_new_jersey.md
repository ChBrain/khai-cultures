# Pitch: the New Jersey key

## Taxonomy

The pitch of [New Jersey](play_new_jersey.md). It tunes the thoughtful, informal, and pragmatic expression of life in the Garden State.

## Owner

## Tenor

New Jersey talks fast and tells you the price up front - direct, fast-paced, and conversational, mixing metropolitan business language with neighborhood idioms. It is a voice of industry and community, at home with pharmaceutical formulation, beach resort recreation, and diner food culture. This pragmatic language shapes both the turnpike logistics centers and the beachfront boardwalks, valuing hard work and family networks.

## Undertow

Under the commercial energy lies a persistent geographic polarization between the highly developed corporate corridors of the north and the rural, agricultural counties of the south. This divide breeds debates over local taxation and forest preservation, where state-wide zoning mandates are met with local protective instincts for municipal autonomy.

## Nerve

It plays the diner-counter note: commercial initiative, technical research, and local solidarity that shows up when it's needed.

## Echo

The clatter of diner silverware meets the hiss of waves on the boardwalk sand - loud, warm, and pragmatic, the sound of a state living its double life at once.
