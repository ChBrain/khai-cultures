# Plot: Boardwalk Empire and Beach Resorts 1870

## Owner

## Cue

In June 1870, Atlantic City opens the first beach boardwalk in the United States, designed to keep sand out of oceanfront hotels.

## Action

The walkway establishes seaside entertainment industries, developing [boardwalk concession](process_boardwalk_concession.md) standards. Gabriella records ecological impacts of coastal development, while Tony studies early seaside food concession models.

## Stage

- [Asbury Park](place_asbury_park.md) : the beach resort and boardwalk music center in Monmouth County.
- [boardwalk concession](process_boardwalk_concession.md) : the operation of seaside amusement, game, and food stalls.
- [diner booth](piece_diner_booth.md) : the roadside eating structures that evolved alongside tourism routes.

## Tension

The rapid growth of beach resorts causes intense real estate conflicts, privatizing public beaches and excluding low-income residents.
