# Plot: The Industrial Corridor Expansion 1952

## Taxonomy

[play_new_jersey](play_new_jersey.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In January 1952, the full New Jersey Turnpike opens, connecting the industrial north to the agricultural south.

## Action

The turnpike establishes massive shipping and pharmaceutical hubs, transforming the state into a logistics corridor. Gabriella monitors chemical runoff near turnpike crossings, while Tony manages logistics for food shipments to local diners.

## Stage

- [industrial turnpike](position_industrial_turnpike.md) : the blue-collar pragmatism and logistics efficiency of the transport lanes.
- [pharmaceutical formulation](process_pharmaceutical_formulation.md) : the industrial research laboratories established along the corridor.
- [the Pine Barrens plan](plan_pine_barrens_conservation.md) : the environmental plan developed to protect forest aquifers from corridor pollution.

## Tension

The heavy industrial development releases toxic emissions, creating numerous superfund sites and polluting local waterways.
