# Plan: the Pine Barrens plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and water resource conservation designed to protect the Pine Barrens aquifer and pitch pine ecosystem.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Foster aquifer conservation by constructing filtration basins, upgrading runoff rules, restricting housing developments, and conducting understory burns.
