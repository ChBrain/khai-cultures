# New Jersey: a khai culture

New Jersey staged as a full khai play, ISO US-NJ, language en. The Garden State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Menlo Park inventions, Atlantic City boardwalks, and post-war industrial expansions encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_new_jersey.md](play_new_jersey.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_new_jersey.md](pitch_new_jersey.md), the New Jersey key.
- **Plots (history):** the Menlo Park laboratory founding in 1876, the Atlantic City boardwalk construction in 1870, and the post-WWII industrial turnpike corridor expansion.
- **Personas:** Tony (diner owner) and Gabriella (Pine Barrens environmental scientist), written in English.
- **Positions:** Garden State (suburban/agricultural pride), industrial turnpike (logistics/pragmatism).
- **Places:** Asbury Park, Pine Barrens.
- **Processes:** pharmaceutical formulation (chemical development), boardwalk concession (coastal tourism).
- **Pieces:** an incandescent bulb, a diner booth.
- **Plan:** the Pine Barrens conservation plan.
