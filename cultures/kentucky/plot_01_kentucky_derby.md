# Plot: The Kentucky Derby Inauguration 1875

## Taxonomy

[play_kentucky](play_kentucky.md)

## Owner

## Cue

In May 1875, Aristides wins the first Kentucky Derby at the newly constructed Churchill Downs track in Louisville.

## Action

The race establishes Louisville as the center of American horse racing, developing [bluegrass pride](position_bluegrass_pride.md). The [derby cup](piece_derby_cup.md) represents this racing era. Clara coordinates thoroughbred logistics for the event, while Silas documents early African American jockey contributions.

## Stage

- [bluegrass pride](position_bluegrass_pride.md) : the horse breeding excellence, rural musical tradition, and pasture pride.
- [derby cup](piece_derby_cup.md) : the silver trophy awarded to the winning horse owner.
- [Churchill Downs](place_churchill_downs.md) : the historic twin-spire horse racing stadium.

## Tension

Early jockeys face severe commercial racial exclusion after the reconstruction era, leading to their complete displacement from professional tracks.
