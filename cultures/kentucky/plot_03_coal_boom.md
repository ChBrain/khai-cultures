## Cue

In the 1910s, rail expansions into eastern Kentucky launch rapid underground coal mine development across Appalachian counties.

## Action

The boom establishes coal as the state's dominant industrial energy resource, developing [coal mining](process_coal_mining.md). Silas audits labor union safety conditions, while Clara coordinates agricultural land use plans along mine boundaries.

## Stage

- [coal solidarity](position_coal_solidarity.md) : the Appalachian labor union loyalty, mutual defense, and mining heritage.
- [coal pick](piece_coal_pick.md) : the steel hand tool representing traditional coal mining labor.
- [the Kentucky Hills](place_kentucky_hills.md) : the forested mountains and coal seams of eastern Kentucky.

## Tension

Early coal companies enforce strict company town rules and restrict union organizing, leading to violent coal field conflicts.
