# Process: coal mining

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

## Initiated by

Underground coal miners, safety inspectors, and heavy equipment operators, conducted year-round in eastern Kentucky hills.

## Direction

Inside the mine shafts. Operating continuous miners, setting roof support bolts, monitoring ventilation air quality, and hauling coal cars to regional processing hubs.

## Lever

The traditional energy extraction process that fueled the state's industrial growth. It supports high-wage labor and community pride in the valleys, though it faces heavy environmental regulations and carries high physical safety risks.

## Echo

The mine operations require teamwork and trust. The process defines the historical work preserved by [Silas](persona_silas.md) in Harlan County, while trainer [Clara](persona_clara_ky.md) respects the method as a legacy of local industrial discipline.
