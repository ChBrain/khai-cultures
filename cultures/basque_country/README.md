# Basque Country: a khai culture

Basque Country staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-PV, language es. Euskadi: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Fueros abolition, Nervión industrialization, and Concert Económico encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_basque_country.md](play_basque_country.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_basque_country.md](pitch_basque_country.md), the Basque Country key.
- **Plots (history):** the Abolition of the Fueros of 1876, the Industrialization of the Nervión of the 1880s, and the Concert Económico of 1981.
- **Personas:** Iñaki (shipyard steel worker) and Amaia (traditional Basque cook), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** foral right (fiscal independence/historical charters/provincial tax), euskera heritage (linguistic isolation/Basque vocabulary/cuadrilla solidarity).
- **Places:** Nervión Estuary, Baserri Farm.
- **Processes:** cider pouring (traditional cider pouring), pelota play (handball court game).
- **Pieces:** a txistera, a txapela.
- **Plan:** the Euskera normalisation plan.
