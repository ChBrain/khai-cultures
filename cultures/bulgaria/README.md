# Bulgaria: a khai culture

Bulgaria staged as a full khai play, ISO BG, language bg. An ancient nation that
gave the Slavs their alphabet and yet stayed small, as a theatre: its history as
the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its language and faith encode,
its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_bulgaria.md](play_bulgaria.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_bg.md](pitch_bg.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Bulgaria's
  profile (deference to hierarchy, a strong need for certainty, fatalism and
  endurance, modesty, a melancholic and pessimistic streak, and warm hospitality)
  and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Golden Age of the First Empire and the birth of
  Cyrillic (9th-10th c.), the Yoke (the Ottoman conquest of 1396 and five
  centuries), the National Revival and Liberation (Paisius's spark of 1762, Levski,
  the April Uprising of 1876, and the Liberation of 1878), and the People's
  Republic and 1989 - each casting the elements it needs.
- **Personas:** Clement of Ohrid, Paisius of Hilendar, and Vasil Levski as the
  historic cast, plus two defining archetypes, Nevena (older, Orthodox, the
  rose-picking, grateful to "Grandfather Ivan") and Georgi (younger, the emigrant
  to the West whose Bulgarian thins amid the world's fastest depopulation). Each is
  wired to the language engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** кирилицата (Cyrillic and the language Bulgaria gave the Slavs),
  игото (the five-century Ottoman yoke, the defining trauma), дядо Иван
  (Grandfather Ivan, the deep and complicated bond with Russia).
- **Places:** Рилски манастир (Rila Monastery, which kept the faith and the
  language through the yoke), Шипка (Shipka Pass, the heroic defence of the
  Liberation).
- **Processes:** 24 май (the Day of the Slavic Alphabet, a nation with a holiday
  for its letters), розобер (the rose harvest of the Valley of Roses).
- **Pieces:** гайда (the Bulgarian bagpipe, whose song flew into space on the
  Voyager Golden Record), кисело мляко (Bulgarian yogurt, the Lactobacillus
  bulgaricus and the longevity pride).
