# Austria: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind
the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_at.md](pitch_at.md) is written from Austria's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no
number. Austria's profile differs sharply from Germany's - far lower power
distance, higher achievement, a touch more indulgence - which is part of why the
two German-speaking cultures feel so distinct. The scores and their source are
recorded here so the data is on the record without entering the prose.

| Dimension                     | Austria score (approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 11                      |
| Individualism                 | 55                      |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 79                      |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 70                      |
| Long Term Orientation         | 60                      |
| Indulgence                    | 63                      |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the Country Comparison tool and the
six-dimensions model of national culture). Scores are approximate, as published
by Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group.
