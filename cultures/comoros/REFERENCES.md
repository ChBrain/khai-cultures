# the Comoros: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain sources behind the plots,
personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_fr.md](pitch_fr.md) is written from the Comorian profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
National scores for the Comoros are not all separately published by Hofstede
Insights; the profile below is the orientation used to tune the pitch, read in line
with the regional and East African / Indian-Ocean values clusters and the
country's own collective, faith-centred, community-first self-understanding. The
orientation is recorded here so the basis is on the record without entering the
prose.

| Dimension                     | Comorian orientation (approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Power Distance                | high                           |
| Individualism                 | low (strongly collective)      |
| Motivation toward Achievement | moderate                       |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | moderate to high               |
| Long Term Orientation         | moderate                       |
| Indulgence                    | low (restrained)               |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the six-dimensions model of national
culture and the Country Comparison tool). The orientation is approximate and, where
country-specific scores are not published, is read from regional clusters and the
country's documented social profile; it never enters the prose.

from the public record. They are credited here and never claimed; the staging is
original work. The colonial period and the building of a young union across

taken on questions of governance.

The one archetype, [the ylang-ylang distiller](persona_le_distillateur.md), is not
a historical individual but a composite: the cueilleur and distiller who toils on
the volcanic slopes and at sea, saving for the grand marriage that will give him
his place among the notables.
