# Ukraine: a khai culture

Ukraine staged as a full khai play, ISO UA, language uk. A distinct nation of
ancient root and late statehood, as a theatre: its history as the plot line, its
people as the cast, the roles its language and freedom encode, its places, its
practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_ukraine.md](play_ukraine.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_uk.md](pitch_uk.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Ukraine's
  profile (warmth and collectivism, hierarchy yet a freedom-loving Cossack streak,
  deep endurance, an anxious need for certainty after much suffering, generosity,
  and an indomitable will) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** Kyivan Rus and the baptism (c. 988), the Cossack era and
  the Sich (1648), Shevchenko and the banned language (19th century), and
  Independence and Dignity (the Holodomor of 1932-1933, the 1991 independence, the
  2014 Revolution of Dignity, and the war since 2014 and 2022) - each casting the
  elements it needs.
- **Personas:** Taras Shevchenko, Bohdan Khmelnytsky, and Lesya Ukrainka as the
  historic cast, plus two defining archetypes, Oksana (older, the black earth, the
  village, the handed-down Cossack spirit and mother tongue) and Andriy (younger,
  Russian-speaking by upbringing, who switched to Ukrainian as an act of will after
  2014 and 2022). Each is wired to the language engine by the language-crossing it
  holds.
- **Positions:** українська мова (the Ukrainian tongue, called a Russian dialect
  and banned by the empire, revived by Shevchenko and reclaimed after 2014), воля
  (freedom, the Cossack freedom-spirit of the Zaporozhian Sich and the steppe), and
  незалежність (independence, the centuries-long assertion of distinct nationhood
  against the imperial "one people" claim).
- **Places:** Київ (Kyiv, the ancient capital and cradle of Rus, the golden domes
  and the Maidan), Січ (the Zaporozhian Sich, the Cossack island-fortress on the
  Dnipro).
- **Processes:** кобзарство (the kobzar and bandura tradition of the dumas), жнива
  (the harvest of the breadbasket and the black earth).
- **Pieces:** вишиванка (the embroidered shirt of woven identity), борщ (borscht,
  the national dish, recognized by UNESCO as Ukrainian intangible heritage).
