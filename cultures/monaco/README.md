# Monaco (Principauté de Monaco): a khai culture

Monaco staged as a full khai play, ISO MC, language fr. The sovereign principality of the French Riviera: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its maritime and glamorous life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_monaco.md](play_monaco.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_monaco.md](pitch_monaco.md), the Monaco key.
- **Plots (history):** the Grimaldi disguise of 1297, the independence charter of 1489, and the casino & tourism transformation of the 19th century.
- **Personas:** Albert (traditional Monegasque fisherman archetype, representing local identity) and Sophie (modern wealth management advisor), written in French.
- **Positions:** la dynastie Grimaldi (dynasty), le Rocher (the Rock/territory), le casino de Monte-Carlo (casino).
- **Places:** Palais Princier (the Palace on the Rocher), Port Hercule (yacht harbor).
- **Processes:** le Grand Prix de Monaco (Formula 1 street race).
- **Pieces:** la couronne princière (crown), le yacht de luxe (yacht).
- **Plan:** l'expansion maritime (offshore land extension).
