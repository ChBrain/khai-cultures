# La Rioja: a khai culture

La Rioja staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-RI, language es. La Rioja: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its San Millán Glosses, Rioja Wine Classification, and Ebro Riparian Forest Protection encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_la_rioja.md](play_la_rioja.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_la_rioja.md](pitch_la_rioja.md), the La Rioja key.
- **Plots (history):** the San Millán Glosses of the 10th century, the Rioja wine classification of 1925, and the Ebro riparian forest conservation.
- **Personas:** Gonzalo (Tempranillo vineyard cooper) and Olaya (vegetable canning worker), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** ebro ribera (fertile valley/market gardens/vineyards), sierra riojana (high mountains/sheep farming/sobriety).
- **Places:** San Millán Monasteries, Rioja Vineyards.
- **Processes:** wine barrel aging (oak barrel wine aging), vegetable canning (asparagus/pepper canning).
- **Pieces:** wine oak barrel, vineyard pruning shears.
- **Plan:** the Ebro riparian protection plan.
