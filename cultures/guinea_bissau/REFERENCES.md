# Guinea-Bissau: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede orientation that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain cultural and historical
sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_pt.md](pitch_pt.md) is written from Guinea-Bissau's orientation on
Geert Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no
number. Hofstede Insights does not publish a full standalone score set for
Guinea-Bissau; the pitch is written from the broadly attested West African
regional profile (low individualism / strongly collectivist, higher power
distance, and a communal, present-focused orientation), so the orientation is on
the record without entering the prose.

| Dimension                     | West African orientation (qualitative) |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | high                                   |
| Individualism                 | low (strongly collectivist)            |
| Motivation toward Achievement | moderate                               |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | moderate                               |
| Long Term Orientation         | lower (present-focused)                |
| Indulgence                    | moderate to higher                     |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the six-dimensions model of national
culture and the West Africa regional data). Values are qualitative and regional,
as published by Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group; no full standalone
Guinea-Bissau score set is published.
