# Cantabria: a khai culture

Cantabria staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-CB, language es. Cantabria: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Discovery of Altamira, Great Fire of Santander, and Coastal Sanitation Plan encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_cantabria.md](play_cantabria.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_cantabria.md](pitch_cantabria.md), the Cantabria key.
- **Plots (history):** the Discovery of Altamira of 1879, the Great Fire of Santander of 1941, and the Cantabrian coastal sanitation plan.
- **Personas:** Nel (Pasiego dairy farmer) and Sabela (anchovy salter and cooperative manager), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** mar cantabrico (coastal fishing/maritime reliance), valle pasiego (green mountain valleys/dairy pasture/sobriety).
- **Places:** Altamira Cave, Santoña Marshlands.
- **Processes:** anchovy salting (anchovy curing), sobao baking (sponge cake baking).
- **Pieces:** anchovy tin, pasiego basket.
- **Plan:** the Cantabrian coastal sanitation plan.
