# Plan: the Missouri River plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and water resource science designed to regulate reservoir levels and manage runoff into the Missouri River.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Manage the river watershed by optimizing reservoir releases, constructing shoreline erosion barriers, regulating agricultural fertilizer runoff, and monitoring water chemical levels.

## Orders

- To the State Water Commission: Enforce reservoir safety standards and audit shoreline repair contracts.
- To wheat farmers like [Harvey](persona_harvey.md): Support farm windbreak planting projects and report agricultural runoff changes near river basins.
- To drilling technicians like [Lincoln](persona_lincoln_nd.md): Support drilling pad water quality telemetry audits and maintain safe wastewater disposal codes.

## Implementation

The reservoir stage height measurements; the shoreline erosion audits; and the deployment of field water monitors.
