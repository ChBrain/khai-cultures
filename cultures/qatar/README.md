# Qatar: a khai culture

Qatar staged as a full khai play, ISO QA, language ar. A pearling people of the
Gulf and the desert keeping its heritage through swift change, staged as a
theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its
sea and desert encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all
tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_qatar.md](play_qatar.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_ar.md](pitch_ar.md), the Hofstede layer, written from
  Qatar's profile (a quiet, dignified collectivism, deference and the keeping of
  face, an unhurried patience inherited from the pearling years, and an
  uncompromising hospitality) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the pearling age, the founding of modern Qatar under
  Sheikh Jassim (1878), the pearl crash and the turn to oil, and the rise and
  the keeping of heritage - each casting the elements it needs, and each staging
  the sober cost of the hard years honestly.
- **Personas:** Sheikh Jassim bin Mohammed Al Thani (the founder), Sheikh
  Abdullah bin Jassim Al Thani (who held the people through the pearl crash to
  oil), the Nabati poets Majid bin Saleh Al-Khulaifi and Abdulla bin Hamad
  Al-Attiyah, plus one archetype, the pearl diver who drew the nation's living
  from the seabed breath by breath. Each is wired to the language engine by the
  relationship it holds to the mother tongue.
- **Positions:** the sea (the pearling heritage and the patience it bred), the
  desert (the Bedouin heritage of falconry, the horse, the camel, and Nabati
  verse), hospitality (the majlis, the coffee, and the keeping of the guest).
- **Places:** Doha (the pearling village at Al Bidda become the modern capital),
  Al Zubarah (the old pearling town now kept as heritage).
- **Processes:** pearl diving (the long summer voyage and the breath drawn at
  the seabed), falconry (the desert companionship of the falcon, kept as a
  living tradition).
- **Pieces:** the dhow (the wooden sailing vessel that carried the divers and
  the nation's living), sadu weaving (the women's craft of the desert tent and
  its memory of patterns).

## Provenance
