# Qatar: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain sources behind the plots,
personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_ar.md](pitch_ar.md) is written from Qatar's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without
entering the prose.

| Dimension                     | Qatar score (approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 93                    |
| Individualism                 | 25                    |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 55                    |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 80                    |
| Long Term Orientation         | 30                    |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the Country Comparison tool and the
six-dimensions model of national culture). Scores are approximate, as published by
Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group; figures for the Gulf states are
often grouped and should be read as indicative.

from the public historical and cultural record. They are credited here and never
claimed; the staging is original work. The hard years - the pearl crash and the
lean years before oil - are staged soberly on the public record, taking no
political side, and contemporary politics and regional disputes are out of scope.
