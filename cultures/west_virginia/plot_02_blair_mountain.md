## Cue

In August 1921, ten thousand armed coal miners clash with state police and guards, launching the Battle of Blair Mountain.

## Action

The conflict establishes the state's deep labor union heritage, developing [coal shoveling](process_coal_shoveling.md). Billy preserves historic miners' union badges, while Lilly documents environmental recovery files on the battlefield ridge.
