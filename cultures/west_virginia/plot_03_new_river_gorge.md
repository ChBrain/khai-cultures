# Plot: New River Gorge Tourism 1978

## Cue

In November 1978, Congress establishes the New River Gorge National River corridor, launching the tourism economy.

## Action

The declaration establishes Fayetteville as a white-water capital, developing [raft guiding](process_raft_guiding.md). Lilly coordinates river rapid navigation programs, while Billy manages safety database archives at regional coal museums.

## Stage

- [gorge stewardship](position_gorge_stewardship.md) : the wilderness recreation, river rapid safety, and environmental preservation.
- [river raft](piece_river_raft.md) : the inflatable rubber raft representing white-water navigation.
- [the New River Gorge](place_new_river_gorge.md) : the deep canyon and arch bridge site.

## Tension

Expanded tourism generates municipal waste and traffic issues in small river towns, prompting local park boundary disputes.
