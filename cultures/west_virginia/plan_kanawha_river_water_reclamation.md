# Plan: the reclamation plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and chemistry science designed to manage industrial run-off and restore river water quality in the Kanawha River basin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Reclaim water quality by regulating chemical discharges, neutralizing acid mine drainage, constructing filtration wetlands, and monitoring heavy metal levels.

## Orders

- To the Department of Environmental Protection: Enforce wastewater standards and audit chemical discharge permits.
- To river guides like [Lilly](persona_lilly.md): Coordinate watershed flora surveys and lead stream cleanup efforts.
- To coal miners like [Billy](persona_billy.md): Support mine drainage neutralization projects and report acid spills near mine pits.

## Implementation

The heavy metal water counts; the stream acidity tests; and the deployment of river filtration units.
