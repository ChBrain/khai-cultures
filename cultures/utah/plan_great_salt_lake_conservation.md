# Plan: the Great Salt Lake plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and watershed science designed to manage agricultural water diversions and protect the Great Salt Lake.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Conserve lake levels by auditing river inflows, regulating canal water rights, promoting water reuse, and monitoring lakebed dust emissions.

## Orders

- To the Division of Water Resources: Coordinate river basin water budgets and audit agricultural irrigation claims.
- To database developers like [Joseph](persona_joseph.md): Support water telemetry data audits and design public conservation tracking portals.
- To fruit farmers like [Martha](persona_martha.md): Implement drip irrigation systems and monitor topsoil runoff near river banks.

## Implementation

The river inflow meters; the lake level gauge audits; and the deployment of air quality dust monitors.
