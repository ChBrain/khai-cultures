## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_utah.md](pitch_utah.md) is written from Utah's profile inflected by its pioneer history, software technology corridors, and national park regions, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Utah base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 44                                     |
| Individualism                 | 78                                     |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 66                                     |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 44                                     |
| Long Term Orientation         | 28                                     |
| Indulgence                    | 62                                     |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Utah's low power distance in software design crews, active community panels, and low uncertainty avoidance in water conservation innovation).
