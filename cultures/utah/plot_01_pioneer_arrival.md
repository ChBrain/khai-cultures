# Plot: The Mormon Pioneer Arrival 1847

## Cue

In July 1847, Brigham Young leads the first pioneer company into the Salt Lake Valley, starting the settlement.

## Action

The arrival establishes cooperative irrigation networks, developing [pioneer industry](position_pioneer_industry.md). The [canning jar](piece_canning_jar.md) represents this preservation era. Martha coordinates crop logistics in Salt Lake agricultural districts, while Joseph records early pioneer genealogical files.

## Stage

- [pioneer industry](position_pioneer_industry.md) : the communal work ethics, family resourcefulness, and valley farming pride.
- [canning jar](piece_canning_jar.md) : the glass jar used to preserve seasonal orchard fruits.
- [Salt Lake Temple](place_salt_lake_temple.md) : the historic granite pioneer temple and central square.

## Tension

The rapid settlement displaces the Ute and Shoshone families from their traditional hunting grounds, leading to local conflicts.
