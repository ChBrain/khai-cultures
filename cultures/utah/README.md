# Utah: a khai culture

Utah staged as a full khai play, ISO US-UT, language en. The Beehive State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Mormon pioneer arrival, Transcontinental railroad completion, and Zion National Park expansion encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_utah.md](play_utah.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_utah.md](pitch_utah.md), the Utah key.
- **Plots (history):** the Mormon Pioneer Arrival of 1847, the Transcontinental Railroad Completion of 1869, and the Zion National Park Expansion of 1919.
- **Personas:** Joseph (Silicon Slopes developer) and Martha (fruit farmer), written in English.
- **Positions:** pioneer industry (communal work ethics/resourcefulness), outdoor adventure (recreational stewardship/mountain endurance).
- **Places:** Salt Lake Temple, Zion Canyon.
- **Processes:** fruit canning (food preservation), software coding (web development).
- **Pieces:** a golden spike, a canning jar.
- **Plan:** the great salt lake plan.
