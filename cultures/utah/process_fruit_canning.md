# Process: fruit canning

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Orchard growers, family canning instructors, and county cooperative volunteers, conducted during the late summer harvest season.

## Direction

In the farm kitchen. Washing harvested peaches, slicing fruit, boiling sugar syrups, packing glass jars, and executing boiling-water bath sterilization.

## Lever

The traditional food preservation process that secures family self-reliance and community food reserves. It supports local county fairs and heritage tourism, though it is limited by seasonal harvests and requires careful sterilization checks.
