## Shown

The capital on its volcanic ridge, the castle above the Old Town's steep closes and tenements, the Athens of the North in stone. A city of law, learning, and argument, where the reek of the tenements and the order of the New Town sit side by side.

## Offers

The place where the nation is governed, schooled, and argued into shape, where the [democratic intellect](position_egalitarianism.md) and the reformed [Kirk](position_the_kirk.md) take their public form.

## Withheld

The romance of the lost glen. Edinburgh is the Lowland, lettered, lawyerly Scotland, and it holds back the Highland grief that belongs to the country beyond it.
