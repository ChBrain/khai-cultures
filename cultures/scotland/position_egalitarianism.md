# Position: egalitarianism

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The power of a single levelling creed: we're a' Jock Tamson's bairns, all children of the one father, nane better born than another. It joins the democratic intellect - the belief that learning is the right of the lad o' pairts - to a deep scorn for side and rank.

## Orders

That a person be weighed by what they can argue and what they can do, not by birth or wealth, and that the able poor be given the schooling to rise. One does not put on airs, and one does not bow to those who do.

## Loses

Ground where the levelling sours into chippiness and envy, mistaking every success for showing off and pulling down whoever rises. Egalitarianism loses when it stops lifting the low and starts only resenting the high.

## Drives

It lives in [Eilidh](persona_eilidh.md), who takes nobody's rank on trust, and it gave the world the [Scottish Enlightenment](plot_04_enlightenment.md), the flowering of the democratic intellect.
