# Plot: Culloden 1746

## Taxonomy

[play_scotland](play_scotland.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

The Jacobite rising has marched far and then fallen back. On a cold moor near Inverness the clans of [the Highlands](place_the_highlands.md) draw up, hungry and outgunned, against a disciplined government army.

## Action

In less than an hour the Highland charge is broken by grapeshot and musketry, and the rising is crushed. What follows is harder than the battle: the breaking of the clan system itself, the proscription of the tartan and the pipes, and in the decades after, the Clearances that empty the glens of people for sheep. A whole kindred-ordered way of life, with its chiefs and its Gaelic, is torn out by the root.

## Stage

- [Mairi](persona_mairi.md): the older Highlander who watches the old life destroyed.
- [the Highlands](place_the_highlands.md): the glens and isles whose clan world is shattered.

## Tension

The defeat ends not just a rising but a civilisation, and the Highland tragedy becomes the nation's deepest grief. Out of the loss comes a long ache of emigration and a romance of the lost glen, even as the Lowland country turns its mind to other things.
