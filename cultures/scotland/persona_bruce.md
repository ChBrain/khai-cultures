# Persona: Robert the Bruce

## Taxonomy

[the clan](position_the_clan.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

The king who would not yield: a hard, patient, and cunning leader who fought his way from outlaw and fugitive to crowned and victorious sovereign of an independent Scotland. He learns from defeat, binds the kindreds to a common cause, and stakes everything on freedom rather than glory. He [speaks, hears, and thinks in](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) the mother tongue of his people as the plain ground beneath his crown, the tongue that binds the kindreds to a common cause. The Latin of the Church and the Norman French of the feudal court he [handles as borrowed tools](process_speaking_carried.md): there is the gap, the king rallies the folk in their own tongue while the charters and the chivalry of his age run on languages that are not theirs.

## Action

At [Bannockburn 1314](plot_01_bannockburn_1314.md) he holds his ground against the great English host and wins back the independence of the realm, gathering [the clan](position_the_clan.md) and the spearmen of the schiltron to a single, stubborn line.

## Shadow

The freedom he wins rests on a kingship taken in blood and on a unity held by force as much as loyalty. The independence he secures will have to be defended again and again, and the kindred bonds he leans on can fracture into feud as easily as they cohere.

## Tell

He rises again after every fall, choosing the long, hard road to freedom over the quick bargain that would buy him peace.
