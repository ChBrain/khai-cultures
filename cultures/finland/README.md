# Finland: a khai culture

Finland staged as a full khai play, ISO FI, language fi. A quiet, hardy country
of forest and lake as a theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as the
cast, the roles its language encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining
objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_finland.md](play_finland.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_fi.md](pitch_fi.md), the Hofstede layer, written from
  Finland's profile (sisu, comfort with silence, equality, modesty, trust) and
  naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Kalevala (1835), independence (1917), the Winter War
  (1939-40), and the modern education miracle - each casting the elements it
  needs.
- **Personas:** Elias Lönnrot and C.G.E. Mannerheim as the historic cast, plus
  two defining archetypes, Aino (older, sisu and the sauna) and Eero (younger,
  technology and the open world). Each is wired to the language engine by the
  language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** sisu (the grit that endures), tasa-arvo (equality),
  jokamiehenoikeus (everyman's right).
- **Places:** Helsinki (the capital), Suomenlinna (the sea fortress).
- **Processes:** sauna, kahvi (the coffee culture).
- **Pieces:** siniristilippu (the flag), puukko (the knife).
