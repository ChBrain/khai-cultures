# Castilla–La Mancha: a khai culture

Castilla–La Mancha staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-CM, language es. Castilla-La Mancha: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Las Navas de Tolosa, Don Quixote, and Aquifer Declaration encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## The production

- **Plots (history):** the Battle of Las Navas de Tolosa of 1212, the Publication of Don Quixote of 1605, and the La Mancha aquifer declaration of the 1980s.

- **Places:** Consuegra Windmills, Tablas de Daimiel.
- **Processes:** saffron picking (saffron harvesting), manchego pressing (cheese pressing).
- **Pieces:** saffron ceramic box, cheese esparto mold.
- **Plan:** the La Mancha aquifer restoration plan.
