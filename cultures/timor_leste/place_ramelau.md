# Place: Tatamailau

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Foho aas liu hotu iha rai-ki'ik, ne'ebe ema bolu Tatamailau, katak bei-foho. Iha nia rohan iha estatua Nossa Senhora ne'ebe hare ba rai tomak, no ema sa'e foho atu reza no atu hare loron sa'e husi leten. Iha foho nia okos iha to'os kafe ne'ebe fo aroma ba mundu, no aldeia ki'ik ne'ebe guarda lisan no lian lokal. Tatamailau mak fatin ne'ebe lulik no Igreja hasoru malu iha foho-rohan.

## Holds

- [kruz nia mai](plot_02_kruz_nia_mai.md): foho-rohan ne'ebe lulik no kruz hasoru malu no moris hamutuk.

## Offers

Fatin ne'ebe rai hatudu nia hun no nia haket ba bei-ala. Iha foho ema sente besik liu lulik no besik liu lalehan, no iha to'os kafe ema servisu rai ne'ebe nia inan-bei sira servisu ona. Tatamailau hatudu katak rai-ki'ik iha buat aas no kle'an ne'ebe la fa'an.

## Withheld

Moris ne'ebe la susar. Foho fo kafe maibe nia mos haruka servisu todan ho liman, no aldeia iha foho sente dook husi sidade no husi dalan. Tatamailau lori dignidade no terus-naruk hamutuk: rai ne'ebe susar maibe ne'ebe povu la husik.
