# Persona: Xanana Gusmao

## Taxonomy

[dalan naruk ba ukun-rasik-an](position_dalan.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Mane husi foho ne'ebe sai lider ba povu iha dalan naruk, no depois sai prezidente dahuluk ba nasaun foun. Nia hatene poezia no nia hatene povu, no nia lori sira-nia esperansa iha tempu ne'ebe esperansa susar atu hetan. Hakmatek, matenek, no laran-luak, nia ko'alia ba dame no ba hamutuk, no nia haruka povu atu lori istoria todan ho honra, la ho odi. [Lia-tetun](position_language_tet.md) nia iha nakloke: nia [ko'alia](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [hanoin](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), no [hakerek](process_writing_mother_tongue.md) iha lian rasik to'o nia poezia sai povu nian. [Portuges](../portugal/position_language_pt_pt.md) nia [lori hanesan sasan rasik](process_speaking_carried.md), no buat ne'ebe mundu li'ur hakerek nia [le no tuir iha papel](process_reading_followed.md). Maibe iha tempu balun terus la bele tama liafuan, no buat ne'ebe nia hetan iha dalan naruk iha fatin ne'ebe lian rohan: iha-ne'e luan loke no liafuan para.

## Action

Iha [restaurasaun 2002](plot_04_restaurasaun.md) nia simu povu nia esperansa no sai prezidente dahuluk ba nasaun foun, no nia haruka povu atu lori istoria todan ho honra no atu harii dame husi ne'e. Husi lider ba dalan naruk nia sai lider ba dame, no nia hatudu katak [dalan naruk ba ukun-rasik-an](position_dalan.md) bele sai hun ba hamutuk, la ba odi.

## Shadow

Lider ne'ebe lori povu liu tempu todan bele hetan susar atu lori sira iha tempu dame, bainhira luta la sai ema-ho-ema maibe sai oinsa harii nasaun. Tentasaun mak atu hanoin katan dame mai lais hanesan honra, bainhira nia presiza tempu naruk no pasiensia hanesan dalan ne'ebe liu ona.

## Tell

Nia ko'alia ba inimigu uluk ho lia-dame, no nia haruka povu atu la rai odi, tanba nia hatene katak nasaun foun la bele harii iha laran-todan.
