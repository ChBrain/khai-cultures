# Algeria: a khai culture

Algeria staged as a full khai play, ISO DZ, language ar (Modern Standard Arabic
carrying a distinctly Algerian voice, with the everyday Darija in its grain). The
largest country in Africa, mostly Sahara, where the Amazigh root and the Arab
branch meet as one cloth, as a theatre: its history as the plot line, its people
as the cast, the roles its languages and faith encode, its places, its practices,
and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_algeria.md](play_algeria.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_ar.md](pitch_ar.md), the Hofstede layer, written from
  Algeria's profile (a warm, group-first life lived out loud, hospitality and a
  fierce sense of dignity, wariness of what comes from above or far away, and a
  present-leaning, communal grain) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** Numidia and Massinissa, the Conquest and al-Andalus, the
  Ordeal of Independence, and Modern Algeria - each casting the elements it needs.
  The war of independence and the later hard years are staged soberly and with
  dignity, with no atrocity detail and taking no side; contemporary politics is
  out of scope.
- **Personas:** Emir Abdelkader (the scholar-statesman of honor and mercy),
  Massinissa (the Numidian king, the Amazigh root in a crown), Mouloud Mammeri
  (the Amazigh scholar who saved a tongue by writing it), and Cheb Khaled (the rai
  voice who carried Oran to the world), plus one archetype, the Kabyle villager
  (the open door, the mountain tongue, dignity lived daily). Each is wired to the
  language engine by the language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** hospitality and dignity (the open door and the proud, unbought
  self), the Amazigh root (the deep root honored beside the Arab branch as one
  cloth), and faith (the rest of the heart and the support of dignity, never a
  sword on the weak).
- **Places:** Algiers and the Casbah (the white city of layered history above the
  sea), the Hoggar and the Sahara (the deep south, the Tuareg, the four-fifths of
  the land that is living desert).
- **Processes:** couscous (the Amazigh dish become everyone's table, the one
  bowl that gathers the house and the guest), rai music (born in Oran, the song
  of the people carried from the quarter to the world).
- **Pieces:** the burnous (the Amazigh woolen cloak become the dress of dignity),
  Kabyle silver jewelry (the mountain's identity written in nielloed silver and
  coral, handed mother to daughter).

## Provenance
