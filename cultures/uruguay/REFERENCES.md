The defining persona [el oriental](persona_el_oriental.md) is an archetype, not a
historical individual: a composite who carries the culture's living temper - the
secular, modest, equal everyman of the mate, the rambla, the celeste, and the
candombe drum, in whom the country's rare equality and its quiet pride are lived.

The cultural framing - the secular welfare state and "la Suiza de America," la
garra charrua and the football pride, the federal-agrarian founding ideal and the
continental conscience, the mate, the candombe, and the small, equal, decent
republic - is drawn from Uruguay's own public self-understanding and staged
without taking sides.
