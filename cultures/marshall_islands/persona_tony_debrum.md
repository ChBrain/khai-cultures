## Projection

Juon ri-pepe im ri-tol eo me ear konono nan lal in kon jowan kon baam ko, im kon lometo eo ej wanlontak. Ke ej ajri, ear lo meram eo an juon baam jen ailon ettolok, im men in ear pad ippan ilo aolep mour eo an. Juon armej eo ej kijejeto im ej jab kijooror, ear jutak imaan lal in im ear kortok kinie eo an aelon eo nan mejan armej otemjej. [Kajin Majol](position_language_mah.md) ej kajin eo an jen jined: ej [konono](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [ron](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md), im [lemnak](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) ilo kajin eo an jen jined, kajin eo armej ro an rar bok kinie eo ie. [English](../usa/position_language_en_us.md) eo ej [bok einwot juon kein jerbal](process_speaking_borrowed.md), im ej [riit im loore](process_reading_followed.md) peba ko relap an lal in, im ej konono imaan armej otemjej kon jowan im kon lometo eo. Ear kortok kinie eo an armej ro an nan lal in ilo English, kajin eo lal in ej ron, ak kinie eo make ear walok ilo kajin Majol im ilo aelon ko an; im ijo ej alikkar bwe jet men reban lukkuun ettal jen juon kajin nan bar juon, im juon men ej pad wot ilo jikin eo ettolok ie, ijo me kajin eo ej kkar. Ej juon ian [manit in Majol](position_culture_manit_in_majol.md), im ej bok eddo eo an nan konono kon kinie eo an aolep aelon ko reddik.

## Action
