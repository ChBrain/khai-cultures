## Projection

Juon armej eo ej peran im ej buruon emman, ear jutak imaan armej otemjej im ear kwalok men eo ear pad nooj iio ko. [Kajin Majol](position_language_mah.md) ej kajin eo an jen jined: ej [konono](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) im [ron](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) ilo kajin eo an jen jined ippan armej ro an, im ej kojeraik bunton eo an kora ro im jined ro ilo kajin eo. [English](../usa/position_language_en_us.md) eo ej [bok einwot juon kein jerbal](process_speaking_borrowed.md) nan kwalok nan lal in, im ej [riit im loore ioon peba](process_reading_followed.md) jeje ko relap.
