# Plot: republic im compact

## Taxonomy

[play_marshall_islands](play_marshall_islands.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Tok jen iio ko an pata eo im baam ko, ri-Majol rej bukot bwe ren make jutak. [Amata Kabua](persona_amata_kabua.md), juon iroij eo elap im juon ri-tol, ej jutak nan tol aelon eo jen iumin pein ailon ko jet nan an erom juon republic eo ej make tol.

## Action

Aelon eo ej erom juon republic eo ej make jutak, im [Amata Kabua](persona_amata_kabua.md) ej erom president eo kein kajuon an. Aelon eo ej kcommane Compact eo (Compact of Free Association) ippan United States: aelon eo ej make jutak im ej make tol, ak ej barainwot kobaik ippan United States, im armej ro rej maron etal nan ailon eo im jerbal ie, im aelon eo ej bok jiban. Im aelon eo ej bukot jowan kon baam ko: ej kortok kinie eo an armej ro nan mejan lal in im ej bukot kowadon kon men eo ear walok. Aelon eo ej jutak ilo [Majuro](place_majuro.md), im ej dapij make jutak eo an im barainwot kobaik eo ippan aelon eo elap.

## Stage

- [Amata Kabua](persona_amata_kabua.md): president eo kein kajuon im iroij eo elap me ej tol aelon eo nan an make jutak.
- [Majuro](place_majuro.md): aelon eo jikin kapitol ijo me aelon eo ej erom juon republic ie.
- [the atolls and the Compact](position_aelon_kein_ad_im_compact.md): lemnak eo kon make jutak eo an aelon eo im kon Compact eo ej kobaik e ippan United States.

## Tension

Aelon eo ej make jutak, ak ej barainwot pad iturin juon aelon eo elap: ettolok ej elap ikotan make jutak im kobaik, im aelon eo ej aikuj jela ekojkan an dapij ainikien eo an make im barainwot bok jiban. Im jowan eo kon baam ko ejjanin lukkuun itok; aelon eo ej dapij bukot e ke ej jutak imaan lal in einwot juon republic eo edik im make jutak.
