# Piece: ballot box

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Maintained in county archives and displayed in the Wyoming State Museum in Cheyenne.

## Load Bearing

It represents the preservation of early women's suffrage, the scale of regional voter turnout, and the legacy of civic voting labor.

## Apparent

A small, polished walnut ballot box showing a narrow paper slot, a brass lock mechanism, and county registration codes.

## Yearbook

- Monitored and documented by historian [Esther](persona_esther.md) in her historic voting archives.
- Traditional democratic tool featured in the plot [The Women's Suffrage Act 1869](plot_01_suffrage.md).
