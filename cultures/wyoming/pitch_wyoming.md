# Pitch: the Wyoming key

## Taxonomy

The pitch of [Wyoming](play_wyoming.md). It tunes the thoughtful, polite, and mechanical expression of life in the Equality State.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Tenor

A thoughtful, polite, and mechanical tone. The communication is conversational, formal, and story-driven, combining geyser monitoring vocabulary with livestock herding and voting rights terms. It is a voice of basins and geysers - prioritizing aquifer protection plans, cleanroom spectrometer tests, and historic courthouse preservation. This formal language shapes both the grazing cooperatives and the conservation boards, valuing family labor and technical competence.

## Undertow

Under the hospitality lies a persistent geographic polarization between the rapidly expanding mountain tourist towns and the resource-dependent ranch lands. This resource divide breeds debates over deep-well groundwater pumping and watershed regulations, where state-led developmental projects are met with local protective instincts for traditional land properties.

## Nerve

The conviction that shared family labor, advanced technical competence, and respect for local grazing and conservation boards are the true anchors of a stable society.

## Echo

The hiss of a steam geyser and the creak of a leather saddle : an echo that is loud, wet, and mechanical.
