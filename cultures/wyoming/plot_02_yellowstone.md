## Cue

## Action

The park creation establishes public land conservation, developing [wyoming wilderness](position_wyoming_wilderness.md). The [ranger badge](piece_ranger_badge.md) represents park safety. Bruce coordinates geyser monitoring systems and park maps, while Esther manages tourist registry archives.
