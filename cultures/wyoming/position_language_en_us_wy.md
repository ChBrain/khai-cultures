## Has

The Western English of the Equality State, the plain mountain cadence of the Yellowstone high country and the prairie drawl of the Laramie plains. It holds a spare, self-reliant frontier plainness that both the ranger station and the town council carry, a Mountain West speech shaped by wide distances and thin air.

## Orders

Where it passes as native ground: the Yellowstone ranger station, the alpine trailhead, the Laramie town council, the prairie county road. It orders which turns of phrase and vowels mark a speaker as Wyomingite and sets the high-country ear against the plains one.

## Loses

Ground to General American, the flat national broadcast standard the screen and the commercial board carry, which trims the regional edges off the young. The corporate tourism register also crowds the slower civic and law-keeping speech of the plains toward the margin, treating the patient talk of the town council as secondary to the vocabulary of high-volume visitation.
