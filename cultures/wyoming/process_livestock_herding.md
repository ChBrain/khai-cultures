# Process: livestock herding

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Ranchers, cowboys, and livestock handlers, conducted during spring and autumn roundups.

## Direction

Across the open range. Riding horses to gather cattle, driving herds to summer pastures or winter shelters, branding young calves, and logging herd headcounts.

## Lever

The traditional ranching process that defines the state's agriculture. It supports thousands of cattle ranching jobs and beef exports, though it is limited by drought conditions and requires strict federal grazing permit audits.

## Echo

The herding requires range management. It defines the archive logs of [Esther](persona_esther.md) in Laramie, while park ranger [Bruce](persona_bruce.md) respects the method as an analog to detailed wildlife migrations.
