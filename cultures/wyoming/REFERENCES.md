## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_wyoming.md](pitch_wyoming.md) is written from Wyoming's profile inflected by its mountain ranges, geothermal geysers, and cattle ranches, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Wyoming base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 40                                        |
| Individualism                 | 78                                        |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Wyoming's low power distance in grazing boards, active community panels, and low uncertainty avoidance in geothermal and wilderness conservation innovations).
