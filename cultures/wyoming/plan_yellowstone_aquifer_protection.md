# Plan: the Yellowstone plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and geothermal science designed to protect hot springs and manage water resources in the Yellowstone Basin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Protect the geothermal aquifers by restricting nearby thermal extraction, monitoring geyser basin water tables, regulating municipal groundwater pumping, and auditing chemical concentrations.

## Orders

- To the National Park Service: Enforce geothermal safety standards and audit park watershed permits.
- To park rangers like [Bruce](persona_bruce.md): Support geyser basin temperature telemetry audits and maintain safe boardwalk visitor limits.
- To civic coordinators like [Esther](persona_esther.md): Coordinate local community conservation committee logs and advocate for traditional hot spring preservation.

## Implementation

The aquifer pressure measurements; the geyser basin temperature audits; and the deployment of telemetry monitors.
