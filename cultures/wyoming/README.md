# Wyoming: a khai culture

Wyoming staged as a full khai play, ISO US-WY, language en. The Equality State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Women's Suffrage Act, Yellowstone National Park establishment, and Devil's Tower designation encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_wyoming.md](play_wyoming.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_wyoming.md](pitch_wyoming.md), the Wyoming key.
- **Plots (history):** the Women's Suffrage Act of 1869, the Yellowstone National Park Establishment of 1872, and the Devil's Tower Memorial of 1906.
- **Personas:** Esther (suffrage historian) and Bruce (Yellowstone ranger), written in English.
- **Positions:** equality charter (voting equality/civic reform/legal justice), wyoming wilderness (alpine recreation/wildlife conservation/geothermal protection).
- **Places:** Yellowstone Canyon, Devil's Tower.
- **Processes:** geyser monitoring (logging geothermal activity), livestock herding (cattle cowboy herding).
- **Pieces:** a ballot box, a ranger badge.
- **Plan:** the Yellowstone aquifer plan.
