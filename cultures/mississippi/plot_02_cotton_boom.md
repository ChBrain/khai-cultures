## Cue

In the 1830s, forced Native land cessions and enslaved labor expand cotton plantations, launching high-volume exports.

## Action

The boom establishes the Yazoo Basin as a global cotton capital, developing [cotton picking](process_cotton_picking.md). The [cotton boll](piece_cotton_boll.md) represents this plantation era. Ida coordinates historical plantation land rolls, while Charles documents early delta transport routes.
