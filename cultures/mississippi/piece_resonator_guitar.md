## Load Bearing

It represents the preservation of early acoustic delta blues, the achievement of metal resonator cone amplification, and the legacy of slide performers.

## Apparent

A steel-body resonator guitar featuring a single aluminum spun cone coverplate, wooden neck, and slotted headstock.
