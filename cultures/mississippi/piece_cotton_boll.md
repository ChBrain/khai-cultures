# Piece: cotton boll

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Maintained in historic farm buildings and displayed in the Mississippi Agriculture Museum in Jackson.

## Load Bearing

It represents the memory of plantation cotton labor, the scale of early Southern exports, and the history of agricultural sharecropping.

## Apparent

A dry, brown cotton capsule displaying four open segments filled with fluffy white cotton fibers and small black seeds.
