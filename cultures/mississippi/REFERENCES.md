# Mississippi: References

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_mississippi.md](pitch_mississippi.md) is written from Mississippi's profile inflected by its blues plantations, cotton fields, and civil rights centers, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Mississippi base score (estimated / US proxy) |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 44                                            |
| Individualism                 | 78                                            |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 68                                            |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 44                                            |
| Long Term Orientation         | 24                                            |
| Indulgence                    | 64                                            |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Mississippi's low power distance in blues sessions, active local community panels, and low uncertainty avoidance in levee restoration innovation).
