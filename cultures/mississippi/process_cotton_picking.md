# Process: cotton picking

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Enslaved agricultural laborers, tenant sharecroppers, and seasonal field hands, conducted during the autumn harvest season.

## Direction

In the cotton field. Dragging canvas sacks along crop rows, hand-pulling white cotton fibers from sharp bolls, weighing sacks at trailers, and loading gin presses.

## Lever

The traditional agricultural harvesting process that drove the early plantation economy. It supports historical labor memory and civil rights histories, though it is limited by soil erosion and associated with systemic racial exploitation.

## Echo
