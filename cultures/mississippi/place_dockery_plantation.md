# Place: the Dockery Plantation

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

The historic wood-and-sheet-metal cotton farm complex in Sunflower County, featuring old barns, cotton gin houses, and boarding cabins.

## Holds

- the blues birth history in the plot [The Delta Blues Birth 1900](plot_01_delta_blues.md) : the boarding cabins.

## Offers

The historical farm tours and acoustic blues briefings where [Charles](persona_charles_ms.md) coordinates music workshops.

## Withheld

The tenant farmer debt cycle. While celebrated today as a monument to musical heritage, the farm historically operated on sharecropping systems that kept Black laborers in permanent debt and poverty, a legacy of economic control downplayed in local tourism pamphlets.
