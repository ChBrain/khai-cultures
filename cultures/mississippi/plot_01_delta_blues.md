# Plot: The Delta Blues Birth 1900

## Taxonomy

[play_mississippi](play_mississippi.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In the 1900s, musicians at Dockery Plantation implement slide guitar techniques, launching a global musical legacy.

## Action

The birth establishes the state as a blues heritage center, developing [slide guitar playing](process_slide_guitar_playing.md). The [resonator guitar](piece_resonator_guitar.md) represents this acoustic era. Charles coordinates blues workshops at community libraries, while Ida preserves historic recording ledger sheets.

## Stage

- [blues legacy](position_blues_legacy.md) : the musical expression, community oral traditions, and delta pride.
- [resonator guitar](piece_resonator_guitar.md) : the steel body acoustic guitar representing delta blues.
- [place_dockery_plantation](place_dockery_plantation.md) : the Sunflower County farm celebrated as the birthplace of the blues.

## Tension

Early commercial blues recordings exploit native delta musicians, denying them fair copyright royalties and financial security.
