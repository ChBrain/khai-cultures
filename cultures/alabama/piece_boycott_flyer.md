# Piece: boycott flyer

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

## Place

Maintained in historic museum archives and displayed in the Civil Rights Memorial Center in Montgomery.

## Load Bearing

It represents the coordination of peaceful protest, the legacy of Montgomery streetcar/bus activism, and the value of community-supported ride-shares.

## Apparent

A small, hand-printed paper pamphlet featuring mimeographed text that details boycott schedules and ride-share coordinates.
