# Plot: The Montgomery Bus Boycott 1955

## Taxonomy

[play_alabama](play_alabama.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

In December 1955, Rosa Parks is arrested for refusing to give up her seat on a Montgomery city bus, launching the boycott.

## Action

The boycott establishes coordinated community protest methods, developing [civil rights](position_civil_rights.md) standards. The [boycott flyer](piece_boycott_flyer.md) represents this organizing era. Albert curates historic boycott planning documents, while Sarah records oral histories of early civil rights families.

## Stage

- [civil rights](position_civil_rights.md) : the justice struggle, community organizing, and solidarity of the movement.
- [boycott flyer](piece_boycott_flyer.md) : the printed protest pamphlet used to coordinate ride-shares.
- [Dexter Avenue Church](place_dexter_avenue_church.md) : the historic church building that served as an organizing center.

## Tension

Boycott leaders face intense legal harassment and violent bombings, demanding extreme physical courage and cooperative protection.
