# Plan: the estuary plan

## Taxonomy

An operational directive: the strategic planning and environmental science designed to manage industrial waste runoff and protect the Mobile Bay Estuary.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Manage the estuary by monitoring chemical runoff at ports, restoring delta wetlands, upgrading wastewater systems, and regulating shipping ballast.

## Orders

- To the Department of Environmental Management: Enforce industrial discharge limits and audit shipping port water quality.

## Implementation

The chemical runoff measurements; the delta wetland surveys; and the deployment of harbor water monitors.
