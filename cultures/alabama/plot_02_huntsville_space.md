## Cue

In July 1960, NASA opens the Marshall Space Flight Center at Redstone Arsenal in Huntsville.

## Action

The facility establishes the city as a space engineering hub, generating rockets through [rocket propulsion](process_rocket_propulsion.md). Sarah designs micro-scale rocket control circuits, while Albert records oral histories of early space program workers.

## Stage

- [space pioneer](position_space_pioneer.md) : the scientific ambition, aerospace engineering, and technological design focus.
- [rocket propulsion](process_rocket_propulsion.md) : the calibration and testing of combustion engine stages.
- [Huntsville Test Stand](place_huntsville_test_stand.md) : the static test stands used to verify rocket booster engines.

## Tension

The rapid expansion of the space facility creates deep economic divisions between the high-tech suburbs and the rural Black Belt agricultural counties.
