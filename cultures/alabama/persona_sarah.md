# Persona: Sarah

## Taxonomy

[space pioneer](position_space_pioneer.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A thirty-six-year-old aerospace engineer in Huntsville. English, spoken with a fast-paced technical drawl, is her mother tongue, the language in which she [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) and writes her engineering logs. She speaks no foreign languages. Yet the loss lies in the seam: The slower, community-bound talk of historians like Albert and the traditional organizing histories of the Black Belt [she hears only like a distant echo](process_hearing_followed.md), feeling that the social legacy of the state is treated as a secondary constraint in the race for technological space breakthroughs.

## Action

She preserves [rocket nozzle](piece_rocket_nozzle.md) designs, coordinates testing protocols under [the estuary plan](plan_mobile_bay_estuary_protection.md), and studies [space pioneer](position_space_pioneer.md).

## Shadow

Her intense focus on aerospace research metrics and corporate testing investments can make her indifferent to municipal wastewater infrastructure disinvestment in the rural interior.

## Tell

She looks at a propulsion computer and says directly: "We are calibrating heavy thrust systems for planetary transport; you can't optimize advanced rocket designs if you're only focused on historic local preservation."
