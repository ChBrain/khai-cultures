# Persona: Sarah

## Taxonomy

[space pioneer](position_space_pioneer.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

A thirty-six-year-old aerospace engineer in Huntsville. [Alabama English](position_language_en_us_al.md), clipped into a fast technical drawl, is her mother tongue, the language she [speaks](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) at the console and writes into her engineering logs. She speaks no foreign languages. Here is where it thins out for her: the slower, porch-and-pew talk of historians like Albert, and the Black Belt's organizing history, [reach her only secondhand, like a signal off another frequency](process_hearing_followed.md), and she catches herself treating that history as a footnote to the harder problem of getting a rocket off the pad. She belongs to the [Heart of Dixie](position_culture_heart_of_dixie.md), the state's double inheritance, and she carries the half raised at the Huntsville test stands.

## Action

She preserves [rocket nozzle](piece_rocket_nozzle.md) designs, coordinates testing protocols under [the estuary plan](plan_mobile_bay_estuary_protection.md), and studies [space pioneer](position_space_pioneer.md).

## Shadow

Her intense focus on aerospace research metrics and corporate testing investments can make her indifferent to municipal wastewater infrastructure disinvestment in the rural interior.

## Tell

She looks at a propulsion computer and says directly: "We are calibrating heavy thrust systems for planetary transport; you can't optimize advanced rocket designs if you're only focused on historic local preservation."
