# Oklahoma: a khai culture

Oklahoma staged as a full khai play, ISO US-OK, language en. The Sooner State: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Land Run of 1889, Oklahoma oil boom, and Dust Bowl migration encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_oklahoma.md](play_oklahoma.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_oklahoma.md](pitch_oklahoma.md), the Oklahoma key.
- **Plots (history):** the Land Run of 1889, the Oklahoma Oil Boom of 1897, and the Dust Bowl Migration of the 1930s.
- **Personas:** Jimmy (oil rig manager) and Mary (Cherokee basket maker), written in English.
- **Positions:** sooner spirit (pioneer determination/oil rush ambition), tribal sovereignty (indigenous resilience/cultural preservation).
- **Places:** Oil Derrick, Cherokee Cultural Center.
- **Processes:** petroleum drilling (oil extraction), rivercane weaving (traditional basketry).
- **Pieces:** an oil drill bit, a rivercane basket.
- **Plan:** the soil conservation plan.
