## Cue

In April 1897, the Nellie Johnstone No. 1 well strikes oil in Bartlesville, initiating the commercial oil boom.

## Action

The strike establishes Oklahoma as a petroleum capital, developing [petroleum drilling](process_petroleum_drilling.md). Jimmy manages rig drilling logistics, while Mary coordinates agricultural soil safety programs around the well boundaries.

## Stage

- [sooner spirit](position_sooner_spirit.md) : the industrial ambition, resource extraction focus, and drilling pride.
- [oil drill bit](piece_oil_drill_bit.md) : the steel drill bit used in petroleum extraction.
- [the Oil Derrick](place_oil_derrick.md) : the historic drilling tower site.

## Tension

Rapid drilling expansion causes severe environmental pollution and infringes on communal tribal land allotments.
