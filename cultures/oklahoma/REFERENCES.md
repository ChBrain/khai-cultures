## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_oklahoma.md](pitch_oklahoma.md) is written from Oklahoma's profile inflected by its oil drilling fields, tribal headquarters, and prairie agricultural history, naming no dimension and carrying no number.

| Dimension                     | Oklahoma base score (estimated / US proxy) |

| Power Distance                | 44                                         |
| Individualism                 | 78                                         |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (US comparison, adjusted for Oklahoma's low power distance in drilling crews, active community panels, and low uncertainty avoidance in soil conservation innovation).
