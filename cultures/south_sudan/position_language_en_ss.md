# Position: English

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The authority of English as the young nation's chosen tongue of office, laid over Dinka and Nuer, Bari and Shilluk, Azande and the many mother tongues the land has always held. It is the language of the school, the court, the statute and the state, taken up as the working tongue of a country that many peoples must each be able to call their own. It was chosen over the Arabic of the north the south had long lived under, a tongue lifted above the many rather than born from any one of them, in which the newest nation on earth writes itself down and speaks to the world.

## Orders

What the new country sets down and conducts its public life in: the lesson, the ledger, the printed page and the business of the state. It orders that the affairs of the whole nation pass through one common tongue, so that peoples who carry different mother tongues at the cattle camp and the river can meet on level ground in the office, the assembly and the school, and it gives the young country a single language in which to address itself and the wider world at once, and to stand apart from the tongue of the seat of power it broke from.

## Loses

Ground to the mother tongues that carry the home and the name, the Dinka and the Nuer and the dozens more the working tongue never wholly holds, and ground to the praise-song and the named ox that were never English at all. The deepest things, the colours of the cattle and the grief of the long road, sit in the tongues of the clans and thin the moment they are carried over. It loses most where it reaches only the town and the school and never the camp, so that the official tongue of the new nation stays a far thing to the young man by the herd, who holds his whole world in the mother tongue.

## Drives

It drives a young nation of many peoples to hold its public life and its place in the world in one shared tongue. It is carried, not born, by [John Garang](persona_garang.md), who put the long waiting into words the whole gathering could follow; by [Taban lo Liyong](persona_taban.md), who set the oral songs of a dozen peoples down in it on the written page; by [Emmanuel Jal](persona_jal.md), who carried the country's hope out to the world in it from the stage; and by [Yaba Angelosi](persona_yaba.md), who reaches the young across all the tongues at once in it. It reached [the cattle-camp youth](persona_the_cattle_camp_youth.md) only faintly, a few worn words of school and state come far from the town, while his whole world stays in the tongue of the camp. So the working tongue shows who carries it and whom it merely reached, and binds the nation's peoples without erasing the mother tongues they were born to.
