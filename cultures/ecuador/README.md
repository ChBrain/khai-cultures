# Ecuador: a khai culture

Ecuador staged as a full khai play, ISO EC, language es. A small plurinational
country at the middle of the world, between sierra and coast, as a theatre: its
history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its languages encode,
its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.
The prose is written in Spanish; only the structural headings are in English.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_ecuador.md](play_ecuador.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_es.md](pitch_es.md), the Hofstede layer, written from
  Ecuador's profile (courteous and hierarchical, formal in the sierra and
  exuberant on the coast, hospitable, devout, indirect, with a deep pride of
  place) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** Quito the Andean heart (pre-conquest Quito, the conquest,
  the Audiencia de Quito, and Espejo's enlightenment), the Liberty of Pichincha
  (1822), the Indigenous Awakening, and the Middle of the World - each casting the
  elements it needs, and each staging the gap between promise and reality soberly.
- **Personas:** Eugenio Espejo, Antonio Jose de Sucre, Oswaldo Guayasamin, and
  Dolores Cacuango as the historic cast, plus the montubio, the coastal mestizo
  farmer of the lowlands, as the defining archetype. Each is wired to the language
  engine by the language-crossing it holds; for the Kichwa-rooted Cacuango the
  mother tongue is Kichwa with Spanish carried.
- **Positions:** the plurinational nation (the indigenous Kichwa peoples and the
  buen vivir, sumak kawsay), sierra and coast (the two Ecuadors, Quito versus
  Guayaquil), and Andean liberty (independence won in the mountains within the
  Bolivarian dream).
- **Places:** Quito (the Andean colonial capital among volcanoes, at the middle
  of the world) and the Galapagos (the enchanted isles, Darwin, the unique living
  laboratory).
- **Processes:** the Inti Raymi (the Kichwa solstice festival of the sun and the
  harvest), and La Mama Negra (the syncretic mestizo festival of Latacunga).
- **Pieces:** the toquilla straw hat (the finely woven hat the world wrongly calls
  the "Panama hat," truly Ecuadorian), and cacao (the "pepa de oro," the world's
  fine aroma cacao).

## Provenance

The Hofstede scores behind the pitch and the public-domain historical sources are
recorded in [REFERENCES.md](REFERENCES.md). The data never enters the prose; the
pitch is its invisible hand. Sources in the public domain are credited, never
claimed; the conquest, the inherited social strata, and the sierra and coast
divide are treated soberly on the public record and never luridly; the staging and
the architecture are original work.
