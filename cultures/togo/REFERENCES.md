# Togo: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch
(kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain cultural and historical
sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_fr.md](pitch_fr.md) is written from Togo's profile on Geert
Hofstede's model of national culture and names no dimension and carries no number.
The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without
entering the prose. Where a country-specific value is not published, the figure is
taken from the West African regional estimate Hofstede reports for the area.

| Dimension                     | Togo score (approx., regional) |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Power Distance                | 77                             |
| Individualism                 | 20                             |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 46                             |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 54                             |
| Long Term Orientation         | 16                             |
| Indulgence                    | 40                             |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the Country Comparison tool and the
six-dimensions model of national culture). Scores are approximate, as published by
Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group; where Togo itself is not separately
listed, the West African regional profile (high power distance, low individualism)
is used as the published proxy.

from the public cultural and historical record. They are credited here and never
claimed; the staging is original work. The coast's harder passages and the

the north and the south equally and taking no political side.
