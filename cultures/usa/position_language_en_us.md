# Position: American English

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

American English: the tongue a nation of immigrants made its own and the one the whole earth now has to learn. No law names it the official language, yet it is the speech the republic runs on, the tongue the creed was written in and the Gettysburg Address was cut down to. Plain, informal, and self-made, it flattened the ranks of the old country's English and took in words from every people that landed, and it is the solvent of the melting pot, turning a hundred mother tongues into one within a generation.

## Orders

That whoever you are and wherever you came from, you become American in English: it is the tongue of school, of work, and of the Dream, the price of the ticket and the proof of arrival. It asks every heritage language to step back to the kitchen table and the first generation, and it hands the newcomer the standard - speak it and you are heard, and your children will think in it.

## Loses

It rarely loses ground; it gains it, but at a cost paid by others. The mother tongues that come with each wave thin in one generation and vanish in the next, until a grandchild cannot speak to a grandmother, and the very ease of one shared tongue lets the native-born forget they never had to cross a border of speech - that it is always someone else who meets them halfway. Holding no official charter, it can also mistake sheer reach for a settled belonging it never had to earn.

## Drives

It is whole in the mouths of the men who wrote the country down: [Thomas Jefferson](persona_jefferson.md) drafted the creed in it, [Abraham Lincoln](persona_lincoln.md) forged its scripture-prose at Gettysburg out of a plain frontier American, and [Martin Luther King Jr.](persona_mlk.md) turned it into a weapon of conscience and read the founders' own words back as an unpaid bill. It is the only tongue [Hank](persona_hank.md) has ever needed, the frontier self-reliance turned into speech, the earth coming to him in his own words. And it is the working tongue [Maya](persona_maya.md) carries to buy the new country, the coin she spends as her parents' Spanish thins in her mouth. So the one tongue drives the nation to take in the whole world and make it over in English, and to forget, in the ease of it, the many tongues it quietly boils away.
