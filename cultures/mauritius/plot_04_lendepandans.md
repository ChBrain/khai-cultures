# Plot: lendepandans

## Taxonomy

[play_mauritius](play_mauritius.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Apre plizier zenerasion, sa zil ki ti vid ek ki tou bann pep ti ranpli ti vinn enn sosiete antie: indou, mizilman, kreol, sinwa, franko, sak enn ar so lalang, so lafwa, so kiltir, tou lor enn sel later. Ler ti vini pou sa pep la pran so prop destin dan so prop lame.

## Action

An 1968, [Port Louis](place_port_louis.md) trouv enn pei nef pran nesans: Moris vinn enn pei lib. Apre, li vinn enn repiblik. Sa nasion nef la pa ti fonde lor enn sel pep ni enn sel lafwa, me lor enn lide ki tou bann kominote viv ansam dan enn sel diniti, [pei larkansiel](plan_larkansiel.md). Diwali, Eid, Cavadee, Nouvel An Sinwa, Nwel vinn fet partaze; Morisien vinn lalang ki tou pep koz ansam; ek sega, ravann ek seggae vinn lam komin. Dan so liv ek so sante, bann vwa kouma [Malcolm de Chazal](persona_chazal.md) ti ed sa pei la trouv so prop figir ek konpran ki so melanz ek so larkansiel vo enn sante.

## Stage

- [Port Louis](place_port_louis.md): la kapital kot enn pei nef finn pran nesans.
- [Malcolm de Chazal](persona_chazal.md): la poet ki ti ed enn pei nef trouv so prop lam.
- [pei larkansiel](plan_larkansiel.md): la mandeman pou tou kominote viv ansam dan enn sel diniti.
- [lalit ansam](position_lalit_ansam.md): la lide ki tini sa nasion nef la ansam.

## Tension

Enn pei nef ne pa lor enn sel pep me lor enn larkansiel, ek sa li so pli gran riches ek so pli gran defi. Tini tou kominote ansam dan enn sel diniti, gard lakord bann lafwa, protez Morisien, sa li enn travay ki pa zame fini: sak zenerasion nef bizin reaprann viv ansam. Moris fer paret lemond ki diferan kapav viv ansam, ek tini sa larkansiel la, delika ek presye, kouma so pli gran trezor.
