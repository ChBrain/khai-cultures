# Position: lakord bann lafwa

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Lor enn sel zil, bann lapriyer pe monte ansam: tanp indou, moske, legliz, pagod sinwa, sak enn akote lot. Diwali, Eid, Cavadee, Nouvel An Sinwa, Nwel, Maha Shivaratri, sa bann fet la pa res ferme dan enn sel kominote, zot debord lor tou. Enn vwazin amenn gato pou Eid, enn lot partaz so gato pou Diwali, ek bann zanfan konn tou bann fet kouma zot prop. Sa lakord la, kot lafwa enn dimoun respekte par lot, se enn bann pli zoli kado sa zil la.

## Orders

Ki sak lafwa ena so plas egal, ek ki personn lafwa pa lor lot. Ki bann fet partaze, pa zis tolere me selebre ansam, parski enn lafet enn vwazin vinn enn lafet pou tou. Ek ki sa respe la, li pa enn favar me enn devwar: dan enn zil kot tou pep finn debarke depi lwen, respe lafwa lot se baz pou viv ansam.

## Loses

Li pers later kan dimoun servi lafwa pou separe plito ki pou rasanble, kan enn group ferm lor li meme. Li pers later osi kan dimoun pran sa lakord la kouma enn aki ki touletan pou la, ek bliye ki li delika, ki li bizin antretni avek respe sak zour, avek sak gato partaze ek sak lafet ki nou selebre ansam.

## Drives

Viv dan enn ekrivin ki ti ekrir lor lavi bann ki ti amenn zot lafwa depi Lenn dan kann, ek dan zwer ravann, ki fer tou kominote, tou lafwa, danse otour enn sel ravann. Li pous Moris pou gard so lakord bann lafwa, kot Diwali, Eid ek Cavadee briye ansam dan enn sel lesiel.
