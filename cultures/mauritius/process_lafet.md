# Process: bann lafet partaze

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Enn fami ki alim bann lalanp pou Diwali, enn vwazin ki prepar gato pou Eid, enn kominote ki marse pou Cavadee, enn lakaz ki met rouz pou Nouvel An Sinwa, enn fami ki dekor sapin pou Nwel. Sak fwa, sa lafet la pa res ferme dan enn sel lakaz: vwazin partaz gato, zanfan al gete, ek tou enn vwazinaz vinn dan lafet.

## Direction

Ver respe ek partaz: bann lafet partaze fer ki enn lazwa enn kominote vinn enn lazwa pou tou. Diwali, Eid, Cavadee, Nouvel An Sinwa, Nwel, sak enn vinn enn moman kot tou pep zwenn, kot enn vwazin selebre ar enn lot, ek kot bann zanfan grandi konn tou bann fet kouma zot prop.

## Lever

Li pouse par sa konviksion ki dan enn zil kot tou pep finn debarke depi lwen, respe lafwa lot se baz pou viv ansam. Li tini kont sa danze ki kominote ferm lor zot meme; kont li ena sa rapel ki sa lakord la li delika, ek bizin antretni avek sak gato partaze ek sak lafet ki nou selebre ansam.

## Echo

Bann lafet partaze fer tande seki [lakord bann lafwa](position_lakord_lafwa.md) bizin: enn fason pou diferan lafwa selebre ansam ek pa zis akote. Dan sa partaz la [zwer ravann](persona_ravann_zwer.md) trouv so prop verite, kot personn pa demann ki ki lafwa avan tou dimoun danse.
