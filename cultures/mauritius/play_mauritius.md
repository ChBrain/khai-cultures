# Play: Moris

## Name

Moris, enn ti zil dan losean Indien, dan lwes Lamerik, lwen depi tou kontinan, ek so kapital Port Louis. ISO MU. Isi nou koz Morisien, sa kreol baze lor franse ki preske tou Morisien partaze, akote franse, angle ek bhojpuri. Enn zil kot personn pa ti pe viv avan, ek ki finn vinn enn pei kot tou bann pep latere finn rasanble lor enn sel later.

## Arc

Moris li pa enn pei kot enn sel pep finn grandi lor so prop later. Li enn zil ki ti vid, ki dimoun finn vinn rampli depi partou: bann Indo-Morisien indou ek mizilman, bann Kreol desandan Lafrik ek Madagascar, bann Sino-Morisien, bann Franko-Morisien. Sak vag inn amenn so lalang, so lapriyer, so manze, so lamizik, ek tousala finn melanze san konfonn. Listwar inn pas par bann moman dir, ki nou rapel avek respe ek diniti: lesklavaz, ki Le Morne so montagn tini dan memwar; ek lavenement bann angaze ki ti debarke kot Aapravasi Ghat pou travay dan kann. Apre, zil la inn vinn enn pei lib, enn larkansiel kot diferans pa enn miray me enn riches. Anba tousala bat enn sel ker: "lalit ansam", viv ansam. Diwali, Eid, Cavadee, Nouvel An Sinwa, Nwel, tou se bann fet ki tou dimoun partaze. Sega ek so ravann fer later tranble, dholl puri ek gato pima sant dan bazar, ek montagn ek lagon vey lor enn pep ki finn aprann ki diferan kapav viv ansam ek touletan res ansam.
