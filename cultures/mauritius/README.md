# Mauritius: a khai culture

Mauritius staged as a full khai play, ISO MU, language mfe (Mauritian Creole /
Morisien). A rainbow island where many peoples live together as one, staged as a
theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its
languages and faiths encode, its places, its practices, and its defining objects,
all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house
that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its
Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_mauritius.md](play_mauritius.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_mfe.md](pitch_mfe.md), the Hofstede layer, written from
  Mauritius' profile (a warm, relationship-first society that holds family and
  community close, defers to elders and to one another's faiths, prefers to
  accommodate rather than to break, and carries a small island's care for a
  delicate balance) and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the peopling of the island, slavery, the indentured, and
  independence - each casting the elements it needs, and each treating the hard
  passages (slavery and indenture) soberly and with dignity, never in atrocity
  detail.
- **Personas:** Malcolm de Chazal (poet and painter), Ananda Devi (novelist), Ti
  Frer (the sega legend), and Abhimanyu Unnuth (the writer of the indentured in
  Hindi) as the real cast, plus one archetype, the ravanne player, who makes every
  community dance around a single drum. Each is wired to the language engine by the
  language-crossing it holds.
- **Positions:** lalit ansam (living together, the island held by the daily choice
  to live as one), Morisien (the creole that binds a people drawn from six
  continents into one mouth), lakord bann lafwa (the harmony of faiths, Diwali,
  Eid, Cavadee, Chinese New Year and Christmas all shared).
- **Places:** Port Louis (the capital, its market and the Aapravasi Ghat where the
  indentured arrived), Le Morne Brabant (the mountain of the maroons, holding the
  memory of slavery with dignity).
- **Processes:** sega (the music and dance of the ravanne, the island's shared
  soul, and its child seggae), and the shared festivals (the faiths celebrated
  together rather than merely side by side).
- **Pieces:** the dodo (the lost bird, the island's tender emblem), the ravanne
  (the drum at the heart of sega around which all communities dance).

## Provenance
