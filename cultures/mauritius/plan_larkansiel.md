# Plan: pei larkansiel

## Taxonomy

Mandeman ki sa zil la finn donn limem depi li vinn lib: travay konstan pou tini [lalit ansam](position_lalit_ansam.md), kot tou kominote viv ansam dan enn sel diniti, pou protez [Morisien](position_language_mfe.md) ek tou lalang ki form sa larkansiel la, ek pou gard [lakord bann lafwa](position_lakord_lafwa.md) kot Diwali, Eid ek Cavadee briye ansam dan enn sel lesiel.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Direction

Tini tou kominote dan enn sel diniti egal, kot personn pa lor lot; gard sa lakord bann lafwa kot tou fet partaze; protez Morisien ek tou lalang manman kouma enn trezor; rapel avek respe seki finn pase, lesklavaz ek bann angaze, san kasiet me san efas; ek fer ki sak zenerasion nef reaprann ki diferan dwatet viv ansam, parski sa larkansiel la li delika ek bizin tini li sak zour.

## Orders

- A enn dimoun senp kouma [zwer ravann](persona_ravann_zwer.md): tap to ravann ek fer tou kominote danse ansam, kot personn pa demann ki ki lafwa.
- A enn ekrivin kouma [Ananda Devi](persona_devi.md): get to zil drwat, gard plas pou seki ankor bizin geri, ek donn enn vwa bann ki sa larkansiel la kapav bliye.
- A nasion la: gard "lalit ansam" vivan, protez to lalang ek to lakord bann lafwa, ek pa zame pran sa larkansiel la kouma enn aki ki pa bizin antretni.

## Implementation

Enn pei ki finn vinn lib depi 1968, ki finn fer enn nasion sorti depi enn zil ki ti vid, ek ki finn tini so larkansiel: Diwali, Eid, Cavadee, Nouvel An Sinwa, Nwel partaze, Morisien dan tou bus, ek bann fet ki tou pep selebre ansam. Me sa lakord la res enn travay konstan, ki bizin antretni avek respe sak zour, pou ki diferans res enn riches ek pa vinn enn miray.
