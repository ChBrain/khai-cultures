# Piece: tafuliae

## Taxonomy

Parent group: pieces

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Place

Long maritim, long peimen, an long stretem: longfala stringgi blong olketa smol pis sel wea olketa brekem, holem, an stringim tugeta, samfala red, samfala waet, samfala blak, han go long han kasem hem kamap longfala olsem wanfala man. Hem nao tru seleni blong olketa solwata pipol blong Malaita, mo bigwan from Langa Langa, wea olketa givim long maritim, yusum blong peim braed, an putim long bigfala kastom stretem.

## Load Bearing

Hem holem [solwata na bus](position_solwata_na_bus.md), bikos hem nao rod wea joinim solwata an bus. Long wanfala neson blong planti laen, tafuliae hem wanfala samting wea evriwan luksave: solwata pipol wakem hem, an bus pipol tekem hem long tred blong kaikai blong gaden, so seleni ia ran bitwin tufala wol an joinim olketa. Hem peim braed, hem peim long stretem afta wanfala rong, an hem soem se wanfala famili garem manas an save. Aninit long evri kala an evri pis sel i garem wanfala save blong kastom wea ol olfala givim go. Sapos no garem disfala seleni, ol solwata pipol bae lus wanfala bigfala pat blong save blong olketa.

## Apparent

Wanfala stringgi blong olketa smol raon pis sel, garem hol insaed, stringim tugeta long wanfala rop. Long lukluk hem nomoa sel an rop. Bat hevi blong hem hem stap insaed long wanem kastom putim long hem: hu nao wakem hem, humas waka hem tekem, an wanem hem fit blong long maritim an long stretem.

## Yearbook

- Olketa solwata pipol blong Langa Langa an Malaita wakem disfala seleni longfala taem bifo, han go long han.
- Hem nao seleni blong peim braed an blong putim long bigfala kastom stretem afta wanfala rong.
- Kasem tude hem stap yet wanfala tru seleni long olketa kastom samting, nomata seleni blong govman kam tu.
