# Persona: Sir Peter Kenilorea

## Taxonomy

[wantok na kastom](position_wantok_na_kastom.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Wanfala man blong Malaita wea kam from wanfala vilis, pikinini blong wanfala pasta, wea go skul, kamap tisa, an bihaen leadim neson go long independens long 1978 olsem fastaem Praem Minista. Hem wanfala man wea kwaet, garem strong tingting, an holem tufala samting wanfala: kastom blong ples blong hem an niufala wei blong wanfala neson. Tok ples blong hem an [Pijin](position_language_pis.md) hem garem fulwan: hem [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) an [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) long tok blong ples blong hem an long Pijin, tok blong wantok blong hem. [English](../united_kingdom/position_language_en_gb.md) hem [karem olsem wanfala tok blong waka](process_speaking_carried.md), an hem [ridim an folom hem gud](process_reading_followed.md) long skul, long govman, an long olketa bigfala miting wetem ol nara kantri, an wetem English ia hem [raetem](process_writing_drafted.md) ol pepa wea putim niufala neson antap long graon. Bat taem hem mas tokim wantok blong hem wanem hem fil tru long bel, hem savve se English no garem ol toktok ia: ol samting wea mo dip insaed long hem, blong ples an blong laen, hem nidim tok ples blong sei, an long datfala ples nao tok lus klosap long edge, wea wanfala niufala neson mas sei wetem wanfala olfala tok.

## Action

Long [independens na Maasina Rul](plot_03_independens_na_maasina_rul.md) hem leadim neson go aot from protektoret an putim hem antap long graon olsem wanfala niufala kantri, holem [wantok na kastom](position_wantok_na_kastom.md) wanfala wetem niufala govman. Hem tekem disfala save blong Maasina Rul, se olketa pipol mas garem mo se long olketa seleva, an mekem hem kamap wanfala neson wea olketa Solomon man nao leadim hem.

## Shadow

Disfala man wea joinim kastom an neson karem hevi tu: long wanfala kantri blong planti aelan an planti laen, hem had tru blong holem evri saet wanfala, an wantok wea hem mas sapotem savve pulum hem long wan saet winim narawan. Hem mas wakem wanfala neson aot long olketa laen wea no semsem, an datfala wo bitwin wantok an neson no finis long taem blong hem.

## Tell

Hem standap long disfala bigfala dei blong independens olsem wanfala leada blong olgeta neson, bat hem savve gud yet hu hem from ples blong hem, from laen blong hem, an from tok ples blong hem.
