# Plot: Gwadalkanal

## Taxonomy

[play_solomon_islands](play_solomon_islands.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Long 1942, long Wol Wo Tu, bigfala faet kam kasem olketa aelan blong Solomon. Olketa nara kantri faet antap long Gwadalkanal blong holem disfala graon, bikos hem important blong rod blong Pasifik wo. Olketa Solomon man, nomata hem no faet blong olketa, kam insaed blong helpem long skaut, long karem load tru long bus, an long lukaftarem olketa soldia.

## Action

Long Gwadalkanal nao wanfala bigfala faet go on, long bus an long si an long skae, plande man pundaon long tufala saet. Olketa Solomon man olsem [Sir Jacob Vouza](persona_vouza.md) kam insaed olsem skaut, karem nius tru long bus, helpem karem load an helpem olketa soldia wea kasem soa. Disfala faet kamap wanfala turning point blong Pasifik wo: bihaen long Gwadalkanal, rod blong wo sentem. Bat hem nao wo blong olketa nara kantri, an olketa Solomon man pundaon insaed long wanfala faet wea no statem long olketa, wea putim trabol an seksek antap long graon blong olketa.

## Stage

- [Sir Jacob Vouza](persona_vouza.md): skaut an hiro wea stap strong long bigfala faet an karem nius nomata hem safa.
- [Gwadalkanal](place_gwadalkanal.md): aelan wea kamap ples blong bigfala faet wea sentem rod blong Pasifik wo.
- [bigfala faet na stretem](position_bigfala_faet_na_stretem.md): disfala bigfala faet wea aelan ia luksave, wea mas garem wanfala stretem bihaen.

## Tension

Gwadalkanal givim neson ia wanfala hiro olsem Vouza an wanfala ples long bigfala stori blong wol, an olketa Solomon man soem strong an wantok long taem blong nid. Bat disfala faet hem no blong olketa: hem wo blong olketa nara kantri wea kam faet antap long graon blong olketa, an hem lusim plande man, plande gun an aian wea stap yet long bus, an wanfala save se nomata neson ia helpem long disfala bigfala samting, hem no garem se long wanem nao happen long graon blong hem.
