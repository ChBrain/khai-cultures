# Persona: Sir Jacob Vouza

## Taxonomy

[bigfala faet na stretem](position_bigfala_faet_na_stretem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Wanfala man blong Gwadalkanal, wanfala olfala polis man, wea long Wol Wo Tu kamap skaut blong helpem olketa soldia, an nomata olketa enemi holem hem an mekem hem safa, hem no talem eni samting, an bihaen hem ranawe an karem nius go bak. Hem wanfala man wea strong tru, garem hat blong faet, bat garem kaen hat tu blong wantok. Tok ples blong hem an Pijin hem garem fulwan: hem [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), an [harem](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) long tok blong ples blong hem an long Pijin, tok wea hem yusum blong story wetem evri wantok long aelan. English hem [karem olsem wanfala tok blong waka](process_speaking_carried.md) wea hem lanem long taem blong polis an long taem blong wo, blong story wetem olketa soldia, an wanem ol olketa raetem long pepa hem [folom](process_hearing_followed.md) gud nomata hem no go skul longfala. Bat ol samting wea mo dip insaed long bel blong hem, blong wanem nao hem stap strong taem olketa mekem hem safa, blong laen an blong ples wea hem laekem winim laef blong hem, ol samting ia English no garem tok blong hem; long datfala edge nao tok lus, an hem nidim tok ples blong holem wanem hem kanduit no savve sei long tok blong narafala man.

## Action

Long [Gwadalkanal](plot_02_gwadalkanal.md) hem stap strong long bigfala faet wea sentem rod blong Pasifik wo, helpem olketa soldia tru long bus an karem nius nomata hem safa tumas, an karem disfala [bigfala faet](position_bigfala_faet_na_stretem.md) olsem wanfala man blong neson. Bihaen long wo hem standap blong stretem an joinim olketa man bak, mekem laen an laen kam wanfala moa.

## Shadow

Disfala hiro wea karem skar blong wo karem hevi tu: hem faet long wanfala bigfala wo blong olketa nara kantri, no blong olketa Solomon man, an datfala wo putim trabol an seksek antap long graon blong olketa. Wanfala man wea strong tru long faet mas lanem tu hao blong leidaon spia an stretem, bikos faet seleva no savve joinim olketa man bak.

## Tell

Hem savve karem evri hevi blong wo an no talem eni samting long olketa enemi, bat hem givim olgeta hat blong hem long wantok blong hem an long ples wea hem kam from.
