# Position: bigfala faet na stretem

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Disfala save se aelan ia luksave tufala kaen faet, an save tu se afta eni faet i mas garem wanfala stretem. Fastaem hem nao bigfala faet blong Wol Wo Tu, taem olketa bigfala kantri faet antap long Gwadalkanal, an olketa Solomon man helpem long skaut an long karem load tru long bus, nomata hem no faet blong olketa. Seken hem nao ol Tensions, long 1998 go kasem 2003, taem trabol kamap bitwin samfala laen long Gwadalkanal an samfala laen wea kam from Malaita, wanfala trabol bitwin wantok an wantok long insaed long neson seleva. Bat aninit long evri faet i garem wanfala save wea olfala olsem aelan ia: se trabol no savve finis sapos pipol no sidaon, story, kilim pig, givim seleni, an stretem bak. Stretem hem nao wei blong kastom blong joinim bak olketa man afta wo, blong tekem awe kros an mekem laen kam wanfala moa.

## Orders

Se afta eni rong o eni faet, olketa saet mas kam tugeta, talem trutok, an stretem long kastom wei wetem pig an seleni an kaikai; se trabol wea olketa haedem nomoa bae kamap bigwan bihaen, so hem mo gud blong fes hem an stretem hem stret nao; an se stretem hem no minim fogetem, bat minim joinim bak olketa man blong olketa savve stap tugeta gen.

## Loses

Hem lus graon taem pipol haedem kros an no stretem, taem trabol go andanit an wet, olsem hem happen bifo long ol Tensions. Hem lus graon tu taem stretem hem kamap nomoa wanfala seremoni antap, taem olketa kilim pig an talem tok bat kros i stap yet insaed long bel, kasem stretem no garem rut an trabol savve kam bak.

## Drives

Hem stap insaed long evri man wea long Gwadalkanal stap strong tru long bigfala faet an kamap hiro, an wea bihaen long laef blong hem hem standap blong stretem an joinim olketa man bak. Hem pusum Solomon Aelan blong luksave se afta evri faet, smolwan o bigwan, neson ia mas sidaon an stretem bak, bikos hem nao onli rod blong wantok an neson blong stap tugeta gen.
