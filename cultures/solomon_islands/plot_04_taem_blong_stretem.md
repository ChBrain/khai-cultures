Disfala trabol gru go go kamap wanfala bigfala hevi wea olketa kolem ol Tensions, wanfala trabol bitwin wantok an wantok long insaed long neson seleva.

## Action

Long 1998 go kasem 2003, ol Tensions putim hevi antap long neson: govman wik, ekonomi foldaon, an plande man mas lusim hom blong olketa. Olketa man blong fising no savve go aot seif, maket klos, an laef blong evridei pipol kam had. Long 2003 wanfala rejinol misin kam, RAMSI, blong helpem mekem peace kam bak an mekem govman strong gen. Bihaen long datwan nao longfala wok blong stretem statem: tru long kot, tru long wanfala komisin blong trutok, an mo important tru long kastom stretem, taem olketa laen sidaon, talem trutok, kilim pig, an joinim bak. Disfala wok blong stretem no finis kwik; hem nao wanfala longfala wok wea go on kasem tude.
