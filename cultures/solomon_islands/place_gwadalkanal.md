## Shown

Wanfala bigfala aelan blong neson, wea garem Honiara, taon blong kapitol, sidaon long not kos blong hem. Antap long disfala aelan i garem maunten an bus, longfala rif, an olketa vilis blong solwata pipol an bus pipol. Bifo long Wol Wo Tu disfala graon kamap ples blong wanfala bigfala faet bitwin olketa nara kantri, an plande man foldaon long bus an long si raon long hem. Bihaen, long taem blong ol Tensions, Honiara an graon raon long hem kamap ples blong trabol bitwin samfala laen. Hem nao aelan wea garem tufala bigfala stori blong neson antap long graon blong hem.

## Offers

Hem givim aelan ia wanfala stori blong faet an wanfala stori blong wei blong kam tugeta bak.

## Withheld

Hem no givim wanfala kwaet histri. Gwadalkanal karem hevi blong tufala bigfala trabol antap long graon blong hem: wanfala wo blong olketa nara kantri wea olketa Solomon man pundaon insaed, an ol Tensions wea brekem laen long laen long insaed long neson seleva. Hem holem disfala save se nomata bigfala samting happen hia, neson ia mas faendem rod blong stretem bak.
