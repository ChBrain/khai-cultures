# Persona: man blong fising

## Taxonomy

[solwata na bus](position_solwata_na_bus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Wanfala solwata man blong lagun, wea go aot long kanu evri moning blong kasem fis, an wea save evri ples insaed long rif, evri ples wea fis stap an evri taem blong taed. Hem wanfala man wea isi, wok had, an laef blong hem an blong famili blong hem kam from solwata. Hem save lukluk long klaod an long si an talem yu sapos wo bae kam. Tok ples blong hem an Pijin hem garem fulwan: hem [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), an [harem](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) long tok blong ples blong hem an long Pijin, tok wea hem yusum wetem wantok long lagun an long maket. English wea kam long rajio an long govman hem [folom](process_hearing_followed.md) lelebet nomoa, an wanem ol man raetem long pepa hem [tirim go go](process_reading_deciphered.md) had taem hem mas. Bat evri save blong solwata, ol nem blong fis, ol nem blong wind an taed, an hao blong ridim si, ol samting ia stap onli long tok ples blong hem; long datfala edge tok lus, an wanfala man wea no garem disfala tok no savve harem wanem nao si i tok long disfala man.

## Action

Long [taem blong stretem](plot_04_taem_blong_stretem.md) hem nao man blong evridei, wea long taem blong ol Tensions wantem nomoa se laef i kam gud bak, se hem savve go aot long lagun seif, salem fis long maket, an mekem famili kaikai. Hem karem [solwata na bus](position_solwata_na_bus.md) tru long evridei laef, an hem nao saes wea soem sapos neson ia stretem o nogat: long taem hem savve go fising seif gen.

## Shadow

Disfala isi laef savve foldaon kwik taem trabol kam: long ol Tensions, wanfala man blong fising savve no go aot from fraet, maket savve klos, an famili savve no garem kaikai. Hem nao fastaem man wea fil taem neson no stretem, an hem nao fastaem man wea fil taem hem stretem bak.

## Tell

Hem askem onli wanfala samting blong evri leada an evri stretem: se tumora hem savve go aot long lagun, kasem fis, an kam bak hom seif long famili blong hem.
