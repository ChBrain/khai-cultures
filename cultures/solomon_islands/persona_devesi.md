# Persona: Sir Baddeley Devesi

## Taxonomy

[wantok na kastom](position_wantok_na_kastom.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Wanfala man blong Gwadalkanal wea go skul, kamap wanfala leada, an long taem blong independens long 1978 hem kamap fastaem Governor-General blong niufala neson, holem ples blong Kwin an blong neson seleva olsem wanfala simbol blong evri laen tugeta. Hem wanfala man wea garem manas, kwaet, an savve gud hao blong holem evriwan wanfala. Tok ples blong hem an [Pijin](position_language_pis.md) hem garem fulwan: hem [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) an [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md) long tok blong ples blong hem an long Pijin, tok wea joinim hem long evri wantok long aelan. English hem [karem olsem wanfala tok blong waka](process_writing_mother_tongue.md) an [ridim gud](process_reading_followed.md) long govman an long ofis blong Governor-General, an wanem ol bigman blong nara kantri raetem hem [ridim an folom](process_reading_deciphered.md) gud nomata olketa wod ia kam from farawe ples. Bat taem hem mas tokim olketa pipol blong ples blong hem wanem nao disfala niufala neson minim long bel blong olketa, English no garem ol toktok ia; long datfala edge tok lus, an hem nidim tok ples blong joinim olketa wantok long wanfala niufala samting wea no garem yet olfala tok blong hem.

## Action

Long [independens na Maasina Rul](plot_03_independens_na_maasina_rul.md) hem kamap fastaem Governor-General an holem ples blong neson olsem wanfala saet wea evri laen savve lukluk antap, holem [wantok na kastom](position_wantok_na_kastom.md) wanfala wetem niufala neson. Hem standap olsem wanfala simbol se Solomon man nao holem ples blong hed blong neson, no moa wanfala waitman.

## Shadow

Disfala man wea holem wanfala bigfala ples karem hevi tu: long wanfala neson blong planti aelan, wanfala simbol seleva no savve joinim olketa man sapos wantok an wantok pull each ada. Ples blong hed blong neson savve luk strong antap, bat hem mas garem strong rut long olketa laen aninit, o hem kamap nomoa wanfala titol.

## Tell

Hem holem wanfala bigfala ples wetem manas an kwaet, an luksave se ples ia hem no blong hem seleva, bat blong olgeta laen blong neson wea hem mas holem wanfala.
