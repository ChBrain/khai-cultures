# Process: kastom stretem

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Tufala laen wea garem trabol bitwin olketa, wea sidaon tugeta long wanfala bigfala kaikai, kilim pig, givim seleni blong sel, talem trutok, an stretem rong. Hem nidim ol elda blong tufala saet, wanfala ples blong sidaon, pig an seleni an kaikai, an evri man blong tufala laen blong kam an luk. Hem no waka blong wan man seleva: hem waka blong tufala laen tugeta.

## Direction

Go long stretem an joinim bak. Afta wanfala rong o wanfala faet, kastom stretem nao rod blong tekem awe kros an mekem tufala laen kam wanfala moa. Hem no minim fogetem wanem happen, bat minim talem hem aotsaed, peim hem long pig an seleni, an joinim olketa man bak blong olketa savve stap tugeta gen. Hem nao olfala wei blong olketa aelan blong fiksim trabol, longfala taem bifo kot o polis kam.

## Lever

Hem ran from disfala nid: long wanfala neson blong planti laen, trabol bitwin wantok an wantok savve brekem evri samting sapos pipol no stretem hem. Hem holem strong taem olketa saet wantem tru blong stretem an talem trutok; agensem hem standap kros wea pipol haedem long bel, taem olketa kilim pig an talem tok bat no stretem tru insaed, kasem stretem hem kamap nomoa wanfala seremoni antap.

## Echo

Kastom stretem mekem evriwan luksave wanem [bigfala faet na stretem](position_bigfala_faet_na_stretem.md) nidim: wanfala wei blong joinim bak olketa man afta faet, semsem long Wol Wo Tu o long ol Tensions. Long disfala wei nao [Sir Jacob Vouza](persona_vouza.md) standap, no blong faet moa, bat blong stretem an mekem laen an laen kam wanfala.
