# Plot: independens na Maasina Rul

## Taxonomy

[play_solomon_islands](play_solomon_islands.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Cue

Bihaen long Wol Wo Tu, olketa pipol blong Malaita an blong nara ples lukluk long bigfala wol wea kam an go, an statem askem mo se long olketa seleva. Long disfala taem nao Maasina Rul kamap, wanfala bigfala muvmen blong olketa pipol blong sapotem kastom, joinim olketa laen tugeta, an askem govman blong harem voes blong olketa.

## Action

Maasina Rul joinim plande pipol andanit long wanfala save: se olketa Solomon man mas garem mo se long olketa seleva, mas sapotem kastom blong olketa, an mas standap tugeta. Govman blong protektoret no laekem disfala muvmen fastaem, bat hem soem se olketa pipol wantem mo paoa long olketa seleva. Tru long olketa yia, govman givim olketa Solomon man mo an mo se long govman, kasem long 7 Julae 1978 neson ia kasem independens. [Sir Peter Kenilorea](persona_kenilorea.md) kamap fastaem Praem Minista, an [Sir Baddeley Devesi](persona_devesi.md) kamap fastaem Governor-General, holem ples blong hed blong neson. Long fastaem taem, Solomon man nao leadim Solomon Aelan.

## Stage

- [Sir Peter Kenilorea](persona_kenilorea.md): fastaem Praem Minista wea leadim neson go long independens.
- [Sir Baddeley Devesi](persona_devesi.md): fastaem Governor-General wea holem ples blong neson olsem simbol blong evri laen.
- [wantok na kastom](position_wantok_na_kastom.md): laen blong wantok an kastom wea Maasina Rul sapotem an wea niufala neson mas holem.

## Tension

Independens givim neson ia wanfala bigfala samting: long fastaem taem, Solomon man nao holem govman blong olketa seleva, an Maasina Rul soem se voes blong olketa pipol garem paoa. Bat hem givim wanfala had wok tu: blong wakem wanfala neson aot long planti aelan an planti laen, blong holem wantok an neson wanfala taem olketa sometaem pull each ada. Disfala had wok no finis long dei blong independens; hem nao statem nomoa.
