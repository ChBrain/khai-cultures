# Position: wantok na kastom

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Disfala save se neson ia hem no holem tugeta long wanfala tok ples nomoa, no long wanfala laen blong blad nomoa, bat long laen blong wantok an long kastom. Wantok minim olketa man wea sherem wan tok, wan laen, wan ples, an olketa man ia mas lukaftarem each ada. Kastom hem nao olketa olfala wei, olketa lo wea kam from bifo, wea talem hao blong stretem trabol, hao blong maritim, an hao blong sherem. Long wanfala kantri wea garem klosap wan handred tok ples, Pijin nao kamap tok wea brekem daon ol wol bitwin olketa laen, mekem se olketa man blong difren ples savve story tugeta. Wantok hem strong blong holem evriwan, bat hem garem hevi tu, bikos hem savve lukluk long laen fastaem winim neson.

## Orders

Se wan man hem no stap seleva, bat hem wanfala laen, an laen ia hem holem hem an hem mas lukaftarem laen tu; se kastom an ol olfala save hem garem paoa winim eni titol o ofis blong waitman; an se wanem wan man garem hem blong sherem wetem wantok, an blong holem ples blong sapos wan brata kam askem.

## Loses

Hem lus graon taem wantok kam mo strong winim neson, taem wan man putim laen blong hem fastaem an lukluk long narafala laen olsem enemi, olsem hem happen long ol Tensions. Hem lus graon tu taem wantok kamap wanfala chen wea pulum wan man bak, taem hem mas givim go go kasem hem no garem eni samting moa, o taem kastom kamap nomoa wanfala eskius blong no folom lo blong neson.

## Drives

Hem stap insaed long fastaem praem minista, wea leadim neson go long independens an traem blong holem wantok an neson tugeta long wanfala saet, an insaed long fastaem Governor-General, wea holem ples blong Kwin an blong neson olsem wanfala simbol blong evri laen tugeta. Hem pusum Solomon Aelan blong holem evri laen wanfala, an blong mekem wantok an neson stap saedbaesaed nomata olketa sometaem pull olsem tu rop.
