# Pitch: tok blong Solomon

## Taxonomy

Hem nao ki wea olketa plei stori blong [Solomon Aelan](play_solomon_islands.md) long hem. Hem no sentem eni samting blong wanem i happen, no Gwadalkanal, no independens, no ol Tensions. Hem sentem nomoa hao evriting saond, disfala wei wea evriting kam aot long hem.

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Tenor

Plei isi nomoa, sloa, an tugeta. Hem nao wanfala pipol wea no ranran, no haredap, bat tekem taem, lukluk long wantok fastaem. Eni man hem brata o sista insaed long laen blong wantok, an wanem wan man garem hem blong evriwan. Yu no mas standap antap long narawan; mo gud yu sidaon, story longfala, an stretem samting kwaet wei. Kastom an ol olfala save i pull mo strong winim eni titol o ofis. Aninit long disfala isi laef i garem wanfala save se laef hem no waka blong wan man seleva: hem waka blong wantok, an wan man no savve standap sapos hem no garem wantok bihaen long hem.

## Undertow

Aninit long disfala wantok i garem wanfala hevi tu. Wantok wea hem gud blong holem evriwan i savve kam wanfala chen tu, wea wan man wea garem samting mas givim go go go kasem hem no garem eni samting moa, an wan man wea wantem muvfowad i savve fil olsem wantok pulum hem bak. Sapos kala blong wan ples o wan aelan kam mo strong winim laen blong neson, hem savve brekem pis, olsem hem happen long ol Tensions. Long disfala save se evriwan mas stap tugeta i garem wei tu blong haedem disagri an pusum trabol go andanit kasem hem kamap bigwan.

## Nerve

Hem stretem long disfala save: yu no yu seleva nomoa, bat yu wanfala laen, an laen blong yu hem holem yu. Hem plei long prout blong wantok, long save se yu garem ples blong yu an pipol blong yu, an se nomata yu farawe yu no lus. Hem plei tu long disfala save se nomata trabol kam, evriwan savve sidaon, story, kilim pig, an stretem bak; an long hevi aninit, se sometaem wantok i pull tumas an wan man i mas givim winim wanem hem garem.

## Echo

Wanem i stap bihaen hem nao wanfala pipol wea holem wantok strong: isi, tugeta, sloa, an luk long kastom. Wanfala neson wea ran long save se evri trabol savve stretem long sidaon an story, an se nomata planti aelan an planti tok divaidim olketa, wantok an kastom holem olketa wanfala. Wan pipol wea bilivim se wan man hem nating sapos hem standap seleva, bat hem strong sapos olketa man blong hem standap wetem hem.
