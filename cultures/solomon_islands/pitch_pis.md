# Pitch: tok blong Solomon

## Taxonomy

Hem nao ki wea olketa plei stori blong [Solomon Aelan](play_solomon_islands.md) long hem. Hem no sentem eni samting blong wanem i happen, no Gwadalkanal, no independens, no ol Tensions. Hem sentem nomoa hao evriting saond, disfala wei wea evriting kam aot long hem.

## Tenor

Plei witim tufala kaen pipol insaed long wanfala kantri: solwata pipol wea laef klosap solwata an fising, an bus pipol wea laef antap long maunten wetem gaden blong olketa. Tufala save eksenjem samting fastaem winim eni gavman rul: solwata i sendem fish an sel mane i go antap, bus i sendem taro, yam, an pig i go daon, an from displa eksenj nao fren an bisnis i statem. Long Langalanga Lagun, ol savve mekem sel mane long smol sel wea olketa polisim gud, an displa mane i no just mane blong pei samting, hem wanfala rekod blong praes blong marit, promis, an fren wea joinim tufala famili. Wantok i no minim man blong wan viles nomoa, hem minim brata o sista blong yu nomata hemi stap long nara aelan farawe long solwata, an sapos yu tekem sip go long narapela ples, wantok blong yu save openem doa fo yu an sherem kaikai olsem yu stap long haos blong yu seleva. Taem trabol i kamap namel long tufala lain, kastom i garem rod blong stretem hem: olketa lida save mit tugeta, storyim samting stret, mekem seramoni blong kompensesen, an long en tufala lain savve sekhan bak.

## Undertow

Aninit long displa eksenj hemi garem presa tu: sam taem solwata i savve tingting se bus i no givimbak inaf, bus i savve tingting se solwata i tekem mo winim wanem hem salim, an displa balans hemi no oltaim ese fo holem. Sel mane blong Langalanga i garem presa tu, praes blong marit savve kamap bighaus, an famili savve dinim planti yia fo peim bak fulwan. An taem gavman lo an kot i traem stretem trabol bifoa kastom fasin i redi fo mekem waka blong hem, olsem long taem blong ol Tensions, tufala rod savve klaes, an trabol i tekem longtaem moa blong hem stretem gud. Wantok wea openem doa fo aelan farawe savve kamap hevi tu, staka man i mas givim mo winim wanem hem garem fo holem promis blong wantok, an sam taem trabol i stap hipap kwaet bifoa hem brekaot bigwan.

## Nerve

Hem stretem long displa save: solwata an bus tufala i nidim narawan, an eksenj hem nao pawa wea joinim tufala lain wetim narawan. Sel mane hem no just mane, hem promis wea go daon fo staka jenareson, an sel mane blong Langalanga stap olsem wanfala buk wea rekodim laef blong famili. Hem plei long prout blong seramoni blong kastom wea savve stretem eni trabol, nomata hemi bigwan olsem ol Tensions, taem pipol sidaon fesim each narawan, storyim trutru, an mekem kompensesen kwaet wei. Hem plei tu long wantok wea joinim aelan tugeta akros solwata, doa wea open fo eni brata o sista nomata hao fawe hemi stap.

## Echo

Wanem i stap bihaen hem nao wanfala pipol wea holem solwata an bus tugeta wetem eksenj blong fish an gaden, wea sel mane blong Langalanga rekodim promis blong marit an fren, an wea kastom seramoni, no kot, savve stretem trabol we deep olsem ol Tensions. Wantok i joinim olketa akros solwata gap blong evri aelan, mekem se nomata planti aelan an planti ples divaidim olketa, wantok an kastom holem olketa tugeta olsem wanfala.
