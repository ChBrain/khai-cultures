# Place: Langa Langa

## Taxonomy

Parent group: places

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Shown

Wanfala lagun long wes kos blong Malaita, wea ol solwata pipol stap long olketa aelan wea olketa wakem olketa seleva wetem han, ston antap long ston antap long rif, kasem aelan kamap antap long si. Long disfala lagun ol man wakem tafuliae, seleni blong sel, brekem sel an stringim hem kasem hem kamap longfala seleni. Hia nao solwata pipol blong Langa Langa stap saedbaesaed long bus pipol blong insaed graon, an tredem fis an seleni blong kasem kaikai blong gaden. Hem nao ples wea wanfala olfala save blong wakem aelan an wakem seleni hem stap yet laef.

## Offers

Wanfala ples wea neson ia luksave hu hem from solwata pipol an from olfala save: ples wea man savve wakem wanfala aelan antap long si wetem onli han, ples wea seleni blong sel kam from, an ples wea solwata an bus joinim tru long tred. Hem givim aelan ia wanfala save blong han, blong sel, an blong wei blong joinim olketa man wea no semsem.

## Withheld

Hem no givim wanfala save wea bae stap fri foeva. Disfala olfala save blong wakem aelan an wakem seleni hem nidim pipol blong holem hem an pasim hem go long olketa pikinini; sapos yangfala go long taon an no lanem, sapos seleni kamap nomoa wanfala samting blong tourist, disfala save savve klosap lus. Langa Langa holem disfala save se olfala samting hem savve laef onli sapos pipol holem hem strong.
