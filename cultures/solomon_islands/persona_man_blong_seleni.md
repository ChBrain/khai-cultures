# Persona: man blong seleni

## Taxonomy

[solwata na bus](position_solwata_na_bus.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Projection

Wanfala solwata man blong Langa Langa Lagoon, wea stap antap long wanfala aelan wea olketa wakem wetem han antap long rif, an wea laef blong hem kam from wakem tafuliae, seleni blong sel. Hem save tekem sel, brekem hem, holem hem, mekem hol insaed, an stringim hem kasem hem kamap longfala seleni wea olketa yusum long maritim an long peimen an long stretem. Hem garem disfala save wea ol olfala givim go go go kasem hem, han go long han. Tok ples blong hem an [Pijin](position_language_pis.md) hem garem fulwan: hem [toktok](process_speaking_mother_tongue.md), [tingting](process_thinking_mother_tongue.md), an [harem](process_hearing_mother_tongue.md) long tok blong solwata pipol blong hem an long Pijin, an evri save blong wakem seleni hem kam long hem tru long disfala tok, no tru long buk. English wea kam wetem ol tourist an ol man blong baem seleni hem [kasem long ear](process_speaking_borrowed.md) nomoa, wanfala wod ia an wanfala wod ia, blong talem praes nomoa. Bat ol nem blong evri kaen sel, evri kala, an wanem each kaen seleni minim long kastom, ol samting ia stap onli long tok ples; long datfala edge tok lus, an wanfala man wea no garem disfala tok no savve harem wanem disfala seleni i tok tru. Hem holem [wei blong Solomon Aelan](position_culture_wei_blong_solomon_aelan.md) tru long han blong hem, wakem seleni wea joinim solwata pipol an bus pipol antap long wanfala aelan wea garem planti tok ples bat wanfala wei.

## Action

Long [ol aelan blong Solomon](plot_01_ol_aelan_blong_solomon.md) hem nao man wea wakem seleni blong sel, wanfala save wea ol solwata pipol holem longfala taem, an wea joinim solwata an bus tru long tred. Hem karem [solwata na bus](position_solwata_na_bus.md) tru long han blong hem, wakem disfala seleni wea hem rod blong maritim, peimen, an stretem long olketa aelan.

## Shadow

Disfala save savve lus: sapos yangfala go long taon an no lanem hao blong wakem seleni, sapos seleni kamap nomoa wanfala samting blong tourist an no moa wanfala tru seleni blong kastom, disfala olfala save wea olketa holem longfala taem savve foldaon long wan o tu jeneresen nomoa.

## Tell

Hem savve luk wanfala stringgi blong seleni an talem yu hu nao wakem hem, from wea, an wanem hem fit blong, semsem olsem narafala man savve ridim wanfala raeting.
