# Process: au

## Taxonomy

Parent group: processes

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Initiated by

Wanfala grup blong man wea garem ol bambu paep blong difren saes, sidaon o standap tugeta, an pleim olketa wanfala kasem wanfala bigfala saon kamap. Wan man no savve pleim au seleva: each man holem wan o tu not, an onli taem evriwan pleim tugeta long stret taem nao miusik kamap. Hem nidim onli wanfala grup, ol bambu paep, an olketa man wea save folom each ada.

## Direction

Go long wanfala save se miusik kam from grup, no from wan man seleva. Au hem soem hao wantok waka: each man garem onli wan smol pat, bat taem evriwan joinim ol smol pat tugeta long stret taem, wanfala bigfala saon kamap wea no wan man savve mekem seleva. Hem nao miusik blong olketa solwata pipol an bus pipol blong olketa aelan, wea olketa pleim long olketa hapi taem an long olketa kastom seremoni.

## Lever

Hem ran from disfala nid blong wanfala wei blong soem se evriwan mas joinim tugeta blong samting bigwan i kamap. Hem holem strong taem pipol pleim tugeta an lanem yangfala blong holem ol not blong olketa; agensem hem standap miusik blong rajio an blong taon, wea wan man savve harem seleva an no nidim eni nara man blong joinim hem.

## Echo

Au mekem evriwan luksave wanem [solwata na bus](position_solwata_na_bus.md) nidim: wanfala wei blong soem se olketa man blong difren ples savve joinim tugeta an mekem wanfala samting wea no wan man savve mekem seleva. Long disfala miusik nao [man blong fising](persona_man_blong_fising.md) joinim wantok blong hem, holem wan smol pat blong wanfala bigfala saon.
