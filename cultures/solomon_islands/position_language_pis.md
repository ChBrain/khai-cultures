# Position: Pijin

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Disfala paoa blong Pijin, tok wea klosap evri man an woman long Solomon Aelan savve, wea joinim evri wantok namel long staka tok ples blong ol aelan, tok ples blong Malaita, blong Gwadalkanal, blong Western, blong Makira an blong evri ples. Hem no tok ples blong wanfala laen nomoa, bat tok wea evriwan tekem blong story wetem narafala laen wea no garem sem tok ples. Pijin hem bon long taem bifo, taem ol pipol kam tugeta long plantesin an long maket, an hem grow kamap tok blong evriwan, wanfala tok wea holem ol pipol blong staka aelan wanfala andanit long wanfala flag. English nao stap antap olsem tok blong govman an blong skul, bat Pijin nao stap long bel blong pipol, long stret an long kastom haos, tok blong evri dei laef.

## Orders

Hem markem tok wea joinim disfala niufala neson namel long staka tok ples: tok blong maket, tok blong rod, tok blong rajio, an tok wea tufala man blong tufala difren ples yusum blong minim wanfala narafala. Hem talem wanem tok nao evriwan savve harem taem ol laen kam tugeta, mekem se ol pipol wea no garem sem tok ples savve stanap long wanfala graon, story tugeta, an wakem wanfala samting tugeta. Nomata English nao stap long pepa blong govman, Pijin nao karem tok blong pipol go kasem evri kona blong kantri, an hem mekem staka laen kamap wanfala neson.

## Loses

Hem lusim graon long ol tok ples blong ples an blong kastom haos, tok blong Malaita, blong Gwadalkanal an blong evri aelan, wea insaed long olketa nao pipol tingting, prea, kaont, an raorao, an wea Pijin no savve holem gud olgeta save blong solwata, ol nem blong fis, an ol nem blong wind an taed. Hem lusim graon tu long English blong skul an blong ofis, wea olketa holem antap olsem tok blong paoa an blong pepa, olsem sapos Pijin hem no wanfala tok stret bat wanfala English wea brokbrok nomoa. An taem ol pikinini long taon stat blong tok English fastaem, tok ples blong olketa an Pijin blong olketa save go daon lelebet.

## Drives

Hem pushim disfala neson blong tok wetem wanfala voes wea joinim evri wantok antap long staka tok ples blong olketa. Ol wea holem hem fulwan: [Peter Kenilorea](persona_kenilorea.md), fastaem Praem Minista, wea toktok Pijin an tok ples blong hem tugeta; [Baddeley Devesi](persona_devesi.md), fastaem Governor-General, wea holem ples blong neson long Pijin an tok ples; [Jacob Vouza](persona_vouza.md), skaut blong Wol Wo Tu, wea yusum Pijin blong story wetem evri wantok; [man blong fising](persona_man_blong_fising.md), solwata man blong lagun; an [man blong seleni](persona_man_blong_seleni.md), wea wakem tafuliae long Langa Langa. Evriwan blong olketa garem tok ples blong olketa long bel, bat Pijin nao tok wea joinim olketa wetem ol narafala wantok. Olsem, disfala tok blong evriwan hem soem hu nao holem hem, an hao evri laen savve kam tugeta kamap wanfala neson.
