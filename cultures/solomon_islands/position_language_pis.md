# Position: Pijin

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

Pijin i holem wanfala sistem blong pronaon wea gud moa bitim English: hem no jes sei "yumi", hem save sei stret hamas man i stap insaed, tufala nomoa (yumitufala), tri (yumitrifala), o staka (yumifala), an hem save soem sapos man wea harem tok hem stap insaed tu (yumi) o hem aotsaed (mifala). Hem save joinim tufala vaeb kamap wanfala tingting nomoa taem hem putum tufala vaeb saedbaesaed, olsem "hem go kaikai" wea minim hem go blong kaikai, an hem save daboldaboldaon wanfala vaeb blong soem se samting i gohed evritaem, olsem wakabaot, lukluk-lukluk, wanfala save wea wanfala vaeb nomoa long English no save karem isi.

## Orders

Hem markem wanfala paticel blong taem i mas kam bifo evri vaeb, bikos vaeb seleva no save jenis: "bin" blong soem samting we i bin hapen bifo, "bae" blong soem samting we bae i hapen biaen, "stap" blong soem samting we i stap hapen nao. Evri taem man i tok, hem mas sanem stret hamas man i stap insaed long "yumi" o "mifala" blong tok, an "long" nao stap olsem wanfala wed nomoa blong soem ples, taem, tul, o wei, insted blong staka defren wed olsem long English.

## Loses

Hem no garem kes o jenda long nating, olsem wanfala nem i sem mak nomoa nomata em i savve blong wanem samting o wanem wok, an olsem, staka fain save wea English o narafala tok save mekem wetem endin blong wed, Pijin mas putum evriwan insaed long "long" wetem "bilong" nomoa, samting wea save mekem sam save i go tugeta lelebet. Hem no garem oldfala raeting blong hemseleva, hem yusum leta blong English, an hem no save holem gud ol nem blong fis, wind, an taed blong tok ples blong wan wan aelan, samting wea i stap strong tumas insaed long tok ples nomoa.

## Drives

Hem mekem man wea tok Pijin evritaem save stret hamas man i stap insaed long tok, an hu nao insaed an hu nao aotsaed, bifo hem tok. Hem trenem ia blong harem taem long wanfala smol paticel bifo vaeb, no long endin blong vaeb, an hem mekem simpol tingting kamap kastom: wanfala wed olsem "long" savve karem staka wok wea narafala tok mas seraotem long staka defren wed.
