# Moldova: a khai culture

Moldova staged as a full khai play, ISO MD (a sovereign state), language ro. The land between the Prut and Dniester rivers as a theatre: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its borderland and agricultural life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it. This culture logs the house as its Estate; a culture with no Estate is not yet a production.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_moldova.md](play_moldova.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_moldova.md](pitch_moldova.md), the Hofstede layer, written from Moldova's profile inflected by its position at the crossroads of empires and naming no dimension.
- **Plots (history):** the Principality of Moldavia under Stephen the Great, the annexation of Bessarabia by the Russian Empire and Soviet period, and the independence and Transnistria conflict — each casting the elements that event needs.
- **Personas:** Ștefan cel Mare as the historic prince, plus defining personas in generational tension: Radu (older, traditional winemaker) and Elena (younger, Chișinău student), written with Romanian/Moldovan language reads.
- **Positions:** das Grenzland (the borderland identity), das Moldauische (the Romanian variety), der Weinbauer (the winemaker).
- **Places:** Chișinău (the capital), Mileștii Mici (the wine cellars).
- **Processes:** die Weinherstellung (winemaking).
- **Pieces:** der Mărțișor (the spring talisman).
