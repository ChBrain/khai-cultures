# Moldova: References

The provenance behind this culture: the Hofstede source data that tunes the pitch (kept here, never in the prose) and the public-domain historical sources behind the plots, personas, places, and pieces.

## Hofstede source data

The pitch [pitch_moldova.md](pitch_moldova.md) is written from Moldova's profile on Geert Hofstede's model of national culture — it names no dimension and carries no number. The scores and their source are recorded here so the data is on the record without entering the prose.

| Dimension                     | Moldova score (approx.) |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Power Distance                | 85                      |
| Individualism                 | 20                      |
| Motivation toward Achievement | 30                      |
| Uncertainty Avoidance         | 90                      |
| Long Term Orientation         | 65                      |
| Indulgence                    | 15                      |

Source: Geert Hofstede, Hofstede Insights (the Country Comparison tool and the six-dimensions model of national culture). Scores are approximate, as published by Hofstede Insights / The Culture Factor Group.
