# Extremadura: a khai culture

Extremadura staged as a full khai play, ISO ES-EX, language es. Extremadura: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its Conquistador Depopulation, Plan Badajoz, and Dehesa Oak Decree encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_extremadura.md](play_extremadura.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_extremadura.md](pitch_extremadura.md), the Extremadura key.
- **Plots (history):** the Conquistador Depopulation of the 16th century, the Plan Badajoz of 1952, and the Dehesa oak preservation decree.
- **Personas:** Jeronimo (Iberian pig shepherd) and Maria (La Vera paprika farmer), written in Spanish.
- **Positions:** dehesa stewardship (holm/cork oak savanna, pig grazing, cork harvest), borderland reliance (arid borderland reliance, pastoralism).
- **Places:** Monfragüe, La Vera Gorges.
- **Processes:** paprika smoking (paprika smoking), dehesa grazing (montanera acorn grazing).
- **Pieces:** paprika tin, cork bark container.
- **Plan:** the Dehesa sustainable forestry plan.
