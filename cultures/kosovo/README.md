# Kosovo (Kosova): a khai culture

Kosovo staged as a full khai play, ISO XK, language sq. The young sovereign republic of the Balkans: its history as the plot line, its people as the cast, the roles its resilient and hospitable life encodes, its places, its practices, and its defining objects, all tuned by a single pitch.

## Estate

[khai-cultures](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-cultures): the Cultures house that holds this culture and answers for it.

## The production

- **Anchor:** [play_kosovo.md](play_kosovo.md), the culture itself.
- **Pitch:** [pitch_kosovo.md](pitch_kosovo.md), the Kosovo key.
- **Plots (history):** the Battle of Kosovo in 1389, the League of Prizren in 1878, and the declaration of independence in 2008.
- **Personas:** Adem (traditional farmer/rhapsodist archetype, representing memory and land) and Besa (modern human rights lawyer/diplomat), written in Albanian.
- **Positions:** besa (the sworn word / honor), Kosova (the homeland), ndërtimi i shtetit (state-building).
- **Places:** Prishtina (the vibrant capital), Rugova (the rugged mountain canyon).
- **Processes:** pjekja e flisë (the baking of flija, a traditional layered dish).
- **Pieces:** plisi (the white felt cap), çiftelia (the two-stringed instrument).
- **Plan:** njohja ndërkombëtare (international recognition and integration).
