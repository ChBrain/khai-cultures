# Position: four states, one nation

## Taxonomy

Parent group: positions

## Owner

- Project: khai-cultures

## Has

The proposition that the nation is held together not by one people or one tongue, but by a choice: four distinct island cultures - Yap, Chuuk, Pohnpei, and Kosrae - each with its own language, its own clans, and its own ways, agreeing to stand as one Micronesia. It is a federation made, not inherited, where the states keep most of the power and the federal frame is the thin shared roof above them. English is the bridge across the four tongues, the language of the constitution, the capitol, and the school, the one speech the whole nation has in common. And it is a unity founded in difference: the very distance between the states - in language, in custom, in island - is both what makes the federation remarkable and what forever threatens to pull it back into four.

## Orders

That the nation is a partnership of equals among unlike peoples, so no state and no tongue rules the rest, and the small island is weighed with the large; that the federal frame must be kept deliberately light, leaving each state its own custom and its own clan law; and that the work of the country is the patient holding-together of four that could so easily be four again.

## Loses

Ground when the differences harden into rivalry - when a state pulls back toward its own island and its own tongue and reckons what it gives to the union greater than what it gains; and ground, too, when the thin federal frame is asked to carry more than four proud peoples will grant it, so the roof strains against the walls that hold it up, and the choice to be one nation is quietly unmade.

## Drives

Lives in [Tosiwo Nakayama](persona_tosiwo_nakayama.md), the leader who drew the four states into one federation and gave the union its first shape, and in [the Chuukese clan mother](persona_the_chuukese_clan_mother.md), who keeps the matrilineal clan that is the deepest belonging beneath the nation. It drives the Federated States of Micronesia to hold its four apart-and-together, and to prove that unlike peoples can choose to be one.
