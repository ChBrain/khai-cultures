# Instructions: Canon

The law of this house, declared once so it reads system-wide. It sits above the
coding contract in `CLAUDE.md` and `GEMINI.md` (branching, versioning, the
gates) and names what the canon requires of every culture and group: the
language engine each persona reads through, the casting that must resolve, and
the Estate that answers for the run. The chapters spell HACKS: Human, Agent,
Collaboration, Knowledge, System.

## Human

- Sets the requirements: which culture, which event, which tongue.
- Reviews and approves the staging before it lands.
- Holds all authority to merge and deploy; the gate is theirs to open.

## Agent

- Works the lane the guard computes, never one chosen by hand, and stays inside
  its scope.
- Stages in canon: every instance fields its khai type whole, in the culture's
  own tongue, and enacts rather than narrates.
- Never `--no-verify`, never merges; opens the pull request and stops.

## Collaboration

- One change, one lane, one pull request: `culture/*` owns `cultures/**`,
  `group/*` owns `groups/**`, `governance/*` owns the gates and this file.
- The language-engine and plans rollout runs **one culture per pull request,
  sequential** (do one, merge, the next), mirroring the build phase; each is a
  non-count enrichment shipped with a `patch` changeset.
- A culture casts within its own play; a group plot casts its member plays so the
  cross-culture link resolves. Every cast must resolve to a real owned file.

## Knowledge

- **The engine law.** A persona uses language through the ladder rooted at
  [Using Language](process_using_language.md): five channels - speaking and
  writing outward, hearing and reading inward, thinking underneath - each held at
  the width it truly has, from the tongue gripped as a foreign object to the
  tongue that needs no grip because it is already the body. Every persona reads
  **per language, per channel, at its true width**, and the prose enacts the
  **gap**: what is heard is not what is understood, what is understood is not what
  is said. That gap is where a culture is lost at the limits of a tongue, and it
  is shown in the first beat, never named.
- **Plans field the canon's `plan` type:** a deliberate national scheme a culture
  carries, with Direction, Orders, Implementation, and a Targets checklist of
  canon verdicts, listed in the play's Company and not counted in the version.
- `README.md` is the house's Estate identity: every culture and group logs the
  house in its Estate, and the link must resolve.

## System

- `main` is protected: the pull request and the gate checks (`khai-tests`,
  `khai-guard`, `khai-branch-scope`) are required before merge.
- The minor version IS the culture count, computed by the registry build, the
  single writer of the version; never hand-edit it.
- Content is CC-BY-NC-SA, code is MIT; public-domain sources are credited where
  they stand, never claimed.
