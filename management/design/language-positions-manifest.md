# Language-position rollout manifest

standard is in `REFERENCES.md` and `language-engine-and-plans.md` §2.6).

the `language:` markers across that culture's content files** (personas drive
the set). 304 positions across 286 cultures; 274 cultures are single-language,
12 multilingual. Pilot landed: Switzerland (split to 4), France (add), Germany
(rename). The rest follow one batch at a time, gates green per batch.
