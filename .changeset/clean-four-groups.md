Add four khai groups over existing cultures: Benelux (Belgium, the Netherlands, Luxembourg), the Visegrad Four (Poland, Czechia, Slovakia, Hungary), the Baltics (Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania), and Iberia (Spain, Portugal). Each is a referencing play (no geo, no pitch) whose membership the registry build derives from its casts, and each carries one shared cross-culture plot: the Benelux customs union, the 1991 Visegrad Declaration, the 1989 Baltic Way, and the 1494 Treaty of Tordesillas. Referencing collections do not move the culture count, so this ships at the same count.
