Add the NATO group: the transatlantic alliance of all thirty-two members, the largest group in the house. A referencing play (no geo, no pitch) whose membership the registry build derives from its casts, listing every ally with its accession year. Unlike the other groups, its four plots trace NATO's growth and change rather than a single event: the Founding (1949), the Cold War Line (the 1952-1982 enlargements), Out of Area or Out of Business (the post-1991 reinvention and the only Article 5 invocation after 9/11), and the Return East (the eastward enlargement and the 2023-2024 accession of Finland and Sweden).
